Pierre Achille Webó Kouamo (born 20 January 1982) is a Cameroonian football manager and former professional player who played as a striker. He is the assistant manager of Turkish club İstanbul Başakşehir. He spent eight years of his career in La Liga, appearing in a combined 227 matches for Osasuna and Mallorca and scoring 47 goals (in Spain he also represented Leganés). He also competed professionally in Uruguay and Turkey. A Cameroon international in the 2000s, Webó appeared with the country in two World Cups and as many Africa Cup of Nations.
