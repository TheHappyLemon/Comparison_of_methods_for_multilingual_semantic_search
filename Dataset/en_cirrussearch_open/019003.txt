Nshan Sergeyevich Galustyan (Russian: Ншан Сергеевич Галустян; born 25 October 1979), known professionally as Mikhail Galustyan (Russian: Михаил Галустян), is a Russian-Armenian showman, comedian, screenwriter, and producer. Mikhail Galustyan was also a former participant in KVN and a show called Comedy Club. Awards Champion League KVN 2003, Winner of the Summer Cup of KVN 2004, 2005, 2009.
