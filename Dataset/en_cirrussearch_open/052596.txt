Iryna Volodymyrivna Koliadenko (Ukrainian: Ірина Володимирівна Коляденко, born 28 August 1998) is a Ukrainian freestyle wrestler. She won one of the bronze medals in the women's freestyle 62 kg event at the 2020 Summer Olympics held in Tokyo, Japan. In 2019, Koliadenko won the silver medal at the World Wrestling Championships held in Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan in the women's freestyle 65 kg event. She is also a three-time gold medalist at the European Wrestling Championships.
