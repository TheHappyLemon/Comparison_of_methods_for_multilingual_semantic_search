Walid Regragui (Arabic: وليد الركراكي; born 23 September 1975), sometimes known as Hoalid or Oualid Regragui, is a Moroccan professional football manager and former player who played as a right-back. He is the head coach of the Morocco national team. He made history when he led the team to the semi-finals of the FIFA World Cup 2022, the first African nation to do so.
