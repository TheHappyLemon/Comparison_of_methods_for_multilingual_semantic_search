Dimitar Kovačevski (Macedonian: Димитар Ковачевски, pronounced [di'mitar kɔ'vat͡ʃɛfski]; born 24 July 1974) is a Macedonian politician and economist who served as prime minister of North Macedonia from January 2022 to January 2024. A member and currently president of the Social Democratic Union of Macedonia, Kovačevski previously served as deputy finance minister from 2020 until his appointment as prime minister in 2022 after the resignation of Zoran Zaev.
