Munir Mohand Mohamedi El Kajoui (Arabic: منير مهند محمدي الكجوي; born 10 May 1989), known simply as Munir, is a professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Saudi Professional League club Al Wehda and the Morocco national team. He played for Ceuta B, Almería B, Melilla, Numancia, Málaga, Hatayspor and Al Wehda. Born in Spain, Munir represented Morocco at international level, making his debut in 2015 and being part of the squads at two World Cups and four Africa Cup of Nations tournaments.
