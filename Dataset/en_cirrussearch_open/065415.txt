Club Atlético Talleres (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈkluβ aˈtletiko taˈʎeɾes]; lit. 'Workshops Athletic Club'), mostly known simply as Talleres [taˈʎeɾes]; lit. 'Workshops') or (Talleres de Córdoba [taˈʎeɾes ðe ˈkoɾðoβa]; Córdoba Workshops), is an Argentine professional sports club based the city of Córdoba. The institution is mostly known for its football team, which currently plays in the Argentine Primera División. Talleres' main rival is Belgrano: Their rivalry is known as "el clásico cordobés". In field hockey, the club is affiliated to amateur Córdoba Field Hockey Federation, where its teams compete.
