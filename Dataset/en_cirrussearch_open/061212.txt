Hadiach (Ukrainian: Га́дяч, romanized: Hadyach, pronounced [ˈɦɑdʲɐt͡ʃ]), sometimes spelled Hadyach, Gadyach, Gadiach, Haditch, or Hadziacz, is a city in Myrhorod Raion, Poltava Oblast (province) in the central-east part of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Hadiach urban hromada [uk], one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Hadiach is located on the Psel River. Population: 22,851 (2022 estimate).
