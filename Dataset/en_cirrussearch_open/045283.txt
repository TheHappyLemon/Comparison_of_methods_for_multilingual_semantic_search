An Yangfeng (Chinese: 安艳凤; born 1 September 1963) is a Chinese chess player, who holds the title Woman International Master. She competed in two Women's Interzonal tournaments, at Havana in 1985 and at Smederevska Palanka in 1987. An was a member of the Chinese national team at the Women's Chess Olympiad four times in 1980−1986. She played a total of 53 games scoring 28 wins, 13 draws, and 12 losses. An Yangfeng plays for the Guangdong chess club in the China Chess League (CCL).
