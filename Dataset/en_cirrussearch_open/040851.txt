Jonas Armin Hector (German pronunciation: [ˈjoːnas ˈhɛktoːɐ̯]; born 27 May 1990) is a German former professional footballer. Hector played the entirety of his senior professional career for German club 1. FC Köln, making 347 appearances for the side across an eleven-year period. He also represented the Germany national team from 2014 to 2019.
