Zaslawye or Zaslavl (Belarusian: Заслаўе, romanized: Zaslaŭje, IPA: [zaˈsɫau̯je]; Russian: Заславль; Polish: Zasław; Lithuanian: Zaslavlis) is a town in Minsk District, Minsk Region, Belarus. It is located 20 kilometres (12 mi) northwest of Minsk. In 2009, its population was 14,400. As of 2023, it has a population of 17,419.
