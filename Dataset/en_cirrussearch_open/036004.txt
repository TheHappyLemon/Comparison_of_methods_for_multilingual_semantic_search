Alexander Onischuk (also known as Alexandr Onyschuk, Ukrainian: Олександр Васильович Оніщук, romanized: Oleksandr Vasylʹovych Onishchuk; Russian: Александр Васильевич Онищук, romanized: Aleksandr Vasil'evich Onishchuk; born September 3, 1975) is a Soviet-Ukrainian-American chess player. He was awarded the title of Grandmaster by FIDE in 1994, and won the 2006 U.S. championship.
