Surazh (Russian: Сураж) is a town and the administrative center of Surazhsky District in Bryansk Oblast, Russia, located on the Iput River 177 kilometers (110 mi) southwest of Bryansk, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: 11,176 (2021 Census); 11,640 (2010 Census); 12,046 (2002 Census); 12,559 (1989 Census); 1,599 (1897).
