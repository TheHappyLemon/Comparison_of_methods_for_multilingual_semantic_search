Skvyra (Ukrainian: Скви́ра, pronounced [ˈskʋɪrɐ]) is a city in Bila Tserkva Raion, Kyiv Oblast (region) of central Ukraine. Skvyra has an area of 6,328 km2 (2,443 sq mi). It hosts the administration of Skvyra urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Its population is approximately 15,165 (2022 estimate).
