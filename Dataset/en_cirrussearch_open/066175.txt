Melitopol Museum of Local History (Ukrainian: Мелітопольський краєзнавчий музей, romanized: Melitopol's'kyy krayeznavchyy muzey) is a museum in Melitopol, Ukraine. It exhibits objects relating to the history and nature of the region. The museum is located in the former Chernikov Mansion, built in 1913. As of 2022 its director was Leila Ibragimova.
