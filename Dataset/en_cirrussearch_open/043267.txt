Seyed Saman Ghoddoos (Persian: سامان قدّوس; born 6 September 1993) is an Iranian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or striker for Premier League club Brentford and the Iran national team. A versatile player, Ghoddos can be deployed as a winger, full-back or wing-back. Born in Sweden to Iranian parents, he initially played for the Sweden national team, before switching allegiance to Iran in 2017.
