Rahachow or Rogachev (Belarusian: Рагачо́ў, romanized: Rahačoŭ, IPA: [raɣaˈtʂou̯]; Russian: Рогачёв, romanized: Rogachyov; Polish: Rohaczów; Yiddish: ראגאטשאוו, IPA: [ragat͡ʃov]) is a town in Gomel Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Rahachow District. Rahachow is located between the Drut and Dnieper rivers. As of 2023, it has a population of 32,029.
