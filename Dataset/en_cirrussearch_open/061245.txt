Odesa Sevastopol Kyiv Kerch Volnovakha Hostomel Mariupol Kharkiv Kherson Chernihiv Bucha Irpin Okhtyrka Mykolaiv Hero City of Ukraine (Ukrainian: Місто-герой України, romanized: Misto-heroi Ukrainy) is a Ukrainian honorary title awarded for outstanding heroism during the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine. It was awarded to ten cities in March 2022, in addition to four already-named Hero Cities of the Soviet Union. This symbolic distinction for a city corresponds to the distinction of Hero of Ukraine awarded to individuals.
