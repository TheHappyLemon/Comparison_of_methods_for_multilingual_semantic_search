Sergi Roberto Carnicer (Catalan pronunciation: [ˈsɛɾʒi ruˈβɛɾtu kəɾniˈse], born 7 February 1992) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for La Liga club Barcelona, which he captains. Mainly a central midfielder, he can also operate as a defensive midfielder, full-back or winger. He has spent most of his career with Barcelona after making his first-team debut at the age of 18, going on to win several accolades with the club including seven La Liga championships and two Champions Leagues. Roberto made his first full appearance for Spain in 2016.
