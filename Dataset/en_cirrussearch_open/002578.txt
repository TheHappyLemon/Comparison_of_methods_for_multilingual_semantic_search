Milan Stanković (Serbian Cyrillic: Милан Станковић; born 9 September 1987) is a Serbian singer-songwriter. He rose to prominence as a finalist on Zvezde Granda in 2007. Moreover, Stanković acquired wider fame by representing Serbia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 in Oslo, Norway with "Ovo je Balkan", finishing in 13th place. Additionally to his recording career, he served as a judge on the children's singing competition Pinkove Zvezdice (2014–2018).
