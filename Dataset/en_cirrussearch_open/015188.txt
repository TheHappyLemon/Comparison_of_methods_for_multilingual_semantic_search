Andrei Pervyshin (born February 2, 1985) is a Russian professional ice hockey player who currently plays Avangard Omsk in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was selected by St. Louis Blues in the 8th round (253rd overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. Pervyshin has previously played with HC CSKA Moscow for the 2013–14 season, before signing a one-year contract with expansion club HC Sochi on May 21, 2014. Peryshin was later selected as Sochi's inaugural team Captain for the 2014–15 season.
