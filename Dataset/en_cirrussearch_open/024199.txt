Berezan (Ukrainian: Березань, Polish: Berezań, Yiddish: בערעזאַן, Russian: Березань) is a city in Brovary Raion, Kyiv Oblast (region) of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Berezan urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 16,047 (2022 estimate). There is an important railway junction in the city. By rail, the distance to Kiev is 65 km.
