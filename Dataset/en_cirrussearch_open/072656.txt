Ozoliņa (Old orthography: O(h)solin(g); masculine: Ozoliņš) is a Latvian surname, derived from the Latvian word for "oak" (ozols). Individuals with the surname include: Agnese Ozoliņa (born 1979), Latvian swimmer Elvīra Ozoliņa (born 1939), Soviet javelin thrower Sinta Ozoliņa-Kovala (born 1988), Latvian javelin thrower
