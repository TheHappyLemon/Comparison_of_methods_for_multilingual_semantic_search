Giedo Gijsbertus Gerrit van der Garde (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈɣiːdɔ vɑn dər ˈɣɑr.də]; born 25 April 1985) is a Dutch former racing driver, who last competed in the 2023 IMSA SportsCar Championship for TDS Racing. From 2018 to 2021 he competed in the FIA World Endurance Championship for Racing Team Nederland in the LMP2 class. He is best known for driving in Formula 1 for the Caterham F1 team in 2013 and joining Sauber as a reserve driver in 2014.
