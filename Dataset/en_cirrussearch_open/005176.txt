Igors Troickis (Russian: Игорь Анатольевич Троицкий, Igor Anatolyevich Troitskiy; born 11 January 1969) is a former football defender from Latvia. He obtained a total number of 41 caps for the Latvia national team between 1992 and 2001. His last club was FK Rīga. Troickis also played as a professional in Russia during his career.
