10,000 BC is a 2008 American action-adventure film co-written, co-produced and directed by Roland Emmerich, co-written, co-scored, and executive produced by Harald Kloser, and starring Steven Strait and Camilla Belle. The film depicts the journeys of a prehistoric tribe of mammoth hunters. The film was a box office hit, but was consistently regarded by professional critics as Emmerich's worst film, as well as one of the worst films of 2008.
