Guntis Rēķis (born 3 November 1974) is a Latvian luger who has competed since 1997. He won two bronze medals in the mixed team event at the FIL World Luge Championships, earning them in 2008 and 2009. Competing in three Winter Olympics, Rēķis earned his best finish of 21st in the men's singles event at Turin in 2006.
