Slavsk (Russian: Славск; German: Heinrichswalde; Lithuanian: Gastos) is a town and the administrative center of Slavsky District in the Kaliningrad Oblast, Russia, located 105 kilometers (65 mi) northeast of Kaliningrad. Population figures: 4,614 (2010 Census); 5,172 (2002 Census); 4,682 (1989 Census).
