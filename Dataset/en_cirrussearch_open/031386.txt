Count William Fermor (Russian: Ви́ллим Ви́ллимович Фе́рмор, romanized: Víllim Víllimovich Férmor/Villim Villimovich Fermor) was an Imperial Russian Army officer best known for leading his country’s army at the Battle of Zorndorf during the Seven Years’ War. His name is sometimes styled Wilhelm Graf von Fermor.
