Giorgi Chakvetadze (Georgian: გიორგი ჩაკვეტაძე; born 29 August 1999) is a Georgian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or left winger for EFL Championship club Watford, and the Georgia national team. Chakvetadze has earned the Player of the Year award in Georgia. He has also won the Slovak First League with Slovan Bratislava.
