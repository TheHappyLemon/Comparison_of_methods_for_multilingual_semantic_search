Dagestanskiye Ogni (Russian: Дагестанские Огни [dəgʲɪˈstanskɪɪ ɐˈgnʲi]; Tabassaran: Дагъустнан ЦIаяр; Azerbaijani: Дағыстан Ишыглары, Dağıstan İşıqları; Lezgian: Дагъустандин ЦӀаяр) is a town in the Republic of Dagestan, Russia, located on the coast of the Caspian Sea, 120 kilometers (75 mi) south of Makhachkala. Population: 31,412 (2021 Census); 27,923 (2010 Census); 26,346 (2002 Census); 21,676 (1989 Census).
