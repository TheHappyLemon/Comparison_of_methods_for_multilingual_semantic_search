Ozolnieki Municipality (Latvian: Ozolnieku novads) is a former municipality in Semigallia, Latvia. The municipality was formed in 2003 by merging Ozolnieki Parish and Cena Parish; the administrative centre being Ozolnieki. In 2009 municipality absorbed Sidrabene parish (in 2011 renamed to Salgale parish), too. The population in 2020 was 10,019. On 1 July 2021, Ozolnieki Municipality ceased to exist and its territory was merged into Jelgava Municipality.
