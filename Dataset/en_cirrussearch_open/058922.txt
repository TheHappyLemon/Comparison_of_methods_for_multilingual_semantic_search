Florin Valeriu Răducioiu (born 17 March 1970) is a Romanian former professional footballer who played as a striker for Dinamo București, AC Milan, Brescia, West Ham United, Espanyol, VfB Stuttgart and AS Monaco. He played for Romania at the 1990 FIFA World Cup, the 1994 FIFA World Cup and the UEFA Euro 1996. He is known as the first footballer who played and scored at least a single goal at all of the Big Five leagues.
