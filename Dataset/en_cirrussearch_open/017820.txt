Ustyluh or Ustilug (Ukrainian: Устилу́г; Polish: Uściług; Yiddish: אוסטילע, romanized: Ustile) is a small city in Volodymyr Raion, Volyn Oblast, Ukraine. It is situated on the east side of the border with Poland, and 13 kilometres (8.1 mi) west of the city of Volodymyr. Population: 2,060 (2022 estimate). Igor Stravinsky had an estate in Ustyluh and visited it frequently between 1890 and 1914. His mansion is now a museum.
