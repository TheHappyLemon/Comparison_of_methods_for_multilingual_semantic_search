Hunter Shinkaruk (born October 13, 1994) is a Canadian professional ice hockey winger who is currently an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played for HC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Shinkaruk was originally selected by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round (24th overall) of the 2013 NHL Entry Draft. Shinkaruk played junior hockey with the Medicine Hat Tigers of the Western Hockey League (WHL) before turning pro with the Utica Comets of the American Hockey League (AHL) within the Vancouver Canucks organization.
