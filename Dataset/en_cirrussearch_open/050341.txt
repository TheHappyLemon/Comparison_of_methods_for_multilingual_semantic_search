"Hatrið mun sigra" (Icelandic: [ˈhaːtrɪð mʏːn ˈsɪɣra]; transl. "Hatred will prevail") is a song by Icelandic band Hatari. It won Söngvakeppnin 2019 and finished 10th at the Eurovision Song Contest 2019 in Tel Aviv, Israel. The song was the first Eurovision Song Contest entry to be performed in Icelandic since 2013. An extended version of the song is included in the band's debut studio album, Neyslutrans (2020).
