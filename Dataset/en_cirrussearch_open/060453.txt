Stepan Falkovsky (Belarusian: Сцяпан Мікалаевіч Фалькоўскі, romanized: Sciapan Mikałajevič Falkoŭski; born 18 December 1996) is a Belarusian professional ice hockey defenceman playing with the SKA Saint Petersburg organization of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was selected by the Calgary Flames in the seventh round (186th overall) at the 2016 NHL Entry Draft.
