Mala Vyska (Ukrainian: Мала Виска, Russian: Ма́лая Ви́ска) is a city in Novoukrainka Raion, Kirovohrad Oblast (region) of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Mala Vyska urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. As of 2022, the population is 9,960 (2022 estimate), down from 13,132 in 2001. The city is situated on the river Vysi (Вись).
