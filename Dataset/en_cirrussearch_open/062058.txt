Enn Kasak (born on 24 September 1954 Navi, Võru County) is an Estonian philosopher and astrophysicist. 1981 he graduated from University of Tartu in astrophysics speciality. 1981-1995 he worked at Tõravere Observatory. 1998-2007 he taught at Tallinn University. Since 2007 he is teaching at Tartu University. 1995–1997 he was the head of Võro Institute. In 2001 he was awarded with Order of the White Star, medal class.
