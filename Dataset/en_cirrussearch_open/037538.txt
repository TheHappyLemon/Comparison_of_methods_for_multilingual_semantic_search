Koriukivka (Ukrainian: Корю́ківка, pronounced [koˈrʲukiu̯kɐ]) is a small city in Chernihiv Oblast (province) of Ukraine. It was founded in 1657, over 350 years ago, and it is the administrative center of Koriukivka Raion. The city hosts the administration of Koriukivka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The population in 2021 is estimated to be 12,202 (2022 estimate).
