The E9 European long distance path, E9 path or European Coastal Path (French: Sentier européen du Littoral) is one of more European long-distance paths, running for 9.880 km from Tarifa, Spain to Narva-Jõesuu in Estonia. In 2019, the southern terminus was extended from Cabo de São Vicente in Portugal to Tarifa in Spain, the southern-most point of continental Europe, and also the starting point of E4 and E12.
