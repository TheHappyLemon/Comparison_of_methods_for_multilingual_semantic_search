Zakumi the Leopard was the official mascot for the 2010 FIFA World Cup, which was held in South Africa. He was an anthropomorphized leopard with green hair, introduced on September 22, 2008. His name comes from "ZA", the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code for South Africa, and Kumi, a word that means "ten" in several African languages, referencing the year 2010.
