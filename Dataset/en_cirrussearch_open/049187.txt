Zhyrmuny (Belarusian: Жырмуны, romanized: Žyrmuny; Russian: Жирмуны, romanized: Zhirmuny; Polish: Żyrmuny; Lithuanian: Žirmūnai) is an agrotown in Voranava District, Grodno Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Zhyrmuny rural council (selsoviet). It is located 16 kilometres (9.9 mi) from the border with Lithuania.
