The Samuel Goldwyn Theatre is a screening-only movie theater named after filmmaker Samuel Goldwyn.[citation needed] It is located at 8949 Wilshire Boulevard, Beverly Hills, California, at headquarters of the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS). The Academy uses this theater each January most years to announce the nominations for its Academy Awards.
