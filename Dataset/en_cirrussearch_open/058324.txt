Alexey Vasilyevich Sarana (Russian: Алексей Васильевич Сарана, Serbian Cyrillic: Алексеј Васиљевич Сарана; born 26 January 2000) is a Russian-born Serbian chess grandmaster. He won the European Individual Chess Championship in 2023. His father is Ukrainian and lives near Kyiv. Sarana left Russia in March 2022, stating that he does not plan to return until the end of the Russian invasion of Ukraine.
