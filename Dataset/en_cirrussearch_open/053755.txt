Kastuś Jezavitaŭ (Belarusian: Касту́сь Езаві́таў; also known as Kanstancin Barysavič Jezavitaŭ, Belarusian: Канстанці́н Бары́савіч Езаві́таў; Russian: Константи́н Бори́сович Езови́тов; 17 November 1893 – 23 May 1946) was a political and military leader within the Belarusian independence movement of the early 20th century.
