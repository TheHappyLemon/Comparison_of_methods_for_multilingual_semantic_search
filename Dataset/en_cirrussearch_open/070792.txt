The Boy's Word: Blood on the Asphalt (Russian: Слово пацана. Кровь на асфальте, romanized: Slovo patsana. Krov na asfalte) is a 2023 Russian crime drama television series directed by Zhora Kryzhovnikov and written by Kryzhovnikov and Andrey Zolotarev. It is based on the novel "The boy's Word: Criminal Tatarstan 1970—2010s" by Robert Garaev about the "Kazan phenomenon". The series gained wide popularity in Russia and other post-Soviet countries.
