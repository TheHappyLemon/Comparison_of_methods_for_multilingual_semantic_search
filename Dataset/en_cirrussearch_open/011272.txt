Maxim Valeryevich Chudinov (Russian: Максим Валерьевич Чудинов; born 25 March 1990) is a Russian professional ice hockey player who is currently on under contract with Admiral Vladivostok of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Chudinov was drafted by the Boston Bruins in the 7th round (195th overall) of the 2010 NHL Entry Draft.
