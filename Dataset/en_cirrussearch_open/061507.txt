Quo Vadis, Aida? (lit. Where are you going, Aida?) is a 2020 internationally co-produced war drama film written, produced and directed by Jasmila Žbanić. An international co-production of twelve production companies, the film was shown in the main competition section of the 77th Venice International Film Festival. Quo Vadis, Aida? received critical acclaim, earning a nomination for Best International Feature Film at the 93rd Academy Awards, and receiving two BAFTA Award nominations. It also won four European Film Awards, including Best Film.
