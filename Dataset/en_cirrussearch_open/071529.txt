The 2024 Latvian Higher League, known as the TonyBet Virslīga for sponsorship reasons, will be the 33rd season of top-tier football in Latvia. The season began on 8 March 2024 and will end on 9 November. The winners qualify for the 2025–26 Champions League first qualifying round. The second-placed team qualify for the 2025–26 Conference League second qualifying round. The third and fourth-placed teams qualify for the 2025–26 Conference League first qualifying round. The ninth-placed team qualify for the Latvian Higher League play-off. The bottom team are relegated to the 2025 Latvian First League.
