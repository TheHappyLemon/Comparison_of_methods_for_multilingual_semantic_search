56°57′18″N 24°09′32″E﻿ / ﻿56.9549973°N 24.158839°E﻿ / 56.9549973; 24.158839 Daugava National Stadium (Latvian: Daugavas stadions) is a multifunctional stadium in Riga, Latvia, which was first opened in 1927. It holds football and athletics competitions. Since 1992 the Daugava Stadium has been designated as a sports facility of national importance. It is owned by the Government of Latvia.
