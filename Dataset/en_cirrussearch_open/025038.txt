Nadvirna Raion (Ukrainian: Надвірня́нський райо́н, romanized: Nadvirnianśkyi raion) is a raion (district) of Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast (region). The town of Nadvirna is the administrative center of the raion. Population: 129,328 (2022 estimate). On 18 July 2020, as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, the number of raions of Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast was reduced to six, and the area of Nadvirna Raion was significantly expanded. Yaremche Municipality was merged into Nadvirna Raion. The January 2020 estimate of the raion population was 114,625 (2020 est.). In 2015 oil production stopped in the raion (due to the license expiration).
