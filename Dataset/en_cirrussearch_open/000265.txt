Trubchevsk (Russian: Трубче́вск, Polish: Trubczewsk) is a town and the administrative center of Trubchevsky District in Bryansk Oblast, Russia, located about 95 kilometers (59 mi) south of the city of Bryansk, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: 13,287 (2021 Census); 15,014 (2010 Census); 16,342 (2002 Census); 16,301 (1989 Census).
