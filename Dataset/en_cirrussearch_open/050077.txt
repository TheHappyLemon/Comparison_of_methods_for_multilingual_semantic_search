Nathan Daniel Jerome Redmond (born 6 March 1994) is an English professional footballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Burnley. Redmond began his career as a youngster with Birmingham City, his hometown club. He made his first-team debut in the League Cup second-round tie against Rochdale in August 2010, becoming the club's second-youngest player ever at the age of 16 years and 173 days. After representing England at levels up to under-19, Redmond made his first under-21 debut in the 2013 European Under-21 Championship finals. He joined Premier League club Norwich City in 2013, before making the switch to Southampton in 2016. Just over six years later, he signed for Turkish Süper Lig club Beşiktaş. Redmond represented England at youth levels from under-16 through to under-21, and played once for the senior England team, in 2017. He typically plays as a wide midfielder. He is right-footed, but has regularly played on both right and left wings, often cutting inside. He has also occasionally played in the centre behind a lone striker.
