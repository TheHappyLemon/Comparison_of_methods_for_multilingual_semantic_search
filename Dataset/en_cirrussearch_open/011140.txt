Maxim Pestushko (born (1985-02-09)February 9, 1985) is a Russia professional ice hockey player currently an unrestricted free agent, who most recently played within the Ak Bars Kazan organization in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He previously played for HC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk, HC Dynamo Moscow, Avangard Omsk, HC Yugra and the Severstal Cherepovets in the KHL.
