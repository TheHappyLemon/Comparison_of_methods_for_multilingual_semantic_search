Mikel Oyarzabal Ugarte (born 21 April 1997) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a left winger or forward for La Liga club Real Sociedad, which he captains, and the Spain national team. He has spent his entire professional career with Real Sociedad, making over 300 appearances and scoring over 90 goals. He won the 2020 Copa del Rey with the club, scoring in the final. Oyarzabal made his full debut for the Spain national team in 2016, representing the side at UEFA Euro 2020.
