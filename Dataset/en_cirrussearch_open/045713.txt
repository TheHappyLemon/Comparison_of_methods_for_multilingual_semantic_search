Natalia Edzgveradze (born 23 July 1975) is a Georgian chess Woman Grandmaster and FIDE Trainer since 2005 (World Chess Federation certified trainer) , currently working in Georgia as Deputy Director of Maia Chiburdanidze Chess Academy (Chess Queen Academy). Natalia is a coach with almost 20 years of experience. From 2005 to 2019 she worked as a chess coach in Singapore. In 2019 she moved back to Georgia and joined Maya Chiburdanidze Chess Queen Academy.
