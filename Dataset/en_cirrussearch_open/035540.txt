Cherykaw District or Čerykaŭ District (Belarusian: Чэрыкаўскі раён; Russian: Чериковский район, romanized: Cherikovsky rayon) is a district (raion) of Mogilev Region in Belarus. The administrative center is the town of Cherykaw. As of 2009, its population was 14,875. The population of Cherykaw accounts for 55.0% of the district's population.
