Alisa Brunovna Freindlich or Freindlih (Russian: Алиса Бруновна Фрейндлих; German: Alissa Brunowna Freindlich, born 8 December 1934) is a Soviet and Russian actress. People's Artist of the USSR (1981). Since 1983, Freindlich has been a leading actress of the Bolshoi Drama Theater in Saint Petersburg, Russia.
