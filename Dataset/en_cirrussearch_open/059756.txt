Kamilla Bartone (born 7 June 2002) is a Latvian tennis player. Bartone has a career-high ITF junior combined ranking of 6, achieved on 21 October 2019. She won the 2019 US Open on girls' doubles with Oksana Selekhmeteva. Bartone made her WTA Tour main-draw debut at the 2019 Baltic Open in the doubles draw, partnering Ksenia Aleshina.
