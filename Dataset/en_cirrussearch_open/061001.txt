Dobrovelychkivka (Ukrainian: Добровеличківка) is a rural settlement in Novoukrainka Raion, Kirovohrad Oblast, southern Ukraine. Dobrovelychkivka hosts the administration of Dobrovelychkivka settlement hromada [uk], one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The population of the settlement is 5,358 (2022 estimate). It hosts a regional bazaar on Wednesday and Friday mornings, and is noted for being the purported geographical center of Ukraine.
