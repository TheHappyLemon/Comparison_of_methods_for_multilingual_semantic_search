Duje Ćaleta-Car (Croatian pronunciation: [dǔːje tɕâleta tsâr]; born 17 September 1996) is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Ligue 1 club Lyon, on loan from EFL Championship club Southampton, and the Croatia national team. He was a member of the Croatian squad which ended as runners-up to France in the 2018 FIFA World Cup.
