Pavlo Oleksandrovych Kyrylenko (Ukrainian: Павло Олександрович Кириленко; born 5 May 1986) is a Ukrainian prosecutor and politician. He has served as head of the Ukrainian Anti-Monopoly Committee since September 2023. Kyrylenko is a former Governor of Donetsk Oblast. Under martial law during the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine, Kyrylenko served as the Head of the Donetsk Regional Military Administration from July 2019 to September 2023.
