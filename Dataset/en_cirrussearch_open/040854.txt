Lars Edi Stindl (born 26 August 1988) is a German professional footballer who plays as a attacking midfielder for 2. Bundesliga club Karlsruher SC. He can also play as a forward. Stindl represented the Germany national team from 2017 to 2018. He scored the only goal in the final of the 2017 FIFA Confederations Cup against Chile to ensure Germany's first ever title.
