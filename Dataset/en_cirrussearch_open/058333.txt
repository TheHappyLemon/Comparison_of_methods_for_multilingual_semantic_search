Zhou Qian (Chinese: 周倩; pinyin: Zhōu Qiàn; born March 11, 1989) is a wrestler from China. She won the bronze medal at the 2014 World Wrestling Championships. She also won the silver medal at the 2015 World Wrestling Championships and the bronze medal at the 2015 Asian Wrestling Championships. In 2021, she won one of the bronze medals in the women's 76 kg event at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan.
