Arianne Bo Caoili (/ˈæriːɔːn kaʊˈiːliː/ kah-WEE-lee; 22 December 1986 – 30 March 2020) was a Filipino and Australian chess player. She held the FIDE title of Woman International Master, won the Oceania women's chess championship in 2009 and competed in seven Women's Chess Olympiads. Outside of chess, she was a financial consultant and singer, and served as an advisor to Armenian prime minister Karen Karapetyan.
