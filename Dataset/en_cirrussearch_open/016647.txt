Baiba Indriksone' (born February 22, 1932, Riga) is Latvian film and stage actress, affiliated with the Latvian National Theatre. She graduated from the Latvian State Conservatory, theatre class, in 1952. Her first role was that of Abelīte in the film Rainis [lt] (1949). Her last role on a grand stage was in Henrik Ibsen's Peer Gynt directed by Viestur Kairish in 2016. She is a widow of the film director Aleksandrs Leimanis
