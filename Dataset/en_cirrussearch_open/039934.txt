Uldis is a Latvian male given name that is a variant of the German name Ulrich, which means "powerful heritage". The name may refer to: Uldis Augulis (born 1972), Latvian politician Uldis Bērziņš (1944–2021), Latvian writer Uldis Briedis (born 1942), Latvian politician Uldis Ģērmanis (1915–1997), Latvian historian Uldis Osis (born 1948), Latvian economist Uldis Pūcītis (1937–2000), Latvian actor Uldis Saulīte (born 1980), Latvian rugby union footballer Uldis Sesks (born 1962), Latvian politician
