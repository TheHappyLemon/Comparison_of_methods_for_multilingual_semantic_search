Talysh Mountains (Talysh: Tolışə Bandon, Persian: کوه‌های تالش, romanized: Kuhhâye Tâleš; Azerbaijani: Talış dağları) is a mountain range in far southeastern Azerbaijan and far northwestern Iran within Ardabil Province and Gilan Province. The Talysh are a northwestern subrange of the Alborz Mountains that run along the southern Caspian Sea on the Iranian Plateau.
