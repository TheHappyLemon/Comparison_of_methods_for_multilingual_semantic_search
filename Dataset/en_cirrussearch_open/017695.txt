Lyakhavichy (Belarusian: Ляхавічы, IPA: [ˈlʲaxavʲitʂɨ]; Russian: Ляховичи, romanized: Lyakhovichi; Polish: Lachowicze; Yiddish: לעכאוויטש, romanized: Lekhavitsh; Lithuanian: Liachivičai) is a town in Brest Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Lyakhavichy District. As of 2023, it has a population of 10,613.
