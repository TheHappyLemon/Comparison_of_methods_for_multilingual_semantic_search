Sergey Leonidovich Dolgopolov (Russian: Сергей Леонидович Долгополов, Latvian: Sergejs Dolgopolovs; born 3 September 1941) is a Latvian Russian politician. He is a member of Harmony, former deputy mayor of Riga (2001–2005) and a deputy of the 9th, 10th, 11th, 12th and 13th Saeima (2006-present). In 2015 he was the Harmony's candidate in the election for President of Latvia.
