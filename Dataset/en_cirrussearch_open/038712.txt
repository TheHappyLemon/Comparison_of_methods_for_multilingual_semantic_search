Stratiotes is a genus of submerged aquatic plant commonly known as water soldiers, described as a genus by Linnaeus in 1753. Several specific names have been coined within the genus, but at present only one is recognized: Stratiotes aloides. native to Europe and NW Asia. formerly included in genus moved to other genera: Enhalus Hydrocleys Ottelia Stratiotes acoroides - Enhalus acoroides Stratiotes alismoides - Ottelia alismoides Stratiotes nymphoides - Hydrocleys nymphoides Stratiotes quinquealatus - Ottelia alismoides
