Eric García Martret (born 9 January 2001) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for La Liga club Girona, on loan from Barcelona, and the Spain national team. García moved from Barcelona at the age of 17 to Manchester City. He made his Premier League debut on 21 September 2019. In his final season at the club, he won the league and was runner-up in the UEFA Champions League. In June 2021, Garcia rejoined Barcelona on a free transfer, following the expiration of his contract.
