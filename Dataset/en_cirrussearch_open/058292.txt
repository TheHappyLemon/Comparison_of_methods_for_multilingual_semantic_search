Yui Susaki (須崎 優衣, Susaki Yui, born 30 June 1999) is a Japanese freestyle wrestler. She won the gold medal in the women's 50 kg event at the 2020 Summer Olympics without conceding a single point. She has not been beaten in a single bout since 2010, except for three losses to Yuki Irie in 2015, 2017 and 2019. She has been coached by Shoko Yoshimura, five-time world champion, since she was 13 years old.
