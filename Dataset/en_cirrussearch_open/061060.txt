Novyi Buh (Ukrainian: Нови́й Буг, pronounced [ˈnɔwɪj buɦ] ) is a city in Bashtanka Raion, Mykolaiv Oblast, Bashtanka Raion, Kryvyi Rih metropolitan area, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Novyi Buh urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 14,782 (2022 estimate). In 2001, population was 16,250. The Inhul River Park partly falls within the town.
