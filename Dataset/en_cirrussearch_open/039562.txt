Svešā seja is a Latvian television series that premiered in Latvia on TV3 on September 18, 2017. The series was created by the same team which had worked on popular Latvian TV series UgunsGrēks. The series starred actors Dārta Daneviča and Vita Vārpiņa from Dailes Theatre and actors Kaspars Dumburs and Egils Melbārdis from the Latvian National Theatre. The opening theme was "Atbalss" by the band Musiqq. On April 11, 2018, it was announced that channel TV3 had decided to discontinue the series. The series finale aired on May 17, 2018.
