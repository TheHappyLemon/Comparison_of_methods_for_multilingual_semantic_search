Andrej Šustr (born 29 November 1990) is a Czech professional ice hockey defenceman for the Kölner Haie of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). Previously he played in the NHL for the Tampa Bay Lightning and Anaheim Ducks. An undrafted player, Šustr made his NHL debut with the Lightning in 2013 after playing three seasons of college ice hockey with the University of Nebraska Omaha.
