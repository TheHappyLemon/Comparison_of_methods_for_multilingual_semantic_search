Turkey competed at the 1928 Summer Olympics in Amsterdam, Netherlands. 31 competitors participated at six sports branches of athletics, cycling, fencing, football, weightlifting and wrestling. In the Amsterdam Olympics, Turkey received fourth place for the first time ever. Tayyar Yalaz was the Turkish Olympian, whose name was listed in the official table of honor, which lists six best contestants in each event.
