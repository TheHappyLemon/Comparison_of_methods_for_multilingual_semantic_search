Dzisna (Belarusian: Дзісна; Russian: Дисна, romanized: Disna; Lithuanian: Dysna; Polish: Dzisna) is a town in Miory District, Vitebsk Region, Belarus. It is located on the left bank of the Daugava River, near the confluence of the Dysna. In 2017, its population was 1,500 inhabitants. As of 2023, it has a population of 1,417.
