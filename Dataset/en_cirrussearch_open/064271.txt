Teun Koopmeiners (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈtøːŋ ˈkoːpmɛi̯nərs]; born 28 February 1998) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie A club Atalanta and the Netherlands national team. Having represented the Netherlands at various youth levels, Koopmeiners was called up to the senior team's preliminary UEFA Nations League squad in August 2020. He was later part of their squad for the 2022 FIFA World Cup.
