Kakhovka (Ukrainian: Кахо́вка, pronounced [kɐˈxɔu̯kɐ]) is a port city on the Dnieper River in Kakhovka Raion, Kherson Oblast, of southern Ukraine. It hosts the administration of the Kakhovka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. It had a population of 34,749 (2022 estimate). It is home to the KZEZO (Electro-Welding Equipment Plant) and the Tavria Games festival.
