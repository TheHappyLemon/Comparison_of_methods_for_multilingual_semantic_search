Tora is an Australian electronic band formed in Byron Bay, New South Wales in May 2013. The founding members are Tobias Tunis, Thorne Davis (drums), Shaun Johnston (bass guitar), Jo Loewenthal (lead vocals, guitar, and keys), and Jai Piccone (vocals, guitar, and keys). They have undertaken Australian and international tours. Their tracks have received rotation on the Australian national radio station Triple J.[citation needed]
