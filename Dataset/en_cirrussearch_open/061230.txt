Lokhvytsia (Ukrainian: Ло́хвиця, pronounced [ˈɫɔxwɪt͡sʲɐ]) is a city in Myrhorod Raion, Poltava Oblast, central Ukraine. It is located on the banks of the Lokhvytsia River [uk]. Lokhvytsia hosts the administration of Lokhvytsia urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 11,014 (2022 estimate).
