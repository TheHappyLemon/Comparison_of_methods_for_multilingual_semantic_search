Eliomys (Greek: Ἡλίομυς Hēlíomus) is a genus of rodent in the family Gliridae, commonly known as garden dormice. It contains the following extant species: Asian garden dormouse, Eliomys melanurus Maghreb garden dormouse, Eliomys munbyanus Garden dormouse, Eliomys quercinus The earliest records of the genus are known from the Late Miocene (Tortonian) of the Iberian Peninsula. Fossil species assigned to Eliomys include: †Eliomys truci †Eliomys yevesi †Eliomys intermedius †Eliomys assimilis †Eliomys lafarguei †Eliomys reductus
