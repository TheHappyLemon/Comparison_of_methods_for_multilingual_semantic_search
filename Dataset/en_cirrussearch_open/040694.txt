Konstantin Yuryevich Bogomolov (Russian: Константи́н Ю́рьевич Богомо́лов; born July 23, 1975) is a Russian theater director, poet, and actor. He has served as the art director of the Theater on Malaya Bronnaya [ru]. He is the son of the film critic Yuri Bogomolov [ru]. He has been honoured with a Golden Mask Award.
