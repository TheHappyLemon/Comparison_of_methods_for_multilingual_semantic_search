59°19′20″N 18°08′55″E﻿ / ﻿59.32222°N 18.14861°E﻿ / 59.32222; 18.14861 The Thiel Gallery (Swedish: Thielska galleriet) is an art museum in the Djurgården park area of Stockholm, Sweden. Represented are the members of the Artists Association (Konstnärsförbundet) from the early 1900s as well as one of the world's largest collections of works by Edvard Munch.
