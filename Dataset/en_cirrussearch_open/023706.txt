Orsha (Belarusian: Орша, romanized: Orša; Russian: Орша, IPA: [ˈorʂə]; Lithuanian: Orša, Polish: Orsza) is a city in Vitebsk Region, Belarus. It is situated on the fork of the Dnieper and Arshytsa river, and it serves as the administrative center of Orsha District. As of 2023, it has a population of 103,658.
