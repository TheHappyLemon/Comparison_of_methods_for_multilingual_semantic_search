Gordana Marković (née Jovanović; born 4 January 1951), also known as Gordana Marković-Jovanović, (Serbian Cyrillic: Гордана Марковић-Јовановић) is a Serbian chess player who holds the title of Woman International Master (WIM, 1979). She is a two-time winner of the Yugoslav Women's Chess Championship (1977, 1981) and won a team bronze medal with the Yugoslav women's team at the 1988 Chess Olympiad.
