Craig George Cathcart (born 6 February 1989) is a Northern Irish former professional footballer who played as a centre-back, mostly for Watford. He won 73 caps for the Northern Ireland national team. He also captained his country's under-21 side. A product of the Manchester United youth academy, Cathcart spent time on loan at Belgian club Royal Antwerp, and English Football League Championship sides Plymouth Argyle and Watford, before joining newly promoted Blackpool in 2010. Cathcart returned to Watford in July 2014 on a permanent deal where he became one of their longest serving players, achieving two promotions and an FA Cup Final.
