Dzelzava is a village in Latvia, Madona district. In 2000 in Dzelzava were 375 inhabitants. In Dzelzava is located Dzelzava estate, built in 1767, in baroque style. It was rebuilt after burning in revolution of 1905. By the estate is set a park with many local and foreign trees and bushes. From 1940 to nowadays building is occupied by Dzelzava primary school.
