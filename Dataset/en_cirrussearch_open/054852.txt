He specialized in the 400 metres hurdles and won a silver medal in Athletics at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, his birthplace. Tauzin also competed in the 200 metre hurdles, finishing fourth in his semifinal heat to not advance to the final. An administrative officer in the army, Tauzin was killed in action during World War I.
