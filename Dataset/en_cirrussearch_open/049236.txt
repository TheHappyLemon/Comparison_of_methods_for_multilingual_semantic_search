Houssem-Eddine Chaâbane Aouar (Arabic: حسام الدين شعبان عوار; born 30 June 1998) is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie A club Roma and the Algeria national team. Born in France, he made one appearance for his country of birth before switching his allegiance to Algeria. He made his debut for the latter in June 2023, and represented the national team at the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations.
