Sinapis is a genus of plants in the family Brassicaceae. As of November 2017[update], six species are recognised by The Plant List: Sinapis alba L. – white mustard, formerly Brassica alba Sinapis allionii Jacq. Sinapis arvensis L. — charlock mustard, field mustard, wild mustard, charlock Sinapis circinata Desf. Sinapis flexuosa Poir. Sinapis pubescens L.
