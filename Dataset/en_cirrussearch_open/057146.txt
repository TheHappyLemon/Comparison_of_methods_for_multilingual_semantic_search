Legrand is a French industrial group historically based in Limoges in the Limousin region. Legrand is established in 90 countries and its products are distributed in nearly 180 countries. It generates 85% of its sales internationally. The group has expanded its product range in sustainable development and energy saving technologies, and has developed new products for EV charging/electric vehicles, lighting control and datacenters. Legrand has more than 300 000 references.
