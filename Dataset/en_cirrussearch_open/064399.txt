Miloš Degenek (/ˈmɪlɒʃ ˈdɛɡənɛk/ MIL-osh DEG-ə-nek; Serbian Cyrillic: Милош Дегенек, pronounced [mǐloʃ děɡenek]; born 28 April 1994) is a professional soccer player who plays as a defender for Serbian SuperLiga club Red Star Belgrade. Born in Croatia, he represents the Australia national team. Degenek represented both Serbia and Australia at youth levels before making his senior international debut for Australia against England in 2016.
