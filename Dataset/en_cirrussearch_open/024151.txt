Nyasvizh or Nesvizh (Belarusian: Нясвіж, romanized: Niasviž, IPA: [nʲaˈsʲvʲiʐ]; Russian: Несвиж; Lithuanian: Nesvyžius; Polish: Nieśwież; Yiddish: ניעסוויז; Latin: Nesvisium) is a town in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative centre of Nyasvizh District. Nyasvizh is the site of Nesvizh Castle, a World Heritage Site. In 2009, its population was 14,300. As of 2023, it has a population of 15,907.
