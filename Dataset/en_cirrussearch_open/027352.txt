Denis Godla (born 4 April 1995) is a Slovak professional ice hockey goaltender who currently playing for HC Slovan Bratislava of the Slovak Extraliga. Godla was Slovakia's starting goaltender at the 2015 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships where he led the team to a bronze medal, also being named in the tournament's All-Star team, the tournament's best goaltender, and overall tournament MVP.
