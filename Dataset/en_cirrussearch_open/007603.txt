Bo John Fredrik Warg (born May 3, 1979 in Skellefteå, Sweden) is a retired forward who last played for the ERC Ingolstadt hockey team in Austria. He also played for Björklöven, Modo and Timrå IK. He reached an agreement with Dinamo Riga of KHL and joined his fellow countryman Björn Melin for 11-12 season.
