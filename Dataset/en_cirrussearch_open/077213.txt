Biysk (Russian: Бийск, IPA: [bʲijsk]; Altay: Јаш-Тура) is a city in Altai Krai, Russia, located on the Biya River not far from its confluence with the Katun River. It is the second largest city of the krai (after Barnaul, the administrative center of the krai). Population: 183,852 (2021 Census); 210,115 (2010 Census); 218,562 (2002 Census); 233,238 (1989 Census).
