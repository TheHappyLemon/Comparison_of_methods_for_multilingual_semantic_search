Andriy Mykolayovych Yarmolenko (Ukrainian: Андрій Миколайович Ярмоленко; born 23 October 1989) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a winger or forward for Ukrainian Premier League club Dynamo Kyiv and captains the Ukraine national team. Yarmolenko has been a full international for Ukraine since 2009, scoring 46 goals in 116 matches and playing at UEFA Euro 2012, Euro 2016, and Euro 2020.
