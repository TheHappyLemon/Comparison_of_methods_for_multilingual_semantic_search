Kirilenko (Russian: Кириле́нко) is a Russian surname of Ukrainian descent derived from the name of Cyril. Notable people with this name include: Andrei Kirilenko (born 1981), Russian basketball player who played in the National Basketball Association Andrei Kirilenko (politician) (1906–1990), Soviet politician Artur Kirilenko (born 1972), Russian and European real estate developer Denis Kirilenko (born 1984), Russian footballer Maria Kirilenko (born 1987), Russian tennis player
