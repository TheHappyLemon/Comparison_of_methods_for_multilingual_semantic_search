Anastasia Viktorovna Bratchikova (Russian: Анастасия Викторовна Братчикова; born 21 February 1989) is a Russian female freestyle wrestler who currently competes in the 68 kg weight category. Bratchikova is a three-time National champion with her latest national title being captured in Smolensk in August 2018. Bratchikova is also a two-time European champion, having won gold in Novi Sad in 2017 and in Kaspiysk in 2018.
