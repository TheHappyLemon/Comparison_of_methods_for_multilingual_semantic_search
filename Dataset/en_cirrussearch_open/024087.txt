Kapyl (Belarusian: Капыль, romanized: Kapyĺ; Russian: Копыль, romanized: Kopyl; Polish: Kopyl; Lithuanian: Kapylius; Yiddish: קאפּוליע) is a town in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Kapyl District. It is located 34 kilometers (21 mi) west-northwest of Slutsk and 90 kilometers (56 mi) south-southwest of Minsk. In 2009, its estimated population was 9,900 based on the provisional figures from the 2009 census. As of 2023, it has a population of 10,087.
