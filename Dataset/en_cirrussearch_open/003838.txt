David Range The David Range (67°54′S 62°30′E﻿ / ﻿67.900°S 62.500°E﻿ / -67.900; 62.500 is a mountain range that extends for 26 kilometres (16 mi) in the Framnes Mountains of Mac.Robertson Land in Antarctica. The range is surrounded by, and largely covered by, an ice sheet. Only the peaks are visible.
