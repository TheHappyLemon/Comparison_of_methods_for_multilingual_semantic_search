Frank Bryan Ntilikina (French: [fʁɑ̃ŋk nilikina]; born 28 July 1998) is a French professional basketball player who last played for the Charlotte Hornets of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He was selected by the New York Knicks as the eighth overall pick during the 2017 NBA draft. Ntilikina was born in Belgium and grew up in the French city of Strasbourg. He stands at 6 ft 4 in (1.93 m) tall and plays the point guard position.
