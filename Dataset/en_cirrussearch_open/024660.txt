Novodnistrovsk (Ukrainian: Новодністровськ, pronounced [ˌnɔwod⁽ʲ⁾n⁽ʲ⁾isˈtrɔu̯sʲk]) is a city in Dnistrovskyi Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast (province) of Ukraine. Novodnistrovsk is located in the historical region of Bessarabia. Population: 10,463 (2022 estimate). It is the center of Novodnistrovsk urban hromada [uk].
