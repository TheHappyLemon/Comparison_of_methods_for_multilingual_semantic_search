Horodyshche (Ukrainian: Городище, IPA: [ɦoroˈdɪʃtʃe]) is a city located in Cherkasy Raion of Cherkasy Oblast (province) in central Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Horodyshche urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The city rests on the Vilshanka River. Population: 13,062 (2022 estimate).
