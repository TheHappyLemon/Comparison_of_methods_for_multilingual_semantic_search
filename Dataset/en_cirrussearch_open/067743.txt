Ipatovo (Russian: Ипа́тово) is a town and the administrative center of Ipatovsky District in Stavropol Krai, Russia, located on the Kalaus River about 120 kilometers (75 mi) northeast of Stavropol, the administrative center of the krai. Population: 26,053 (2010 Census); 28,594 (2002 Census); 26,425 (1989 Census). It was previously known as Chemrek, Vinodelnoye (until 1935).
