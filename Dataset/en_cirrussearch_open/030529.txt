Druya (Belarusian: Друя; Russian: Друя; Polish: Druja) is an agrotown in Braslaw District, Vitebsk Region, Belarus. It is located on the left bank of the Western Dvina, at the mouth of the Druyka River, opposite the Latvian parish of Piedruja. It is situated about 30 kilometres (19 mi) northeast of Braslaw. The population is about 1,500 (2006).
