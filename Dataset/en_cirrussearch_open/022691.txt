Sandris Bērziņš (born 15 July 1976) is a Latvian luger who has competed from 1996 to 2005. He won the silver medal in the mixed team event at the 2003 FIL World Luge Championships in Sigulda, Latvia. Berzins competed in two Winter Olympics, earning his best finish of tenth in the men's doubles event at Salt Lake City in 2002.
