All for Latvia! (Latvian: Visu Latvijai!) was a right-wing ethnic nationalist political party in Latvia led by Raivis Dzintars and Imants Parādnieks [lv]. Formed in 2000, it entered a right-wing nationalist coalition in 2010, and ultimately merged with the For Fatherland and Freedom/LNNK in 2011 to form the National Alliance.
