Óscar de Marcos Arana (Basque: [os̺kar de maɾkos̺ aɾana]; Spanish: [ˈoskaɾ ðe ˈmaɾkos aˈɾana]; born 14 April 1989) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for La Liga club Athletic Bilbao mainly as a full-back. He began his career at Alavés, but went on to spend most of it at Athletic Bilbao, making over 500 official appearances and winning two Supercopa de España titles with the club.
