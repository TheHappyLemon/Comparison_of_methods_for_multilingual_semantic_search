Matthew Charles Turner (born June 24, 1994) is an American professional soccer player who plays as a goalkeeper for Premier League club Nottingham Forest and the United States national team. Not playing soccer until 14 years old, Turner played for Fairfield University and went undrafted in the 2016 MLS SuperDraft, though signed with Major League Soccer club New England Revolution after a successful preseason trial. After 2 seasons he became the club's primary goalkeeper, later winning the MLS Goalkeeper of the Year award in 2021.
