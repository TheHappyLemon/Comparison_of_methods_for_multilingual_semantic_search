Jarkko Samuli Ruutu (Finnish pronunciation: [ˈjɑrk:o 'sɑmuli ˈruːtu]); born August 23, 1975) is a Finnish former professional ice hockey forward who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) with the Vancouver Canucks, Pittsburgh Penguins, Ottawa Senators and Anaheim Ducks. He is currently serving as a European development coach for the Columbus Blue Jackets.
