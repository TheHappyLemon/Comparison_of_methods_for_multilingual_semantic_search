Apostolove (Ukrainian: Апостолове, IPA: [ɐˈpɔstɔɫɔʋe] ; Russian: Апо́столово) is a city in Kryvyi Rih Raion, Dnipropetrovsk Oblast, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Apostolove urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 13,069 (2022 estimate). In 2001, the population was 16,439. Until the raion was abolished on 18 July 2020, Apostolove was the administrative center of Apostolove Raion. After that date the town became part of Kryvyi Rih Raion.
