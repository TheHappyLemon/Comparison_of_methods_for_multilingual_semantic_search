Dobropillia (Ukrainian: Добропі́лля, IPA: [dobroˈp⁽ʲ⁾ilʲːɐ] ; Russian: Добропо́лье, romanized: Dobropolye) is a city in Pokrovsk Raion, Donetsk Oblast, Ukraine. Prior to 2020, it was a city of oblast significance and served as the administrative center of the former Dobropillia Raion, though it did not belong to the raion. It is located 94 kilometres (58 mi) from Donetsk, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: 28,170 (2022 estimate); 40,064 (1989);
