Valeria Yuryevna Lazinskaya (Russian: Валерия Юрьевна Лазинская; born 10 December 1992, in Shakhtinsk, Kazakhstan) is a Kazakh naturalized Russian female freestyle wrestler. She won the bronze medal the 2014 World Wrestling Championships. In the gold medal match of 2015 European Games, she won rematch World Champion Yuliya Tkach of Ukraine.
