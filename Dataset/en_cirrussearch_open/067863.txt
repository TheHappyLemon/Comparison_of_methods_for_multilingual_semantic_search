Chapayevsk (Russian: Чапа́евск) is a town in Samara Oblast, Russia, located 40 kilometers (25 mi) from the city of Samara, on the right bank of the Chapayevka River (a tributary of the Volga River). Population: 72,692 (2010 Census); 73,912 (2002 Census); 97,984 (1989 Census). It was previously known as Ivashchenkovo (until 1927), Trotsk (after Leon Trotsky until 1929).
