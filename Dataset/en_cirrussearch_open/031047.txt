Suzhou is a city in southern Jiangsu of eastern China. Since economic reforms began in 1978, Suzhou attained GDP growth rates of about 14% in 35 years. In 2023, Suzhou had 5 million registered residents in total. Suzhou is listed as the 48th cities by scientific output according to the Nature Index 2022.
