August Theodor Lauezzari (18 May 1880 – ) was a German rower. He competed in the men's eight event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. Lauezzari was born to Theodor Lauezzari and Agnes Courvoisier. He had a younger brother, Alphons Courvoisier Lauezzari (1882–1946). Theodor married Theresa MacNeill in 1924 in Montreal, Quebec.
