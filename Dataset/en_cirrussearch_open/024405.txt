Oleksandriia (Ukrainian: Олександрі́я) is a city in Kirovohrad Oblast, central Ukraine. It serves as the administrative center of Oleksandriia Raion. It also hosts the administration of Oleksandriia urban hromada [uk], one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Oleksandriia is located within the Kryvyi Rih metropolitan area. In 2001, it had a population of 93,357, and including the villages (selo) and urban type settlements in the city municipality a population of 103,856. In 2022, it had a population of 76,097 (2022 estimate).
