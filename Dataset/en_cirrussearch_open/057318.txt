Vladislav Dmitriyevich Kamenev (Russian: Владислав Дмитриевич Каменев, born 12 August 1996) is a Russian professional ice hockey forward. He is currently playing with HC CSKA Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Kamenev was originally selected by the Nashville Predators in the second round (42nd overall) of the 2014 NHL Entry Draft.
