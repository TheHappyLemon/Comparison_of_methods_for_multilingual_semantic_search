Robin Leon Koch (German pronunciation: [ˈʁɔbɪn ˈkɔx]; born 17 July 1996) is a German professional footballer who plays as a centre back or defensive midfielder for Bundesliga club Eintracht Frankfurt and the Germany national team. Koch made his first-team debut for 1. FC Kaiserslautern after graduated from the club's youth system and also played in Bundesliga with SC Freiburg. He has previously played for Eintracht Trier II, Eintracht Trier, 1. FC Kaiserslautern II, 1. FC Kaiserslautern and SC Freiburg. He was included in the Germany U21 team before joining the first-team in November 2019. In August 2020, he signed for Leeds United ahead of their Premier League return and in 2021, represented Germany at UEFA Euro 2020.
