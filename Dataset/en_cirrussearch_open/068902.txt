David Owen Vaughan (born 18 February 1983) is a Welsh former footballer who is manager of Crewe Alexandra's under-18 team. Vaughan previously played for Crewe Alexandra, where he spent seven years between 2000 and 2007. He then spent one season playing in Spain with Real Sociedad before returning to England to join Blackpool for three seasons. He then played for Sunderland, Nottingham Forest and Notts County. He played internationally for Wales, having previously played for the Welsh under-19 and under-21 sides earlier in his career. In total, he earned 42 caps for the senior side, scoring once.
