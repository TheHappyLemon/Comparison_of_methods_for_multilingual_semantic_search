Murmastiene Parish (Latvian: Murmastienes pagasts, Latgalian: Mūrmastinis pogosts) is an administrative unit of Varakļāni Municipality, Latvia. The parish covers the northern and western parts of the municipality. Its center is the village of Murmastiene. The parish was split off Barkava Parish in 1990 and initially was a part of Madona District.
