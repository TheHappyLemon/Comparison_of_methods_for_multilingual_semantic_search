Dairis Bertāns (born September 9, 1989) is a Latvian professional basketball player for VEF Rīga of the Latvian–Estonian Basketball League. Bertāns also plays for the Latvia national basketball team. He is the older brother of Dāvis Bertāns. He is a 6-foot-4-inch (1.93 m), 200-pound (91 kg) shooting guard.
