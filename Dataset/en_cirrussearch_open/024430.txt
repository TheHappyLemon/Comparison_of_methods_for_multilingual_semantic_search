Yuzhnoukrainsk (Ukrainian: Южноукраїнськ, pronounced [jʊʒ.no.ʊ.krɐˈjinʲsʲk]) is a city on the Southern Bug river, in Voznesensk Raion, Mykolaiv Oblast, Ukraine, about 350 kilometers (over 200 miles) south of the capital Kyiv. It hosts the administration of Yuzhnoukrainsk urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 38,560 (2022 estimate).
