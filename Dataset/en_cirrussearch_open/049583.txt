Ádám Nagy (born 17 June 1995) is a Hungarian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie B club Spezia on loan from Pisa, and the Hungary national team. He began his career at Ferencváros, making his professional debut for the reserves in August 2013 and for the first team in May 2015. Nagy won eight caps and scored one goal for Hungary at youth level. He made his full international debut against Northern Ireland on 7 September 2015 coming on as a substitute, and was chosen for UEFA Euro 2016.
