Bartashevich is a Belarussian-language patronymic surname derived from the diminutive Bartash (Bartosz) of the name Bartholomew. Polish counterpart: Bartoszewicz, Russian : Bartoshevich, Lihuuanian: Bartoševičius. Notable people with the surname incllude: Marharyta Bartashevich (born 1990), Belarusian female acrobatic gymnast Yaroslava Bartashevich [de] (2005), Russian female tennis player
