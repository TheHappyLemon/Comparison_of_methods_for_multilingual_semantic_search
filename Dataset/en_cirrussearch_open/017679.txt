Vysokaye (Belarusian: Высокае, romanized: Vysokaje; Russian: Высокое, romanized: Vysokoye; Ukrainian: Високе, romanized: Vysoke; Polish: Wysokie) is a town in Brest Region, Belarus. The westernmost point of Belarus is located a few kilometers to the southwest from Vysokaye on the Bug River. As of 2023, it has a population of 4,843. The town has a railway station on the Brest line.
