Gaizinkalns Nature Park is can be found in Latvia in Madona Municipality Vestienas, Aronas and Bērzaunes parishes , Natura 2000 territory. The territory of the park covers Vidzeme Uplands around Gaizinkalns, Lake Talejas  [lv] and Viešūrs [lv] ( Lake Kaķīša ), as well as several small lakes. Here is the highest place in Latvia - Gaizinkalns - 311.94 m.
