Byerazino or Berezino (Belarusian: Беразіно, romanized: Bierazino; Russian: Березино; Polish: Berezyna; Lithuanian: Berezinas; Yiddish: בערעזין, romanized: Berezin) is a town on the Berezina River in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Byerazino District. As of 2023, the population is 11,395.
