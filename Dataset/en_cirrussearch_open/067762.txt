Izobilny (Russian: Изоби́льный) is a town and the administrative center of Izobilnensky District in Stavropol Krai, Russia, located 54 kilometers (34 mi) northwest of Stavropol, the administrative center of the krai. Population: 40,555 (2010 Census); 38,926 (2002 Census); 32,905 (1989 Census). It was previously known as Izobilnoye (until 1965).
