The Monastery of Yuste is a monastery in the small village now called Cuacos de Yuste (in older works San Yuste or San Just) in the province of CÃ¡ceres in the autonomous community of Extremadura, Spain. The monastery was founded by the Hieronymite order of monks in 1402.
