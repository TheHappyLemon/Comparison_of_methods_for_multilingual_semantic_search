Sara Blicavs (Latvian: SÄra Blicava; born 15 February 1993) is an Australian professional basketball player. She currently plays for the Melbourne Boomers in the WNBL. Blicavs was a member of the Australian Women's basketball team (Opals) at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. The Opals were eliminated after losing to the USA in the quarterfinals.
