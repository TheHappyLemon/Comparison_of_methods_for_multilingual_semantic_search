Bad Roads (Russian: Плохие дороги, romanized: Plokhiye dorogi, Ukrainian: Погані дороги, romanized: Pokhani dorokhy) is a Ukrainian-Russian-language Ukrainian drama film directed by Nataliia Vorozhbyt and released in 2020. Its world premiere took place on 3 September 2020 at the 35th Venice International Critics' Week, where it was screened in competition. In September 2021 it was selected as the Ukrainian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 94th Academy Awards.
