Carl Henrik Jordan Larsson (born 20 June 1997) is a professional footballer who plays as a forward for Danish club Copenhagen. Born in Scotland, he represents the Sweden national team. Starting his career with Högaborgs BK in Sweden in the early 2010s, Larsson represented Helsingborgs IF, NEC Nijmegen, and IFK Norrköping before joining Spartak Moscow in 2019. After the Russian invasion of Ukraine he left the team, playing for AIK, Schalke 04 and Copenhagen. He made his full international debut for the Sweden national team in 2018 and was a squad member at UEFA Euro 2020.
