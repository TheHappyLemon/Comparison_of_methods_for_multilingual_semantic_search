Mikhail Zhukov (born January 3, 1985, in Leningrad, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union) is a Russian professional ice hockey center who is currently an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played for Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod of the Kontinental Hockey League Zhukov was selected by the Edmonton Oilers in the 3rd round (72nd overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft.
