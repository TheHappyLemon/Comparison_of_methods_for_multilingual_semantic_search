Shchuchyn (Belarusian: Шчучын, romanized: Ščučyn, listen; Russian: Щучин, romanized: Shchuchin, IPA: [ˈɕːʉtɕɪn]; Polish: Szczuczyn; Yiddish: שטשוטשין, romanized: Shtshutshin) is a town in Grodno Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Shchuchyn District. As of 2023, it has a population of 15,653.
