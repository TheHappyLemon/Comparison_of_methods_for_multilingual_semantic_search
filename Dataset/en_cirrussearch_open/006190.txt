The ruble, rouble or rubel (Belarusian: рубель, romanized: rubieĺ, Russian: рубль, romanized: rubl'; abbreviation: руб or р. in Cyrillic, Rbl in Latin (plural: Rbls); ISO code: BYN) is the currency of Belarus. It is subdivided into 100 kopecks (Belarusian: капейка, romanized: kapiejka, Russian: копейка, romanized: kopeyka).
