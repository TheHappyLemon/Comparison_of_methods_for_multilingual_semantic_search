Lyuban (Russian: Любань, IPA: [lʲʊˈbanʲ]) is a town in Tosnensky District of Leningrad Oblast, Russia, located on the Tigoda River 85 kilometers (53 mi) southeast of St. Petersburg. Population: 4,188 (2010 Census); 4,616 (2002 Census); 5,078 (1989 Census). The median age is 40.9 and the population of Lyuban is 53.5% female.
