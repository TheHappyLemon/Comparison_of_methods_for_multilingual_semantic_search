Sokyriany (Ukrainian: Сокиряни, pronounced [sokɪˈrʲɑnɪ]; Romanian: Târgu Secureni) is a small city in Dnistrovskyi Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast (province) of Ukraine, Northern Bessarabia. It hosts the administration of Sokyriany urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 8,547 (2022 estimate).
