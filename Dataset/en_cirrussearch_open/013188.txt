Disgaea 3: Absence of Justice (魔界戦記ディスガイア3, Makai Senki Disugaia Surī) is the third video game in the Disgaea series by Nippon Ichi. It was released for the PlayStation 3 video game console. Jack Niida, Localization Director for NIS America, said, "Disgaea 3 will have a downloadable content feature through the PlayStation Network to download new and old returning characters."
