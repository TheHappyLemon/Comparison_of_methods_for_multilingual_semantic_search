Starogard Gdański (Polish pronunciation: [staˈrɔɡard ˈɡdaj̃skʲi] ; until 1950: Starogard; Kashubian: Starogarda; formerly German: Preußisch Stargard) is a city in Pomeranian Voivodeship in northern Poland with 48,328 inhabitants (2004). Starogard is the capital of Starogard County. It is also the second biggest city (after Tczew) of the ethnocultural region Kociewie and is populated by Kociewians.
