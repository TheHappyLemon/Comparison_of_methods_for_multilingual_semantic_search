Football for Friendship (Russian: ФУТБОЛ ДЛЯ ДРУЖБЫ) is an annual International Children's Social Programme implemented by Gazprom. Within the framework of the programme, football players at the age of 12 from different countries take part in the annual International Football for Friendship Children's Forum, Football for Friendship World Championship, International Day of Football and Friendship The global operator of the programme is AGT Communications Group (Russia).
