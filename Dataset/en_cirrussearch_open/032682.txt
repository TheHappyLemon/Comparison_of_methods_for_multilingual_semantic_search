Palkino (Russian: Палкино) is an urban locality (a work settlement) and the administrative center of Palkinsky District of Pskov Oblast, Russia, located 36 kilometers (22 mi) southwest of Pskov. Municipally, it is incorporated as Palkino Urban Settlement, the only urban settlement in the district. Population: 2,924 (2010 Census); 3,201 (2002 Census); 3,406 (1989 Census).
