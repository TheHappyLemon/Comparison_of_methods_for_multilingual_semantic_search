Rakhiv (Ukrainian: Рахів, pronounced [ˈrɑxiu̯]; see also other names) is a city located in Zakarpattia Oblast (province) in western Ukraine. It is the administrative center of Rakhiv Raion (district). Population: 15,536 (2022 estimate). Rakhiv's date of the foundation is often taken to be 1447, although a written mentions of this settlement are attested since AD 910.
