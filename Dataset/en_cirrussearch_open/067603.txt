Fatezh (Russian: Фате́ж) is a town and the administrative center of Fatezhsky District in Kursk Oblast, Russia, located on the Usozha River 45 kilometers (28 mi) north of Kursk, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: 4,691 (2021 Census); 5,404 (2010 Census); 5,710 (2002 Census); 5,712 (1989 Census). 4,959 (1897).
