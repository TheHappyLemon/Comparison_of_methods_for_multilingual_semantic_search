Taj Jami Gibson (born June 24, 1985) is an American professional basketball player for the Detroit Pistons of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the USC Trojans and was selected 26th overall by the Chicago Bulls in the 2009 NBA draft. A power forward/center, Gibson has previously played for the Chicago Bulls, Oklahoma City Thunder, Minnesota Timberwolves, New York Knicks, and Washington Wizards.
