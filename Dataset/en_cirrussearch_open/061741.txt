Dmitry Ilyich Gordon (Ukrainian: Дмитро Ілліч Гордон, romanized: Dmytro Illich Hordon; Russian: Дмитрий Ильич Гордон; born 21 October 1967) is a Ukrainian journalist, interviewer and politician. He is also the editor-in-chief of the newspaper "Boulevard" (from June 1995), later "Gordon Boulevard". In June 2019, Gordon became the head of the election headquarters of the party Strength and Honor.
