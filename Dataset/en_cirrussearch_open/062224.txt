Gali (Georgian: გალი [ɡali] ; Abkhaz: Гал, Gal) is a town in Abkhazia, Georgia 77 km southeast to Sukhumi. It is the centre of Gali District and was in the United Nations security zone prior to the Russian veto of the UNOMIG Mission in 2009. As of 2011 the town has a population of 7 605 inhabitants.
