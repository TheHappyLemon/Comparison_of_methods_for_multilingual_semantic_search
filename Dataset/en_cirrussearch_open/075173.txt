Babīte Municipality (Latvian: Babītes novads) is a former municipality in Latvia. The municipality was formed in 2009 when Babīte parish and Sala parish merged with the administrative centre becoming Piņķi. On 1 July 2021, Babīte Municipality ceased to exist and its territory was merged into Mārupe Municipality Large bog Cenas Tīrelis in Babīte Municipality
