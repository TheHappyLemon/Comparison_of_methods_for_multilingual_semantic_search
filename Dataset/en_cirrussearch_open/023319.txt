Marina Evgenevna Trandenkova (Russian: Марина Евгеньевна Транденкова; born 7 January 1967) is a Russian athlete who competed mainly in the 100 metres. She competed for the Unified Team at the 1992 Summer Olympics held in Barcelona, Spain in the 4 x 100 metres where she won the silver medal with her teammates Olga Bogoslovskaya, Galina Malchugina and Irina Privalova. She was reprimanded in 1996 after testing positive for Bromantane. She is married to the former pole vaulter Igor Trandenkov.
