Zinkiv (Ukrainian: Зінькі́в, pronounced [z⁽ʲ⁾inʲˈk⁽ʲ⁾iu̯]) is a city on the Tashan River in the Poltava Oblast in central Ukraine. The city was previously the administrative center of the Zinkiv Raion until 2020, when it became part of the Poltava Raion. It hosts the administration of Zinkiv urban hromada [uk], one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The city's estimated population in 2022 was 9,168 (2022 estimate).
