Konergino (Russian: Конергино; Chukchi: Кэӈиергын, Kèňiergyn) is a village (selo) in Iultinsky District of Chukotka Autonomous Okrug, in the Far Eastern Federal District of Russia. Population 424 (2010 Census); Municipally, Konergino is subordinated to Iultinsky Municipal District and incorporated as Konergino Rural Settlement.
