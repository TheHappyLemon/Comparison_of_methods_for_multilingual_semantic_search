Her Loss is the first collaborative studio album by Canadian rapper and singer Drake and British-American rapper 21 Savage. It was released on November 4, 2022, through OVO Sound and Republic Records. The album features a sole guest appearance from Travis Scott. It is the third part of what Drake described as "a trilogy of albums", following Certified Lover Boy (2021) and Honestly, Nevermind (2022). Despite receiving mixed reviews from critics, Her Loss performed well commercially, debuting at number one on the Canadian Albums and US Billboard 200 charts, among other countries.
