Novoselytsia (Ukrainian: Новоселиця [ˌnɔwoˈsɛlɪtsʲɐ]; Romanian: Noua Suliță [ˈnowa ˈsulitsə]; Yiddish: נאוואסעליץ, romanized: Novoselitz; German: Nowoselitza) is a city in Chernivtsi Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast (province) of Ukraine. It stands at the northern tip of Bessarabia region, on its border with Bukovina. It hosts the administration of Novoselytsia urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 7,399 (2022 estimate).
