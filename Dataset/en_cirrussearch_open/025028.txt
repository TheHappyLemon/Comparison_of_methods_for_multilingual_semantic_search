Perevalsk (Ukrainian: Перевальськ; Russian: Перевальск) is a city in the southwestern part of the Luhansk Oblast (province) of Ukraine, Donbas. Population: 24,817 (2022 estimate), 25,941 (2013 est.). Perevalsk borders immediately on the larger town of Alchevsk which in 1961–1991 also was named after the Paris Commune as Kommunarsk.[citation needed] Perevalsk has been occupied by Russia and its proxy the Luhansk People's Republic since 2014.
