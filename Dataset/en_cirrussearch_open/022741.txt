Kommunar (Russian: Коммуна́р) is a town in Gatchinsky District of Leningrad Oblast, Russia, located on the banks of the Izhora River 35 kilometers (22 mi) south of the center of St. Petersburg. Population: 20,211 (2010 Census); 17,164 (2002 Census); 17,791 (1989 Census). The name of the town means "Communards", in reference to supporters of the 1871 popular uprising in Paris.
