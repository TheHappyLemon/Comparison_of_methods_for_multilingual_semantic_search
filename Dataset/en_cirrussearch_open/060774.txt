Tetyana Kit (Ukrainian: Тетяна Кіт, born 1 September 1994 in Lviv) is a Ukrainian freestyle wrestler of Spartak sports club. She was bronze medalist of the 2015 World Championships and silver medalist of the European championships in 2016. In 2021, she won one of the bronze medals in her event at the 2021 Poland Open held in Warsaw, Poland. She represented Ukraine at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Japan. She competed in the women's 57 kg event.
