Smalyavichy District or Smaliavičy District (Belarusian: Смалявіцкі раён, romanized: Smalavicki rajon; Russian: Смолевичский район, romanized: Smolevičskij raion) is a district (raion) of Minsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is the town of Smalyavichy. The town of Zhodzina is administratively separated from the district.
