Kateryna Burmistrova (Ukrainian: Катерина Володимирівна Бурмістрова, sometimes written as Burmystrova, born 20 November 1979) is a Ukrainian wrestler. Burmistrova was born on 20 November 1979 in Sumy, Ukraine. At the 2012 Summer Olympics held in London, United Kingdom, she competed in the women's freestyle 72 kg event where she lost her first bout, in the round of 16, against Russia's Natalia Vorobieva.
