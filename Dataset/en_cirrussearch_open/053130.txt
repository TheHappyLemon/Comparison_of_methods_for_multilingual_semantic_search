She was the 1984 World silver medalist and four-time European bronze medalist. She competed at two Winter Olympic Games. Levandi was born Anna Anatolevna Kondrashova, and grew up in Moscow, Russia (then Soviet Union). She is married to Allar Levandi, an Estonian former Olympic Nordic combined skier she met at the 1988 Winter Olympics. The couple lives in Estonia and she now works as a coach in Tallinn. Their son, Arlet Levandi, is a figure skater who competes for Estonia.
