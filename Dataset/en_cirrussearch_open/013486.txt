Aleksandr Aleksejevich Muzychenko (Russian: Александр Алексеевич Музыченко; born 7 May 1955) is a retired Soviet/Russian sailor, Olympic champion for the USSR team. He started to sail in the age of 6, in Omsk, at the river Irtish, He scored his Olympic triumph in star class at the 1980 Summer Olympics in Tallinn, together with Valentyn Mankin. Now Aleksandr Muzychenko lives in Latvia, Riga and sails the Dragon class.
