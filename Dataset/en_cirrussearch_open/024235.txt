Chervyen or Cherven (Belarusian: Чэрвень, romanized: Červień, IPA: [ˈtʂɛrvʲenʲ]; Russian: Червень; Polish: Czerwień; Lithuanian: Červenė), previously known as Ihumen (Ігумен) until 1923, is a town in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Chervyen District. In 2016, its population was 9,718. As of 2023, it has a population of 10,542.
