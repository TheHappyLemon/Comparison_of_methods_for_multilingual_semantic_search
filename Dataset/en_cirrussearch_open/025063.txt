Sukhodilsk (Ukrainian: Суходільськ; Russian: Суходольск, romanized: Sukhodolsk) is a city in Krasnodon Municipality in Luhansk Oblast of Ukraine. Population: 20,390 (2022 estimate), 21,061 (2013 est.). Since 2014, Sukhodilsk has been under the effective control of the self-proclaimed by the Luhansk People's Republic.[better source needed]
