Anheuser-Busch InBev SA/NV, commonly known as AB InBev, is a Belgian multinational drink and brewing company based in Leuven, Belgium and is the largest brewer in the world. In 2023, the company was ranked 72nd in the Forbes Global 2000. Anheuser-Busch InBev SA/NV is a publicly listed company, with its primary listing on the Euronext Brussels.
