Gvardeysk (Russian: Гварде́йск, IPA: [ɡvɐrˈdʲejsk] ; known prior to 1946 by its German name Tapiau Tapiau; Lithuanian: Tepliava; Polish: Tapiawa/Tapiewo), is a town and the administrative center of Gvardeysky District in Kaliningrad Oblast, Russia, located on the right bank of the Pregolya River 38 kilometers (24 mi) east of Kaliningrad. Population figures: 13,353 (2021 Census); 13,899 (2010 Census); 14,572 (2002 Census); 11,904 (1989 Census). It is located within the historic region of Sambia.
