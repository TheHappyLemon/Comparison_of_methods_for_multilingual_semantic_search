Kickboxer is a 1989 American martial arts film directed by David Worth and starring Jean-Claude Van Damme. Former world kickboxing champion Dennis Alexio is also featured. It spawned several sequels. The film was produced by Kings Road Entertainment and released by The Cannon Group, Inc. on September 8, 1989. It was a box-office success, grossing $50 million on a budget of $2.7 million.
