Masami Ihara (井原 正巳, Ihara Masami, born September 18, 1967) is a Japanese former footballer and manager. He is the currently head coach of J1 League club of Kashiwa Reysol. A defender, Ihara was captain of the Japan national team for more than a decade in the 1990s, together with striker Kazuyoshi Miura and Brazilian-born midfielder Ruy Ramos. Ihara's long standing record of 122 national team appearances was surpassed by Yasuhito Endo on October 16, 2012.
