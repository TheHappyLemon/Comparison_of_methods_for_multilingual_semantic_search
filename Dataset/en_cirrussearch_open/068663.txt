Carbonia (Italian: [karˈbɔːnja] ; Sardinian: Crabònia [kraβɔɲa]) is a town and comune in the Province of South Sardinia, Sardinia, Italy. Along with Iglesias it was a co-capital of the former province of Carbonia-Iglesias, now suppressed. It is located in the south-west of the island, at about an hour by car or train from the regional capital, Cagliari.
