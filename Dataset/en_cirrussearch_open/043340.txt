Marwan Mohsen Fahmy Tharwat Gameldin Mahmoud Fahmy (Arabic: مروان محسن فهمي ثروت جمال الدين محمود فهمي; born 26 February 1989) is an Egyptian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Future. Mohsen made his debut with Egypt on 3 September 2011 against Sierra Leone in an 2012 Africa Cup of Nations qualifier, where he also scored his first international goal.
