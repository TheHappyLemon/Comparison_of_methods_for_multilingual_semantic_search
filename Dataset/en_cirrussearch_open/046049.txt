Vladislav Dimitrievich Kartaev (Картаев, Владислав Дмитриевич ; born February 10, 1992) is a Russian ice hockey player. He is currently an unrestricted free agent who most recently played with Salavat Yulaev Ufa of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Kartaev made his Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) debut playing with Salavat Yulaev during the 2010–11 KHL season.
