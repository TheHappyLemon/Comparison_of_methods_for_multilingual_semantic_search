Victorious Return' (Latvian: Mājup ar uzvaru, Russian: Возвращение с победой, romanized: Vozvrashchenye s pobedoy) is a 1947 Soviet Latvian film directed by Aleksander Ivanov and Pāvels Armands [lv]. The film was directed by the Riga Film Studio. The television broadcast of the film on Latvian TV in 1954 was the full first television broadcast in Latvian history.
