Phil Bauhaus (born 8 July 1994 in Bocholt) is a German cyclist, who currently rides for UCI WorldTeam Team Bahrain Victorious. He was named in the start list for the 2017 Giro d'Italia. Bauhaus joined Bahrain–Merida for the 2019 season on an initial one-year contract. He was named in the startlist for the 2019 Vuelta a España. Bauhaus withdrew from the 2023 Tour de France.
