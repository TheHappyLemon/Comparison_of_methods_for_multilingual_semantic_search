Nikolaos "Nikos" Morakis (Greek: Νικόλαος "Νίκος" Μωράκης), sometimes seen as Dorakis (Greek: Δοράκης), was a Greek shooter. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Morakis came third in the military pistol event with 205 points behind the American brothers John and Sumner Paine, and fourth in the free pistol event.
