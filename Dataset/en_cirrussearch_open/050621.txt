Joshua David Manson (born October 7, 1991) is an American and Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman for the Colorado Avalanche of the National Hockey League (NHL). Manson was selected by the Anaheim Ducks in the sixth round, 160th overall, of the 2011 NHL Entry Draft. He is the son of former longtime NHL defenceman, Dave Manson. Manson won the Stanley Cup with the Avalanche in 2022.
