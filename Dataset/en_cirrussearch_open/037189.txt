Korsun-Shevchenkivskyi (Ukrainian: Ко́рсунь-Шевче́нківський, IPA: [ˈkɔrsʊnʲ ʃeu̯ˈtʃɛnkiu̯sʲkɪj]) is a small city located in Cherkasy Raion, Cherkasy Oblast, central Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Korsun-Shevchenkivskyi urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The city rests on the banks of the Ros River. Population: 17,216 (2022 estimate).
