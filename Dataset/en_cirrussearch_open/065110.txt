Bushehr (Persian: بوشهر [buːˈʃe(h)ɾ] ), also known as Bandar-e-Bushehr, is a city in the Central District of Bushehr County, Bushehr province, Iran. It is a port city and the capital of the province, county, and district. At the 2006 census, its population was 161,674 people in 25,158 households. The census in 2011 counted 195,222 people in 52,204 households. The latest census in 2016 showed the population had risen to 223,504 people in 63,820 households.
