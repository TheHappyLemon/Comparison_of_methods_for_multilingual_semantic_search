Mārtiņš Porejs (born July 6, 1991 in Riga) is a Latvian ice hockey player, currently playing for Dinamo Riga of Kontinental Hockey League. He played for SK Riga 18 and SK LSPA/Riga teams before joining HK Riga. On November 23, 2012, he made his debut in KHL playing for Dinamo Riga on 1–0 win against Neftekhimik.
