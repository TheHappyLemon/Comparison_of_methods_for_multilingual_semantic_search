The Lužnice (Czech pronunciation: [ˈluʒɲɪtsɛ] ; German: Lainsitz) is a river in the Czech Republic and Austria, a right tributary of the Vltava River. It flows through Lower Austria and through the South Bohemian Region. It is 197.9 km (123.0 mi) long, of which 157.7 km (98.0 mi) is the Czech Republic, which makes it the 11th longest river in the Czech Republic.
