Mišo Brečko (Slovene pronunciation: [ˈmíːʃɔ ˈbréːtʃkɔ]; born 1 May 1984) is a Slovenian former professional footballer who played as a right-back. He spent most of his career in Germany, most notably with 1. FC Köln. At international level, he made 77 appearances for the Slovenia national team, also participating at the 2010 FIFA World Cup.
