Yevgeny Vladimirovich Lukyanov (Russian: Евгений Владимирович Лукьянов; born on 10 December 1951), is a Russian politician and diplomat who had served as the ambassador to Belarus from 2021 to 2022. He also served as the ambassador to Latvia from 2016 to 2021. He also served as the Deputy Secretary of the Security Council of the Russia from 2012 to 2016. Lukyanov had also served as the Deputy Plenipotentiary Representative of the Northwestern Federal District from 2007 to 2010.
