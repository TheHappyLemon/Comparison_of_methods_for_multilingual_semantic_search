Hola Prystan (Ukrainian: Го́ла При́стань, pronounced [ˈɦɔɫɐ ˈprɪstɐnʲ]) is a city in Skadovsk Raion, Kherson Oblast, southern Ukraine occupied by Russia. It hosts the administration of the Hola Prystan urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. It has a population of 13,544 (2022 estimate). The Konka, a tributary of the Dnieper, flows through the city.
