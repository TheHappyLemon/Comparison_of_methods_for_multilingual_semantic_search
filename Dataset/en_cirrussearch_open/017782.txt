This page deals with a city in Ukraine. For the film named after this city, see Luboml (film). Liuboml (Ukrainian: Любомль, romanized: Ľubomľ; Russian: Любомль; Belarusian: Любамль, romanized: Lubaml; Polish and German: Luboml; Yiddish: ליבעוונע, romanized: Libevne) is a town in Kovel Raion, Volyn Oblast, western Ukraine. It is located close to the border with Poland. It serves as the administrative center of Liuboml urban hromada. Population: 10,295 (2022 estimate).
