Troy Stecher (/ˈstɛtʃər/ STETCH-ər; born April 7, 1994) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League (NHL). He previously played for the Vancouver Canucks, Detroit Red Wings, Los Angeles Kings and Calgary Flames. Undrafted into the NHL, Stecher played for the University of North Dakota for three seasons. In his last season, he was honoured as a Second-Team All-American West, All-NCHC Second Team, and NCHC Offensive Defenseman of the Year.
