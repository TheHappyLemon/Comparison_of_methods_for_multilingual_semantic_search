Svetlogorsk (Russian: Светлого́рск​; German: Rauschen [ˈʁaʊ̯ʃən] ; Lithuanian: Raušiai) is a coastal resort town and the administrative center of Svetlogorsky District of Kaliningrad Oblast, Russia, located on the coast of the Baltic Sea on the Sambia Peninsula, 39 kilometers (24 mi) northwest of Kaliningrad. Population figures: 16,099 (2021 Census); 10,772 (2010 Census); 10,950 (2002 Census); 11,881 (1989 Census).
