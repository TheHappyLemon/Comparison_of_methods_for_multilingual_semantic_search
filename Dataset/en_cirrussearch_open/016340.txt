Terebratella is a genus of brachiopods belonging to the family Terebratellidae. The genus has almost cosmopolitan distribution. Species: Terebratella crenulata Sowerby, 1846 Terebratella crofti Owen, 1980 Terebratella dorsata (Gmelin, 1790) Terebratella labradorensis Sowerby, 1846 Terebratella sanguinea (Leach, 1814) Terebratella sookensis Clark & Arnold, 1923 Terebratella tenuis Tort, 2003
