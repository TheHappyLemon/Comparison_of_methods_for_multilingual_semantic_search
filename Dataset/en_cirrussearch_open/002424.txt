Marc Staal (born January 13, 1987) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defenceman who is currently playing for the Philadelphia Flyers of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has played over 1,000 regular season games and was previously a member of the New York Rangers, Detroit Red Wings, and Florida Panthers. He is the second oldest of the Staal brothers to play in the NHL. Of the four brothers, Marc is the only defenceman and only one who has never played for the Carolina Hurricanes.
