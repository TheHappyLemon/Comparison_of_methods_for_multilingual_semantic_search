Force of Nature is a 2020 American action thriller film directed by Michael Polish from a screenplay by Cory Miller. The film stars Emile Hirsch, Kate Bosworth, Mel Gibson, David Zayas, Stephanie Cayo, Will Catlett, Swen Temmel, Tyler Jon Olsen, and Jorge Luis Ramos. Force of Nature was released on digital, DVD, and Blu-ray disc by Lionsgate on June 30, 2020, receiving negative reviews from critics.
