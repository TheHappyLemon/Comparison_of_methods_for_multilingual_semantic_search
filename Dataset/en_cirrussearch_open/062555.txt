Petro Antonovych Zakovorot (Ukrainian: Петро Антонович Заковорот, Russian: Пётр Антонович Заковорот; 1871 – 5 March 1951) was a Ukrainian fencer. Born in Kup'ievakha village, now the Kharkiv region in Ukraine, he competed in the individual masters sabre event at the 1900 Summer Olympics representing Russia, and finished 7th.
