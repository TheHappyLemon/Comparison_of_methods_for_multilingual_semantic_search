Cameron Island is one of the uninhabited members of the Queen Elizabeth Islands in the Canadian arctic islands in Nunavut, Canada. Located in the Arctic Ocean, close to Bathurst Island, it has an area of 1,059 km2 (409 sq mi), 42 to 43 kilometres (26 to 27 mi) long and 47 to 38 kilometres (29 to 24 mi) wide. Île Vanier lies immediately to the south, across the Arnott Strait.
