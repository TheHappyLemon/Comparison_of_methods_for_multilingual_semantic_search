Bogdan Macovei (born 28 January 1983 in Vatra Dornei, Romania) is a Romanian-born Moldovan luger who has competed since 2003. Competing in two Winter Olympics, he earned his best finish of 30th in the men's singles event at Turin in 2006. Macovei also finished 44th in the men's singles event at the 2007 FIL World Luge Championships in Igls.
