Turov or Turaw (Belarusian: Тураў, romanized: Turaŭ; Russian: Туров; Lithuanian: Turava; Ukrainian: Турів; Polish: Turów; Yiddish: טוראָוו) is a town in Zhytkavichy District, Gomel Region, Belarus. As of 2023, it has a population of 2,766. It served as the capital of the Principality of Turov during the Middle Ages.
