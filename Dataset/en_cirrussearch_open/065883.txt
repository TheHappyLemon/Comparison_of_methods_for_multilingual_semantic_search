Agate Caune (Latvian pronunciation: [ɑːgɑːtɛ saʊnɛ] ah-GAH-teh sown-eh; born 7 August 2004) is a Latvian track and field athlete. A multiple national champion, she is also the Latvian national record holder over 3000 metres. She was named the Rising Star of the Year and the female U20 Track and Field Athlete of the Year in 2022.
