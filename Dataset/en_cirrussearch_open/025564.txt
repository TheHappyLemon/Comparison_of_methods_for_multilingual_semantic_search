Peeter Järvelaid (born 28 November 1957) is an Estonian socialite and formerly legal historian. Järvelaid was a professor in the University of Tallinn. He thinks that he has developed semiotic and personality-centered research direction. Järvelaid published fewf articles mostly about the European and Estonian legal history and education, published in Estonian, English, German, French, Russian, Latvian, Finnish, Lithuanian and Swedish.
