Historical affiliations Pskov Republic 1414–1510 Grand Duchy of Moscow 1510–1547 Tsardom of Russia 1547–1721  Russian Empire 1721–1917 Russian Republic 1917 Soviet Russia 1917–1920 Latvian Republic 1920–1940  Soviet Union 1940–1991  Russian Federation 1991–present Vyshgorodok (Russian: Вышгородок; Latvian: Višgorodoka or Augšpils) is a selo ('village') in Gavry volost, Pytalovsky District of Pskov Oblast, Russia, founded in the 15th century as a borderline fortress.
