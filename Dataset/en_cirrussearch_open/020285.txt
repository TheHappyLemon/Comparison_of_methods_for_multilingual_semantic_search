Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Anisin (born 11 July 1951) is a Russian retired professional ice hockey player. Anisin played in the Soviet Championship League for HC CSKA Moscow, Krylya Sovetov Moscow, SKA Leningrad and HC Spartak Moscow. He would later play in the Yugoslav Ice Hockey League for KHL Medveščak and in Serie A in Italy for HC Milano Saima. He also played for the Soviet team during the 1972 Summit Series against Canada. He was inducted into the Russian and Soviet Hockey Hall of Fame in 1973.
