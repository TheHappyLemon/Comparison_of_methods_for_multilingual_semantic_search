Cesvaine Municipality (Latvian: Cesvaines novads) is a former municipality in Vidzeme, Latvia. The municipality was formed in 2009 by reorganization of Cesvaine town with its countryside territory, the administrative centre being Cesvaine. In 2010 Cesvaine Parish was created from the countryside territory of Cesvaine town. As of 2020, the population was 2,266. On 1 July 2021, Cesvaine Municipality ceased to exist and its territory was merged into Madona Municipality.
