Aleksandrs Beļavskis (Russian: Александр Иосифович Белявский, Belarusian: Аляксандр Іосіфавіч Бяляўскі; born January 17, 1964) is a former Latvian/Soviet ice hockey player and captain of team Latvia, and current coach for the Latvia men's national ice hockey team. He played for two clubs for most of his player career Dinamo Riga where he started his professional career in 1985 and IF Björklöven. He was a prolific goal scorer and skilled playmaker. Beļavskis has played for Latvia at the 2002 Winter Olympics.
