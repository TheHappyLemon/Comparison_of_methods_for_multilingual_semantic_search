Chyormoz (Russian: Чёрмоз, Russian pronunciation: [ˈtɕɵrməs]) is a town in Ilyinsky District of Perm Krai, Russia, located on the shores of the Kama Reservoir, 102 kilometers (63 mi) north of Perm, the administrative center of the krai. Population: 3,861 (2010 Census); 4,376 (2002 Census); 5,907 (1989 Census)..
