Fokino (Russian: Фо́кино) is a closed town in Primorsky Krai, Russia, located on the coast of the Peter the Great Gulf, on the Abrek Bay, about 45 kilometers (28 mi) south of Vladivostok, the administrative center of the krai. Population: 23,696 (2010 Census); 26,457 (2002 Census). It was previously known as Tikhookeansky (until 1980), Shkotovo-17 (until 1994).
