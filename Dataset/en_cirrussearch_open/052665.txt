ESC Clermont Business School is a business school located in city of Clermont-Ferrand, France. Established in 1919, the school of management is a Grande Ecole recognized by the French Ministry of Higher Education and Research. The business school holds AACSB accreditation since 2005 and its Bachelor programme received EPAS accreditation in 2018. The school obtained the AMBA accreditation in 2020. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. The school is part of Conférence des Grandes écoles, the Consortium of Graduate Schools of Management. The school has an alumni network of over 13,000 graduates.
