The Esperanto Wikipedia (Esperanto: Vikipedio en Esperanto, IPA [vikipeˈdio en espeˈɾanto] or Esperanta Vikipedio [espeˈɾanta vikipeˈdio]) is the Esperanto version of Wikipedia, which was started on 11 May 2001, alongside the Basque Wikipedia. With over 352,000 articles as of April 2024[update], it is the 37th-largest Wikipedia as measured by the number of articles, and the largest Wikipedia in a constructed language.
