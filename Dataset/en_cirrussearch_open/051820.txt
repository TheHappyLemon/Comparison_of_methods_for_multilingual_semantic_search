The 2020 Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race was a road cycling race that was held on 2 February 2020 in Geelong, Australia. It was the sixth edition of the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race and the second event of the 2020 UCI World Tour. The race was won in a two-up sprint by Dries Devenyns of the Deceuninck–Quick-Step team, ahead of Team Ineos rider Pavel Sivakov, with Mitchelton–Scott's Daryl Impey finishing third for the third year in succession.
