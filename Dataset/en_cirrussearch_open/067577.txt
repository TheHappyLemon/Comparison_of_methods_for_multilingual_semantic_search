Apsheronsk (Russian: Апшеро́нск) is a town and the administrative center of Apsheronsky District of Krasnodar Krai, Russia, located on the Pshekha River (in the Kuban's basin). Population: 39,488 (2020), [40,225 (2010 Census); 39,608 (2002 Census); 34,505 (1989 Census); 33,400 (1968). It was previously known as Apsheronskaya.
