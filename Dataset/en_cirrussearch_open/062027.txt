Iryna Andriivna Vereshchuk (Ukrainian: Ірина Андріївна Верещук, IPA: [iˈrɪ.nɐ ɐnˈdrʲi.jiu̯nɐ ʋe.reˈʃtʃuk]; born 30 November 1979) is a Ukrainian social activist, politician, and former People's Deputy of Ukraine of the 9th convocation. On 4 November 2021, Vereshchuk was appointed Deputy Prime Minister of Ukraine and Minister of Reintegration of Temporarily Occupied Territories.
