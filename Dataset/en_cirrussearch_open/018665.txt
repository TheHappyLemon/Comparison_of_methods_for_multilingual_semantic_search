Aija Kukule (born 1 October 1956) is a Latvian singer. She performed at the 1981 Mikrofona aptauja with Līga Kreicberga. Their song was "Dāvāja Māriņa meitenei mūžiņu" (English: Dear Māra gave the girl life). The song was later a big hit in Soviet Union and covered by many artist, most famously by Alla Pugacheva. Aija Kukule received the Grand Music Award in 1993.
