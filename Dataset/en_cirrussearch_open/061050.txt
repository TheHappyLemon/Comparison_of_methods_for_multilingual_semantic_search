Oleksiy Miacheslavovych Danilov (Ukrainian: Олексій Мячеславович Данілов; born 7 September 1962) is a Ukrainian politician who was the secretary of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine since 3 October 2019, until 26 March 2024, the day that he was replaced by Oleksandr Lytvynenko, the former head of the Foreign Intelligence Service.
