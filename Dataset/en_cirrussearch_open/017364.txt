Astravyets (Belarusian: Астравец, romanized: Astraviec, IPA: [astraˈvʲet͡s]; Russian: Островец, romanized: Ostrovets, IPA: [ɐstrɐˈvʲets]; Polish: Ostrowiec; Lithuanian: Astravas) is a town in Grodno Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Astravyets District. As of 2023, it has a population of 14,805.
