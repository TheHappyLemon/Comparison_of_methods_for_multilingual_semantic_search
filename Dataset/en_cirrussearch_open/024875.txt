Kypuche (Ukrainian: Кипуче; Russian: Кипуче, romanized: Kipuche) or Artemivsk (Ukrainian: Артемівськ; Russian: Артёмовск, romanized: Artyomovsk) is a city in Luhansk Oblast, Ukraine. Population: 7,162 (2022 estimate), 7,506 (2013 est.). It is situated in the historical and economic Donbas region of eastern Ukraine. The city has been under the control of Russia and its proxy, the Luhansk People's Republic, since early 2014.
