Simone Zaza (born 25 June 1991) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a striker, most recently for Serie A club Torino. Zaza began his professional club career with Atalanta in 2008, where he remained for two seasons. In 2010, he moved to Sampdoria, where he was sent on loan spells to Juve Stabia, Viareggio and Ascoli. In 2013 he joined Sassuolo, and in 2015 Juventus. In his first season with the latter club he immediately won a domestic double. At the international level, Zaza made his senior debut for Italy in 2014, and went on to represent his nation at UEFA Euro 2016.
