Lozova (Ukrainian: Лозова́, IPA: [ɫozoˈʋɑ] ) or Lozovaya (Russian: Лозова́я) is a city in Kharkiv Oblast, eastern Ukraine. It serves as the administrative center of Lozova Raion. Lozova hosts the administration of Lozova urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 53,126 (2022 estimate). Lozova is the 2nd largest city in Kharkiv Oblast after Kharkiv in terms of population.
