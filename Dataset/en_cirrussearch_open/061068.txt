Lucas Tolentino Coelho de Lima (born 27 August 1997), better known as Lucas Paquetá (Brazilian Portuguese: [ˈlukas pakɛˈta]), is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays either as an attacking-midfielder or winger for Premier League club West Ham United and the Brazil national team. For the Brazil national team, he has played for them in the Copa América - which he won once in 2019 - and in one World Cup. He began his career in 2007 for CR Flamengo where he won the 2016 Copa São Paulo de Júniores title. He has also played for Flamengo, AC Milan and Lyon.
