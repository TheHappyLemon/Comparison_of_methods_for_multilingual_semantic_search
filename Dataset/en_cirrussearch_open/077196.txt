Kyzyl (/kɪˈzɪl/; Russian: Кызыл, Russian pronunciation: [kɨˈzɨɫ]; Tuvan: Кызыл, romanized: Kızıl, Tuvan pronunciation: [qɤˈzɤɫ], ) is the capital city of the republic of Tuva, Russia. The name of the city means "red" or "crimson" in Tuvan (and in many other Turkic languages). Its population was 120,067 (2021 Census);
