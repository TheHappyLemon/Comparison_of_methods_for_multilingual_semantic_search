Almazna (Ukrainian: Алма́зна, Russian: Алмáзная, romanized: Almaznaya) is a small city in Kadiivka Municipality, Luhansk Oblast of Ukraine. The population of Almazna was estimated to be 4,148 (2022 estimate). Industries within Almazna include metallurgy and manufacturing; a coal mine is also located near the city. Since 2014, Almazna has been under the effective control of the self-proclaimed Luhansk People's Republic.
