58°31′16.05″N 31°16′30.87″E﻿ / ﻿58.5211250°N 31.2752417°E﻿ / 58.5211250; 31.2752417 The Millennium of Russia (Russian: Тысячелетие России, romanized: Tysyacheletiye Rossii) is a bronze monument in the Novgorod Kremlin. It was erected in 1862 to celebrate the millennium of Rurik's arrival to Novgorod, an event traditionally taken as a starting point of the history of Russian statehood.
