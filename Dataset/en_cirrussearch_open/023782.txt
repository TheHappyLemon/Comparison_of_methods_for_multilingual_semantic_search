Antratsyt or Antratsit (Ukrainian and Russian: Антрацит) is a city in Luhansk Oblast, Ukraine. Until 1962 it was known as Bokove-Antratsyt. Its population is approximately 52,150 (2022 estimate). Since early 2014, Antratsyt has been under the occupation of Russia and its puppet state the Luhansk People's Republic.
