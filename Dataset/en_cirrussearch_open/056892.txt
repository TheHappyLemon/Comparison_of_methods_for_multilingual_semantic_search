Albina Grčić (Croatian pronunciation: [ǎlbiːna ɡr̩̂tʃitɕ]; born 6 February 1999), known professionally by the mononym Albina, is a Croatian singer. She began her career after participating in season three of The Voice Hrvatska where she finished third. She represented Croatia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2021 in Rotterdam with the song "Tick-Tock”.
