Merav Michaeli (Hebrew: מֵרַב מִיכָאֵלִי, romanized: Mērav Mīxāʾēlī; born 24 November 1966) is an Israeli politician, journalist, TV anchor, radio broadcaster, feminist, and activist. She is the leader of the Israeli Labor Party, and in her last government position she was the Minister of Transport in the thirty-sixth government of Israel.
