La Rioja (Spanish pronunciation: [la ˈrjoxa], local pronunciation Spanish pronunciation: [la ˈʂjoxa]), is the capital and largest city of La Rioja Province, Argentina, located in the east of the province. La Rioja is located on the foot of the Velasco Sierras, 1,167 km (725 mi) from Buenos Aires, and 430 km (267 mi) from Córdoba.
