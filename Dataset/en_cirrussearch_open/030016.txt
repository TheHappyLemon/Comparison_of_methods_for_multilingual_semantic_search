Kārlis Čukste (born on June 17, 1997) is a Latvian ice hockey player and defenseman. Currently (2023), he represents Brynäs IF, the second-strongest league in Sweden, Hockeyallsvenskan. His father, Ainārs Čukste, is a Latvian basketball coach, while his mother, Baiba Čukste, is the former captain of the Latvian women's ice hockey team.
