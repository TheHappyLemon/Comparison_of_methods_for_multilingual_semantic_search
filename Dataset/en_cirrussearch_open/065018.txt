Final Cut (French: Coupez !) is a 2022 zombie comedy film written and directed by Michel Hazanavicius. It is a French remake of the 2017 Japanese film One Cut of the Dead. It stars Romain Duris and Bérénice Bejo. The film revolves around a crew remaking the film depicted in the original film. Yoshiko Takehara reprises her role as a producer. The film was released in France on 17 May 2022, and for screening at the Cannes Film Festival as its opening film the same day.
