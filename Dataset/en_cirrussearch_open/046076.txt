Shrook Wafa (born 13 May 1997) is an Egyptian chess player who holds the title of Woman Grandmaster. She won the Women's African Chess Championship in 2013. Thanks to this achievement she earned the title of Woman grandmaster (WGM) and qualified for the Women's World Chess Championship 2015. She has won the Women's African Chess Championship a further three times: 2014, 2016 and 2019. In the Women's World Chess Championship 2015 she was knocked out in the first round by the second seed Ju Wenjun. Her sister Shahenda Wafa also is a Woman Grandmaster.
