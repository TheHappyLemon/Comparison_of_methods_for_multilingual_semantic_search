Ivars Ciaguns (born 14 March 1977) is a Latvian alpine skier. In his early career he competed at the 1994 and 1995 Junior World Championships, finishing lowly. He competed at the 1998, 2002 and the 2006 Winter Olympics. His best placement was a 25th place in slalom at the 2002 edition.
