Ashmyany or Oshmyany (Belarusian: Ашмяны, romanized: Ašmiany; Russian: Ошмяны; Lithuanian: Ašmena; Polish: Oszmiana; Yiddish: אָשמענע, romanized: Oshmene) is a town in Grodno Region, Belarus. It is located 50 kilometres (31 mi) from Vilnius in Lithuania, and serves as the administrative center of Ashmyany District. It lies in Ashmyanka's river basin. As of 2023, it has a population of 16,870. The town was the birthplace of the general Lucjan Żeligowski and Jewish Soviet partisan Abba Kovner.
