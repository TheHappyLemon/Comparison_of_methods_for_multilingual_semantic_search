Hellendoorn (Dutch pronunciation: [ˌɦɛlə(n)ˈdoːr(ə)n] ; Tweants: Heldern or Healndoorn) is a municipality and town in the middle of the Dutch province of Overijssel. As of 2019, the municipality had a population of 35,808. There is an amusement park near the town of Hellendoorn called Avonturenpark Hellendoorn [nl]. At the outskirts of the town there is an ice cream factory from Unilever, where Ben & Jerry's is produced for the European market.
