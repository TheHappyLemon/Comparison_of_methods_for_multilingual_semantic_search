Yury Alexandrovich Alexandrov (Russian: Юрий Александров, Russian pronunciation: [ˈjʉrʲij ɐlʲɪˈksandrəf]; born June 24, 1988) is a Russian professional ice hockey defenceman currently an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played as an alternate captain for HC Sochi in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).
