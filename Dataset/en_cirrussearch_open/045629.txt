Biskupiec [bʲisˈkupʲɛt͡s] (German: Bischofsburg, [ˈbɪʃɔfsbʊʁk] ) is a town in northern Poland, in Warmia, in the Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship. It is located in Olsztyn County and, as of December 2021, it has a population of 10,496. The countryside surrounding Biskupiec is a popular tourist destination, part of the Masurian Lake District.
