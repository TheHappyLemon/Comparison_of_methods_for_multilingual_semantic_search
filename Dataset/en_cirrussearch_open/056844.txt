Nataša Andonova (Macedonian: Наташа Андонова; born 4 December 1993) is a North Macedonian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Spanish Liga F club Levante UD and captains the North Macedonia women's national team. She is the younger sister of Sijce Andonova, who is also a Macedonian footballer. She is the top scorer in the history of the North Macedonian national team.
