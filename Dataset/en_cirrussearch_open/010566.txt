Amadora (Portuguese pronunciation: [ɐmɐˈðoɾɐ] ) is a municipality and urbanized city in the northwest of the Lisbon metropolitan area and 10 km from central Lisbon. The population in 2011 was 175,136, in an area of 23.78 km² (9.2 sq mi). It is the fourth biggest, and most densely populated, city in Portugal.
