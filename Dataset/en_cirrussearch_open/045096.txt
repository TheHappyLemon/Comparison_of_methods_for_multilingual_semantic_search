Oscar Anders Klefbom (born 20 July 1993) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman who played for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League (NHL). He is injured and is generally considered to have finished his playing career, but he has not retired to date. He was selected 19th overall in the 2011 NHL Entry Draft by the Oilers.
