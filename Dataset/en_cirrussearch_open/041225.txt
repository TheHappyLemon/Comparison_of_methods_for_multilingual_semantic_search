Visa requirements for Latvian citizens are administrative entry restrictions by the authorities of other states placed on citizens of Latvia. As of January 2024, Latvian citizens had visa-free or visa on arrival access to 186 countries and territories, ranking the Latvian passport 9th (tied with Slovakia and Slovenia) in terms of travel freedom according to the Henley-on-Thames Passport Index. There are different visa requirements for Latvian non-citizens.
