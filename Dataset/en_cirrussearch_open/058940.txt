Big Hit Music (Korean: 빅히트 뮤직, romanized: Bighiteu Myujik, stylized in all caps), formerly Big Hit Entertainment (Korean: 빅히트 엔터테인먼트, romanized: Bighiteu Enteoteinmeonteu), is a South Korean record label established in 2005 by Bang Si-hyuk. It was re-branded and re-structured into an independent label under Hybe Corporation (formerly Big Hit Entertainment Co., Ltd.) in March 2021. As of date, the label is home to soloist Lee Hyun, and boy groups BTS and Tomorrow X Together. It previously managed soloist Lim Jeong-hee, and groups 8Eight, 2AM, and co-managed Glam.
