Klimavichy (Belarusian: Клiмавiчы, romanized: Klimavičy; Russian: Климовичи, romanized: Klimovichi; Lithuanian: Klimavičai) is a town in Mogilev Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Klimavichy District. Klimavichy is located 124 km (77.05 mi) east of Mogilev on the bank of the Kalinica River. In 2009, its population was 17,064. As of 2023, it has a population of 15,121.
