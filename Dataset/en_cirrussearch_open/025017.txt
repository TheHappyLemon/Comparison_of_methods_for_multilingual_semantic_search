Horodenka Raion (Ukrainian: Городе́нківський райо́н, romanized: Horodenkiwśkyj rajon) was a raion (district) of Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast (region) of Ukraine. The city of Horodenka was the administrative center of the raion. The raion was abolished on 18 July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast Oblast to six. The area of Horodenka Raion was merged into Kolomyia Raion. The last estimate of the raion population was 51,370 (2020 est.).
