2008–09 GP2 Asia Series Drivers' Champion: Kamui Kobayashi Teams' Champion: DAMS Previous 2008 Next 2009–10 Support series: Speedcar Series The 2008–09 GP2 Asia Series season was the second season of the GP2 Asia Series. It began on 18 October 2008 and ended on 26 April 2009 and consisted of eleven races at six events.
