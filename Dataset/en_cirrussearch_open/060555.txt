Petr Koukal (Czech pronunciation: [ˈpɛtr̩ ˈkoukal]; born 16 August 1982) is a Czech professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for HK Hradec Kralove in the Czech Extraliga (ELH). He participated at the 2010 IIHF World Championship as a member of the Czech Republic National men's ice hockey team. His older brother Martin is a cross-country skiing world champion.
