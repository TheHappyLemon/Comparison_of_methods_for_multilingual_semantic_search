Kamianka-Buzka (Ukrainian: Кам'янка-Бузька, romanized: Kamianka-Buzka, pronounced [ˈkɑmjɐnkɐ ˈbuzʲkɐ]) is a city in Lviv Raion, Lviv Oblast, of western Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Kamianka-Buzka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The city was previously known as Kamianka Strumilova, and was a district city in Galicia. From 1918 to 1939 it was part of Poland, and called Kamionka Strumiłowa, was the capital of a county of the Tarnopol Voivodeship. Population: 10,397 (2022 estimate).
