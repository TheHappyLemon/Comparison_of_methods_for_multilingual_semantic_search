Daniel Ceballos Fernández (born 7 August 1996) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for La Liga club Real Madrid and the Spain national team. Ceballos started his career at Spanish club Real Betis where he helped them win the Segunda División. He was signed by Real Madrid in 2017, and two years later signed for English Premier League club Arsenal on loan. Ceballos won his first cap for Spain in September 2018, in 6–0 home win over Croatia in the UEFA Nations League.
