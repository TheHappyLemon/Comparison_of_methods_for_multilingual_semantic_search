Raymond Manuel Albert Domenech (French pronunciation: [ʁɛ.mɔ̃ dɔ.me.nɛk]; born 24 January 1952) is a French football manager and former player. He managed the France national team from 2004 to 2010, reaching the 2006 FIFA World Cup Final. He was dismissed after their elimination from the 2010 FIFA World Cup, for serious misconduct.
