The Monument to Salavat Yulaev (Russian: Памятник Салавату Юлаеву, Pamyatnik Salavatu Yulayevu; Bashkir: Салауат Юлаев һәйкәле, Salawat Yulayev häykäle) is a monument to Salawat Yulayev located in Ufa, Russia. The monument is represented in the coat of arms of Bashkortostan. It was built in 1967. Wikimedia Commons has media related to Monument to Salawat Yulayev, Ufa.
