Fudbalski klub Inđija Toyo Tires (Serbian Cyrillic: Фудбалски клуб Инђија Тојо тајерс) is a football club based in the city of Inđija, Serbia. They compete in the Serbian First League, the second tier of the national league system. During Yugoslavia the club mainly participated in the amateur ranks of competition. The club's biggest success were two promotions to the Serbian SuperLiga for 2010–11 and 2019–20 seasons.
