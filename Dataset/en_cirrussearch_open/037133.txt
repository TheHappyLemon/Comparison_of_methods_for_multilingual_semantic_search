Kamianka (Ukrainian: Кам'янка; Ukrainian pronunciation: [ˈkɑmjɐnkɐ]) is a small city in the Cherkasy Raion of Cherkasy Oblast of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Kamianka urban hromada [uk], one of the hromadas of Ukraine. It had a population of 10,945 (2022 estimate). It is a countryside town approximately 300 kilometres (190 mi) southeast from Kyiv, located on the bank of the Tiasmyn River.
