Vynnyky (Ukrainian: Ви́нники, pronounced [ˈʋɪnːɪkɪ], Polish: Winniki) is a small city in Lviv Raion, Lviv Oblast (region) of Ukraine. It belongs to Lviv urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. As of 2021, its population was estimated to be 19,037 (2022 estimate). The city is part of Lychakiv Raion of the city of Lviv.
