Andrei Vladimirovich Kobyakov (born 21 November 1960) is a Belarusian politician. He served as the Prime Minister of Belarus between 2014 and 2018, appointed by president Alexander Lukashenko on 27 December 2014. Before this appointment, Kobyakov served as Lukashenko's Chief of Staff from 2012 to 2014. He was a Deputy Prime Minister from December 2003 to 28 December 2010.
