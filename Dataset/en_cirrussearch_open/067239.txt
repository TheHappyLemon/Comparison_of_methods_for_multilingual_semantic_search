The Polish Volunteer Corps (Polish: Polski Korpus Ochotniczy; Ukrainian: Польський добровольчий корпус, romanized: Polskyi dobrovolchyi korpus, PDK/ПДК) is a volunteer group of Poles fighting on the side of Ukraine during the Russian invasion of the country. The PDK fought in the Russian invasion of Ukraine against Russia. The PDK also fought in the Belgorod incursions with the Russian Volunteer Corps and Freedom of Russia Legion.
