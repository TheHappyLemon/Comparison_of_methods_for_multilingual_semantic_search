Riga United FC is a Latvian football club, founded in 2007. The club plays at the School 49 ground in Riga. Riga United men's first team play in the Latvian League 2 Riga Division, the men's reserve team play in Latvian League 3 and the Ladies team play in the Latvian Women's League. Riga United is recognized as being the first truly multinational football club in the Baltic states, and to date, more than 50 different nationalities have played for the club.
