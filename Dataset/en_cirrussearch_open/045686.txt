Robert Sabolič (born September 18, 1988) is a Slovenian ice hockey player currently playing for EC VSV in the ICE Hockey League (ICEHL). He participated at several IIHF World Championships as a member of the Slovenia men's national ice hockey team. He has previously played with Swedish team, Södertälje. During the 2013–14 season he has won the German championship with Ingolstadt. Sabolič was an important part in Ingolstadt's success, with 14 points (9 goals, 4 assists) in 21 postseason games. In addition, he has scored the most goals during the 2013–14 DEL playoffs. After parts of two seasons with Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod, Sabolič left the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) following the 2018–19 season, signing a one-year contract with Swiss club, HC Ambrì-Piotta of the NL, on 26 July 2019.
