Bogdan Ionuț Lobonț (Romanian pronunciation: [boɡˈdan joˈnut͡s lobont͡s]; born 18 January 1978) is a Romanian professional football coach and former player who played as a goalkeeper. Currently he is the goalkeeping coach at Rapid București. He amassed 87 appearances for the Romania national team between 1998 and 2018, representing the country in two European Championships. He was popularly nicknamed Pisica ("The Cat") in Romania, due to his quick reflexes and relative small body frame for a goalkeeper.
