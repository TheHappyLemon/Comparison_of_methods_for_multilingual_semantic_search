Sunzha (Russian: Сунжа, romanized: Sunzha; Ingush: Шолжа-Пхье, romanized: Šolža-Pꜧe) is a town and the administrative center of the Sunzhensky District of the Republic of Ingushetia, Russia. Before 2016 it was called Ordzhonikidzevskaya. Population: 61,598 (2010 Census); 65,112 (2002 Census); 17,318 (1989 Census). As of the 2010 Census, it was the most populous rural locality in Russia.
