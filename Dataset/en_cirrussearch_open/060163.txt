Ivan Dmitriyevich Morozov (Russian: Иван Дмитриевич Морозов; born May 5, 2000) is a Russian ice hockey forward currently playing for HC Spartak Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) on loan as a prospect for the Vegas Golden Knights of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was drafted in the second round, 61st overall, by Vegas in the 2018 NHL Entry Draft.
