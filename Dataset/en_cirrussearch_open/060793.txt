René Ressejac-Duparc (28 September 1880 – 19 April 1941) was a French footballer who played as a midfielder and who competed in the 1900 Olympic Games, winning a silver medal as a member of the USFSA team, which was primarily Club Français players. With Club Français, he reached the finals of the 1900 Challenge International du Nord, and the 1899 and 1900 Championnat de Paris [fr], and won the Coupe Manier [fr] three consecutive times at the turn of the century between 1899 and 1901.
