Ozoliņš (Old orthography: O(h)solin(g); feminine: Ozoliņa) is a Latvian surname, derived from the Latvian word for "oak" (ozols). Individuals with the surname include: Alberts Ozoliņš (1896–1985), Latvian weightlifter Kārlis Ozoliņš (1905—1987), Latvian Soviet politician and journalist Kārlis Ozoliņš (born 1994), Latvian ice hockey player Kārlis Ozoliņš (born 2002), Latvian tennis player Sandis Ozoliņš (born 1972), Latvian ice hockey player Valdemārs Ozoliņš (1896–1973), Latvian composer and conductor
