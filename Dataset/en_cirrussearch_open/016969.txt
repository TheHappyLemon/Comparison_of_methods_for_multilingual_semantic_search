Nick Leonardus van de Wall (Dutch: [ˈnɪk leːoːˈnɑrdʏs fɑn də ˈʋɑl]; born 9 September 1987), better known as Afrojack, is a Dutch DJ, music producer and remixer. In 2007, he founded the record label Wall Recordings; his debut album Forget the World was released in 2014. Afrojack regularly features as one of the ten best artists in the Top 100 DJs published by DJ Mag. He is also the CEO of LDH Europe.
