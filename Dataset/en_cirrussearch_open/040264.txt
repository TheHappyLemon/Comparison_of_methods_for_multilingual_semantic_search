Romowe Rikoito is a neofolk band currently based in Vilnius, Lithuania. It was formed by Glabbis Niktorius in 1995 and named after ancient Prussian spiritual center Romowe (also known as Rikoyto). The group has published 6 albums – first two albums Narcissism and L’Automne Eternel based on ideas of melancholy, death and grief, while Āustradēiwa, Undēina ,Nawamār and UKA are dedicated to Old Prussia. They sing in the Old Prussian language.
