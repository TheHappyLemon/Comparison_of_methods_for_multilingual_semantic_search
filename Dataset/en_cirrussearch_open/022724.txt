Pikalyovo (Russian: Пикалёво), sometimes Pikalevo (Russian: Пикалево), is a town in Boksitogorsky District of Leningrad Oblast, Russia, located 246 kilometers (153 mi) southeast of St. Petersburg and 25 kilometers (16 mi) east of Boksitogorsk. Population: 21,562 (2010 Census); 23,325 (2002 Census); 24,510 (1989 Census).
