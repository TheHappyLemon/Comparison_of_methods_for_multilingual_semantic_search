The Ivanovs vs. The Ivanovs (Russian: Ивановы-Ивановы, romanized: Ivanovy-Ivanovy) is a Russian TV series produced since 2017. Made by "Yellow, Black and White" (episodes 1 to 40) and "Pick Up Film" film companies. The series has very high television ratings and has won award "TEFI". The producer of 1 and 2 season was a Ukrainian media executive Daria Leygonie-Fialko.
