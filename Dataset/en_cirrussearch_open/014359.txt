Alexander Alexandrovich Galchenyuk (born February 12, 1994) is an American-Belarusian professional ice hockey center currently under contract with SKA Saint Petersburg of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He has previously played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Montreal Canadiens, Arizona Coyotes, Pittsburgh Penguins, Minnesota Wild, Ottawa Senators, Toronto Maple Leafs, and Colorado Avalanche. He was drafted third overall by the Canadiens in the 2012 NHL Entry Draft. Galchenyuk is of Belarusian descent, but was born in the United States and has represented the United States internationally.
