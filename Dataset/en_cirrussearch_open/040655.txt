The prime minister, literally the minister of state (French: Ministre d'Ã‰tat), is the head of government of Monaco, being appointed by and subordinate to the Prince of Monaco. During their term of office, the officeholder is responsible for directing the work of the government and in charge of foreign relations. As the monarch's representative, the prime minister also presides (with voting powers) over the Council of Government, directs the executive services and commands the police and military. Since 1 September 2020, Pierre Dartout has been the prime minister.
