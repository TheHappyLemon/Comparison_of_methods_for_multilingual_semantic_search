17 Again is a 2009 American teen fantasy comedy film directed by Burr Steers. It stars Matthew Perry, Zac Efron, Leslie Mann, Thomas Lennon, and Michelle Trachtenberg, with Melora Hardin and Sterling Knight in supporting roles. The film follows a 37-year-old man named Mike (Perry) who becomes his 17-year-old self (Efron) after a chance accident. The film was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures and released in the United States on April 17, 2009, to mixed reviews from critics and grossed $139.5 million.
