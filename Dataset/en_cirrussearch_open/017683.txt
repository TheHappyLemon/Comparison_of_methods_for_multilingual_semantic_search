Luninyets or Luninets (Belarusian: Лунінец, romanized: Luniniec; Russian: Лунинец; Polish: Łuniniec; Lithuanian: Luninecas; Yiddish: לונינייץ, romanized: Luninitz) is a town in Brest Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Luninyets District. As of 2023, it has a population of 23,548. It is home to Luninets air base.
