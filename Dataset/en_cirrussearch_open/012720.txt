Jarosław (Polish: [jaˈrɔswaf] ; Ukrainian: Ярослав, romanized: Yaroslav, IPA: [jɐroˈslɑu̯]; Yiddish: יאַרעסלאָוו, romanized: Yareslov; German: Jaroslau) is a town in southeastern Poland, situated on the San River. The town had 37,479 inhabitants in 2019. It is the capital of Jarosław County in the Subcarpathian Voivodeship.
