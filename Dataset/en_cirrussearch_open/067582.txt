Labinsk (Russian: Лаби́нск) is a town in Krasnodar Krai, Russia, located on the Bolshaya Laba River (a tributary of the Kuban) 145 kilometers (90 mi) southeast of Krasnodar and 50 kilometers (31 mi) southwest of Armavir. Population: 57,428 (2021), 59,330 (2020), 62,864 (2010 Census); 61,446 (2002 Census); 57,958 (1989 Census); 53,000 (1972).
