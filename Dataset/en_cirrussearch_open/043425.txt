Nahitan Michel Nández Acosta (born 28 December 1995) is a Uruguayan professional footballer who plays for Serie A club Cagliari and the Uruguay national team. A versatile player, Nández can play as a central midfielder, right-back, attacking midfielder or winger. After starting his career at Peñarol, he later played in Argentina for Boca Juniors and in Italy for Cagliari. A full international for Uruguay since 2015, he played at the 2018 FIFA World Cup and the 2019 and 2021 Copa América.
