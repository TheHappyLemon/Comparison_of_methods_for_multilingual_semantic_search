Ilya Andreyevich Kablukov (Russian: Илья Андреевич Каблуков) (born 18 January 1988) is a Russian professional ice hockey forward. He is currently playing with HC Dynamo Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Kablukov was selected by the Vancouver Canucks in the 5th round (146th overall) of the 2007 NHL Entry Draft.
