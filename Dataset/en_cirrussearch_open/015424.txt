Jeļena "Aļona" Ostapenko (born 8 June 1997) is a Latvian professional tennis player. She has career-high WTA rankings of No. 5 in singles, achieved on 19 March 2018, and No. 7 in doubles, reached on 12 September 2022. Ostapenko won the 2017 French Open singles title, becoming the first player from Latvia to win a major singles tournament and the first unseeded player to win the French Open since 1933. She has won eight WTA singles and seven doubles titles. She has also won fifteen singles titles and fifteen doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit, and she also won the junior singles event at the 2014 Wimbledon Championships. In addition to her singles career, she has played as a member of the Latvia Fed Cup team.
