Denis Vladimirovich Gurianov (Russian: Денис Владимирович Гурьянов; born 7 June 1997) is a Russian professional ice hockey winger for the Philadelphia Flyers of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was selected in the first round, 12th overall, by the Dallas Stars in the 2015 NHL Entry Draft. Gurianov has previously played for the Montreal Canadiens and Nashville Predators.
