Dmitri Semin (Russian: Дмитрий Сёмин, born August 14, 1983) is a Russian professional ice hockey centre who is currently an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played for Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was selected by the St. Louis Blues in the 5th round (159th overall) of the 2001 NHL Entry Draft.
