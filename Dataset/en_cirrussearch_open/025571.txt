Zalizne (Ukrainian: Залі́зне, IPA: [zɐˈl⁽ʲ⁾izne]), formerly Artemove (Ukrainian: Арте́мове; Russian: Артёмово), is a city in Donetsk Oblast, Ukraine. It is part of the Toretsk urban hromada. Population: 4,928 (2022 estimate); 6,725 (2001). On 19 May 2016, Artemove was renamed to Zalizne, conforming to the law prohibiting names of communist origin.
