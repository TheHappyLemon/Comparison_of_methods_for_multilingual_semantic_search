Lake Manitoba (French: Lac Manitoba) is the 14th largest lake in Canada and the 33rd largest lake in the world with a total area of 4,624 km2 (1,785 sq mi). It is located within the Canadian province of Manitoba about 75 km (47 mi) northwest of the province's capital, Winnipeg, at 50°59′N 98°48′W﻿ / ﻿50.983°N 98.800°W﻿ / 50.983; -98.800.
