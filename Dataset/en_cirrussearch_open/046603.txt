Blizzard of Souls (Latvian: Dvēseļu putenis) or The Rifleman (U.K. title) is a 2019 Latvian historical drama directed by Dzintars Dreibergs [lv]. It premiered on 8 November 2019 in Latvia and on 20 February 2020 internationally at the European Film Market. The film is an adaptation of Aleksandrs Grīns' novel of the same name [lv] written about his service as a Latvian Rifleman in World War I. It was selected as the Latvian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 93rd Academy Awards, but it was not nominated.
