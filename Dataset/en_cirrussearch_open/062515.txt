39°51′45″N 45°13′49″E﻿ / ﻿39.86250°N 45.23028°E﻿ / 39.86250; 45.23028 Hors (Armenian: Հորս) is a village in the Yeghegis Municipality of the Vayots Dzor Province in Armenia. It is located 24 km northwest of the Province center, on the bank of the right-hand Hors tributary of Selimaget, 6–7 km northwest of the confluence of the Selimaget and Yeghegis rivers.
