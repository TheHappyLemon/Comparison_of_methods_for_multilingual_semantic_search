Murat Yakin (Turkish: YakÄ±n; born 15 September 1974) is a Swiss football coach and former player. He is the manager of the Switzerland national football team. In 2021, following stints as manager at Swiss clubs including Basel, Grasshoppers and Sion, he was appointed as coach of the Swiss national team, guiding them to qualification for 2022 FIFA World Cup and UEFA Euro 2024.
