Dunalka Parish (Latvian: Dunalkas pagasts) is one of the administrative territories of South Kurzeme Municipality, in Latvia. On 1 July 2010, the parish had a population of 802. It covers an area of 87.5 km2. It is bordered by the parishes of Tadaiķi, Durbe, Vecpils, Medze, Vērgale, Saka, Cīrava and Aizpute. The center of the parish is Dunalka.
