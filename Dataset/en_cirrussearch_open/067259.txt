Andrii Andriiovych Herus (Ukrainian: Андрій Андрійович Герус; born November 10, 1995) is a Ukrainian military personnel, captain of the Ukrainian Armed Forces Air Force, MiG-29 fighter pilot, and participant in the Russian-Ukrainian war. He is a Hero of Ukraine (2022). In 2022, he was included in Forbes "30 Under 30: Faces of the Future" ranking.
