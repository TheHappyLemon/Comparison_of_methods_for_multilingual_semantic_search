Henrik Dalsgaard (born 27 July 1989) is a Danish professional footballer who plays as a right-back for Danish Superliga club FC Midtjylland. He transferred to Zulte Waregem in 2015 and won the Belgian Cup in the second of his two seasons with the club. Dalsgaard is a current Denmark international and was a member of the Danes' 2018 World Cup squad.
