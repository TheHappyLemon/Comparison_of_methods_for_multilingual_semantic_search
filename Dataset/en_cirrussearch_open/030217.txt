The Republic of the Congo is divided into 12 départements (départements). Departments are divided into communes and/or districts; which are further subdivided into urban communities (communautés urbaines) and rural communities (communautés rurales); which are further subdivided into quarters or neighborhoods (quartiers) and villages. These are: Bouenza Cuvette Cuvette-Ouest Kouilou Lékoumou Brazzaville Likouala Niari Plateaux Pool Sangha Pointe-Noire
