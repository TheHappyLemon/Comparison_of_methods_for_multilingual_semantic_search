Ukrainka (Ukrainian: Українка, romanized: Ukrayinka) is a city in Obukhiv Raion, Kyiv Oblast (province) of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Ukrainka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Ukrainka has a population of 16,081 (2022 estimate). In 2001, the population was 14,163. Since 2010, Ukrainka hosts the International Video Art and Short Film festival "VAU-Fest", held annually in the summer.
