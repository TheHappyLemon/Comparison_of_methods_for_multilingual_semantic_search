Druzhkivka (Ukrainian: Дружківка, pronounced [drʊʒˈk⁽ʲ⁾iu̯kɐ] ; Russian: Дружковка, romanized: Druzhkovka) is a city of oblast significance in Donetsk Oblast (province) of Ukraine. Population: 53,977 (2022 estimate); 64,557 (2001). The area of the city is 46 km2.[citation needed] Druzhkivka is located near the confluence of the Kryvyi Torets [uk] and Kazennyi Torets [uk] rivers, about 80 km northeast of Donetsk.
