Stanislav Lugailo (Ukrainian: Станіслав Антонович Люгайло; 1 January 1938 – 11 April 2021) was a Ukrainian volleyball player who competed for the Soviet Union in the 1964 Summer Olympics. In 1964, he was a squad member of the Soviet team which won the gold medal in the Olympic tournament. He also served as head coach of the women's national team of the Philippines from 1990 to 1993.
