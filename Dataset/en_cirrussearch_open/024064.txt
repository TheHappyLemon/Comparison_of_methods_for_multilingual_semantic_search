Dzyarzhynsk District or Dziaržynsk District (Belarusian: Дзяржынскі раён; Russian: Дзержинский район) is a district (raion) of Minsk Region in Belarus. The administrative center of the district is Dzyarzhynsk. The highest point of Belarus is situated in the district. It is 345 meters above level sea and called Dzyarzhynskaya Hara.
