Alexei Vasilievich Gusarov (Russian: Алексей Васильевич Гусаров) (born July 8, 1964) is a Russian former ice hockey defenceman. He played for the Quebec Nordiques, Colorado Avalanche, New York Rangers and St. Louis Blues. Him and Valeri Kamensky are the First Russian Born Players to Earn: The Triple Gold Club, along with being one of the First 10 Members in the Same :Triple Gold Club, Category. Earned with The Colorado Avalanche in 1996.
