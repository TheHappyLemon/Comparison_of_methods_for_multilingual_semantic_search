Rasmus Erik Dahlin (Swedish pronunciation: [ˈrǎsːmɵs da'li:n]; born 13 April 2000) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman and alternate captain for the Buffalo Sabres of the National Hockey League (NHL). Having been referred to as the most talented player available in the 2018 NHL Entry Draft class, Dahlin was selected first overall in the draft by the Sabres.
