Danko Lazović (Serbian Cyrillic: Данко Лазовић, Serbo-Croatian pronunciation: [dâːŋko lâːzoʋitɕ], Dutch pronunciation: [dɑŋkɔ lɑzɔvɪt͡ɕ]; born 17 May 1983) is a Serbian-born Dutch former footballer who played as a forward or winger. He represented the Serbia national team at the 2010 FIFA World Cup.
