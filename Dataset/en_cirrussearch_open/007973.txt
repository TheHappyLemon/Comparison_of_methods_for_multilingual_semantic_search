Weyinmi Efejuku Rose (born September 15, 1986) is a Jamaican professional basketball player who currently plays for Mets de Guaynabo of the FIBA Americas League. He was born in New York, New York. He has played for Providence Friars men's basketball and attended Brewster Academy.
