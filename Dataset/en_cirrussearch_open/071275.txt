"Hollow" is a song by Latvian singer-songwriter Dons. Self-described as a song that speaks about how one's inner insecurities can hold someone down, the song was written by Artūrs Šingirejs, Kate Northrop, and Liam Geddes. The song was released on 2 January 2024 by Universal. It is set to represent Latvia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2024, which will be held in Malmö, Sweden.
