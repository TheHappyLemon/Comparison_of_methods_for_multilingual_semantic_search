Borodianka (Ukrainian: Бородянка, pronounced [boroˈdʲɑnkɐ]) is a rural settlement in Bucha Raion, Kyiv Oblast, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Borodianka settlement hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 13,832 (2022 estimate). According to the 2001 census, its population was 12,535. The town is located on the Zdvyzh River [uk] which is a tributary of the Teteriv River.
