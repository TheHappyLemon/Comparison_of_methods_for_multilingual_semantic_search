Microsoft Teams is a team collaboration application developed by Microsoft as part of the Microsoft 365 family of products, offering workspace chat and video conferencing, file storage, and proprietary and third-party application integration. Teams replaced other Microsoft-operated business messaging and collaboration platforms, including Skype for Business and Microsoft Classroom. Throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, Teams, and software as Zoom, Slack, Google Meet, among others gained much interest as many meetings moved to a virtual environment. As of January 2023[update], it had around 280 million monthly users.
