Ismaël Bennacer (French pronunciation: [ismaɛl benasɛʁ]; Arabic: إسمَاعِيْل بن نَاصِر, romanized: İsmâîl bin Nâsır; Arabic pronunciation: [ʔismaːʕiːl bin naːsˁir]; born 1 December 1997) is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie A club AC Milan. Born in France, he plays for the Algeria national team. At club level, Bennacer has represented teams in France, England and Italy throughout his career. At international level, he made his senior debut for Algeria in 2016, where he was named player of the tournament.
