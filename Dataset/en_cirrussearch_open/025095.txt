Kalush (Ukrainian: Ка́луш, pronounced [ˈkɑlʊʃ] ) is a city set in the foothills of the Carpathian Mountains, in Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast (province) of western Ukraine. It is the administrative centre of Kalush Raion (district) and hosts the administration of Kalush urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Its estimated population was 65,088 (2022 estimate). Important local industries include chemicals and concrete.
