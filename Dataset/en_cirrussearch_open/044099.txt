Līvija Endzelīna (Latvian: Līvija Endzelīna) (October 30, 1927 – March 27, 2008) was a Latvian painter mainly noted for her still life paintings. She was one of the most well known personalities in Latvian art of the 20th century. In 1989, she received the honorary title of Meritorious Artist of the Latvian SSR.
