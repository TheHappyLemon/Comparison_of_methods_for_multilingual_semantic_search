Myadzyel (Belarusian: Мядзел, romanized: Miadziel, IPA: [ˈmʲadzʲel]; Russian: Мядель, romanized: Myadel; Polish: Miadzioł; Lithuanian: Medilas) is a town in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative centre of Myadzyel District. Myadzyel is located on the eastern shore of Lake Miastra, part of the Narach lake group in Narachanski National Park. As of 2023, it has a population of 6,999.
