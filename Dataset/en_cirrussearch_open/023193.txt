Emil Garipov (Tatar: Эмиль Гарипов, born August 15, 1991) is a Russian professional ice hockey goaltender of Tatar descent. He is currently an unrestricted free agent who most recently played with Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Garipov made his KHL debut with Ak Bars Kazan during the 2011–12 season.
