Todd Owen Cantwell (born 27 February 1998) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Scottish Premiership club Rangers. Cantwell began his career at Norwich City, joining the club at under-10 level in 2008 and making his first-team debut in January 2018. He made 129 appearances for Norwich over six seasons and had loan spells at Fortuna Sittard and AFC Bournemouth. He joined Rangers in January 2023. Cantwell played youth international football for England at under-17 and under-21 levels.
