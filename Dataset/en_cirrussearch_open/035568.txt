Kastsyukovichy (Belarusian: Касцюковічы, romanized: Kasciukovičy; Russian: Костюковичи, romanized: Kostyukovichi; Polish: Kostiukowicze) is a town in Mogilev Region, eastern Belarus. It is located in the east of the region, close to the border with Russia, and serves as the administrative center of Kastsyukovichy District. In 2009, its population was 15,993. As of 2023, it has a population of 15,089.
