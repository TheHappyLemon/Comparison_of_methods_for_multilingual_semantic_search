Jake Cyril Leonard Livermore (born 14 November 1989) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Championship club Watford. Livermore began his career at Tottenham Hotspur, spending most of his tenure out on loan at clubs in all three divisions of the English Football League. In 2013, he moved to Hull City, initially on a temporary basis, becoming permanent at the end of the season. After making 144 appearances for the club in all competitions, he joined West Bromwich Albion in January 2017. Livermore earned his first cap for the England national team in August 2012 in a friendly win against Italy.
