Jason David Ian Puncheon (born 18 June 1986) is a football manager and former English professional footballer who played as a midfielder. Following his goal against Everton on his Blackpool debut, he has scored in all of the top four divisions of English league football, scoring for Barnet in League Two, Milton Keynes Dons and Southampton in League One, Millwall in the Championship and Blackpool, Southampton and Crystal Palace in the Premier League.
