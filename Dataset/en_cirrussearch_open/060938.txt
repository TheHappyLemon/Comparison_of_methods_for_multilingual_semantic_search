Bohuslav (Ukrainian: Богуслав, Yiddish: באָסלעוו, romanized: Boslov) is a city on the Ros River in Obukhiv Raion, Kyiv Oblast, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Bohuslav urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 15,789 (2022 estimate); 17,135 (2001). It is known as Boslov by some of its Yiddish speaking residents and Boguslav (by the Russophones).
