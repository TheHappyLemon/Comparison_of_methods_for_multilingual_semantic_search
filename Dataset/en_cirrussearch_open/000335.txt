Rognedino (Russian: Рогне́дино) is an urban-type settlement and the administrative center Rognedinsky District Bryansk Oblast of Russia. Population: 2,775 (2021 Census); 3,158 (2010 Census); 3,354 (2002 Census); 3,452 (1989 Census). Located 96 km north-west of Bryansk, the administrative center of the region, 15km north of Dubrovka railway station.
