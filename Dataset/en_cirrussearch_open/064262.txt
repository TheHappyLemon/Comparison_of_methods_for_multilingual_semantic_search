Andre Dontrel Burt Jr. (born October 3, 2001), better known as SoFaygo (formerly stylized as $ofaygo), is an American rapper and singer. In 2020, Burt released the Angelic 7 mixtape, which included his 2019 breakthrough single "Knock Knock", which went viral on TikTok in late 2020. His debut album Pink Heartz was released on November 11, 2022, through Travis Scott's Cactus Jack Records. He has also collaborated with other artists, such as Don Toliver (also from Cactus Jack), Lil Uzi Vert, Gunna, Nav, Lil Tecca and Trippie Redd.
