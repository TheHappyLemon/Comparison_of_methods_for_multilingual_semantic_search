Beppu (別府市, Beppu-shi) is a city in Ōita Prefecture on the island of Kyushu, Japan. As of November 30, 2023, the city had a population of 113,045 in 62702 households, and a population density of 900 persons per km². The total area of the city is 125.34 km2 (48.39 sq mi). Beppu is famous for its hot springs.
