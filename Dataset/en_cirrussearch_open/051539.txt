Lophogastridae is a family of crustaceans belonging to the order Lophogastrida. Genera: Ceratolepis G.O.Sars, 1883 Chalaraspidum Willemoes-Suhm, 1895 Gnathophausia Willemoes-Suhm, 1873 Lophogaster M.Sars, 1857 Neognathophausia Petryashov, 1992 Paralophogaster Hansen, 1910 Pseudochalaraspidum Birstein & Tchindonova, 1962 Sarsius Tonini, da Silva, Serpa Filho & Freitas, 2016
