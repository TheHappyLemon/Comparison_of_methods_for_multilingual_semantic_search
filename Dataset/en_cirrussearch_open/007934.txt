Moldova is divided administratively into two levels: First level: 32 districts or raions (Romanian: raioane) 3 municipalities (municipii)—specifically Chișinău, Bălți, and Tighina 2 autonomous territorial units: Gagauzia and Left Bank of the Dniester (de facto Transnistria, which is not under control of the government of Moldova) Second level: Villages (Romanian: sate); two or more villages can form together a commune (Romanian: comună) Sectors[citation needed] 10 municipalities (municipii) Cities and towns
