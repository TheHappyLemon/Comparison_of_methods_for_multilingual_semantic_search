1983 January February March April May June July August September October November December Wikimedia Commons has media related to 1983. 1983 (MCMLXXXIII) was a common year starting on Saturday of the Gregorian calendar, the 1983rd year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 983rd year of the 2nd millennium, the 83rd year of the 20th century, and the 4th year of the 1980s decade. 1983 saw both the official beginning of the Internet and the first mobile cellular telephone call.
