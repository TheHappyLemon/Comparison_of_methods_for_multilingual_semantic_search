Daniela Adrija Vismane (born 10 August 2000) is a tennis player from Latvia. She has career-high rankings by the WTA of world No. 228 in singles achieved on 16 May 2022 and No. 162 in doubles achieved on 4 March 2024. On the ITF Circuit, she has won four titles in singles and 10 in doubles. She is a member of the Latvia Fed Cup team.
