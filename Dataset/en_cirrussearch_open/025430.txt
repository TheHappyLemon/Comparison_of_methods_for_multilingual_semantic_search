Finland is divided into: 19 regions (Finnish: maakunta, Swedish: landskap) the regions are divided into 70 sub-regions (Finnish: seutukunta, Swedish: ekonomisk region) the sub-regions are divided into 309 municipalities (Finnish: kunta, Swedish: kommun). Furthermore, health, social and emergency services are responsible of 21 Wellbeing services counties (Finnish: hyvinvointialue, Swedish: välfärdsområde).
