Uhniv (Ukrainian: У́гнів, Polish: Uhnów, Yiddish: הובנוב) is a city in Chervonohrad Raion, Lviv Oblast, western Ukraine. Population: 939 (2022 estimate). Uhniv is the smallest city of Ukraine. It is located 22 kilometres (14 mi) from Belz and 21 kilometres (13 mi) from Rava-Ruska. It is located next to the Poland–Ukraine border. Uhniv belongs to Belz urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. It was passed to Ukraine from Poland as a result of the 1951 Polish–Soviet territorial exchange.
