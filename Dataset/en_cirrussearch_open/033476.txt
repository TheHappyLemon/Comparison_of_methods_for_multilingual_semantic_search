Shinji Okazaki (岡崎 慎司, Okazaki Shinji, born 16 April 1986) is a Japanese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Belgian club Sint-Truiden. He is best known for winning the Premier League with Leicester City in 2016. Internationally, he is currently the top active Japan national team goalscorer and third all-time in team history with 50 goals.
