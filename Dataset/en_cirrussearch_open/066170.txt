Dzon or Džon (Cyrillic: "Џон") is an alternate form for the name John. People by that name Dzon Delarge, Congolese football player Mathias Dzon, Congolese politician Johnny Miljus (Džon Kenet Miljuš), Serbian-American baseball player John Lukic (Jovan "Džon" Lukić), English football player Timothy John Byford (Тимоти Џон Бајфорд), Serbian-English artist
