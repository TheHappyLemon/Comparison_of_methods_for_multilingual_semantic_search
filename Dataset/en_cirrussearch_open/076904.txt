Valdgale parish (Latvian: Valdgales pagasts) is situated in Talsi Municipality in Latvia. It is located approximately 13 km from the city of Talsi, 4 km from Valdemārpils and 20 km from Dundaga. The centre of Valdgale parish is called Pūņas [lv], from where it is about 5 km to the birthplace of the renowned Krišjānis Valdemārs. Valdgale has its own elementary school, shops, local authority building and more.
