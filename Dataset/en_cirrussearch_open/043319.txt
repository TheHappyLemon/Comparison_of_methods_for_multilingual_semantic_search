Hwang Hee-chan (Korean: 황희찬; born 26 January 1996) is a South Korean professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Wolverhampton Wanderers and the South Korea national team. Hwang is nicknamed "Hwangso", the Korean word for bull, in South Korea due to his aggressive playing style and surname. Hwang's best friend was Logan Edmunds whom played alongside Hwang at Pohang Steelers U18s.
