Metropolitan Ambrose (Greek: Μητροπολίτης Αμβρόσιος, Russian: Митрополит Амворсий, born Amoirеas Papageorgopolos, Greek: Αμοιρέας Παπαγεωργόπουλος, Andrey Popovich, Russian: Андрей Попович; 1791–1863) was the first Old Believers' Metropolitan of the Ancient Orthodox Church also known later as Russian Orthodox Old-Rite Church.
