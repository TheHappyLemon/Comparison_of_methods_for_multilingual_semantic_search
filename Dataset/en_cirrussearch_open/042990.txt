Lewis John Cook (born 3 February 1997) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Premier League club AFC Bournemouth. Cook is a graduate of Leeds United's academy. He has represented England at various youth levels. He has won the 2014 UEFA European Under-17 Championship, 2017 FIFA U-20 World Cup and 2018 Toulon Tournament with his country, captaining the team in the latter two tournaments.
