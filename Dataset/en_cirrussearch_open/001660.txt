2015 January February March April May June July August September October November December 2015 (MMXV) was a common year starting on Thursday of the Gregorian calendar, the 2015th year of the Common Era (CE) and Anno Domini (AD) designations, the 15th year of the 3rd millennium and the 21st century, and the 6th year of the 2010s decade. 2015 was designated by the United Nations as: International Year of Light International Year of Soil
