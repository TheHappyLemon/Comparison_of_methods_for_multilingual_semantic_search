Look up Honchar in Wiktionary, the free dictionary. Honchar (Cyrillic: Гончар) is an occupational surname of Ukrainian origin, the English equivalent being Potter. Its other transliterations may include Gonchar (Russian) or Hončar (Slovak). It may refer to: Oles Honchar, Ukrainian writer Serhiy Honchar, Ukrainian racing cyclist Sergei Gonchar, Russian ice hockey player
