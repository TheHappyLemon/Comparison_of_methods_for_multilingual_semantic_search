Mahidevran (Turkish pronunciation: [ˌmaːhidevˈɾan]; Ottoman Turkish: ماه دوران, meaning "moon of fortune", died 1581) also known as Gülbahar, (Turkish pronunciation: [ɟylbaˈhaɾ]; Ottoman Turkish: کل بھار, meaning "rose spring"), was a concubine of sultan Suleiman the Magnificent and the mother of Şehzade Mustafa of the Ottoman Empire.
