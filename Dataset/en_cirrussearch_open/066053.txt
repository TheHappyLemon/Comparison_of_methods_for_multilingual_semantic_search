Alexandros Ioannis "AJ" Ginnis (Greek: Αλέξανδρος Ιωάννης Γκιννής; born 17 November 1994) is a Greek-American World Cup alpine ski racer. Ginnis specializes in the technical events, with a focus on slalom. He made his World Cup debut in December 2014 and gained his first podium in February 2023. He then won the silver medal in the 2023 World Championships.
