Ograzhden (Огражден, pronounced [oˈɡraʒdɛn]) in Bulgarian and [ˈɔɡraʒdɛn] in Macedonian) is a mountain range shared by southwestern Bulgaria and southeastern North Macedonia. The highest peak of the mountain is located in North Macedonia, Ograzhdenets (Огражденец) at 1,744 m, while the highest in Bulgaria is Bilska Chuka (Билска чука) at 1,644 m.
