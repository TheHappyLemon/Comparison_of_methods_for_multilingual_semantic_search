Nabil Fekir (born 18 July 1993) is a French professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for and captains La Liga club Real Betis. An academy graduate of Lyon, he was promoted to the senior squad in July 2013. Fekir became a first-team regular in his second season, when he was named the Ligue 1 Young Player of the Year.
