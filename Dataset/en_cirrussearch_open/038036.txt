Zastavna (Ukrainian: Заставна [zɐsˈtɑu̯nɐ]; Romanian: Zastavna) is a small city in Chernivtsi Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast of Ukraine. Zastavna is located 26 kilometres (16 mi) to the north of the city of Chernivtsi, in the historical region of Bukovina. It hosts the administration of Zastavna urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 7,750 (2022 estimate).
