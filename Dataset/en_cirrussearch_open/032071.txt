The Nokia 5310 is an XpressMusic mobile phone, introduced by Nokia on 29 August 2007 and released in the fourth quarter of 2007. It is less than a centimeter thick and is available with blue, red, purple, pink, orange, silver or black trim, the main body also being available in grey, black or white. It features many music specific features as well as a 2.0-megapixel camera. At 9.9 mm thick, it is one of few mobile phones measuring less than 1 cm thick at the time. It is one of the lightest phones Nokia has ever produced at 71 g. On 19 March 2020, HMD Global released a reimagining of the phone, the Nokia 5310 (2020).
