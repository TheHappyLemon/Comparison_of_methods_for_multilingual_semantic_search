Alisa Marić, PhD (Serbian Cyrillic: Алиса Марић, pronounced [alǐsa mǎːritɕ]; born 10 January 1970) is a Serbian chess player who holds the FIDE titles of Woman Grandmaster (WGM) and International Master (IM). On 27 July 2012, she was elected as Minister of Youth and Sports in the Government of Serbia and held that position until 2 September 2013.
