Novomyrhorod (Ukrainian: Новомиргород) is a city in Novoukrainka Raion, Kirovohrad Oblast, central Ukraine, in the southern part of the Middle Dnieper area. It hosts the administration of Novomyrhorod urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. The population of Novomyrhorod is approximately 10,715 (2022 estimate). Novomyrhorod is situated on the banks of the Velyka Vys River.
