Channel 24 (Ukrainian: 24 Канал, romanized: 24 Kanal) is a Ukrainian 24/7 TV channel. Originally called News Channel 24, it is the part of the Lux Television and Radio Company, a media conglomerate in Ukraine. Channel 24 programming covers politics, the economy, sports and celebrities. The station has been broadcasting continuously in Ukraine since 2006. The channel is owned by TRK Lux media [uk] which is controlled by Kateryna Kit-Sadova (the wife of Lviv Mayor Andriy Sadovyi).
