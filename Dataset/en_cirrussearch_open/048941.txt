Ekaterina Borisovna Bukina (Russian: Екатерина Борисовна Букина; born 5 May 1987 in Angarsk) is a wrestler from Russia. She is a silver medalist in wrestling for Women's freestyle in the 75 kilogram range at the 2015 European Games in Baku. In 2022, she competed in the women's 76 kg event at the Yasar Dogu Tournament held in Istanbul, Turkey.
