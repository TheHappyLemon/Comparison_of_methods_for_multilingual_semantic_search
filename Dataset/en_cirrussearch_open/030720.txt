Ardahan Province (Turkish: Ardahan ili; Kurdish: Parêzgeha Erdêxanê; Georgian: არტაანის რეგიონი) is a province in the north-east of Turkey, bordering Georgia and Armenia. Its area is 4,934 km2, and its population is 92,481 (2022). The provincial capital is the city of Ardahan. Ardahan borders the Turkish provinces of Erzurum, Artvin and Kars.
