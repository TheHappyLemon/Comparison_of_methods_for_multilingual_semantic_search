Denis Platonov (born November 6, 1981) is a Russian former professional ice hockey centre who most notably played for Metallurg Magnitogorsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was selected by the Nashville Predators in the 3rd round (75th overall) of the 2001 NHL Entry Draft.
