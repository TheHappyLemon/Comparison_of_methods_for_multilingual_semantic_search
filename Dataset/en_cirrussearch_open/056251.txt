Le Crêt de la Neige is the highest peak in the Jura Mountains and the department of Ain in France. Its elevation is 1720 m above sea level (reported as 1718 m before 2003). Its prominence is 1260 m. It is located in the commune of Thoiry. Looking towards Geneva from the summit Summit of the Crêt de la Neige, with Le Reculet in the background
