Bryan Tetsadong Marceau Mbeumo (/ˈbər.moʊ/ BƏR-moh; born 7 August 1999) is a professional footballer who plays as a right winger or forward for Premier League club Brentford and the Cameroon national team. Mbeumo is a product of the Troyes academy and began his senior career with the club. He transferred to Brentford in 2019 and was a part of the squad which was promoted to the Premier League in 2021. Mbeumo was capped by France at youth international level and made his full international debut for Cameroon in 2022. He was a member of Cameroon's 2022 World Cup squad.
