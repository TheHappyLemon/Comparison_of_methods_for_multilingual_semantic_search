Mstislaw or Mstislavl (Belarusian: Мсціслаў, romanized: Mscislaŭ, IPA: [msʲt͡sʲiˈsɫau̯] ; Russian: Мстиславль, IPA: [msʲtʲɪˈslavlʲ]; Polish: Mścisław, Lithuanian: Mstislavlis) is a town in Mogilev Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Mstsislaw District. In 2009, its population was 10,804. As of 2023, it has a population of 10,069.
