Dominik Graňák (born June 11, 1983) is a Slovak professional ice hockey defenceman, currently playing for HC Škoda Plzeň of the Czech Extraliga (ELH). On November 4, 2014, Graňák signed a one-year contract in returning to the SHL with Linköpings HC. He joined the club as a free agent after four seasons with Russian club, HC Dynamo Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League. He has represented Slovakia in four IIHF World Championships, including 2007.
