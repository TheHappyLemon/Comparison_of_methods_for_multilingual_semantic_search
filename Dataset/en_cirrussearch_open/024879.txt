Zalishchyky (/zɑːliʃˈtʃiːki/; zaw-leesh-chee-kee; Ukrainian: Залiщики; Polish: Zaleszczyki), also spelled Zalischyky, is a small city located on the Dniester River in Chortkiv Raion, Ternopil Oblast, western Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Zalishchyky urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 8,928 (2022 estimate).
