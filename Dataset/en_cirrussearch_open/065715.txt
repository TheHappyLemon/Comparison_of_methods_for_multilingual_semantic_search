Jakub Piotr Kiwior (born 15 February 2000) is a Polish professional footballer who plays as a centre-back or a left-back for Premier League club Arsenal and the Poland national team. Kiwior is a product of the Anderlecht academy. After being unable to break into the first team, he joined FK Železiarne Podbrezová. Kiwior represented Poland at the 2022 FIFA World Cup.
