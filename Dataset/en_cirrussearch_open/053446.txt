Kościerzyna [kɔɕt͡ɕɛˈʐɨna] (Kashubian and Pomeranian: Kòscérzëna; formerly German: Berent, [ˈbɛʁɛnt] ) is a town in Kashubia in Gdańsk Pomerania region, northern Poland, with 23,327 inhabitants as of June 2023. It has been the capital of Kościerzyna County in Pomeranian Voivodeship since 1999; previously it was in Gdańsk Voivodeship from 1975 to 1998.
