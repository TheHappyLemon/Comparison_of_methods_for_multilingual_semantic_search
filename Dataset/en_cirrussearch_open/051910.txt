Nikkie de Jager-Drossaers (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈnɪki də ˈjaːɣər]; née de Jager; born 2 March 1994), known online as NikkieTutorials, is a Dutch make-up artist and beauty YouTuber. She gained online popularity in 2015 after her YouTube video "The Power of Makeup" became popular and inspired many other videos of people showing their faces with and without makeup.
