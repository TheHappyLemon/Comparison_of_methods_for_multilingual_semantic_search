Staryya Darohi (Belarusian: Старыя Дарогi, romanized: Staryja Darohi; Russian: Старые Дороги, romanized: Staryye Dorogi) is a town in Minsk Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Staryya Darohi District. It is located 107 kilometres (66 mi) south-southeast of the capital Minsk. As of 2023, it has a population of 10,972.
