The flag of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic was adopted by the Georgian SSR on April 11, 1951. The national flag of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic is of red cloth ratio of width to length is 1:2 In the upper corner near the staff box blue, the side of which is half the width of the flag in the middle of the square a circle whose radius is equal to one-third of the square in. range of hammer and sickle, above them a five-pointed star. From the circle to the square sides disagree 24 beam. the hammer and sickle, red star and red rays. From the middle of the right side of the square departs the band of blue across the flag length, width one-third of the square" It was the only Union Republic flag in which the hammer and sickle was not gold in colour, instead being red.
