Alina Lebedeva (Russian: Алина Лебедева, Latvian: Alīna Ļebedeva, born 1985) is a Latvian Russian politician and activist known for slapping then-Charles, Prince of Wales with three red flowers in 2001 at age 16. In other actions, she allegedly set fire to a door of the Latvian Ministry of Education and was arrested for a political protest in Moscow.
