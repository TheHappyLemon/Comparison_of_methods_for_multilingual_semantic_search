Cominotto (Maltese: Kemmunett), sometimes referred to as Cominetto, is an uninhabited Mediterranean island off the northern coast of Malta. It is located near the larger island of Comino and is part of the Maltese archipelago. Measuring at only 0.25 square kilometres (0.10 sq mi) in area, it is the largest uninhabited island of Malta. Cominotto lies 100 metres (328 feet) to the north-west of Comino. Cominotto is known for its picturesque coastline, clear waters, and rich marine life.
