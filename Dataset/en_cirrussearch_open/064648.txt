Marko Livaja (Croatian pronunciation: [mâːrko liʋǎːja, - lǐʋa-]; born 26 August 1993) is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Hajduk Split. He played for Croatia from 2018 to 2023 scoring 4 goals in 21 matches. He won bronze medal with Croatia at FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar.
