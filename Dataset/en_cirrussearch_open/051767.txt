Limassol District (/ˈlɪməsɒl/) or Lemesos (Greek: Λεμεσός) is one of the six districts of Cyprus. As of 2021[update], it had a population of 262,238, 75,7% of which was urban The district's capital city is Limassol. Part of the British Overseas Territory of Akrotiri and Dhekelia forms an enclave on the Akrotiri Peninsula, under the sovereignty of the United Kingdom.
