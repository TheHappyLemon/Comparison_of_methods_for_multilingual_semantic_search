Kamyenyets or Kamenets, also known as Kamyanyets (Belarusian: Камянец), is a town in Brest Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Kamyenyets District. The town is located in the northwestern corner of Brest Region on the Lyasnaya River, about 40 kilometres (25 mi) north from Brest. In 2002, its population was approximately 9,000. As of 2023, it has a population of 8,316. The Leśna Prawa river flows through the town.
