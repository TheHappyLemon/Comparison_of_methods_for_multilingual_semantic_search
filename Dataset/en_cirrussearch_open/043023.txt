Włodawa (Polish pronunciation: [vwɔˈdava]; Yiddish: וולאָדאַווע, romanized: Vlodave; Ukrainian: Володава, romanized: Volodava; Belarusian: Уладава, taraškievica: Uładava) is a town in eastern Poland on the Bug River, close to the borders with Belarus and Ukraine. It is the seat of Włodawa County, situated in the Lublin Voivodeship. As of 2016[update] it has a population of 13,500.
