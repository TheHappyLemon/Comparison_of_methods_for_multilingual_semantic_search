Ondřej Kaše (born 8 November 1995) is a Czech professional ice hockey forward who currently is signed with HC Litvínov of the Czech Extraliga (ELH). He most recently played for the Carolina Hurricanes of the National Hockey League (NHL). He previously played with the Toronto Maple Leafs, Boston Bruins, and Anaheim Ducks. Kase was selected by the Ducks in the seventh round, 205th overall during the 2014 NHL Entry Draft.
