Khmilnyk (Ukrainian: Хмільник, IPA: [xm⁽ʲ⁾ilʲˈnɪk]; Polish: Chmielnik; Russian: Хмельник, romanized: Khmelnik) is a resort town in Vinnytsia Oblast, Ukraine. It serves as the administrative center of Khmilnyk Raion within the oblast. Population: 26,916 (2022 estimate). The town is situated in the upper part of the Southern Bug River, 67 km (42 mi) northeast of Vinnytsia. It is one of the oldest towns of Podillia.
