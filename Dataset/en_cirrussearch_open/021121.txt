Strehla (Upper Sorbian: Strjela, pronounced [ˈstʁʲɛla]) is a small town in the district of Meißen, Saxony, Germany. It is located on the river Elbe, north of Riesa. This place name means arrow in Sorbian. Strehla includes the following subdivisions: Forberge Görzig/Trebnitz Großrügeln Lößnig Oppitzsch Paußnitz Unterreußen
