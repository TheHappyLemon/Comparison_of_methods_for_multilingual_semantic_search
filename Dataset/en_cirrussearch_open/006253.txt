Marcos Baghdatis (Greek: Μάρκος Παγδατής, Arabic: ماركوس بغداتيس; pronounced [ˈmarkos paɣðaˈtis]; born 17 June 1985) is a Cypriot former professional tennis player and coach. He was the runner-up at the 2006 Australian Open and a semifinalist at the 2006 Wimbledon Championships, and reached a career-high ATP singles ranking of world No. 8 in August 2006. In the latter part of his career, Baghdatis endured a series of injuries that impacted his play.
