James Adam Belushi (/bəˈluːʃi/; born June 15, 1954) is an American actor. His television roles include the title role in According to Jim (2001–2009), Saturday Night Live (1983–1985), and Twin Peaks (2017). Belushi appeared in films such as Thief (1981), Salvador (1986), About Last Night (1986), Red Heat (1988), K-9 (1989), Jingle All the Way (1996), Hoodwinked! (2005), The Wild (2006), Underdog (2007) and Sollers Point (2017). He is the younger brother of late comedy actor John Belushi and the father of actor Robert Belushi.
