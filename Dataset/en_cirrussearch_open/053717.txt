Mandiraja (Javanese : ꦩꦤ꧀ꦢꦶꦫꦗ) is a district (kecamatan) in Banjarnegara Regency, Central Java, Indonesia. The district has an area of 52.62 km², with a population of 78,090 people at the 2020 Indonesian population census, which was officially estimated to have reached 81,411 in mid 2023. Mandiraja is also 6 km east of the General Sudirman Airport.
