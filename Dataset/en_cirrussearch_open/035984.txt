Yelsk (Belarusian: Ельск, romanized: Jeĺsk; Russian: Ельск; Polish: Jelsk; Lithuanian: Jelskas) is a town in Gomel Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Yelsk District. As of 2023, it has a population of 8,864. Yelsk was greatly affected by radioactive fallout from the Chernobyl disaster in 1986.
