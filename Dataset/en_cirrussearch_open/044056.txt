Irbe is a Latvian feminine given name and a Latvian surname. The associated name day is March 20. Notable people with the surname Irbe include: Artūrs Irbe (born 1967), Latvian ice hockey coach and former goaltender Kārlis Irbe (1861–1934), bishop of the Evangelical Lutheran Church of Latvia Voldemārs Irbe (1893–1944), Latvian pastelist
