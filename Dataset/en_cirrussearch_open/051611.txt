2020 FIA World Rallycross Championship Previous 2019 Next 2021 Support series: European Rallycross Championship The 2020 FIA World Rallycross Championship was the seventh season of the FIA World Rallycross Championship, an auto racing championship recognised by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) as the highest class of international rallycross. Johan Kristoffersson won the Drivers' Championship for the third time. KYB Team JC won the Teams' Championship.
