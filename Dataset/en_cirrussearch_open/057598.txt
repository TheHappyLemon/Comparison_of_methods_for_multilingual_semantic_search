Clément Turpin (French pronunciation: [klemɑ̃ tyʁpɛ̃]; born 16 May 1982) is a French football referee. He has been a FIFA listed referee since 2010, and an UEFA Elite group referee since 2012. Turpin has received several accolades for his work as a referee[citation needed]. He also works as a physical education teacher in France.[citation needed]
