Pascal Groß (German pronunciation: [pasˈkaːl ˈɡʁoːs], sometimes rendered in English as Gross; born 15 June 1991) is a German professional footballer who plays for Premier League club Brighton & Hove Albion and the Germany national team. A versatile player, Groß has played as a defensive midfielder, right-back, attacking midfielder or forward in his career. Groß played at youth international level for Germany. He was called up to the senior squad for the first time aged 32 in August 2023. He made his debut on 9 September 2023 in a friendly against Japan.
