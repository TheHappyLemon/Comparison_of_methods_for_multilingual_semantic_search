Dovilė Kilty (née Dzindzaletaitė; born 14 July 1993) is a Lithuanian track and field athlete who competes as long and triple jumper. Her personal best triple jump record and former national record for Lithuania is 14.17 metres, reached at the 2012 World Junior Championships in Athletics. Her personal best long jump record is 6.08 metres, reached at the 2012 Šiauliai city championship. Kilty was born in Šiauliai. She represented Lithuania in the 2010 Summer Youth Olympics in Singapore.
