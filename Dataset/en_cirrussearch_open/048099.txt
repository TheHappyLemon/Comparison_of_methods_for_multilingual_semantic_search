Vents Feldmanis (born March 7, 1977, in Jūrmala) is a Latvian ice hockey defenceman, currently playing for HK Metalurgs Liepāja of the Belarusian Extraleague. He has become one of the core players of the team, playing in it already since 1999. He played for the Latvian national team at the 2003 World Championships.
