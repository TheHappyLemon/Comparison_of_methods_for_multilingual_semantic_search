Tāšu-Padure (Schloss Tels–Paddern, Tāšu-Padures muižas pils) is located in the village Kalvene, Kalvene Parish, South Kurzeme Municipality, Latvia. It belonged to the von Keyserling family and it has been hosting the elementary school of Kalvene since 1922. Baltic German writer Eduard von Keyserling was born and grew up in the castle.
