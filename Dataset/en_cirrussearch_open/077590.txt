Mykhailo-Laryne (Ukrainian: Михайло-Ларине; also known as Mikhailo-Larino) is a village in Mykolaiv Raion, Mykolaiv Oblast, Ukraine. It belongs to Voskresenske settlement hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. It is located on the Inhul River, 25 km northeast of Mykolaiv. The village was founded at the end of the 18th century, and had a population of 1,918 in 2007. It was first known as Mikhailovka; its name was changed to Mikhailo-Larievka in 1896 and to Mikhailo-Laryne at the end of World War II in 1945.
