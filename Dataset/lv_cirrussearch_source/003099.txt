A Day to Remember ir amerikāņu rokgrupa no Okalas, Floridas štata, ASV. Grupu 2003. gadā dibināja tās vokālists Džeremijs Makkinnons un ģitārists Neils Vestfoalls. Grupa ir izdevusi četrus studijas albumus, piecpadsmit singlus un ierakstījusi vienpadsmit mūzikas video. Grupa ir ievērojama ar metalcore un poppanka stilu apvienošanu vienā. 2005: And Their Name Was Treason 2007: For Those Who Have Heart 2009: Homesick 2010: What Separates Me from You 2013: Common Courtesy 2016: Bad Vibrations 2006: A Second Glance 2007: The Plot to Bomb the Panhandle 2007: The Danger in Starting a Fire 2008: Since U Been Gone (Kelly Clarkson cover) 2009: The Downfall of Us All 2009: Right Where You Want Me to Be 2010: I'm Made of Wax, Larry, What Are You Made Of? 2010: Have Fight In Me 2011: All I Want 2011: All Sings Point To Lauderdale 2012: 2nd Sucks 2013: Right Back At It Again 2014: End Of Me Džeremijs Makkinnons — vokāls (no 2003 g.)
