The Monster Show ir 2005. gadā izdots grupas Lordi albums. Tas ir viņu iepriekšējo albumu Get Heavy un The Monsterician Dream apvienojums, kas paredzēts pārdošanai Apvienotajā Karalistē. Albumam pievienots DVD ar trīs grupas mūzikas klipiem. CD: "Threatrical Trailer" "Bring it On" "Blood Red Sandman" "My Heaven is your Hell" "Would you Love a Monsterman?" "Devil is a Loser" "Icon of Dominance" "The Children of the Night" "Shotgun Divorce" "Forsaken Fashion Dolls" "Wake the Snake" "Rock the Hell outa You" DVD: "Blood Red Sandman" "Devil is a Loser" "Would you love a Monsterman?"
