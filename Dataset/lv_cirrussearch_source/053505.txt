Hilton Garden Inn ir viens no viesnīcu ķēdes Hilton Worldwide zīmoliem, ko kopš 1996. gada izmanto vairāk nekā 800 viesnīcu visā pasaulē. Hilton Worldwide Holdings Inc. izmanto arī zīmolus Hilton Hotels & Resorts, Conrad Hotels & Resorts, Waldorf Astoria Hotels & Resorts, Curio Collection by Hilton, Tapestry Collection by Hilton, DoubleTree by Hilton, Hampton by Hilton, Tru by Hilton, Embassy Suites by Hilton, Home2 by Hilton, Homewood Suites by Hilton, Canopy by Hilton, Hilton Grand Vacations.
