MTV Hard Rock Live ir kanādiešu poproka grupas Simple Plan koncertieraksta albums, kas tika izdots 2005. gada 4. oktobrī sadarbībā ar ierakstu kompāniju Atlantic Records. "Shut Up!" — 4:18 "Jump" — 4:32 "The Worst Day Ever" — 4:20 "Addicted" — 4:14 "Me Against the World" — 3:46 "Crazy" — 4:58 "God Must Hate Me" — 4:02 "Thank You" — 5:39 "Welcome to My Life" — 4:52 "I'm Just a Kid" — 5:07 "I'd Do Anything" — 4:44 "Untitled (How Could This Happen to Me?)" — 4:23 "Perfect" — 5:45 "Crazy (Acoustic Version)" (Bonus Track) — 3:55 "Welcome to My Life (Acoustic Version)" (Bonus Track) — 3:35 "Perfect (Acoustic Version)" (Bonus Track) — 4:06 "Promise (Live)" (Japanese Bonus Track) Grupas oficiālā mājaslapa Arhivēts 2001. gada 1. aprīlī, Wayback Machine vietnē.
