Oh Uganda, Land of Beauty (Ak Uganda, Skaistuma Zeme) ir Ugandas valsts himna, kura oficiāli pieņemta 1962. gadā. Mūzikas un teksta autors - Džordžs Vilberfors Kakoma (George Wilberforce Kakoma). angliski Oh Uganda! may God uphold thee, We lay our future in thy hand. United, free, For liberty Together we'll always stand. Oh Uganda! the land of freedom. Our love and labour we give, And with neighbours all At our country's call In peace and friendship we'll live. Oh Uganda! the land that feeds us By sun and fertile soil grown. For our own dear land, We'll always stand, The Pearl of Africa's Crown.
