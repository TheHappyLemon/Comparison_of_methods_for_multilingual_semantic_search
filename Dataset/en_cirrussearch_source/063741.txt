A Ghetto Christmas Carol is the tenth and final extended play by American rapper and singer XXXTentacion. It is a Christmas-themed EP released on December 11, 2017, by Bad Vibes Forever. The production was handled primarily by fellow American record producer, Ronny J and XXXTentacion himself, alongside Cubeatz and J Dilla. This EP is a reworked version of his scrapped 4 songs EP named "Ugly".This was the last extended play released before X's death. The EP includes a protest song by XXXTentacion against then-President Donald Trump, "Hate Will Never Win". Credits adapted from Tidal. Credits adapted from Tidal.
