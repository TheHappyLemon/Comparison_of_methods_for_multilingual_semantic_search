Loitz (German pronunciation: [ˈløːts]) is a town in the Vorpommern-Greifswald district, in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany. It is situated on the river Peene, 10 km northeast of Demmin, and 22 km southwest of Greifswald. Erich Gülzow (1888–1954), local historian, philologist and publisher Hendrik Born (1944–2021), vice admiral of the Volksmarine (East German Navy) "Bevölkerungsstand der Kreise, Ämter und Gemeinden 2021" (XLS) (in German).
