Setomaa Parish (Estonian: Setomaa vald) is a rural municipality of Estonia, in Võru County. It has a population of 2,849 (as of 1 January 2022) and an area of 463.1 km². Population in the municipality: 2000: 4,206 2011: 2,990 2022: 2,849 There is one small borough (alevik) Värska and 156 villages (küla) in Setomaa Parish.
