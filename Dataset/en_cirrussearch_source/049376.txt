Eriko Sato (佐藤 衣里子, Satō Eriko, born November 25, 1985) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Sato was born in Shizuoka Prefecture on November 25, 1985. After graduating from Waseda University, she played for TEPCO Mareeze. On July 27, 2003, when Sato was 17 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Australia.
