Ryoko Uno (宇野 涼子, Uno Ryōko, born 9 November 1975) (former name; Ryoko Kobayashi, 小林 涼子) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Uno was born in Sagamihara on 9 November 1975. She joined Yomiuri Beleza (later NTV Beleza) in 1990. The club won L.League championship for 4 years in a row until 1993. She was selected Best Eleven for 3 years in a row from 1991 to 1993. In 2000, she moved to YKK Tohoku Ladies SC Flappers (later TEPCO Mareeze). In 2007, she retired.[citation needed] In May 1991, when Uno was 15 years old, she was selected Japan national team for 1991 AFC Championship in Fukuoka. At this competition, on 3 June, she debuted against Singapore. She also played at 1993 AFC Championship. She was a member of Japan for 1995 World Cup. She played 6 games for Japan until 1996.
