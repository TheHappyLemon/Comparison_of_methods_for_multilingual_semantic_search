Litene Parish (Latvian: Litenes pagasts) is situated in Gulbene Municipality, Latvia. It was an administrative unit of Gulbene District. The administrative center is Litene. Territory - 127 km² Population (01.01.2005) - 1,250. Inhabited places - Atpūtas, Aurova, Fabrikas, Kordona, Lešķi, Litene, Silava, Silenieki, Skujenieki, Zāģernieki.
