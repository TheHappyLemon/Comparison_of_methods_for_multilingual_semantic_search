William Arnold Landvoigt (February 6, 1879 – December 15, 1970) was an American rugby union player who represented the German Empire in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was a member of the German rugby union team, which won the silver medal. Germany was represented at the tournament by the FC 1880 Frankfurt rather than an official national team.
