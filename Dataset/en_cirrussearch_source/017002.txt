Grebņova, also spelled Grebņeva, is a village in Malnava Parish, Ludza Municipality, Latvia. It is located in the northern part of the parish on the Russian border near the A13 road, 13 km (8.1 mi) from the parish center Malnava and 288 km (179 mi) from Riga.
