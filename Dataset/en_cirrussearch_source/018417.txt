Igor Skorokhodov (born May 4, 1986) is a Russian former professional ice hockey forward who played in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He most notably played with HC Yugra for 6 seasons. During the 2018–19 season, Skorokhodov returned to HC Yugra in their first season of the transition to the VHL.
