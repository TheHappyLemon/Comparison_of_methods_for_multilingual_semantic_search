Krieg is a 2010 remix album by industrial rock band KMFDM containing remixes of songs from their 2009 album Blitz. Krieg was released exclusively on the band's own label, KMFDM Records, on 5 January 2010. Krieg received positive reviews. All tracks are written by KMFDM Van Isacker, Bernard (December 22, 2009).
