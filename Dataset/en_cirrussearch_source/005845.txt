Mikhail Olegovich Biryukov (born October 13, 1985) is a Russian professional ice hockey goaltender, who is currently an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played with HC Vityaz in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He joined Vityaz after a second stint with Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod in the KHL.
