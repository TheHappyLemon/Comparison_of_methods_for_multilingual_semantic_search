Vilniansk (Ukrainian: Вільня́нськ, pronounced [wilʲˈnʲɑnʲsʲk]; Russian: Вольнянск, romanized: Volnyansk, pronounced [vɐlʲˈnʲansk]) is a city in Zaporizhzhia Oblast, Ukraine and the administrative center of Vilniansk urban hromada. Population: 14,324 (2022 estimate). It was the administrative center of Vilniansk Raion (district) until 2020, when the raion was abolished in accordance with administrative reforms and its area merged into Zaporizhzhia Raion.
