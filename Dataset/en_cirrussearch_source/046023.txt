Põltsamaa (Estonian: Põltsamaa vald) is a rural municipality of Estonia, in Jõgeva County. It has a population of 2722 (2001) and an area of 416.9 km2 (161.0 mi2). Põltsamaa Parish has one town, 3 small boroughs, and around 60 villages.
