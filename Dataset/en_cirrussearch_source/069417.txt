Hans-Joachim Borzym (born 7 January 1948 in Brandenburg an der Havel) is an East German rower who competed in the early 1970s. He won a bronze medal in the eights event at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich. Borzym competed for SG Dynamo Potsdam/Sportvereinigung (SV) Dynamo and won medals at national and international rowing competitions.
