VIA OpenBook is a laptop reference design from VIA Technologies, announced in 2008. The laptop case design was released as open source. Dimensions: 24.0w x 17.5d x 3.62h cm (at battery), (9.45w x 6.89d x 1.43h in) Weight: under 1 kg Processor: 1.0 GHz VIA Nano ULV Chipset: VIA VX800 unified Memory: DDR2 SO-DIMM up to 2 GB Hard disk: 160GB or above Networking: 10/100/1000 Mbit/s Broadcom Giga NIC Ethernet Wireless: 802.11b/g Broadcom or 802.16e GCT Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, WiMAX, EV-DO /W-CDMA, HSDPA, GPS options.
