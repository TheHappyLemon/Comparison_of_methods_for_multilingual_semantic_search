Dmitri Lugin (born April 1, 1990) is a Russian former professional ice hockey winger who most notably played in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He won the rookie of the year award in the 2011–12 KHL season while with Amur Khabarovsk. "Lugin honored as 2012 KHL rookie of the year". Kontinental Hockey League. 2012-05-23.
