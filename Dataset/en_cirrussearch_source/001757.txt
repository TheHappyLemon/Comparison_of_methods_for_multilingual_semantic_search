Diana Rasimovičiūtė (born 25 February 1984, in Ignalina) is a Lithuanian biathlete. Her event is the biathlon (10 km pursuit and 7.5 km sprint). She placed 18th in the 7.5 km sprint, 27th in the 10 km pursuit and 66th in the 15 km individual at the 2006 Winter Olympics. "2006 Winter Olympics in Turin - Boston Globe - Boston.com".
