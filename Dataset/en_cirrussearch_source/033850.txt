Kenji Tochio (橡尾 健次, Tochio Kenji, born May 26, 1941) is a former Japanese football player. He played for the Japan national team. Tochio was born on May 26, 1941. After graduating from high school, he joined Furukawa Electric SC. In 1965, Furukawa Electric joined the new Japan Soccer League. He retired in 1970. He played 61 games and scored 1 goal in the league. On May 28, 1961, he debuted for Japan national team against Malaya. He played 2 games for Japan in 1961.
