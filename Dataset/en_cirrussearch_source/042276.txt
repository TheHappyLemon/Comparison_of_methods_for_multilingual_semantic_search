George Kweku Davies (born 16 November 1996) is a Sierra Leonean footballer who plays for Austrian Football Second League club Admira Wacker as a left midfielder. Davies has played youth football in Sierra Leone, Ghana and England for FC Johansen, Right to Dream Academy and Manchester City. Davies joined the youth team of German club Greuther Fürth in August 2014. In November 2014 he signed a professional contract with them which runs until June 2019. Davies made his first team league debut for the club on 16 December 2014 as a 71st-minute substitute in a 0-0 draw with VfL Bochum. In January 2017 Davies joined the Austrian club Floridsdorfer AC on loan. On 19 July 2017, he joined SKN St. Pölten.
