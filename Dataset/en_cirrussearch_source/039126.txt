Szentlőrinc (Hungarian: Szentlőrinci járás) is a district in central-western part of Baranya County, Hungary. Szentlőrinc is also the name of the town where the district seat is located. The district is in the Southern Transdanubia Statistical Region. Szentlőrinc District borders with Hegyhát District to the north, Pécs District to the east, Sellye District to the south, Szigetvár District to the west. The number of the inhabited places in Szentlőrinc District is 21. The district has 1 town and 20 villages.
