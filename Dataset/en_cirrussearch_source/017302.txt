Utena District Municipality is one of 60 municipalities in Lithuania. District structure: 1 city – Utena; 8 towns – Daugailiai, Kuktiškės, Leliūnai, Saldutiškis, Sudeikiai, Tauragnai, Užpaliai and Vyžuonos; 592 villages.
