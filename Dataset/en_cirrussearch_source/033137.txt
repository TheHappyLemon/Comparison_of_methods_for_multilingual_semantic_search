Bathing Boys (Latvian: Peldētāji zēni) is a painting by the artist Jānis Valters (later Johann Walter-Kurau) from 1900. The painting is oil on canvas, with dimensions 36 by 45 centimetres (14 in × 18 in). The painting belongs to the Latvian National Museum of Art in Riga. The image depicts three boys bathing at a beach in warm sunshine. Valters painted several pictures of similar design in the period 1900–1926. Bathing Boys , europeana.eu.
