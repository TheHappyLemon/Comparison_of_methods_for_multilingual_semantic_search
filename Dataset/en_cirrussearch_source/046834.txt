Saint Vincent and the Grenadines has appeared in 8 Summer Olympic Games. The country has never participated in the Winter Olympic Games. To date, no athlete from Saint Vincent and the Grenadines has ever won an Olympic medal. The St Vincent and the Grenadines National Olympic Committee was formed in 1982 and recognized by the IOC in 1987.
