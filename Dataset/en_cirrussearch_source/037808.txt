Sabrina Vega Gutiérrez (born 28 February 1987 in Las Palmas) is a Spanish chess player who holds the FIDE titles of International Master (IM) and Woman Grandmaster (WGM). From 1996 to 2007, Vega participated in European Youth Chess Championships and World Youth Chess Championships in different age categories. She fulfilled a Women's Grandmaster norm in Mondariz (2005/06), Lorce (2007) and La Massana (2007). Vega twice in a row won Belgrad chess tournaments (2013, 2014). Vega has played for Spain in seven Chess Olympiads (2004, 2008–2016). Vega has played for Spain in six European Team Chess Championships (2005-2015). She has been eight times Spanish Women's Champion winning the title in 2008, 2012, 2015,, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021. In 2016, in Mamaia, Vega won silver medal in European Individual Women Chess Championship.
