The 2012 World Wrestling Championships was held at Millennium Place in Strathcona County, Alberta, Canada. Traditionally, Wrestling World Championships are not held in Olympic years, but in 2012 a female championship was held because the 2012 Summer Olympics included only four of the seven FILA weight classes for females. 111 competitors from 28 nations participated.  Azerbaijan (7)  Belarus (7)  Brazil (2)  Bulgaria (2)  Canada (7)  China (7)  Chinese Taipei (2)  Czech Republic (2)  Estonia (1)  France (2)  Germany (3)  Great Britain (1)  Greece (2)  India (7)  Israel (1)  Japan (7)  Kazakhstan (7)  Latvia (1)  Mexico (1)  Mongolia (7)  Poland (4)  Romania (3)  Russia (7)  Slovakia (1)  Sweden (2)  Turkey (4)  Ukraine (7)  United States (7) Wrestling at the 2012 Summer Olympics "Site de la Fédération Internationale des Luttes Associées".
