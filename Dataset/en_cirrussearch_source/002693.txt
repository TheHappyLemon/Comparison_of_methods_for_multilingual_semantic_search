Pełczyce [pɛu̯ˈt͡ʂɨt͡sɛ] (German: Bernstein, Kashubian language: Bersztén)[citation needed] is a town in Choszczno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, Poland, with 2,681 inhabitants (2010).
