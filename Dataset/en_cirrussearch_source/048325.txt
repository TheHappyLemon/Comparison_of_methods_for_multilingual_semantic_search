Austria competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Austrian and Hungarian results at early Olympics are generally kept separate despite the union of the two nations as Austria-Hungary at the time. 14 Austrian competitors entered 3 disciplines, with 20 entries across 11 events.
