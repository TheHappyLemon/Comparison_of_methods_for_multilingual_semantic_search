Marianna Sastin (born July 10, 1983 in Mosonmagyaróvár) is a female wrestler from Hungary. She has wrestled competitively with Csepeli BC and competed in the Beijing Olympics in 2008 and the London Olympics in 2012, where she placed 15th and 11th, respectively.
