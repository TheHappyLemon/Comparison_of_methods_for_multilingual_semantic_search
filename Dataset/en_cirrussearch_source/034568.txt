Trebbin (German pronunciation: [tʁɛˈbiːn] ; Polish Trzebin) is a town in the Teltow-Fläming district of Brandenburg, Germany. It is situated on the river Nuthe, 14 km north of Luckenwalde, and 36 km southwest of Berlin (centre).
