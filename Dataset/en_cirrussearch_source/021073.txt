Svechnikov (Russian: Свечников) is a Russian masculine surname derived from the word свечник (svechnik, meaning "chandler"). Its feminine counterpart is Svechnikova. It may refer to: Anastasiya Svechnikova (born 1992), Uzbekistani javelin thrower Andrei Svechnikov (born 2000), Russian ice hockey forward Evgeny Svechnikov (born 1996), Russian ice hockey forward Mikhail Svechnikov (1882–1938), Russian military officer Sveshnikov This page lists people with the surname Svechnikov.
