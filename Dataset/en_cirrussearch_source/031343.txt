Maydell is the name of a Baltic-German noble family and part of the Uradel. The family lived in Estonia for several centuries and was one of its notable families. In documents and texts from earlier centuries, the family name is occasionally written Maydel or Maidel. Gertrud Maydell, mentioned 1554–1559, abbess of St. Michael's Abbey in Reval (now Tallinn).
