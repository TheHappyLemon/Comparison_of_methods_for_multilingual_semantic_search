Seiichi Sakiya (崎谷 誠一, Sakiya Seiichi, born December 1, 1950) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Sakiya was born in Hiroshima on December 1, 1950. After graduating from high school, he joined Yawata Steel (later Nippon Steel) in 1969. He retired in 1981. He played 193 games and scored 48 goals in the league. In September 1971, Sakiya was selected Japan national team for 1972 Summer Olympics qualification. At this qualification, on September 27, he debuted against Philippines. He played 3 games for Japan until 1972.
