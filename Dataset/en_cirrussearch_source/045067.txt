Ana Luisa Carvajal Gamoneda (born 9 March 1962) is a Cuban chess player who holds the FIDE title of Woman International Master (WIM). In 1978, she won the second place in FIDE Zonal Tournament (the first place won her compatriot Asela de Armas Pérez) and was awarded the FIDE Woman International Master (WIM) title.
