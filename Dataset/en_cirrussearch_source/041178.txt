This is a list of the prime ministers of the Kingdom of Sardina (Italian: Presidenti del Consiglio dei Ministri del Regno di Sardegna) from the granting of the Statuto Albertino in 1848 until the proclamation of the Kingdom of Italy in 1861. The Kingdom of Sardinia, which was based in Piedmont, is also known as Piedmon-Sardinia.
