Lin Ye (Chinese: 林燁; pinyin: Lin Ye; born 1974) is a Chinese chess player, Woman FIDE Master (WFM), and chess arbiter. In 1995, Lin Ye participated in Women's World Chess Championship Interzonal Tournament in Chişinău where ranked 40th place. Lin Ye is known as a prominent chess tournament arbiter. In 2008, she became a FIDE Arbiter (FA), and in 2011 she received the International Arbiter (IA) title.
