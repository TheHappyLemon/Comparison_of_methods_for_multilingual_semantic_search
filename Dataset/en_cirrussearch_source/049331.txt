Rie Kimura (木村 理恵, Kimura Rie, born July 30, 1971) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kimura was born in Kyoto Prefecture on July 30, 1971. After graduating from Osaka University of Health and Sport Sciences, she played for Speranza FC Takatsuki from 1994 to 2003. On May 16, 1996, Kimura debuted for Japan national team against United States. She played at 1999 and 2001 AFC Championship. She played 21 games for Japan until 2001.
