SES-15 is a geostationary communications satellite operated by SES S.A. and designed and manufactured by Boeing Satellite Systems. It has a mass of 2,302 kg (5,075 lb) and has a design life of at least 15 years. SES S.A. List of SES satellites "SES 15". Gunter's Space Page. 18 April 2018. Retrieved 13 April 2021.
