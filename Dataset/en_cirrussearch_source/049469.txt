Shiho Tomari (泊 志穂, Tomari Shiho, born March 26, 1990) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a forward. She plays for AC Nagano Parceiro in the WE League. She has also played for the Japan national team. Tomari was born in Nagoya on March 26, 1990. After graduating from Osaka University of Health and Sport Sciences, she joined Urawa Reds in 2012. In 2015, she moved to AC Nagano Parceiro. In 2018, she moved to Austrian Frauenliga club Wacker Innsbruck. On July 27, 2017, Tomari debuted for Japan national team against Brazil. She played 2 games for Japan in 2017.
