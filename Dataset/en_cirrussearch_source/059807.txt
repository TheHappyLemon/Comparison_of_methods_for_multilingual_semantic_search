Carl Wilhelm Hugo Rüster (born 15 January 1872 in Berlin, - 3 April 1962) was a German rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the German crew who won the bronze medal in the coxed fours final A. "Hugo Rüster". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
