Jacobaea paludosa, syn. Senecio paludosus, the fen ragwort, is a species of the genus Jacobaea and the family Asteraceae that can be found in northern Italy, and everywhere in Europe (except for Finland, Iceland, Ireland, Norway, Portugal and Russia).
