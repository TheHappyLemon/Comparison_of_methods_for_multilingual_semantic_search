Ekaterina Borisovna Bukina (Russian: Екатерина Борисовна Букина; born 5 May 1987 in Angarsk) is a wrestler from Russia. She is a silver medalist in wrestling for Women's freestyle in the 75 kilogram range at the 2015 European Games in Baku.
