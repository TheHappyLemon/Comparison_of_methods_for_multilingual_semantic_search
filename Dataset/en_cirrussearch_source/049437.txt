Rie Azami (薊 理絵, Azami Rie, born January 11, 1989) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a defender. She plays for Chifure AS Elfen Saitama. She has also played for the Japan national team. Azami was born in Higashimurayama on January 11, 1989. After graduating from high school, she joined Chifure AS Elfen Saitama in 2007. She also plays as midfielder. On September 26, 2013, Azami debuted for Japan national team against Nigeria. She played two games for Japan until 2015.
