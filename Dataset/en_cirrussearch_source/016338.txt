The Petrel Base (Spanish: Base Petrel) is a non-permanent Argentine scientific station located on Dundee Island, in the Joinville Group, in Graham Land, part of the Argentinian claim on Antarctica. Opened as a permanent base on 22 February 1967, it is being used as a temporary base since 1978.
