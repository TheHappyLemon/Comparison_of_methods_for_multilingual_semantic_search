Viduslaiki ("The Middle Ages") is a solo album by Ingus Baušķenieks, released in 2003. The album is Baušķenieks' first solo album, released by "Ingus Baušķenieka ieraksti". The album was recorded in Baušķenieks' home studio from 1999–2000.
