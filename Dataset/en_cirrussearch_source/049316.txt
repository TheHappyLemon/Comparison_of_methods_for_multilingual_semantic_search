Kaoru Kadohara (門原 かおる, Kadohara Kaoru, born 25 May 1970) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kadohara was born on 25 May 1970. She played for Matsushita Electric Panasonic Bambina. She was selected Best Eleven in 1994. In December 1993, Kadohara was selected Japan national team for 1993 AFC Championship. At this competition, on 4 December, she debuted against Chinese Taipei. She also played at 1994 Asian Games, 1995 AFC Championship. She was a member of Japan for 1995 World Cup and 1996 Summer Olympics. She played 12 games and scored 1 goal for Japan until 1996.
