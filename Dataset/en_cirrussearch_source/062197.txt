Rudolf Brosch was an Austrian fencer. He competed in the individual foil event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. He also worked as a translator of the fencing books of Luigi Barbasetti, who was his fencing teacher, and wrote books about fencing himself. "Rudolf Brosch". Olympedia. Retrieved 26 December 2020.
