Marcel Meran (4 October 1871 — 9 April 1947) was a French sailor, who represented his country at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. With Émile Michelet as helmsman, Meran took the 3rd place in the race of the .5 to 1 ton. Exposition Universelle Internationale de 1900, Concours D'Exercices Physiques et de Sports (PDF) (in French).
