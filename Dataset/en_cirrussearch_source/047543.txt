Akshaya Kalaiyalahan (born 28 August 2001) is a British chess player. She has won the Women's British Chess Championship twice, in 2013 and 2015. She competed in the 41st Chess Olympiad in Norway and the 42nd Chess Olympiad in Baku, Azerbaijan representing the England Women's team.
