Emil Garipov (Tatar: Эмиль Гарипов, born August 15, 1991) is a Russian professional ice hockey goaltender of Tatar descent. He is currently an unrestricted free agent who most recently played with Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).
