Jan Urbas (born January 26, 1989) is a Slovenian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with the Fischtown Pinguins in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He previously played with EHC München in the German Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). On September 30, 2014, he opted to join EC KAC of the EBEL on a one-year contract. He played just 10 games with Klagenfurt in the 2014–15 season, before transferring to VIK Västerås HK of the HockeyAllsvenskan on March 11, 2015. As a free agent after parts of two seasons with VIK in the Allsvenskan, Urbas returned to Austria to continue his professional career with EC VSV on July 14, 2016.
