Adalberts Bubenko (16 January 1910 – 7 July 1983) was a Latvian athlete, who competed mainly in the 50 kilometre walk. He competed for Latvia in the 1936 Summer Olympics held in Berlin, Germany in the 50 kilometre walk where he won the bronze medal. "Adalberts Bubenko". Olimpiade.lv (in Latvian).
