Ulmanis (feminine: Ulmane) is a Latvian surname of German origin (from German surname Ullmann). Individuals with the surname include: Guntis Ulmanis (born 1939), President of Latvia 1993–1999 Gunārs Ulmanis (1938–2010), Latvian footballer Kārlis Ulmanis (1877–1942), First Prime Minister of Latvia Ullmann Ulmann Ullman Ulman This page lists people with the surname Ulmanis.
