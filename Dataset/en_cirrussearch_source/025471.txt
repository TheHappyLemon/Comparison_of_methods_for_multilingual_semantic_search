Ukrainsk (Ukrainian: Украї́нськ, Russian: Украинск, pronounced [ʊkrɐˈjinʲsʲk]) is a city in Selydove urban hromada, Donetsk Oblast (province) of Ukraine. Population: 10,655 (2022 estimate); 13,236 (2001).
