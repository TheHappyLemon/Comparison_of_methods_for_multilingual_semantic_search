.cr is the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) of Costa Rica. .ac.cr - Academic: Universities .co.cr - Commercial .ed.cr - Education: Colleges, High Schools, etc. .fi.cr - Financial institutions such as banks, etc. .go.cr - Governmental .or.cr - Non-profit organizations .sa.cr - Health related institutions. .cr - Other Uses Domains can be bought directly at the registry or through accredited registrars.
