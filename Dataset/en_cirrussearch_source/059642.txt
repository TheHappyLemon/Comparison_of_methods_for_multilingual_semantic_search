Lucien Martinet (born 1878, date of death unknown) was a French rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was a member of the French club Société Nautique de la Marne and his team won the silver medal in the coxed pair. "Lucien Martinet". Olympedia. Retrieved 27 December 2020.
