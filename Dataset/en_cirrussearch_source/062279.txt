Marie Frédéric Marc Soudois (14 February 1865 – 20 August 1914) was a French fencer. He competed in the men's foil event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. Soudois served as a capitaine (captain) in the 344th Infantry Regiment of the French Army during the First World War and was killed in action in Moselle on 20 August 1914. "Frédéric Soudois".
