Verschwende deine Zeit (Waste Your Time) is the debut album by German rock group Silbermond, released on July 12, 2004. Primarily produced by Ingo Politz and Bernd Wendlandt, the album peaked at number 3 on the German Albums Chart and at number in Austria, eventually receiving double platinum status in both countries.
