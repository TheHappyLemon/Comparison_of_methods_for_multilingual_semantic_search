Vantika Agrawal (born September 2002) is an Indian chess player who holds the FIDE title of Woman Grandmaster and International Master. She studies at Shri Ram College of Commerce. In 2016, Agrawal won bronze medal in World Youth Chess Championship U14 girls age group. In 2020 she, with Indian national team, won FIDE Online Chess Olympiad 2020. In 2021 Vantika Agrawal won silver medal in Indian Junior Girls Online Chess Championship and won gold medal in Indian Junior Senior Women Chess Championship. At the same year she also won FIDE Binance Business Schools Supercup. In November 2021 in Riga Vantika Agrawal ranked in 14th place in FIDE Women's Grand Swiss Tournament 2021.
