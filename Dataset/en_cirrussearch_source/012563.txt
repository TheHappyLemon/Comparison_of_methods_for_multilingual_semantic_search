The commemorative coins of Latvia are issued by the National Bank of Latvia, headquartered in Riga, Latvia, but minted outside Latvia by: Rahapaja Oy (Mint of Finland): 3 + 26 coins Valcambi sa (Switzerland): 14 coins Royal Mint: 12 coins Royal Dutch Mint: 6 coins Austrian Mint: 5 coins Berlin Mint 3 coins Paris Mint: 1 coin "Commemorative Coins".
