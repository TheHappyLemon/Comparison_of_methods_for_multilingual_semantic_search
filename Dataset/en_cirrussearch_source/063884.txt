The discography of American rapper and singer Lil Uzi Vert consists of three studio albums, nine extended plays (EPs), four mixtapes and fifty-four singles (including thirty-two as a featured artist). Born and raised in Philadelphia, they gained initial recognition following the release of the commercial mixtape Luv Is Rage (2015), which led to a recording contract with Atlantic Records, to whom they signed under DJ Drama's Generation Now imprint. In 2015, they released their debut commercial mixtape, Luv Is Rage. Song from the album, "7AM" spent a total of ten weeks on the Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop Singles chart, peaking at number five on August 6, 2016. In April 2016, they released their third mixtape, Lil Uzi Vert vs. the World, peaking at number 37 on the US Billboard 200 and spent a total of 55 weeks on the chart. It also peaked at number 18 on the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart. The mixtape was eventually certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for combined sales and album-equivalent units of over 1,000,000 units in the United States. The mixtape was supported by three singles: "Money Longer", "Ps and Qs" and "You Was Right", two of the singles entering the US Billboard Hot 100, and being certified two-times platinim by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). In July 2016, they released their fourth mixtape, The Perfect LUV Tape, debuting at number 55 on the US Billboard 200. In February 2018, the mixtape was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for combined sales, streaming, and track-sale equivalents of 500,000 units. The mixtape was supported by two singles: "Seven Million" and "Erase Your Social". In November 2016, they would release a collaborative extended play with American rapper Gucci Mane, 1017 vs. the World. In February 2017, they would release their second extended play, Luv Is Rage 1.5. The mixtape was supported by a single "XO Tour Llif3", which would enter at number 7 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and number 5 on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs. The song has been certfied diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), for selling 11 million units. With over 11 million copies sold, it has become one of the best-selling singles in the United States. In August 2017, they would release their debut studio album, Luv Is Rage 2, which debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 with 135,000 album-equivalent units, of which 28,000 were pure album sales. In its second week on the chart, Luv Is Rage 2 moved another 73,000 units bringing the total sales to 208,000. On March 19, 2019, the album was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for combined sales, streaming and track-sale equivalents of two million units. In March 2020, they would release their second studio album, Eternal Atake, which debuted atop the US Billboard 200, with 288,000 album-equivalent units (including 9,000 pure album sales). It was supported by two singles: "Futsal Shuffle 2020" and "That Way". The album has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for combined sales, streaming and track-sale equivalents of one million units. A week later, they would release the deluxe edition of Eternal Atake, titled Lil Uzi Vert vs. the World 2. The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200, earning 247,000 album-equivalent units in its first week. The deluxe edition's release helped the original album to maintain its number-one position on the Billboard 200 for a second week, since both versions of the album were counted together for tracking purposes. In November 2020, they would release a collaborative studio album with American rapper Future, titled Pluto √ó Baby Pluto. The album debuted at number two on the US Billboard 200 with 105,000 album-equivalent units (including 5,500 pure album sales) in its first week. In July 2022, they released their third extended play, Red & White. It debuted at number 23 on the US Billboard 200, earning 20,000 album-equivalent units in its first week. In June 2023, they would release their third studio album, Pink Tape. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, opening with 167,000 album-equivalent units consisting of 11,000 album sales and 154,000 streaming units. It was supported by two singles: "Just Wanna Rock" and "Endless Fashion". They are scheduled to release their fourth and final studio album Luv Is Rage 3.
