Mexico competed at the modern Olympic Games for the first time at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Medals awarded to participants of mixed-NOC teams are represented in italics. These medals are not counted towards the individual NOC medal tally. Mexico was one of four nations to compete in the first Olympic polo event.
