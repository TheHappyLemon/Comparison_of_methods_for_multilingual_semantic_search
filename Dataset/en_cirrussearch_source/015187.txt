Stanislav Galimov (born 12 February 1988) is a Russian professional ice hockey goaltender who is currently playing for HC 19 Humenné of the Slovak Extraliga. On 27 July 2021, Galimov signed a one-year contract as a free agent to play in the Liiga with his second non-Russian club, Kärpät.
