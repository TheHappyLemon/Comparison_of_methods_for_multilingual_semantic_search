Saulius Širmelis (born 31 May 1956) is a Lithuanian football coach. On 2 June 2017, Širmelis was appointed as the new manager of Shakhter Karagandy. Širmelis was appointed manager of Swedish club AFC Eskilstuna on 5 September 2019. On 4 November 2019, he left the club again.
