Angelos Fetsis (Greek: Άγγελος Φέτσης, born 1878, date of death unknown) was a Greek athlete. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. He was born in Lefkada. Fetsis competed in the 800 metres event. He placed either fourth or fifth in his preliminary heat, though records do not indicate whether he was ahead or behind countryman Dimitrios Tomprof. Neither advanced to the finals, however, as a second-place finish was required for advancement. He also competed in the 1500 metres event.
