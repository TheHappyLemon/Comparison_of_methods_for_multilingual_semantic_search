Brīvzemnieki Parish (Latvian: Brīvzemnieku pagasts) is an administrative territorial entity of Limbaži Municipality, Latvia. It was an administrative unit of Limbaži District. The administrative center is Puikule village. Buiva Ozoli Ozolmuiža Puikule Ežupīte, Īģe, Jogla, Paktene, Pērļupe, Struņķupīte, Zunda.
