Kalkūni (Lithuanian: Kalkūnai) is the biggest village and the administrative centre of Kalkūne Parish in Augšdaugava Municipality in the Selonia region of Latvia. From 2009 until 2021, it was part of the former Daugavpils Municipality. "Kalkuonė". Visuotinė lietuvių enciklopedija (in Lithuanian). Retrieved 7 November 2023.
