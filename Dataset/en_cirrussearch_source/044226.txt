This is a full list of all Brazilian medalists at the Olympic Games. For more information about Brazil at the Olympics, click here. In 2004, Vanderlei Cordeiro de Lima was awarded the Pierre de Coubertin medal, a rare honor given by the International Olympic Committee. Until that date, it had been awarded only 9 times. According to official data of the International Olympic Committee, this is a list of all athletes with at least two Olympic medals representing Brazil.
