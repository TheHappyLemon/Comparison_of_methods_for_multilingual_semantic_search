Roberto Puķītis (born 16 December 1994, in Ventspils) is a Latvian male short track speed skater. He competed at the 2014 Winter Olympics on 1500 metres and at the 2018 Winter Olympics on 1000 metres and 1500 metres. "Roberto Pukitis". sochi2014.com. Archived from the original on 6 April 2014.
