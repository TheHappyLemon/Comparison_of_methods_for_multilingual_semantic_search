The discography of American rapper and record producer Timbaland consists of 3 studio albums, 17 singles, and 14 music videos. Timbaland's production discography Timbaland & Magoo discography "Pass at Me" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number four on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart. "Hands in the Air" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number two on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart. "Keep Going Up" did not enter the German Singles Chart but debuted and peaked at number 2 on the German Trending Chart. "Keep Going Up" did not enter the New Zealand Singles Chart but debuted and peaked at number 9 on the New Zealand Hot Singles Chart. "Keep Going Up" did not enter the UK Singles Chart, but peaked at number 26 on the UK Singles Downloads Chart. "Scream" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 10 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart. "Dust My Shoulders Off" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 31 on the Digital Song Sales chart. "Ain't I" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 13 on the Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. "We Belong to the Music" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 11 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart. "Diddy Rock" did not enter the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs, but peaked at number 24 on the Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. "All Night Long" did not enter the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 24 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart.
