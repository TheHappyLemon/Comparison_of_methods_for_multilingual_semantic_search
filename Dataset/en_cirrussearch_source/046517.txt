Marcinkonys Eldership (Lithuanian: Marcinkonių seniūnija) is a Lithuanian eldership, located in the south western part of Varėna District Municipality. Marcinkonys Eldership is mostly covered by the Dainava Forest and it is one of the most sparsely populated and largest Lithuanian elderships. It is in the Dainava Plain, undulated with massifs of continental dunes. The Čepkeliai Marsh is located at its southern part. Most of the eldership is inside the Dzūkija National Park. Rivers: Merkys, Ūla, Grūda, Skroblus. Lakes and ponds: Lavysas, Grūda Lake, Kastinis, Trikampis. Marshes: Čepkeliai Marsh Forests: Dainava Forest. Protected areas: Dzūkija National Park, Čepkeliai Nature Reserve.
