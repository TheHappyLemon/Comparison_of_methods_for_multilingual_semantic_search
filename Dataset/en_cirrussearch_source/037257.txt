Norma John is a Finnish duo consisting of pianist Lasse Piirainen and vocalist Leena Tirronen. Tirronen previously placed third on season one of X Factor Suomi. They represented Finland in the Eurovision Song Contest 2017, with the song "Blackbird" but failed to qualify to the final. Maria Pudas (29 January 2017).
