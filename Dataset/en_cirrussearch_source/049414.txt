Yuiko Konno (金野 結子, Konno Yuiko, born October 10, 1980) is a former Japanese football player. She played for the Japan national team. Konno was born in Chiba Prefecture on October 10, 1980. She played for her local club, JEF United Chiba. She retired at the end of the 2010 season. On May 11, 2010, when Konno was 29 years old, she played for the Japan national team against Mexico.
