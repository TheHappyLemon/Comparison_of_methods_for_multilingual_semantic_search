Miroidea is a superfamily of true bugs in the order Hemiptera. There are about 7 families and more than 15,000 described species in Miroidea. These seven families belong to the superfamily Miroidea: Microphysidae Dohrn, 1859 Miridae (plant bugs) Thaumastocoridae Kirkaldy, 1908 Tingidae (lace bugs) † Berstidae Tihelka et al., 2020 † Ebboidae Perrichot et al., 2006 † Hispanocaderidae Golub and Popov, 2012 † Ignotingidae Zhang et al., 2005 "Miroidea Report".
