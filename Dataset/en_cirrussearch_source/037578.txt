Pseudorasbora is a genus of small freshwater fish native to eastern Asia, including China, Korea, Japan and Siberia. P. parva, has been introduced to regions outside its native range and is considered invasive. There are currently 5 recognized species in this genus: Pseudorasbora elongata H. W.
