Szentlőrinc (Croatian: Selurinac, Selovrenac, Selurac, Selurince, Selerenac) is a town in Baranya county, Hungary. The association football club Szentlőrinc SE, currently competing in the Nemzeti Bajnokság II, are based in the town.
