Noriko Baba (馬場 典子, Baba Noriko, born May 4, 1977) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Baba was born in Tokyo on May 4, 1977. She played for Nippon TV Beleza from 1994 to 2006. On August 3, 2001, Baba debuted for Japan national team against South Korea. She played 5 games for Japan until 2002.
