Kazuya Yamamura (山村 和也, Yamamura Kazuya, born 2 December 1989) is a Japanese football player who currently plays for Yokohama F. Marinos. On 23 December 2009 he was named in the squad for Japan's 2011 AFC Asian Cup qualification against Yemen, and made his full international debut for the team in the fixture on 6 January 2010.
