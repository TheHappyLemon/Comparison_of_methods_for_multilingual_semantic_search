An arşın (Ottoman Turkish: آرشين or آرشون) is an old Turkish unit of length, about 27 inches (690 mm) long. The word means 'arm' and thus the measure corresponds to a yard. arş, the Ottoman cubit Ell, similar traditional unit in Europe Diran Kélékian, Dictionnaire Turc-Français, Constantinople: Imprimerie Mihran, 1911 A.D.
