Tomas Kaukėnas (born 1 May 1990) is a Lithuanian biathlete. He competes at the Biathlon World Cup. Kaukėnas best result in the World Championships is 22nd place in sprint event in 2013. In 2014 Kaukėnas was selected to represent Lithuania in 2014 Winter Olympic Games.
