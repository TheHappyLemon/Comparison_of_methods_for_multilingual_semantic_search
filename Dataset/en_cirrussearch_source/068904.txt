The 2023–24 Bundesliga is the 61st season of the Bundesliga, Germany's premier football competition. It began on 18 August 2023 and will conclude on 18 May 2024. Bayern Munich are the eleven-time consecutive defending champions. The fixtures were announced on 30 June 2023. Augsburg Berlin Bochum Bremen Darmstadt Dortmund Frankfurt Freiburg Heidenheim Hoffenheim Köln Leipzig Leverkusen Mainz Gladbach Munich Stuttgart Wolfsburg A total of 18 teams will participate in the 2023–24 edition of the Bundesliga. SV Darmstadt 98 returned to the Bundesliga after a six-year absence while 1. FC Heidenheim will play in the Bundesliga for the first time in the club's history.
