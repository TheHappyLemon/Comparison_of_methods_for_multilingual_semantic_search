Vorozhba (Ukrainian: Ворожба́, pronounced [woroʒˈbɑ]) is a city in Sumy Raion, Sumy Oblast, Ukraine. Population: 6,674 (2022 estimate). Vorozhba is situated on the left bank of the Vyr River. Adjacent upstream is the town of Bilopillia, while 1.5 km downstream lies the village of Stari Vyrki. The river in this area is meandering, forming backwaters, meanders, and marshy lakes. Vorozhba hosts a significant railway junction known as Vorozhba Station. The town is intersected by the T 1908 highway. Wikimedia Commons has media related to Vorozhba.
