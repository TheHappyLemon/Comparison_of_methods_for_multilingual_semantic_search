Ēriks Punculs (born 18 January 1994) is a Latvian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Valmiera and the Latvia national team. Punculs made his international debut for Latvia on 9 September 2019 in a UEFA Euro 2020 qualifying match against North Macedonia, which finished as a 0–2 away loss.
