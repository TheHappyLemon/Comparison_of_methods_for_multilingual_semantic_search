Byarozawka or Beryozovka (Belarusian: Бярозаўка, romanized: Biarozaŭka; Russian: Берёзовка; Polish: Brzozówka; Lithuanian: Biarozauka) is a town in Lida District, Grodno Region, Belarus. It is situated along the Neman River. As of 2023, it has a population of 9,657. According to the 1921 census, the population was 69.9% Polish and 24.8% Belarusian.
