Sawako Yasumoto (安本 紗和子, Yasumoto Sawako, born July 6, 1990) is a Japanese football player. She plays for Changnyeong in the WK League. She played for Japan national team. Yasumoto was born in Shizuoka Prefecture on July 6, 1990. She joined TEPCO Mareeze. However, the club was disbanded for Fukushima Daiichi nuclear disaster in 2011. In May, she moved to JEF United Chiba. In 2012, she moved to Vegalta Sendai (later Mynavi Vegalta Sendai). On May 8, 2010, when Yasumoto was 20 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Mexico. She played 2 games Japan in 2010. She was also a member of Japan U-20 national team for 2010 U-20 World Cup in July.
