Armands Zeiberliņš (born 13 August 1965) is a Latvian football manager and former international player, currently working as the head coach of FK Daugava Rīga in the Latvian Higher League. Zeiberliņš played as a midfielder and obtained a total number of 23 caps for the Latvia national football team, scoring 4 goals. His last club was FC Volyn Lutsk in Ukraine. He also played in Sweden, Russia, Poland and Israel during his career. In May 2014 Zeiberliņš was appointed as the manager of FK Daugava Rīga.
