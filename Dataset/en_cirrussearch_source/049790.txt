Jing Ruixue (景瑞雪, Jǐng Ruìxuě, born July 4, 1988, in Xi'an) is a female wrestler from China. She won the silver medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the women's 63 kg category. She beat Choe Un-Gyong, Monika Michalik and Lyubov Volosova before losing in the final to defending champion Kaori Icho. China at the 2012 Summer Olympics "Jing Ruixue Bio, Stats, and Results".
