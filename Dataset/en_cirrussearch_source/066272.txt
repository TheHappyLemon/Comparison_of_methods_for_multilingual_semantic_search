Lukunor Lukunor is an Island and municipality in the state of Chuuk, Federated States of Micronesia. It is a small atoll part of the Nomoi Islands group, located about 264 km to the southeast of Chuuk. Lukunor was first sighted by Europeans by the Spanish expedition of Álvaro de Saavedra shortly after August 1528 in its first attempt to return to New Spain.
