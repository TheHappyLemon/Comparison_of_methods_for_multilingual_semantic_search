38 (thirty-eight) is the natural number following 37 and preceding 39. specifically, the 11th discrete Semiprime, it being the 7th of the form (2.q). the first member of the third cluster of two discrete semiprimes 38, 39 the next such cluster is 57, 58. with an aliquot sum of 22 in an aliquot sequence of five composite numbers (38,22,14,10,8,7,1,0) to the Prime in the 7-aliquot tree. 34 is the first semiprime within a chain of 4 semiprimes in its aliquot sequence (38,22,14,10). The next semiprime with a four semiprime chain is 166. 38! − 1 yields 523022617466601111760007224100074291199999999 which is the 16th factorial prime. There is no answer to the equation φ(x) = 38, making 38 a nontotient. 38 is the sum of the squares of the first three primes. 37 and 38 are the first pair of consecutive positive integers not divisible by any of their digits. 38 is the largest even number which cannot be written as the sum of two odd composite numbers. The sum of each row of the only non-trivial (order 3) magic hexagon is 38.
