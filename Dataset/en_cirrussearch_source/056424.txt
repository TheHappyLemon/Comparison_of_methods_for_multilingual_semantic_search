Evgeniya Alexandrovna Burtasova (née Pavlova, Russian: Евгения Александровна Буртасова (Павлова); born 9 July 1993) is a Russian biathlete. She has competed in the Biathlon World Cup since 2018, and represented Russia at the Biathlon World Championships 2021.
