Pavel Šimáček (born 28 April 1979) is a Czech chess grandmaster. He won the Czech Chess Championship in 2009. Born in 1979, Šimáček earned his international master title in 2002 and his grandmaster title in 2018. He won the Czech Chess Championship in 2009.
