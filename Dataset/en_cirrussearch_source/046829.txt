FK Minija was a Lithuanian football team from town Kretinga. Club founded in 1962. In soviet occupation period played in "A" klasė and Aukščiausia lyga (First tier). The best season was in 1964, when team took third place in top division. In the same year team won Lithuanian football cup. In Lithuanian Independence period team was in second or third tier. In 2015 m. was in 16th position in Pirma lyga (second tier). In 2016 m. had financial problems, in squad was lot of youngsters and team stayed at last position 16.
