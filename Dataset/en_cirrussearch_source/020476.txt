Kirchberg is a town in the Zwickau district, in Saxony, Germany. It is situated at the western part of the Ore Mountains, 11 km south of Zwickau. Christoph Graupner (1683–1760), musician and composer. The high school in Kirchberg is named after him. Robert Seidel (1850–1933), politician, took part in the founding congress of the SPD.
