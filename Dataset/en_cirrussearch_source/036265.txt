97 (ninety-seven) is the natural number following 96 and preceding 98. It is a prime number and the only prime in the nineties. 97 is: the 25th prime number (the largest two-digit prime number in base 10), following 89 and preceding 101. a Proth prime and a Pierpont prime as it is 3 × 25 + 1. the eleventh member of the Mian–Chowla sequence. a self number in base 10, since there is no integer that added to its own digits, adds up to 97. the smallest odd prime that is not a cluster prime. the highest two-digit number where the sum of its digits is a square. the number of primes <= 29. The numbers 97, 907, 9007, 90007 and 900007 are all primes, and they are all happy primes. However, 9000007 (read as nine million seven) is composite and has the factorisation 277 × 32491. an emirp an isolated prime, since 95 and 99 aren't prime. Ninety-seven is: The atomic number of berkelium, an actinide.
