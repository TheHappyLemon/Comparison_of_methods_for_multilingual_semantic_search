Dmitri Leonidovich Makarov (born December 6, 1983) is a Russian professional ice hockey winger who is an unrestricted free agent. He most recently played for HC Yugra of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). After his second year with Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod in the 2012–13 season, Makarov was traded in the off-season to return to Salavat Yulaev in exchange for Denis Parshin and Sergei Sentyurin on May 5, 2013.
