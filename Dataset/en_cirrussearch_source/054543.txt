Francis Johann Wilhelm "Franz" Duhne (2 July 1880 in Barmbek – 11 December 1945 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin) was a German track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Duhne competed in the 2500 metre steeplechase. He placed last in the six-man field. In the 4000 metre steeplechase, he placed sixth of eight. "Franz Duhne". Olympedia. Retrieved 22 December 2020.
