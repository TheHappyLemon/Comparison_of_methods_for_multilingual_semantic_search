Nature Park "Vilce" is a specially protected nature territory Jelgava Municipality in Vilce Parish. Located in the lower reaches of the Vilces valley at its mouth Svētē. The protected area was established in 2004 to uniformly protect natural values, including river valleys, slope and ravine forests, sandstone outcrops, rare and protected plant species, and Vilce castle mound.
