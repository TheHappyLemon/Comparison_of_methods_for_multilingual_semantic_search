Simonas Paulius (born 12 May 1991) is a Lithuanian footballer who plays as a midfielder for FA Šiauliai. On 1 July 2012 Paulius moved to FK Ventspils. In 2018 in the first half of season he played for FK Kauno Žalgiris, in the second half for Widzew Łódź. FK Ventspils Latvian Cup winner (2): 2013, 2017 Latvian Championship winner (2): 2013, 2014 Latvian Championship 3rd place (2): 2015, 2016 In May 2016 Paulius made his debut game for Lithuania squad against Estonia.
