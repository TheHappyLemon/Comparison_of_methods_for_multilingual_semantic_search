André Jules Henri Six (15 July 1879 in Lambersart – 1 April 1915 in Chelles) was a French swimmer and Olympic medalist. He competed at the 1900 Olympic Games in Paris, where he received a silver medal in underwater swimming. He was killed in action during World War I.
