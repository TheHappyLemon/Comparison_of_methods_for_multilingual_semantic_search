Latvijas Finieris is the leading plywood and its products' manufacturer in Baltic States. The company is also active in forest management, logging and the production of synthetic resins and phenol films. Latvijas Finieris is a bronze patron of the University of Latvia Foundation. In 2001, he donated to the installation of a memorial stone to the patron of the University of Latvia Kristaps Morbergs in Bukaiši, in his native house "Lielstrikaiši". "Latvijas Finieris | Exim".
