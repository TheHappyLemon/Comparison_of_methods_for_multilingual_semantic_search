Alexander Calvert is a Canadian actor, best known for playing the role of Jack Kline on the CW show Supernatural. A native of Vancouver, Calvert studied musical theatre and hip hop dancing until the age of 15, when he became interested in acting. He has appeared in several films and television series, including the Nickelodeon series The Troop, To Be Fat like Me, and The Dead Zone, and was young Justin in Kickin' It Old Skool. On July 17, 2015, it was announced that Calvert was cast as Lonnie Machin for season 4 of the CW's Arrow. In 2017, Calvert was cast as Jack, the son of Lucifer in Supernatural.
