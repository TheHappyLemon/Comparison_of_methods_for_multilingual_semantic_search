Toyohito Mochizuki (望月 豊仁, Mochizuki Toyohito, born September 18, 1953) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Mochizuki was born in Shizuoka on September 18, 1953. After graduating from Tokyo University of Agriculture, he joined Japan Soccer League Division 2 club Fujitsu. The club won Division 2 champions in 1976 and was promoted to Division 1. On July 21, 1978, Mochizuki debuted for Japan national team against Malaysia. On July 23, he also played against Singapore. He played 2 games for Japan in 1978.
