Bohdan Kovalenko (Ukrainian: Богдан Русланович Коваленко; born 24 April 1997) is a professional Ukrainian football striker who plays for FC Mynai. Bohdan is a product of the youth team systems of FC Arsenal Kyiv and FC Shakhtar Donetsk. After playing in the Ukrainian Premier League Reserves, the Ukrainian Second League and the Ukrainian Amateurs, he was signed by Pafos FC, a team from Cyprus.
