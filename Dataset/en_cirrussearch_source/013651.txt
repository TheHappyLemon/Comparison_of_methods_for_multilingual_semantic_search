Percoidei is one of 3 suborders of bony fishes in the order Perciformes. Many commercially harvested fish species are considered to be contained in this suborder, including the snappers, groupers, basses, goatfishes and perches. The Percoidei are further divided into three superfamilies which contain over 50 families and hundreds of genera.
