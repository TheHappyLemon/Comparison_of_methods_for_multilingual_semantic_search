The 2024 IIHF World Championship Division IV will be an international ice hockey tournament run by the International Ice Hockey Federation. The tournament will be held in Kuwait City, Kuwait, between 16 and 19 April 2024. First match(es) will be played: 16 April 2024. Source:[citation needed] Rules for classification: 1) points; 2) head-to-head points; 3) head-to-head goal difference; 4) head-to-head number of goals scored; 5) result against closest best-ranked team outside tied teams; 6) result against second-best ranked team outside tied teams; 7) seeding before tournament.
