Darja Semeņistaja (born 16 September 2002) is a Latvian tennis player. She has career-high WTA rankings of world No. 110 in singles achieved on 4 March 2024 and No. 143 in doubles reached on 29 January 2024. In 2021, Semeņistaja won the most singles trophies on the ITF Women's World Tour, a total of seven titles. She won the first two in Serbia, winning Prokuplje's $15k events for two weeks in a row, then the third in Bratislava in mid-July, the fourth in Chornomorsk, Ukraine, at the end of August, and finally three in Cancún, Mexico in October.
