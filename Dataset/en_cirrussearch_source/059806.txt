Adolf Möller (25 October 1877 in Hamburg – 10 November 1968 in Hamburg) was a German rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the German crew who won the bronze medal in the coxed fours final A. "Adolf Möller". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
