Baltezers is a village in Ādaži Municipality in the historical region of Vidzeme, and the Riga Planning Region in Latvia. Baltezers historically formed around Adazi-Bukultu Castle (Neuermühlen, 1287) as Baltezera-Bukultu surrounding the parish church. In 1562, Gotthard Kettler appointed Heinrich von Don as the landlord.
