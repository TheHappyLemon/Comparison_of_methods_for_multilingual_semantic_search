Khalil Chemmam (Arabic: خليل شمام; born 24 July 1987) is a Tunisian retired professional footballer who played as a left back and centre back for Espérance de Tunis and Vitória de Guimarães. Born in Tunis, Chemmam has played club football for Espérance Tunis, Vitória de Guimarães B and Vitória de Guimarães. He participated at the FIFA Club World Cup in 2011, 2018 and 2019. Chemmam earned 26 caps for the Tunisia national team between 2008 and 2013. In May 2018 he was named in Tunisia’s preliminary 29-man squad for the 2018 World Cup in Russia.
