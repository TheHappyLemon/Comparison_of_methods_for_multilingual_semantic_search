Nevena Ignjatović (Serbian Cyrillic: Невена Игњатовић, pronounced [něʋana iɡɲǎːtoʋitɕ]; born 28 December 1990 in Kragujevac, Republic of Serbia, SFR Yugoslavia) is a Serbian alpine skier. She was named to the Serbian team at the 2010, 2014 and 2018 Winter Olympics. "IGNJATOVIC Nevena - Athlete Information".
