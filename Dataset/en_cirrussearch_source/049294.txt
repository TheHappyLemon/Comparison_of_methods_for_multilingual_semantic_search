Mami Kaneda (金田 真美, Kaneda Mami, born March 9, 1968) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kaneda was born on March 9, 1968. She played for Shimizudaihachi SC and Nissan FC. On October 22, 1984, Kaneda debuted for the Japan national team against Australia when she was 16 years old. She played 3 games for Japan until 1986.
