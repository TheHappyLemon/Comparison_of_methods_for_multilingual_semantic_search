The 2013–14 Latvian Football Cup is the nineteenth season of the Latvian annual football knock-out competition. The winners will qualify for the first qualifying round of the 2014–15 UEFA Europa League. The matches of this round took place between 12 and 22 June 2013. The matches of this round took place between 5 and 14 July 2013. The matches of this round took place between 19 July and 7 September 2013. The matches of this round took place on 4 and 5 April 2014.
