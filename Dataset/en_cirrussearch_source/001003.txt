Galēni Parish (Latvian: Galēnu pagasts, Latgalian: Vydsmuižys pogosts) is an administrative unit of Preiļi Municipality in the Latgale region of Latvia. At the beginning of 2014, the population of the parish was 890. The administrative center is Galēni village.
