Yevgeni Yevgenyevich Kozlov (Russian: Евгений Евгеньевич Козлов; born 4 February 1995) is a Russian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for I-League club Mohammedan SC. He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Vityaz Podolsk on 16 July 2012 in a game against FC Fakel Voronezh. He made his Russian Premier League debut for FC Volga Nizhny Novgorod on 15 May 2014 in a game against FC Ural Sverdlovsk Oblast. On 16 January 2024, I-League club Mohammedan Sporting announced the signing of Kozlov.
