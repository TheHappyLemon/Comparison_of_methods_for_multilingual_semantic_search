Nika Prevc (born 15 March 2005) is a Slovenian ski jumper. Prevc made her FIS Ski Jumping World Cup debut in November 2021 in Nizhny Tagil. On 16 December 2023, in Engelberg, Switzerland, she achieved her first individual World Cup victory after finishing above her countrywoman Ema Klinec. Prevc was born in Kranj to Božidar and Julijana Prevc; the family has since been living in the village of Dolenja Vas. She has three brothers and a sister. All three of her brothers, Peter, Cene and Domen, are also ski jumpers. Her father, who owns a furniture business, is an international ski jumping referee.
