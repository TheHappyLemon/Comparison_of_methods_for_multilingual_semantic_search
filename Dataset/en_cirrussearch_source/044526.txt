Nauris Puntulis (born 17 July 1961) is a Latvian politician. From July 8, 2019 to September 15, 2023, he served as the Minister of Culture in the Kariņš cabinet. He is affiliated with the National Alliance party. He was appointed for his second term as Culture Minister in 2022. His term ceased when the Second Kariņš cabinet fell. "Nauris Puntulis".
