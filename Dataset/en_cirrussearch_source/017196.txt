Vaive Parish (Latvian: Vaives pagasts) is an administrative unit of Cēsis Municipality in the Vidzeme region of Latvia (prior to 2009 - of Cēsis District). Its administrative center is Rīdzene [lv]. The population in 2021 was 1,328. Before 1925, Vaive Parish was called Veismaņi Parish. It was a part of Cēsu apriņķis.
