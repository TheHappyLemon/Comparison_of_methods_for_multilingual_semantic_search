Dāvis Ikaunieks (born 7 January 1994) is a Latvian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Czech First League club Jablonec and the Latvia national team. On 20 June 2018 Ikaunieks signed a three-year deal with Czech First league team FK Jablonec. After spending half a season on loan at FK RFS in Latvia, at the end of 2019 he went on loan to FC Fastav Zlín. Scores and results list Latvia's goal tally first. His younger brother Jānis is also a professional footballer, currently playing for Rīgas FS.
