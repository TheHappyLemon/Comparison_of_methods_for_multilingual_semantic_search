Maho Shimizu (清水 万帆, Shimizu Maho, born March 9, 1960) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Shimizu was born on March 9, 1960. In June 1981, she was selected Japan national team for 1981 AFC Championship. At this competition, on June 7, she debuted against Chinese Taipei.
