Battle of the Psychics (Russian: Битва экстрасенсов, romanized: Bitva extrasensov) is a Russian tv-show which airs on the Russian TNT TV channel, filmed in the format of the British TV show Britain's Psychic Challenge. Such programs show in the U.S. (America's Psychic Challenge), Israel (Reality Koah – "Power"), Bulgaria ("Ясновидци"), Azerbaijan (Ekstra hiss), Ukraine (Bytva ekstrasensiv), Mongolia (Зөн билгийн тулаан), Georgia (Ekstrasensta Brdzola – ექსტრასენსთა ბრძოლა), Kazakhstan (Qıl kópіr), Australia (The One), Lithuania (Ekstrasensų mūšis), Estonia (Selgeltnägijate tuleproov), Latvia (Ekstrasensu cīņas). The first release of the first season was aired on February 25, 2007. Now, the 23rd season has started.
