Aizkraukle District (Latvian: Aizkraukles rajons, [aizkraukles rajuons]) was an administrative division of Latvia from 1967 to 2009. It was located in Vidzeme and Selonia regions, in the country's centre. Districts were eliminated during the administrative-territorial reform in 2009.
