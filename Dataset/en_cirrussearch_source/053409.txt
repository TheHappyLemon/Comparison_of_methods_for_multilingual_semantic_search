Julius Carl Fritz Manteuffel (11 January 1875 in Berlin – 21 April 1941 in Berlin) was a German gymnast. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Manteuffel was a member of the German team that won two gold medals by placing first in both of the team events, the parallel bars and the horizontal bar.
