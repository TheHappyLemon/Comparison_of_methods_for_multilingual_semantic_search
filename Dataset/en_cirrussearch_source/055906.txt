René Pierre Charles Tartara (14 November 1881 – 3 September 1922) was a French freestyle swimmer and water polo player. He competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in water polo and two swimming events, and won a bronze medal in the 200 m team swimming. René Tartara. sports-reference.com "René Tartara". Olympedia.
