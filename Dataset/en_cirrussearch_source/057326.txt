Ramon Untersander (born January 21, 1991) is a Swiss professional ice hockey defenseman currently playing for SC Bern of the National League (NL). He participated for the Swiss national team at the 2017 IIHF World Championship and at the 2018 IIHF World Championship winning a silver medal. "2017 World Championship roster" (PDF).
