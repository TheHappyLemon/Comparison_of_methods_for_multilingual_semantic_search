Panamá is a province of Panama. It is the location of the national capital Panama City, which also serves as the provincial capital. The governor of the province is Judy Meana, appointed by President Laurentino Cortizo and sworn in on 16 April 2020. Panamá Province is now divided into 6 distritos (districts) and subdivided into 55 corregimientos.
