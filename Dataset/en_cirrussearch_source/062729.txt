Georges Louis Paul Sémichon (born 25 September 1865, date of date unknown) was a French sailor who represented his country at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. Semichon, as helmsman, took the 6th place in first race of the 0 to 0.5 ton and finished 6th in the second race.
