Maurice Marie Joseph Vignerot (25 November 1879 in Paris – 28 September 1953 in Gap, Hautes-Alpes) was a French croquet player and Olympic silver medallist. He was beaten to the gold medal by Chrétien Waydelich in Singles, two balls at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris. All the contestants in the competition were French.
