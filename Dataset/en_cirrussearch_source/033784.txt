Tameo Ide (井出 多米夫, Ide Tameo, November 27, 1908 – August 17, 1998) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Ide was born in Shizuoka on November 27, 1908. He played Tokyo OB Club. He won 1933 Emperor's Cup with Shiro Teshima and Teiichi Matsumaru at the club. In May 1930, when Ide was a Waseda University student, he was selected Japan national team for 1930 Far Eastern Championship Games in Tokyo and Japan won the championship. At this competition, on May 25, he debuted against Philippines. Ide died on August 17, 1998, at the age of 89.
