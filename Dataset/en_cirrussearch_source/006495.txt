.mt is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Malta. Since 1 December 2017, it is possible to register .mt domains directly at the second level, such as myname.mt. Registration is possible at the third level under a number of second level domain names. edu.mt: educational institutions gov.mt: Malta government entity com.mt: commercial entities net.mt: Internet-related network service providers org.mt: non-profit entities "New Policy to Register Second Level Domain Names under .mt".
