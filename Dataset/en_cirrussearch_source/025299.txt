Rihards Pīks (born December 31, 1941, in Riga) is a Latvian politician and Member of the European Parliament for the People's Party; part of the European People's Party. He served as the Foreign Minister of Latvia from 2004 to 2009. "Rihards Pīks Curriculum Vitae" (PDF). European Parliament. Archived from the original (PDF) on 2011-06-07. Retrieved 2010-04-05.
