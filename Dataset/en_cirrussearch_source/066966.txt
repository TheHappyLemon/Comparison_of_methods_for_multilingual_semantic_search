Henrik Haukeland (born 6 December 1994) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey player currently playing for Düsseldorfer EG in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He is also a member of the Norwegian national team. He participated at the 2017 IIHF World Championship and 2018 IIHF World Championship.
