Muhannad Ahmed Abu Radeah Assiri (Arabic: مهند أحمد أبو رديه عسيري; born 14 October 1986) is a Saudi Arabian former professional footballer who plays as a striker. On 7 August 2021, Assiri announced his retirement from football. In May 2018, he was named in Saudi Arabia’s preliminary squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.
