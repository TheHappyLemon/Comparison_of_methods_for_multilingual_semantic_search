Julia Demina (born February 3, 1969) is a Russian chess player. She has won the Women's Russian Chess Championship twice, the Soviet championship once before, and competed for the Women's World Chess Championship several times.[citation needed] Demina earned the title of Woman Grandmaster in 1991.
