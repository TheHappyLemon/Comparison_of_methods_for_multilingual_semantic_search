Haradok District (Belarusian: Гарадоцкі раён; Russian: Городокский район) is a district (raion) of Vitebsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative centre is Haradok. The largest lakes of the district are Yezyaryscha Lake (the thirteenth largest in Belarus) and Losvida Lake (the eighteenth largest in Belarus).
