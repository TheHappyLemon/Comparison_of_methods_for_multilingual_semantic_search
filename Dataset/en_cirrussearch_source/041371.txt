Volodar Arturovich Murzin (Russian: Володар Артурович Мурзин; born 18 July 2006) is a Russian chess grandmaster. Originally from Nizhny Tagil, Murzin lives in Khimki. Murzin won the U12 title at the European Youth Chess Championship 2018, with a draw against Jakub Chyzy in the final round. In the Chess World Cup 2021, where he was seeded 151st, he reached the second round shortly before his 15th birthday, losing to 23rd-seeded Vladislav Artemiev by one point in a tiebreaker.
