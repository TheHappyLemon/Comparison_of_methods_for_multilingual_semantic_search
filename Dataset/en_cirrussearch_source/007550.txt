Adaptatsiya (Russian: Адаптация, Russian for Adaptation) is a Kazakhstani punk rock band founded in 1992 in Aktobe. Yermen Anti Erzhanov was born on 26 July 1974. In 1992 Yermen formed the Adaptatsiya. The inspiration for him were Grazhdanskaya Oborona and Alexander Bashlachev. (in 2014) Yermen Anti Erzhanov – vocals, guitar, author of music and lyrics – bass – guitar – drums Adaptatsiya is Russian language band, but some songs are in Kazakh.
