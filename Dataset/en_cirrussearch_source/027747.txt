Mazsalaca Parish (Latvian: Mazsalacas pagasts) is an administrative unit of Valmiera Municipality in the Vidzeme region of Latvia. It was created in 2010 from the countryside territory of Mazsalaca town. At the beginning of 2014, the population of the parish was 650.
