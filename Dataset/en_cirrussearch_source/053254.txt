Gyula Kakas (also known as Gyula Kokas; March 28, 1875 – February 25, 1928) was a Hungarian gymnast. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Kakas competed in the parallel bars, horizontal bar, vault, and pommel horse individual events. He did not win any medals in those competitions, though his exact place in each is unknown.
