Is This Hyperreal? is the fourth studio album from Atari Teenage Riot, and their first album since they effectively disbanded in 2000. It is the first ATR album featuring CX KiDTRONiK, and the first album without former vocalists Hanin Elias and the late Carl Crack. The single version of "Black Flags" features vocals from Boots Riley. The album received mixed to positive reviews from critics.
