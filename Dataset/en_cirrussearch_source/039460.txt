The 2017 Grand Prix Cycliste de Québec is a road cycling one-day race that took place on 8 September. It was the 8th edition of the Grand Prix Cycliste de Québec and the 33rd event of the 2017 UCI World Tour. It was won by Peter Sagan in the sprint. "2017 Grand Prix Cycliste de Québec".
