Zdravko Milev Milev (Bulgarian: Здравко Милев) (October 25, 1929 in Targovishte Bulgaria – January 1, 1984 in Sofia Bulgaria) was a Bulgarian chess International Master. He became an International Master in 1952 after becoming Bulgarian National Champion. He went on to win the national championship again in 1960 and 1961.
