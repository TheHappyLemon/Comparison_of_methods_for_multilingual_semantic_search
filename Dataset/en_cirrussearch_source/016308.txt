Jaungulbene Manor (Latvian: Jaungulbenes muižas pils; German: Schloss Neu-Schwanenburg) is a manor house in the historical region of Vidzeme, in northern Latvia. It was built in Tudor neo-Gothic style and completed in 1878. In the 1920s Jaungulbene Manor was nationalized in accordance with Latvian Land Reform of 1920.
