Émile Michelet (16 July 1867 — 27 April 1935) was a French sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. With crewmember Marcel Meran, Michelet, as helmsman, took the 3rd place in the race of the 0.5 to 1 ton.
