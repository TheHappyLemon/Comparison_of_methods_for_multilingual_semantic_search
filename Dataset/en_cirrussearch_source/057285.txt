Julian Jakobsen (born 11 April 1987) is a Danish professional ice hockey winger who currently plays as Captain for The Aalborg Pirates of Metal Ligaen (DEN). His sister, Josefine Jakobsen, plays professionally for Djurgårdens IF and has been named Danish Women's Player of the Year twice. "Julian Jakobsen player profile". Elite Prospects. 8 July 2016.
