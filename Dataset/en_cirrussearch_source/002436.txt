Armands Pētersons (born 5 December 1990) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Auda and the Latvia national team. Pētersons made his international debut for Latvia on 6 September 2019 in a UEFA Euro 2020 qualifying match against Austria, which finished as a 0–6 away loss.
