Sellye (Hungarian pronunciation: [ˈʃɛjːɛ]; Croatian: Šeljin) is a town in Baranya county, southern Hungary. It is the centre of the Ormánság region located in the southern part of the county. According to László Szita, the settlement was completely Hungarian in the 18th century.
