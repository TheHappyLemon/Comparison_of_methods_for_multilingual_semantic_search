Vjačeslavs Isajevs (born 27 August 1993) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a defender for FK Auda. He was capped once for the Latvia national team. Isajevs made his international debut for Latvia on 9 September 2018, starting in the 2018–19 UEFA Nations League D match against Georgia, which finished as a 0–1 away loss.
