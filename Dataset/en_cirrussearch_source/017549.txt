Voranava District (Belarusian: Воранаўскі раён; Russian: Вороновский район) is a district (raion) of Grodno Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is the urban-type settlement of Voranava. Ethnic composition:[citation needed] Poles - 80.8% Belarusians - 13% Lithuanians and others comprise the rest.
