Imants Zemzaris (born 14 April 1951) is a Latvian composer and teacher. His family is famous in Latvia due to his grandfather Jānis Endzelīns. A native of Riga, he studied in Emīls Dārziņš Music School and Latvia State Conservatory with Ādolfs Skulte.
