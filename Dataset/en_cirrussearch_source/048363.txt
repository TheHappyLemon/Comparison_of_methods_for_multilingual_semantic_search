The Ivica Tunnel is a road tunnel in Montenegro, between the towns of Šavnik and Žabljak in the Drobnjaci region, which opened in December 2010. At 2.2 kilometers long it is the second longest vehicular tunnel in Montenegro. "Road Infrastructure". Bemax. Archived from the original on 5 October 2017. Retrieved 26 April 2016.
