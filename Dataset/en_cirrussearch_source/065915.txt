Tuuli Tomingas (born 4 January 1995) is an Estonian biathlete. She competes in the Biathlon World Cup. Tomingas achieved her best result in the Biathlon World Cup at Soldier Hollow, Utah, where she finished 6th in pursuit during the 2023-24 season.
