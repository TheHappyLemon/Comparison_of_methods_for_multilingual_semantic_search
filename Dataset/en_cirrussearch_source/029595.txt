Ķintu well is a historical object in Cīrava parish, Latvia. It is made of large carved stone blocks up to 2 meters long. The cross-section of the well forms a square with 1.25 m long sides. The Ķintu well is an archaeological monument, possibly the remains of an earlier larger megalithic complex. "Ķintu aka" (in Latvian). Retrieved 2023-01-03. Pāvils, Gatis (May 2003).
