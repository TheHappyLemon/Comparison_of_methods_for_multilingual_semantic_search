2Pac Live is a live album by American rapper 2Pac, released by Death Row Records and Koch Records. It consisted of recordings of live club performances during the All Eyez on Me (1996) period in his career. Originally planned for an August 10, 2004 release, the album's release date was moved to August 6 due to a leaked bootleg version. 2Pac Live peaked at number 54 on the Billboard 200 chart, with 23,000 copies sold in the first week.
