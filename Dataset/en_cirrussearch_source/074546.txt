Betula humilis, known in English as the shrubby birch, is a species of birch that can be found in Europe and Asia. The species has ovate leaves that are 1.2–3.5 centimetres (0.47–1.38 in) long and is related to Betula fruticosa. Shaw, K.; Roy , S.; Wilson, B. (2014). "Betula humilis".
