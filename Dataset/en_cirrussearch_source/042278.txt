Tamirlan Ulubiyevich Dzhamalutdinov (Russian: Тамирлан Улубиевич Джамалутдинов; born 28 July 1996) is a Russian football player who plays as a central midfielder for Uzbekistani club Surkhon Termez. He made his professional debut in the Russian Professional Football League for Anzhi-2 Makhachkala on 12 August 2014 in a game against Alania Vladikavkaz. He made his debut for the main Anzhi Makhachkala squad on 27 May 2016 in a Russian Premier League relegation play-offs game against Volgar Astrakhan.
