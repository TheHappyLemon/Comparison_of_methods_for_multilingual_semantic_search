Johan Arthur Castrén (10 June 1866 – 29 June 1946) was a Finnish politician, born in Turtola. He was a member of the Senate of Finland and the first director of internal affairs from 27 November 1917 to 27 November 1918, before the Ministry of Interior was created. He served as Mayor of Helsinki from 1931 to 1936. He died in Jakobstad. "Council of State - Director of Internal Affairs". Valtioneuvosto.fi. Archived from the original on 2017-12-28.
