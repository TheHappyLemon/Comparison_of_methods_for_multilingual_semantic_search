Anže Lanišek (born 20 April 1996) is a Slovenian ski jumper. Lanišek made his World Cup debut in Planica on 21 March 2014, where he finished in 34th place. He achieved his first World Cup victory on 28 November 2021 in Kuusamo, Finland. Lanišek won gold with the Slovenia national team in the team event at the FIS Ski Flying World Championships 2022 in Vikersund.
