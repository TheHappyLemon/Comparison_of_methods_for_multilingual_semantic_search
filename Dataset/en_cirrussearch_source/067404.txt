Molochnyi Estuary, or Molochnyi Lyman (Ukrainian: Молочний лиман), is an estuary of the Molochna River, located on the north-western coast of the Sea of Azov. About 10 km north is the town of Melitopol. Parameters of the water body: Length 32 km Width 8 km Depth 3 m Area 168 km2 It is connected to the Sea of Azov by an artificial canal. "Molochnyi Lyman".
