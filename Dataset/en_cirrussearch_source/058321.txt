Noravank Sport Club (Armenian: Նորավանք մարզական ակումբ), was a football club based in Vayk, Armenia, which played in the Armenian Premier League. Noravank Sport Club was founded in 2020 and participated in 2020–21 Armenian First League. Noravank trained and held their home matches at the Charentsavan City Stadium whilst renovation work was carried out on their Vayk Stadium.
