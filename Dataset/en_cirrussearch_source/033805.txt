Megumu Tamura (田村 恵, Tamura Megumu, January 10, 1927 – October 8, 1986) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Tamura was born on January 10, 1927. He played for Nippon Oil & Fats. In March 1951, Tamura was selected Japan national team for Japan team first game after World War II, 1951 Asian Games. At this competition, on March 7, he debuted against Iran. He played 3 games for Japan in 1951. On October 8, 1986, Tamura died of cancer in Chuo, Tokyo at the age of 59.
