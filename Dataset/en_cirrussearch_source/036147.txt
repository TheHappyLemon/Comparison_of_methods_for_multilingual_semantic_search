Coptotriche fuscomarginella is a moth of the family Tischeriidae. It was described by Vactor Tousey Chambers in 1875. It is found in the US states of Kentucky, Massachusetts, New Jersey, New York, Ohio and Virginia. The larvae feed on Castanea dentata, Quercus muehlenbergii and Quercus prinus.
