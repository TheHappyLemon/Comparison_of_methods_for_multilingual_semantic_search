Patrycja Wyciszkiewicz-Zawadzka (Polish pronunciation: [paˈtrɨ.t͡sja vɨ.t͡ɕiʂˈkjɛ.vit͡ʂ]; born 8 January 1994) is a Polish sprinter specialising in the 400 metres. She competed in the 4 × 400 m relay event at the 2012 Summer Olympics.
