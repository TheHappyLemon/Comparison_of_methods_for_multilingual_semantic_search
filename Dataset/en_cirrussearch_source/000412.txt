Star (Russian: Старь) is an urban locality (a work settlement) in Dyatkovsky District of Bryansk Oblast, Russia. Population: 4,182 (2021 Census); 5,003 (2010 Census); 5,096 (2002 Census); 5,663 (1989 Census). It was established in 1785 and was granted urban-type settlement status in 1927.
