Maaris Meier (born 22 May 1983) is a Mountain bike rider and road cyclist from Estonia. She represented her nation at the 2004 Summer Olympics in the women's road race. She won the silver medal at the 2008 World University Cycling Championship in the Cross-country. "Maaris Meier". Sports Reference. Archived from the original on 18 April 2020.
