Nadzeya Shushko (28 March 1990) is a Belarusian freestyle wrestler. She represented Belarus at the 2015 European Games held in Baku, Azerbaijan and she won one of the bronze medals in the 53 kg event. In 2014, she competed in the women's freestyle 53 kg event at the World Wrestling Championships held in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.
