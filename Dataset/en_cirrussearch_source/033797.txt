Takeshi Natori (名取 武, Natori Takeshi) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. In May 1934, when Natori was a Waseda University student, he was selected Japan national team for 1934 Far Eastern Championship Games in Manila. At this competition, on May 20, he debuted and scored a goal against Republic of China.
