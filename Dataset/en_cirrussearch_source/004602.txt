Gailītis (feminine: Gailīte) is a Latvian masculine surname, a diminutive of the word gailis, meaning "rooster/cockerel". Individuals with the surname include: Alfrēds Gailītis [lv] (1900–1980), Latvian politician Guntis Gailītis [lv] (bon 1949), Latvian theatre director Jānis Gailītis (born 1985), Latvian basketball player and coach Kārlis Gailītis (1936–1992), Latvian Lutheran archbishop Markuss Gailītis [lv] (1882–1942), Latvian politician Pauls Gailītis [lv] (1869—1943), Layvian Lutheran clergyman, politician and public figure This page lists people with the surname Gailītis.
