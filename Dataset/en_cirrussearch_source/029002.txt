Pope parish (Latvian: Popes pagasts) is an administrative unit of the Ventspils Municipality, Latvia.The parish has a population of 1126 (as of 1/07/2010) and covers an area of 167.745 km2. Pope Wind Farm, currently largest wind farm in Latvia, is located in the parish.
