Ančipova is a village in Šķilbēni Parish, Balvi Municipality, Latvia. It is located in the eastern part of the parish, 8.6 km (5.3 mi) from the parish center Rekova, 13.1 km (8.1 mi) from Viļaka, and 254.7 km (158.3 mi) from Riga. Ančipova is located very close to the Russian border, away from the state roads, on the bank of the border river Kukhva.
