The Small Guild (Latvian: Mazā ģilde) is a building situated in Riga, Latvia, at 3/5 Amatu Street. The building was erected in the years 1864—66 after a project by architect Johann Felsko in Neo-Gothic style. "The Small Guild". citariga.lv.
