Rami Bedoui (Arabic: رامي البدوي; born 19 January 1990) is a Tunisian professional footballer who plays as a centre back for Club Africain and the Tunisia national team. In June 2018 he was named in Tunisia's 23-man squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.
