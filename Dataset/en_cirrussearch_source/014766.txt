Akrochordite is a rare hydrated arsenate mineral with the chemical formula (Mn,Mg)4(AsO4)2(OH)4·4H2O and represents a small group of rare manganese (Mn) arsenates and, similarly to most other Mn-bearing arsenates, possess pinkish colour.
