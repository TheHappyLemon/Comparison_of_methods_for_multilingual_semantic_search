Jevgēņijs Ņerugals (born 26 February 1989) is a Latvian international footballer who plays for RFS, as a goalkeeper. Born in Daugavpils, Ņerugals has played for Dinaburg FC, BFC Daugavpils, FC Daugava, Ilūkstes NSS and Spartaks Jūrmala. He made his international debut for Latvia in 2018. "Jevgēņijs Ņerugals".
