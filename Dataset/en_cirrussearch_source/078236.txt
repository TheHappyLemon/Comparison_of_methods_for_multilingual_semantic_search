The Original Single Kollektion (English: Original Single Collection) is a compilation album limited to 10,000 copies, released by German Neue Deutsche Härte band Rammstein. It was released in June 1998 and contains the first 6 singles of the band, as well as a poster. The cover art of the box set is similar to the East German coat of arms.
