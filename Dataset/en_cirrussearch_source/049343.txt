Miki Sugawara (菅原 美希, Sugawara Miki) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Sugawara played for Iga FC Kunoichi. On May 17, 1998, Sugawara debuted for Japan national team against United States. She played at 1998 Asian Games. She played 7 games and scored 2 goals for Japan in 1998.
