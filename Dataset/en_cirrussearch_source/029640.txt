Lija Laizāne (born 6 July 1993) is a Latvian racing cyclist, who rides for UCI Women's Continental Team Eneicat–CMTeam. She rode at the UCI Road World Championships in 2014, 2015 and 2020. For the 2023 season, Laizāne will join UCI Women's Continental Team Laboral Kutxa–Fundación Euskadi.
