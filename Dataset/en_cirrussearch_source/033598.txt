Tomoyuki Hirase (平瀬 智行, Hirase Tomoyuki, born 23 May 1977) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Hirase was born in Kagoshima Prefecture on 23 May 1977. After graduating from high school, he joined Kashima Antlers in 1996. He played many matches from 1999. In 2000, the club won all three major title in Japan; J1 League, J.League Cup and Emperor's Cup. In 2001, the club won the champions J1 League for 2 years in a row. In July 2002, he moved to Yokohama F. Marinos on loan. He returned to Kashima Antlers in 2003 and moved to Vissel Kobe in August 2004. He moved to Vegalta Sendai in 2008. He retired end of 2010 season. On 5 February 2000, Hirase debuted for Japan national team against Mexico. He played 2 games for Japan in 2000. In September 2000, Hirase was selected Japan U-23 national team for 2000 Summer Olympics. He played 2 matches.
