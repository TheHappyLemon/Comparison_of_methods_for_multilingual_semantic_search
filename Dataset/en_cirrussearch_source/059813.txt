Johannes Hester Lambertus Terwogt (18 May 1878 in Oude Wetering – 22 January 1977 in Amsterdam) was a Dutch rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the Dutch boat Minerva Amsterdam, which won the silver medal in the coxed fours final B. "Johannes Terwogt". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
