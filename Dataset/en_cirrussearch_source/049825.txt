Kossakowski (Lithuanian: Kosakovskiai) was an aristocratic family of Mazovian origin. They were first mentioned in the 13th century and initially settled in Ciechanów County, from where they branched out to the Grand Duchy of Lithuania, Podolia, Volhynia, and Livonia. Coat of arms of the Kossakowski family was Ślepowron.
