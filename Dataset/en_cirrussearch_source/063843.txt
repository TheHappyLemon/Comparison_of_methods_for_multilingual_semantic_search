Stokeley (stylized in all caps) is the debut studio album by American rapper Ski Mask the Slump God. It was released on November 30, 2018, by Victor Victor Worldwide and Republic Records. The album features guest appearances from Juice Wrld, Austin Lam, Lil Baby and Lil Yachty. In the United States, Stokeley debuted at number 6 on the US Billboard 200 chart, earning 51,000 album-equivalent units, with 5,000 coming from pure album sales in its first week.
