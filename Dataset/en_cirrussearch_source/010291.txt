Vitālijs Samoļins (born March 7, 1990, in Jelgava) is a Latvian chess player who holds the FIDE title of International Master (2009). He won the Latvian Chess Championship in 2009 and 2012. Vitālijs Samoļins played for Latvia in Chess Olympiads: In 2006, at second reserve board in the 37th Chess Olympiad in Turin (+3 −3 =0); In 2008, at reserve board in the 38th Chess Olympiad in Dresden (+3 −0 =4).
