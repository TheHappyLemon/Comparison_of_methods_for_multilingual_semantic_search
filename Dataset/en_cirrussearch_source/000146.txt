The South Korea women's national basketball team (Korean: 대한민국 여자농구 국가대표팀) represents South Korea in international women's basketball competitions. They are administered by the Korea Basketball Association. Team South Korea’s greatest success was at the 1984 Los Angeles Summer Games, when they took silver. They finished fourth at the 2000 Summer Olympics and eighth at the 2008 Beijing Games. In February 2020, South Korea qualified for the 2020 Summer Olympics through beating Great Britain during the FIBA Qualifying Tournament in Belgrade, Serbia. It is South Korea's first appearance at the tournament since 2008 Olympics in Beijing. South Korea will play in group A alongside world No. 3 Spain, No. 4 Canada and No. 8 Serbia. The Koreans will look for at least one win in group A as the top two countries of each group advance to the quarterfinals. Roster for the 2023 FIBA Women's Asia Cup.
