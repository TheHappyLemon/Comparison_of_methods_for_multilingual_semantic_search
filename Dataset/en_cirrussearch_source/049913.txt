Cantacaderinae is a subfamily of lace bugs in the family Tingidae. There are about 16 genera and at least 90 described species in Cantacaderinae. These 16 genera belong to the subfamily Cantacaderinae: Afghanoderus B. Lis, 2001 Allocader Drake, 1950 Australocader B.
