Masato Kudo (工藤 壮人, Kudō Masato, 6 May 1990 – 21 October 2022) was a Japanese footballer who played as a forward. On 23 September 2010, Kudo was selected for the Japan Under-21 squad for the 2010 Asian Games held in Guangzhou, China. On 23 May 2013, Kudo was called up to Japan's senior team for the first time prior to an international exhibition game against Bulgaria. On 11 May 2016, Kudo collided violently with goalkeeper Matt Lampson and suffered a fractured jaw. Kudo died from complications of brain surgery on 21 October 2022, at the age of 32.
