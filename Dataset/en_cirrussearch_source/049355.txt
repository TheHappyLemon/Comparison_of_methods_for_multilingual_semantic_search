Yuka Yamazaki (山崎 由加, Yamazaki Yuka, born June 29, 1980) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Yamazaki was born in Tokyo on June 29, 1980. She played for Nippon TV Beleza and Okayama Yunogo Belle. She retired in 2006. On May 31, 2000, when Yamazaki was 19 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Australia.
