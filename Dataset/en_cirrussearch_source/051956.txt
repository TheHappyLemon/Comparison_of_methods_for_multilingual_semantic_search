Lucie Charvátová (Czech pronunciation: [ˈlutsɪjɛ ˈxarvaːtovaː]; born 1 February 1993) is a Czech biathlete. She was born in Hradec Králové. She has competed in the Biathlon World Cup, and represented the Czech Republic at the Biathlon World Championships 2016.
