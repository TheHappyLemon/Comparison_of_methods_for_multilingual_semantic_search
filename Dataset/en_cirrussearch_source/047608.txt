Aleksandrs Zerebkovs (born 6 September 1969) is a Latvian male weightlifter, competing in the 85 kg category and representing Latvia at international competitions. He participated at the 1992 Summer Olympics in the 75 kg event. He competed at world championships, most recently at the 1999 World Weightlifting Championships. "Aleksandrs Zerebkovs". Olympic.org.
