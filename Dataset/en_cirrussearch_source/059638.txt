Antoine "Erneste" Védrenne (17 September 1878 – 13 January 1937) was a French rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the French boat Rowing Club Castillon, which won the bronze medal in the coxed pair. "Antoine Védrenne". Olympedia. Retrieved 27 December 2020.
