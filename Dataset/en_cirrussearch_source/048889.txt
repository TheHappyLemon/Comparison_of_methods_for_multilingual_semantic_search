Andreas Bernard (born 9 June 1990) is an Italian professional ice hockey goaltender who currently plays for HC Pustertal of the ICE Hockey League (ICEHL). He holds the record for most games played as a foreign goaltender in Liiga, the highest level league in Finland, with 216 regular season games played. Bernard speaks fluent Finnish.
