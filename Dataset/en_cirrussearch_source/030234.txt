Rwanda is divided into five provinces (intara) and subdivided into thirty districts (akarere). Rwanda's provinces are: Northern Province Eastern Province Southern Province Western Province Kigali Province Prior to 1 January 2006, Rwanda was composed of twelve provinces (known as prefectures up to 2001), but these were abolished in full and redrawn as part of a program of decentralization and reorganization.
