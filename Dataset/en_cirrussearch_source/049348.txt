Yoshie Kasajima (笠嶋 由恵, Kasajima Yoshie, born May 12, 1975) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kasajima was born on May 12, 1975. She played for Shimizudaihachi SC, Urawa Reds and AS Elfen Sayama FC. She was selected Best Eleven in 2004 season. She retired end of 2011 season. In November 1999, Kasajima was selected Japan national team for 1999 AFC Championship. At this competition, on November 8, she debuted against Thailand.
