"Shine It On" is the second single of Estonian musician Maarja Kivi. "Shine It On" was released on 1 September 2006. The single was a bigger hit than her first single "Could You", and reached number #16 at MTV Baltic TOP 20. In Estonia, the single peaked at #9 in the singles' chart.
