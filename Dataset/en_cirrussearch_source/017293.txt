The Nokia 2610, released in Q1 2006, is a mobile phone manufactured by Nokia. The Nokia 2610 is a candybar-style telephone that weighs 91 grams, with its buttons operated by the thumb. It has a 1.5-inch (38 mm) CSTN colour screen with 65,536-colour display. It uses a D-pad and two selection buttons, one on each side, with Send and End keys similar to native S40 devices. The End key also is used to turn the telephone on and off.
