Two fencers and one rugby player of Haitian nationality competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris for Haiti. It would not be until the 1924 Summer Olympics until the nation sent a team to compete at the Olympic Games. Neither Haitian fencer advanced past the first round.
