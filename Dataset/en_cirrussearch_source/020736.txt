Kārlis Ozoliņš (born March 16, 1994) is a Latvian ice hockey player. He is currently a free agent. Ozoliņš began his hockey career playing in minor and junior Latvian hockey leagues. During 2011–12 and 2012–13, seasons he played for HK Liepājas Metalurgs in the Latvian Hockey Higher League and Belarusian Extraleague. On August 21, 2013, Ozoliņš signed a contract with Dinamo Riga of the KHL. Ozoliņš participated at the 2013 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships as a member of the Latvia men's national junior ice hockey team. Latvian hockey federation.
