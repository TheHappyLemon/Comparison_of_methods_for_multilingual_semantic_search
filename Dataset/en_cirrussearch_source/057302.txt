Kristian Khenkel (Belarusian: Крыстыян Гэнкель; born 7 November 1995) is a Belarusian professional ice hockey defenseman currently playing for Barys Astana in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Khenkel previously played in the KHL for HC Dinamo Minsk and Ak Bars Kazan. as well as in his native Belarusian Extraleague with Yunost Minsk.
