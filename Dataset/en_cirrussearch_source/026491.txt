Crespières (French pronunciation: [kʁɛpjɛʁ]) is a commune in the Yvelines department in the Île-de-France region in north-central France. Along with Chavenay, Feucherolles and Saint-Nom-la-Bretèche Crespières is twinned with Rösrath, Germany.
