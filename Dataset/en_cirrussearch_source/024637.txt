Radyvyliv Raion (Ukrainian: Радивилівський район) was a raion in Rivne Oblast in western Ukraine. Its administrative center was the town of Radyvyliv. The raion was abolished and its territory was merged into Dubno Raion on 18 July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Rivne Oblast to four.
