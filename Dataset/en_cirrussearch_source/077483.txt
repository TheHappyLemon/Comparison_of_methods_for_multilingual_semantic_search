Krimulda Municipality (Latvian: Krimuldas novads) is a former municipality in Vidzeme, Latvia. The municipality was formed in 2009 by merging Krimulda Parish and Lēdurga Parish, the administrative centre being Ragana. On 1 July 2021, Krimulda Municipality ceased to exist and its territory was merged into Sigulda Municipality.
