Kaspars Svārups (born 28 January 1994) is a Latvian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Norwegian club HIF-Stein. In 2012, Svārups was sent on loan to the youth academy of Russian top flight side Rubin from Ventspils in Latvia. Before the second half of 2013–14, he was sent on loan to Polish second division club Nadwiślan Góra. In 2017, he signed for Sortland IL in the Norwegian fourth division. Before the second half of 2020–21, Svārups signed for Serbian team Bačka. On 26 October 2021, he was signed by Italian Serie D club Sambenedettese.
