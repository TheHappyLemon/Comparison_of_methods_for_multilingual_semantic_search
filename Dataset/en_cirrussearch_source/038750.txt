Dāvis Indrāns (born 6 June 1995) is a Latvian football player. He plays for FK Jelgava. He made his debut for the Latvia national football team on 9 June 2017 in a World Cup qualifier group game against Portugal. "Latvia-Portugal game report". FIFA. 9 June 2017.
