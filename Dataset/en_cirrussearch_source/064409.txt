Rudik Makarian (born July 30, 2004 in Alatuman, Georgia) is an Armenian-born Russian chess player. He was awarded the title of International Master in 2019. Makarian won the U-16 World Youth Chess Championship in 2019 and the Russian Junior Chess Championship in 2020. He qualified to play in the Chess World Cup 2021 where he was defeated 1½-½ by Kacper Piorun in the first round.
