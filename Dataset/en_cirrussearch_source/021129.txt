Gabriel de Castilla Base is a Spanish research station located on Deception Island, South Shetland Islands, Antarctica. The station was constructed in late 1989. The station is named for Gabriel de Castilla, a 17th-century Spanish navigator and, according to some reports, the first person to sight the mainland of Antarctica.
