Lyudmila Sergeyevna Kolchanova (Russian: Людмила Серге́евна Колчанова) (born October 1, 1979, in Sharya, Kostroma Oblast) is a Russian long jumper. Her personal best jump is 7.21 metres, achieved in May 2007 in Sochi.
