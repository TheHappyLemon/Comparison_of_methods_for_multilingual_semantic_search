Ilaha Kadimova (Azerbaijani: İlahə Akif qızı Qədimova; born November 5, 1975) is an Azerbaijani Woman Grandmaster chess player. She won the World Youth Chess Championship (Girls) in 1992, 1993 and European Junior Chess Championship (Girls) in 1993. A.Qorxmaz.
