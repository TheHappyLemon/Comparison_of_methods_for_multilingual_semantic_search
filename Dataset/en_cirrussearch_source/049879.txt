Tauraria is an atoll in Fiji, a member of the Ringgold Isles archipelago, which forms an outlier group to the northern island of Vanua Levu. The uninhabited islet is around 40m north of Qelelevu, and 0.9Â km east of Vetauua. Tauraria is a low, upraised and jagged limestone islet covered in dense but scrubby bush.
