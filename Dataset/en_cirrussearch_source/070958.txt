Zrinka Ljutić (born 26 January 2004) is a Croatian World Cup alpine ski racer and specializes in the technical events of slalom and giant slalom. She  made her World Cup debut at age 16 in December 2020 and competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics, in slalom and giant slalom.
