Sala Parish (Latvian: Salas pagasts) is a territorial unit of Mārupe Municipality in Latvia . From 2009 until 2021, it was part of Babīte Municipality. Prior to 2009, it was part of Riga District. Sala Parish is defined by Latvian law as a part of the region of Vidzeme.
