Latvia competed at the Summer Olympic Games for the first time at the 1924 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Ten athletes represented Latvia in 1924. It was the nation's debut appearance in the sport as well as the Games. Ranks given are within the heat. A single boxer represented Latvia at the 1924 Games. It was the nation's debut in the sport as well as the Olympics. Gutmans lost his only bout. Four cyclists represented Latvia in 1924. It was the nation's debut in the sport as well as the Games. Ranks given are within the heat. Latvia competed in the Olympic football tournament for the first time in 1924.
