Miho Manya (万屋 美穂, Manya Miho, born Nonember 5, 1996) is a Japanese former footballer who played as a defender. She plays for Mynavi Sendai Ladies in the WE League. She played for Japan national team. Manya was born in Osaka Prefecture on November 5, 1996. After graduating from high school, she joined Vegalta Sendai (later Mynavi Vegalta Sendai) in 2015. However, she can not play in L.League competition for injury in 2015 and 2016 season. In 2017, she debuted in L.League and became a regular player as left side back. In 2012, Manya was selected Japan U-17 national team for 2012 U-17 World Cup. On July 27, 2017, she debuted for Japan national team against Brazil. She played 7 games for Japan in 2017.
