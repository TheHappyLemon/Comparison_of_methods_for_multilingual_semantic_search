Andrejs Piedels (born 17 September 1970 in Jēkabpils) is a former Latvian football goalkeeper. As a player, he played for Pārdaugava, Daugava, Amstrig, DAG, Skonto FC, FK Jūrmala, FK Daugava Riga and Olimps/RFS.
