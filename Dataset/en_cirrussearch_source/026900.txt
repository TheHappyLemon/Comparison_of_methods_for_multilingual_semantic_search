Daugavpils Theatre (Latvian: Daugavpils teātris) is a theatre in Daugavpils, Latvia. Daugavpils Theatre was founded in 1857 and is one of the oldest professional theatres in Latvia, as well as the only permanent professional theatre in the Latgale region.
