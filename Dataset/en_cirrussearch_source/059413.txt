Nīca Parish (Latvian: Nīcas pagasts) is an administrative unit of South Kurzeme Municipality in the Courland region of Latvia. The parish has a population of 2,896 (as of January 7, 2010) and covers an area of 230.3 square kilometres (88.9 sq mi).
