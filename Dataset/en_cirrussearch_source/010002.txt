Grigori Panin (born November 24, 1985) is a Russian professional ice hockey defenceman who currently plays as captain for Salavat Yulaev Ufa in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Panin first played with Lada Togliatti before spending 8 seasons with Ak Bars Kazan of the KHL. On June 21, 2014, Panin signed a one-year contract as a free agent with CSKA Moscow.
