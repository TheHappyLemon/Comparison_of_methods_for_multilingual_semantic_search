Monument to the Revolution of the people of Moslavina or simply Monument to the Revolution (Croatian: Spomenik revolucije naroda Moslavine) is a World War II memorial sculpture by Dušan Džamonja, located in Podgarić, Berek municipality, Croatia. The monument is about 10m tall and 20m wide. It is dedicated to the people of Moslavina during World War II.
