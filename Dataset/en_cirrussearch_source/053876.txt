Tosmare is a district north of Liepāja, Latvia near the Karosta and lake "Tosmare". Tosmare is well known mostly because of its shipyard "Tosmare", with Tosmare Shipyard currently owned by Rīgas Kuģu Būvētava. The shipyard was founded in 1900 and has two graving docks.
