Shumilina District (Belarusian: Шумілінскі раён; Russian: Шумилинский район) is a district (raion) of Vitebsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is the urban-type settlement of Shumilino (Shumilina). The other urban-type settlement within the district is Obol (Obal).
