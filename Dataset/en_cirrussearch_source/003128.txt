Ansambel Roka Žlindre is a Slovene musical group named after accordion player Rok Žlindra. The other members of the group are Barbara Ogrinc (born 4 February 1990) (vocals), Nejc Drobnič (acoustic guitar) and Rok Modic (double bass). Together with Kalamari, Ansambel Roka Žlindre represented Slovenia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 with the song "Narodnozabavni rock".
