The Latvian national futsal team is governed by the Latvian Football Federation, the governing body for futsal in Latvia and represents the country in international futsal competitions, such as the World Cup and the European Championships. Latvia will make their international futsal debut at the UEFA Futsal Euro in 2026 as co-host alongside Lithuania. Futsal World Ranking Viktors Sopirins (2024-02-08).
