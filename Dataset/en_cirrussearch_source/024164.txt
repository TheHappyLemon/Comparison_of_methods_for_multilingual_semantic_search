Slutsk District or Sluck District (Belarusian: Слуцкі раён; Russian: Слуцкий район) is a district (raion) of Minsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is Slutsk. Alena Kish (1889 or 1896, Ramanava (now Lenina) village - 1949), Belarusian primitivist painter Jurka Listapad (1897, Varkavičy village - 1938), active participant in the Belarusian independence movement and anti-Soviet resistance, publicist and a victim of Stalin's purges of 1937–38. Mikola Statkevich (b. 1956, Liadna village), Belarusian politician and political prisoner Uladzimier Teraŭski (1871, the village of Ramanaŭ (currently known as the village of Lenin) - 1938), Belarusian composer, choirmaster and a victim of Stalin's purges.
