Edmond Georges Richard Wallace (4 October 1876 – 28 February 1915) was a French fencer. He competed in the individual épée event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was killed in action during World War I. He was the brother of fencer Richard Wallace. List of Olympians killed in World War I "Edmond Wallace Olympic Results". sports-reference.com.
