Maxim Soloviev (born 20 February 1979) is a Russian former professional ice hockey defenceman. He last played with HC Dynamo Moscow in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). After completing his eighth season with Dynamo following the 2017–18 campaign, Soloviev announced his retirement from his 21-year professional career on September 7, 2018.
