Nauris Miezis (born 31 March 1991) is a Latvian basketball player for the Latvian 3x3 national team. He represented Latvia at the 2020 Summer Olympics. and was one of the four players in the team who won the gold medal by defeating the Russian Olympic Committee team 21 to 18.
