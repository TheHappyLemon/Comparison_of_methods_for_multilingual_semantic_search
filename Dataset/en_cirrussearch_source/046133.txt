Wonderful Losers: A Different World (Lithuanian: Nuostabieji lūzeriai. Kita planeta) is a 2017 Lithuanian documentary film directed by Arūnas Matelis about the Giro d'Italia. It was selected as the Lithuanian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 91st Academy Awards, but it was not nominated. The film profiles the unsung heroes of medics and water carriers working in the world of competitive cycling.
