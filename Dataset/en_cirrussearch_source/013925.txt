Vjačeslavs Fanduļs (Russian: Вячеслав Викторович Фандуль; born March 17, 1969, in Leningrad, Soviet Union) is a Latvian former professional ice hockey forward. Fanduļs began his career with RASMS Riga before playing in the Soviet Hockey League for Dinamo Riga and Stars Riga between 1988 and 1992. He then moved to Finland's SM-liiga with spells at Ässät, HPK, TPS, and Kärpät between 1992 and 2001. Fanduļs then moved to Germany's Deutsche Eishockey Liga and signed for the Berlin Capitals. He played just one season in the DEL before returning to Ässät. He later returned to Latvia and played for ASK/Ogre.
