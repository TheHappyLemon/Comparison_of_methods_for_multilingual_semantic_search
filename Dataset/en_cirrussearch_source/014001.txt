Sandra Aleksejeva (born 3 May 1991) is a Latvian BMX racer. She competed at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London, reaching the semi-final. Aleksejeva has participated in several European and World Championships. Aleksejeva was born in Krāslava, Latvia. "Veide iekļauts Latvijas sastāvā olimpiādes BMX sacensībām" (in Latvian).
