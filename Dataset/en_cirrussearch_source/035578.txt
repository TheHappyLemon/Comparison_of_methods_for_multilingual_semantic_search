Kirawsk or Kirovsk (Belarusian: Кіраўск, romanized: Kiraŭsk; Russian: Кировск; Polish: Kirowsk) is a town in Mogilev Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Kirawsk District. In 2009, its population was 8,756. As of 2023, it has a population of 7,971. Official transliteration.
