Eduard Latypov (Russian: Эдуард Ратмилевич Латыпов; born 21 March 1994) is a Russian biathlete. He has competed in the Biathlon World Cup since 2018, and represented Russia at the Biathlon World Championships 2020 and Biathlon World Championships 2021.
