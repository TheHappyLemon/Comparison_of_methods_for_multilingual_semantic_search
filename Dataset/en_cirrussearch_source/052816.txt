Ioannis Persakis (Greek: Ιωάννης Περσάκης, 1877 – 1943) was a Greek athlete. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. He was born in Athens. Persakis competed in the triple jump, taking third place. His best jump was 12.52 metres. "Ioannis Persakis". Olympedia. Retrieved 20 December 2020.
