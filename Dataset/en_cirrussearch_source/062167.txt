Juncus conglomeratus is a perennial, herbaceous flowering plant species in the rush family Juncaceae, known as compact rush.: 986  In the British Isles it is one of six rush species that can dominate lowland damp grasslands.[citation needed] Wikimedia Commons has media related to Juncus conglomeratus.
