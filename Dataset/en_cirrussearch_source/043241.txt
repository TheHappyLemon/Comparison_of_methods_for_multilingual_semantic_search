Kosuke Nakamura (中村 航輔, Nakamura Kōsuke, born 27 February 1995) is a Japanese professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Portimonense. He has represented the Japan national team internationally. In June 2011, Nakamura was called up to the Japan U17 national team for the 2011 FIFA U-17 World Cup and he played four matches. In August 2016, he was also selected for the Japan U23 national team for the 2016 Summer Olympics and he played two matches. In May 2018, he was named in the Japan national team's squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia. As of 27 May 2023 Includes Emperor's Cup and Taça de Portugal.
