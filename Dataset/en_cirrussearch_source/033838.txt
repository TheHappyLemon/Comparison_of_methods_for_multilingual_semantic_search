Yozo Aoki (青木 要三, Aoki Yōzō, April 10, 1929 – April 23, 2014) was a Japanese football player. He played for the Japan national team. Aoki was born on April 10, 1929. He played for Chiyoda Life. On January 5, 1955, he debuted for the Japan national team against Burma, his first and only appearance for Japan. Aoki died on April 23, 2014, at the age of 85.
