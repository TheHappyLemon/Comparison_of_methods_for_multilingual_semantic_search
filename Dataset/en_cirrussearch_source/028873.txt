Ehalkivi (lit. 'Sunset Glow Boulder') is an erratic boulder in Letipea, Lääne-Viru County, Estonia; for its volume is the largest of kind in Estonia, and one of the largest of kind in the glaciation area of North Europe. Geologically the boulder is pegmatite. Height: 7.6 m Circumference: 49.6 (measured at 1.5 m from water surface) Volume: 930 m3 Mass: ca 2,500 t[citation needed] AA.VV. (1989).
