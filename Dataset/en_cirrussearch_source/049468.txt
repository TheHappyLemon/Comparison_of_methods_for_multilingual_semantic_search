Mami Ueno (上野 真実, Ueno Mami, born September 27, 1996) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a forward. She plays for Sanfrecce Hiroshima in the WE League. She played for Japan national team. Ueno was born in Kumamoto Prefecture on September 27, 1996. After graduating from high school, she joined Ehime FC in 2015. Ueno was a member of Japan U-20 national team for 2016 U-20 World Cup and Japan won 3rd place. She scored 5 goals and 2 assists, and get Golden Shoe awards. On April 9, 2017, she debuted for Japan national team against Costa Rica. She played 6 games for Japan.
