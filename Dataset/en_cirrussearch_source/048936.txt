Valeria Sergeyevna Koblova, née Zholobova (Russian: Валерия Сергеевна Коблова; born 9 October 1992) is a Russian freestyle wrestler. She competed in the freestyle 55 kg event at the 2012 Summer Olympics; she advanced to the bronze medal match, where she was defeated by Yuliya Ratkevich. At the 2014 World Wrestling Championships she lost in the final match to 10-time World champion and 4-time Olympic champion Kaori Icho. At the 2016 Summer Olympics she lost in the gold medal match to Kaori Icho. She is Grand Master of Sports in freestyle wrestling.[citation needed] "Valeriia Zholobova". London 2012.
