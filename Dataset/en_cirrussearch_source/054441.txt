August Nilsson (October 15, 1872 in Trollenäs – May 23, 1921 in Stockholm) was a Swedish track and field athlete and tug of war competitor who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. He finished ninth in the shot put event and eighth in the pole vault competition. He also participated on the Dano-Swedish tug of war team which won the gold medal against opponents France.
