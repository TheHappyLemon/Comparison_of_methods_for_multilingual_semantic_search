Fülöp Jakab "Kóbi" Kauser (22 March 1878 in Budapest – 27 June 1925 in Budapest) was a Hungarian track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris. He participated in the pole vault competition and finished fourth. "Jakab Kauser". Olympedia. Retrieved 24 December 2020.
