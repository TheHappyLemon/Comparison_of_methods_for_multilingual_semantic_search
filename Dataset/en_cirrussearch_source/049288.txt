Masako Yoshida (吉田 雅子, Yoshida Masako, born August 12, 1957) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Yoshida was born on August 12, 1957. On September 9, 1981, she debuted for Japan national team against Italy. However Japan was defeated this match by a score of 0–9. This is the biggest defeat in the history of Japan national team.
