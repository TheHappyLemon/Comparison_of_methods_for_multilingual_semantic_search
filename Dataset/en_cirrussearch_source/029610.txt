Romāns Apsītis (13 February 1939 – February 2022) was a Latvian jurist and politician. A member of the Latvian Way, he served in the Saeima from 1993 to 1998 and was Minister of Justice from 1994 to 1995. He died in February 2022, at the age of 83. "Tiesībsargs Romāns Apsītis".
