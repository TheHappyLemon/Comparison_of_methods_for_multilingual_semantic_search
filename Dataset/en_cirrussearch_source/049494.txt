Wang Xu (Chinese: 王旭; pinyin: Wáng Xù; born September 27, 1985, in Beijing) is a female Chinese freestyle wrestler who competed at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, winning a gold medal in the 72 kg weight class.
