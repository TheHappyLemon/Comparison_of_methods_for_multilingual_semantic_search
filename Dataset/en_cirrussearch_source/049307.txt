Kyoko Kuroda (黒田 今日子, Kuroda Kyōko, born May 8, 1969) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kuroda was born on May 8, 1969. She played for Prima Ham FC Kunoichi. She was selected Best Eleven in 1991 season. On January 12, 1989, when Kuroda was 19 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Finland. She was a member of Japan for 1991 World Cup. She played at 1989, 1991 AFC Championship, 1990 and 1994 Asian Games. She played 21 games and scored 7 goals for Japan until 1994.
