PSG Berani Zlín is an ice hockey team in the Chance Liga. Their home arena is Trinity Bank Aréna Luďka Čajky in Zlín (The biggest city in the Moravian Wallachia). They won the Extraliga in 2004 and 2014. They were formerly known as Aukro Berani Zlín, PSG Zlín, and HC Hamé Zlín, Now name is Berani Zlín and Their home stadium Is Trinity Bank Aréna Luďka Čajky (Zlín).
