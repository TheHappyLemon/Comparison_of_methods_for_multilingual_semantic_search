Émile Robert Wegelin (24 December 1875 in Lyon – 26 June 1962 in Lyon) was a French rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the French boat Club Nautique de Lyon, which won the silver medal in the coxed fours. "Émile Wegelin". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
