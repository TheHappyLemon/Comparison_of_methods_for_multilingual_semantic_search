Sean Doherty (born June 8, 1995, in Conway, New Hampshire) is an American biathlete. Doherty competed at the 2014 Winter Olympics and the 2018 Winter Olympics. Among other competitions, he participated in the Biathlon World Cup opening in 2015 in November and December. He represented the United States at the 2022 Winter Olympics. He is a specialist in the Vermont Army National Guard.
