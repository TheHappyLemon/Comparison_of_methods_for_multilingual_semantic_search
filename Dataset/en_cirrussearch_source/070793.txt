Tatar edition of Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia The Tatar Wikipedia (Tatar: Татар Википедиясе) is the Tatar language edition of Wikipedia. Launched in September 2003, It currently has 501,065 articles, making it the 30th largest Wikipedia by article count. This Wikipedia has 6 administrators along with 50,862 registered users and 87 active users. The Tatar Wikipedia mainly uses the Cyrillic script. However, unlike most other Cyrillic-based languages, some of the articles are in Latin script; accordingly, the language name appears in the language sidebar in both scripts. One of the most active participators and promoters of Tatar Wikipedia is Farhad Fatkullin, Wikimedian of the Year 2018.
