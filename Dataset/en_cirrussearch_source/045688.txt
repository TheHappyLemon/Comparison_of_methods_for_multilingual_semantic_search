Artyom Alyayev (born January 17, 1995) is a Russian professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with HC Ugra of the Supreme Hockey League (VHL). On September 18, 2014, Alyayev made his Kontinental Hockey League debut playing with Torpedo Nizhny Novgorod during the 2014–15 KHL season. "Artyom Alyayev player profile". Kontinental Hockey League.
