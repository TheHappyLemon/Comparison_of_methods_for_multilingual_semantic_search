Roland Lessing (born 14 April 1978 in Tartu) is a former Estonian biathlete. His first World Cup podium was in Pokljuka Pursuit 20 December 2009. He represented Estonia at the 2018 Winter Olympics, his fifth consecutive Olympic Games. Pokljuka Pursuit Wikimedia Commons has media related to Roland Lessing.
