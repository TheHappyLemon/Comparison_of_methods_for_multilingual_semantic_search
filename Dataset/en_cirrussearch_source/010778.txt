Asvyeya (Belarusian: Асвея, romanized: Asvieja; Russian: Освея, romanized: Osveya) is an urban-type settlement in Vyerkhnyadzvinsk District, Vitebsk Region, Belarus. As of 2023, it has a population of 963.
