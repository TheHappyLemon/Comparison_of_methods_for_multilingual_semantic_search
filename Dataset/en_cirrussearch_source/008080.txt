Nereta (Latvian: Neretiņa) is a river of Biržai district municipality, Panevėžys County, northern Lithuania and Southern Latvia. It flows for 25 kilometres (16 mi). It is Latvia-Lithuania border river for 18 km (11 mi). It is a right tributary of the Nemunėlis.
