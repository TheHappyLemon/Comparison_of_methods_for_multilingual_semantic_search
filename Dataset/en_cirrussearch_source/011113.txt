Elīza Tīruma (also Cauce, born 21 August 1990) is a former Latvian luger who has competed since 2006. She won a bronze medal at the 2014 Winter Olympics in the team relay event and placed 12th individually. Her sister Maija Tīruma is also an Olympic luger. Eipurs, Tālis (11 February 2014).
