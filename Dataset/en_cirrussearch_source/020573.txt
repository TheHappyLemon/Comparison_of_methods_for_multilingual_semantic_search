Hennadiy Viktorovich "Gennady" Razin (Ukrainian: Геннадій Вікторович Разін; born February 3, 1978) is a Ukrainian former professional ice hockey defenceman who last played for HC Donbass of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was selected by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round (122nd overall) of the 1997 NHL Entry Draft. On May 1, 2013, HC Donbass of the KHL signed Razin to a one-year contract.
