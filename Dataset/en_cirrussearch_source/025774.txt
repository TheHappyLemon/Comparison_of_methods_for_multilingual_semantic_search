Rok Tičar (born 3 May 1989) is a Slovenian ice hockey player who is currently playing for Klagenfurt Athletic Sports Club in the ICE Hockey League. He has participated in several IIHF World Championships as a member of the Slovenia men's national ice hockey team, and is a member as of 2021.
