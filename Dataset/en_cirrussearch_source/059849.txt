Charles Jean Baptiste Perrin (6 July 1875 in Lyon – 26 March 1954 in Lyon) was a French rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the French boat Club Nautique de Lyon, which won the silver medal in the coxed four. "Charles Perrin". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
