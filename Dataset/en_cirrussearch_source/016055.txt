Alexei Simakov (born April 7, 1979) is a Russian former professional ice hockey forward. He formerly played with Avtomobilist Yekaterinburg in the KHL. Simakov made his Kontinental Hockey League debut playing with Metallurg Magnitogorsk during the inaugural 2008–09 KHL season.
