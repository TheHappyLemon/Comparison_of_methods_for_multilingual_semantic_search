Jeļena Rubļevska (born 23 March 1976) is a Latvian modern pentathlete. She won a silver medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece, becoming the first woman representing Latvia to win an Olympic medal. Her score of 5380 at the 2004 Olympics consisted of: Shooting—988 Fencing—1028 Swimming—1160 Riding—1116 Running—1088 Rubļevska has competed in the modern pentathlon at four Olympic games: 2000, 2004, 2008, and 2012.
