Daniils Ulimbaševs (born 12 March 1992) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Latvian Higher League club FK Auda. In 2021, Ulimbaševs joined Latvian Higher League club FK Auda. He made his debut for the Latvia national football team on 10 October 2017 in a World Cup qualifier group game against Andorra. FK Auda Latvian Cup : 2022 "Latvia-Andorra game report".
