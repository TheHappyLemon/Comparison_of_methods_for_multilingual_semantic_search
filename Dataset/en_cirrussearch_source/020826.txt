Ebersbach-Neugersdorf (Lower Sorbian: Habrachćicy-Nowe Jěžercy) is a town in the district of Görlitz, in Saxony, Germany. It is situated on the border with the Czech Republic, just across from the Czech town of Jiříkov. It was formed on 1 January 2011 by the merger of the former municipalities of Ebersbach and Neugersdorf. Bürgermeisterwahlen 2018, Freistaat Sachsen, accessed 10 July 2021.
