Atriplex glabriuscula, common names Scotland orache, smooth orache, Babington's orache or seaside orach (/ˈɒrətʃ/) is an Atriplex species native to North America and northern Europe. It is an annual. It is listed as a species of special concern in Connecticut and Rhode Island, and as endangered in New York (state). "orache". Oxford English Dictionary third edition. Oxford University Press.
