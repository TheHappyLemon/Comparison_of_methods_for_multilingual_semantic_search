Viečiūnai Eldership (Lithuanian: Viečiūnų seniūnija) is a Lithuanian eldership, located in the eastern part of Druskininkai Municipality. Viečiūnai Eldership is mostly covered by the Dainava Forest. Rivers: Neman. Lakes and ponds: Latažeris (lake). Forests: Dainava Forest. "2021 census". Statistikos Departamentas (Lithuania). Retrieved November 10, 2021.
