Mārcis Ošs (born 25 July 1991) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a central defender for RFS, and the Latvian national team. On 31 August 2021, he joined Lausanne Ouchy in the Swiss Challenge League on loan. Ošs was named in Latvia's squad for the 2016 Baltic Cup. He scored his first international goal for his country in a match against Austria on the 19th of November, 2019 in a UEFA 2020 qualification game, which was also a winning goal as match ended 1-0.
