Aivars Lazdenieks (26 February 1954 – 20 August 2023) was a Latvian rower who competed for the Soviet Union in the 1976 Summer Olympics. In 1976 he was a crew member of the Soviet boat which won the silver medal in the quadruple sculls event. Lazdenieks died on 20 August 2023, at the age of 69.
