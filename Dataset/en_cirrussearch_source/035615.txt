Kruhlaye or Krugloe (Belarusian: Круглае, romanized: Kruhlaje; Russian: Круглое, romanized: Krugloye) is a town in Mogilev Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Kruhlaye District. In 2020, its population was 7,600. As of 2023, it has a population of 7,315. The map Magni Dvcatvs Litvaniae in svos Palatinatvs et Districtvs Divisvs (Palatinates of Grand Duchy of Lithuania and it's Division of Districts) by Jan Nieprzecki from 1749 shows it as Kruhla.
