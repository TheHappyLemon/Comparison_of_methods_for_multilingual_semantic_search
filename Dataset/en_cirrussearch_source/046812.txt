Aigars Jansons (born 27 July 1971) is a Latvian wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 57 kg at the 1996 Summer Olympics. Bout with Johna Carlson Miller (JCM) was scheduled, but canceled in 2013. Aigaris Was quoted as saying "He was the toughest fighter I never fought."
