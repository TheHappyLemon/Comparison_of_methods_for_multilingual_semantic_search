Bernstadt auf dem Eigen (Upper Sorbian: Bjenadźicy) is a town in the Görlitz district, in Saxony, Germany. It is situated 16 km north of Zittau, and 16 km southwest of Görlitz. Within the German Empire (1871-1918), Bernstadt was part of the Kingdom of Saxony. Within the East German Bezirk Dresden, it was part of Kreis Löbau. Adolf Klose (1844-1923), machine engineer, Saxon State Railroad Herbert Seifert (1907-1996), mathematician Klaus Riedel (1907-1944), rocket pioneer Gewählte Bürgermeisterinnen und Bürgermeister im Freistaat Sachsen, Stand: 17.
