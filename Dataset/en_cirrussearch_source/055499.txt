Albert Philippe Taillandier (8 February 1879 in Paris – 27 July 1945 in Auzances) was a French racing cyclist who competed in the late 19th century and early 20th century. He participated in cycling at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris and won the gold medal in the men's sprint. Stéphane Gachet JO d'été. Tous les médaillés français de 1896 à nos jours, ed.
