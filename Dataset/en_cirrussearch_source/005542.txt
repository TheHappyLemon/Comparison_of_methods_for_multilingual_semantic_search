The Baltic Futsal Cup is a Futsal competition among the national teams of the Baltic countries. The competition is hosted by the UEFA. Not to be confused with club Baltic Futsal League, a competition contested by clubs of the Baltic countries. Medal table after the 2021 tournament As of 2017.
