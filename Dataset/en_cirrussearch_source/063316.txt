The 2022–23 Latvian Hockey League season was the 32nd season of the Latvian Hockey League, the top level of ice hockey in Latvia. This season expanded its participants to nine teams – six from Latvia and three from Lithuania. Zemgale/LLU were the defending champions and won the title for the second season in a row when they defeated Mogo/LSPA in five games.
