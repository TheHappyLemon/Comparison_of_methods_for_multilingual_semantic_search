Lawrence is an English and Scottish surname. It is derived from Middle English or old French given name Laurence; itself derived from Latin Laurentius. The Oxford dictionary of family names of Britain lists Laurence and McLaren as variants. Laurentius, a Latin given name which means "from Laurentium", derives from the word laurel, a Roman symbol of victory.
