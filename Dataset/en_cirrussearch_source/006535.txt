The Livonia Cup (Estonian: Liivimaa karikas, Latvian: Livonijas Kauss) is an international men's association football competition organised by the Estonian Football Association and the Latvian Football Federation, and contested by the reigning champions of the Meistriliiga and the Virslīga.
