The 2024 IIHF World Championship Division I will be an international ice hockey tournament run by the International Ice Hockey Federation. The Group A tournament will be held in Bolzano, Italy from 28 April to 4 May and the Group B tournament in Vilnius, Lithuania from 27 April to 3 May 2024. First match(es) will be played: 28 April 2024.
