Kaarepere railway station (Estonian: Kaarepere raudteejaam) is a historic railway station located in the village of Kassinurme near Kaarepere in eastern Estonia. The station is served by trains heading to Tallinn and Tartu operated by Elron The station is listed as a heritage building by the Estonian government's Muinsuskaitseamet (Estonian National Heritage Board), number 23960. Construction on the building was finished in 1927. A parking lot was added next to the station in 2011. List of railway stations in Estonia Rail transport in Estonia "Network and Stations". Elron.
