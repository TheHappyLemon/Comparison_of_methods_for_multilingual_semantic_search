This is a list of the national parks in the Baltic states of Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania. There are 6 national parks in Estonia. There are 4 national parks in Latvia. There are 5 national parks in Lithuania. They were established in 1991 after Lithuania declared independence from the Soviet Union in 1990. Lithuanian SSR National Park established in 1974 was reorganized and renamed Aukštaitija National Park. Now they cover about 2.3 percent of Lithuania's territory.
