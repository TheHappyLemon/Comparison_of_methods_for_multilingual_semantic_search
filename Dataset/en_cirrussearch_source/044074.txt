Hobro Idræts Klub (Danish pronunciation: [hoˈpʁoˀ]) is a professional association football club based in the town of Hobro, Denmark, that competes in the 1st Division, the second tier of the Danish football league system. Founded in 1913, it is known by the short names 'Hobro IK' or 'HIK' and affiliated to DBU Jutland. The team plays its home matches at Hobro Stadium. The club won the 2016–17 Danish 1st Division.
