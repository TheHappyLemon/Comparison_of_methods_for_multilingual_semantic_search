Daniela Ivanova is a Latvian weightlifter. She won the silver medal in the women's 76 kg event at the 2022 European Weightlifting Championships held in Tirana, Albania. She won the bronze medal in the women's 76 kg event at the 2023 European Weightlifting Championships held in Yerevan, Armenia. Ivanova won the silver medal in her event at the 2022 Junior World Weightlifting Championships held in Heraklion, Greece.
