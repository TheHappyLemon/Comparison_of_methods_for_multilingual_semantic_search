Micropercops is a genus of freshwater sleepers native to eastern Asia. There are currently four recognized species in this genus: Micropercops borealis Nichols, 1930 Micropercops cinctus (Dabry de Thiersant, 1872) Micropercops dabryi Fowler & B. A.
