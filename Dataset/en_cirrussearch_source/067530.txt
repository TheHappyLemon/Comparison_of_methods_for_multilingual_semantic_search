Edvinas Girdvainis (born 17 January 1993) is a Lithuanian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for German 3. Liga club SV Sandhausen. On 28 June 2016, Girdvainis signed a three-year contract with one of the top Poland clubs Piast Gliwice. On 21 July 2017, he was loaned for one season to Russian club Tom Tomsk. On 18 August 2018, Girdvainis signed a one-year contract with Keşla FK. He signed for 3. Liga side SV Sandhausen in December 2023. Girdvainis debuted for the Lithuania national team against Romania on 23 March 2016.
