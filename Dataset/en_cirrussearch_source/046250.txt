Otepää Parish (Estonian: Otepää vald) is a rural municipality in Valga County, southern Estonia. It includes the town of Otepää, that is known to be the "winter capital" of Estonia. The parish was formed in 2017 by merging of the former Otepää parish, Sangaste parish, 7 villages of Palupera parish and 12 villages of Puka parish.[1] The Parish has one city, two small boroughs and 52 villages.
