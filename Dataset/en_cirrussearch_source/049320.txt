Yuko Morimoto (森本 ゆう子, Morimoto Yuko, born January 6, 1974) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Morimoto was born in Osaka Prefecture on January 6, 1974. She played for Prima Ham FC Kunoichi. In 1992 season, she was selected Young Player Awards. In December 1993, Morimoto was selected Japan national team for 1993 AFC Championship. At this competition, on December 6, she debuted against Philippines. She also played at 1997 AFC Championship. She played 10 games and scored 2 goals for Japan until 1998.
