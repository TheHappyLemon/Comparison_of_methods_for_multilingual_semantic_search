Werneuchen (German pronunciation: [vɛɐ̯ˈnɔɪ̯çn̩] ) is a town in Brandenburg, Germany, in the district of Barnim northeast of Berlin within the metropolitan area. Most of the population of Werneuchen commutes to Berlin.[citation needed] From 1815 to 1947, Werneuchen was part of the Prussian Province of Brandenburg, from 1947 to 1952 of the State of Brandenburg, from 1952 to 1990 of the East German Bezirk Frankfurt and since 1990 again of Brandenburg.
