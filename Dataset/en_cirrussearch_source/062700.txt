Arthur Bloomfeld was a German sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was the crew on the German boat Aschenbrödel. Arthur Bloomfeld participated in the ½–1 ton class, but the boat Aschenbrödel weighed in at 1.041 tons instead of less than 1 ton, and was disqualified.
