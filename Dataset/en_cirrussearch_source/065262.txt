SES-11 / EchoStar 105 is a geostationary communications satellite operated by SES S.A. and EchoStar and designed and manufactured by Airbus Defence and Space. It has a mass of 5,200 kg (11,500 lb) and has a design life of at least 15 years. The spacecraft had been ready to beam television programming and video services across the Americas for SES S.A. and EchoStar.
