Janis Krūmiņš (born January 9, 1992) is a Latvian professional football player who last played for FC Yerevan in the Armenian First League. In 2019, Krūmiņš joined FC Yerevan. On 21 February 2020, the Football Federation of Armenia announced that FC Yerevan had withdrawn from the league due to financial and technical problems. Latvia - J.
