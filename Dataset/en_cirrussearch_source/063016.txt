One person from Peru competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris. However, the International Olympic Committee currently does not show Peru as having participated in the 1900 Games. Carlos de Candamo competed in two events in 1900, reaching the quarterfinals in the foil and being eliminated in the first round in the épée. "Peru: 1900 Olympic Results". sports-reference.com.
