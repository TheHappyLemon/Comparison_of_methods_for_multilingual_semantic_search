Jurģis Pučinsks (born 3 January 1973 in Daugavpils) is a former Latvian soccer player who played as a midfielder and is now an assistant in Latvia. He signed with Luch-Energia Vladivostok for the Russian First Division 2004 season, but did not feature in any league matches.
