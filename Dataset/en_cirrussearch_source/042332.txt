Ariagner Steven Smith Medina (born 13 December 1998) is a Nicaraguan professional footballer who plays as a striker for A Lyga club Panevėžys and the Nicaragua national team. He has played club football for Real Estelí and Spartaks Jūrmala. In 2015 he trained with Italian club Udinese. On 7 February 2022, Smith signed for A Lyga club Panevėžys on loan for the season. Smith made his international debut for Nicaragua in 2017.
