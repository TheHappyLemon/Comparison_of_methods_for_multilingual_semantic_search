Diāna Bukājeva (born 16 September 1991 in Riga) is a former Latvian tennis player. Bukājeva holds a win–loss record of 5–0 for Latvia in Fed Cup competition, having represented her country in 2007 and 2008 in Mauritius and Armenia respectively.
