Neusalza-Spremberg (pronunciation) is a town in the district Görlitz, in Saxony, Germany. It is situated on the border with the Czech Republic, on the river Spree, 6 km northwest of Ebersbach, and 17 km southeast of Bautzen. 1925: 3,675 1939: 3,701 1946: 4,436 1950: 4,723 1964: 4,204 1990: 2,862 2000: 2,567 2005: 2,488 2006: 2,442 2007: 3,812 (after incorporation of Friedersdorf) 2009: 3,691 2012: 3,453 2013: 3,420 2015: 3,356 Matthias Lehmann was reelected in June 2022.
