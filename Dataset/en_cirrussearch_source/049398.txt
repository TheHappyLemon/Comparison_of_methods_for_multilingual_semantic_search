Miwa Yonetsu (米津 美和, Yonetsu Miwa, born December 4, 1979) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Yonetsu was born in Osaka Prefecture on December 4, 1979. After graduating from Himeji Gakuin Women's Junior College, she joined her local club Toyotsu Ladies FC in 2000. In 2004, she moved to L.League club INAC Leonessa (later INAC Kobe Leonessa). She retired end of 2011 season. On July 29, 2009, when Yonetsu was 29 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Germany. She played 2 games for Japan in 2009.
