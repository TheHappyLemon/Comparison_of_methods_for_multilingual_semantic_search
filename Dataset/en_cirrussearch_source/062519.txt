Adolphe Thomegeux was a French fencer. He competed in the individual épée event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. In the first round he didn't qualify for the quarter finals. His brother Pierre Thomegeux was also a fencer who competed in the individual épée event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. "Adolphe Thomegeux Olympic Results". sports-reference.com.
