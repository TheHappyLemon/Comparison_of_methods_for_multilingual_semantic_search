95 (ninety-five) is the natural number following 94 and preceding 96. 95 is: the 30th distinct semiprime and the fifth of the form (5.q). the third composite number in the 6-aliquot tree. The aliquot sum of 95 is 25, within the aliquot sequence (95,25,6). the last member in the third triplet of distinct semiprimes 93, 94, and 95. an 11-gonal number. a Thabit number. the lowest integer for which the Mertens function is greater than 1. (The lowest integer producing a Merten's value greater than that of 95 is 218).
