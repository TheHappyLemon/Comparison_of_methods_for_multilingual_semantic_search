Tetyana Viktorivna Lazareva (Ukrainian: Тетяна Вікторівна Лазарева; born 4 July 1981) is a Ukrainian female wrestler. She represented Ukraine at the 2012 Summer Olympics in women's freestyle 55 kg. She finished 5th after losing to Tonya Verbeek in the quarter-finals and to Jackeline Renteria in the repechage. She competed in the same weight category at the 2004 Summer Olympics. Lazareva was coached by her husband, Grigori Shepelyev, with whom she has two sons – Aleksandr and Alexei. "Tetyana Lazareva". london2012.com.
