Diabolical Pitch is a baseball action video game from Grasshopper Manufacture and Suda51 for the Xbox 360 Kinect. It was announced at Microsoft's 2011 TGS conference and was initially scheduled for release at the end of 2011, but slipped to early April 2012. Reception Diabolical Pitch received mixed reviews from critics upon release. On Metacritic, the game holds a score of 56/100 based on 24 reviews.
