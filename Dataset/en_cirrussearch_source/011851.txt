Latvia competed at the 2012 Winter Youth Olympics in Innsbruck, Austria. The Latvian team consisted of 16 athletes in 7 sports. Latvia qualified one boy and girl in alpine skiing. Boy Girl Latvia qualified a full biathlon team of 2 boys and 1 girl. Boys Girl Mixed Latvia qualified one boy. Boys Latvia qualified one boy and girl. Boy Girl Sprint Mixed Latvia qualified one boy to compete in the skills challenge competition. Boy Latvia qualified five athletes. Team Latvia qualified one boy in skeleton.
