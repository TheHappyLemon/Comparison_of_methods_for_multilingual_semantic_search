Lake Snudy (Belarusian: Снуды́, Lithuanian: Snudai) is a freshwater lake in the Braslaw District of Vitebsk Region in northern Belarus. Snudy is the second largest of the Braslaw Lakes and the 9th largest in the country. "Main characteristics of the largest lakes of Belarus". Land of Ancestors.
