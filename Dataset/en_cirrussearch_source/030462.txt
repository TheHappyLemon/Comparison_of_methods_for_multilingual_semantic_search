The 2015–16 Latvian Football Cup is the 21st season of the Latvian annual football knock-out competition. The winners will qualify for the first qualifying round of the 2016–17 UEFA Europa League. The matches of this round took place on 8–22 June 2015. The matches of this round took place on 4–8 July 2015. The matches of this round took place on 11–13 July 2015. The matches of this round took place from 18 July to 16 September 2015. Babīte received a bye. The matches were played on 9, 10 and 13 April 2016. The first leg will be played on 27 April 2016 and the second leg on 4 May 2016. The final will be played on 22 May 2016.
