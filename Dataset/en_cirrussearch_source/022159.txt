Nordic combined at the 2014 Winter Olympics was held at the RusSki Gorki Jumping Center. The three events took place between 12–20 February 2014. The following is the competition schedule for all three events. All times are (UTC+4). A total of 55 quota spots were available to athletes to compete at the games. A maximum of 5 athletes could be entered by a National Olympic Committee. Competitors were eligible to compete if they have scored points at a World or Continental cup event during the qualification period of July 2012 to 19 January 2014.
