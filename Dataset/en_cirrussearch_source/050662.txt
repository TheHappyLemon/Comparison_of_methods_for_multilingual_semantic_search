Corythucha is a large genus of lace bug in the family Tingidae that is primarily distributed in the New World, especially North America. The genus includes pest species such as the “cotton or bean lace bug" (C. gossypii) as well as species that provide maternal care, such as C. hewitti (Drake) These 75 species belong to the genus
