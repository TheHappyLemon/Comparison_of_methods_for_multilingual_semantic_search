The genus Coronilla contains 8 species of flowering plants native to Europe and North Africa. It contained about 20 species before being split into Securigera. Species include: Coronilla coronata Coronilla juncea Coronilla minima Coronilla ramosissima Coronilla repanda Coronilla scorpioides Coronilla vaginalis Coronilla valentina Species that were part of the genus include: Securigera elegans Securigera orientalis Securigera varia L.
