Jānis Vinters (born 19 December 1971) is a Latvian rally racing motorcycle rider and farmer. He competed several times in the Dakar Rally, including every year until 2007 save for a two-year stretch in 2004–2005 due to injuries. In 2006, he finished in tenth place.
