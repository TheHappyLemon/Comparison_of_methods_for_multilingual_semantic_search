Alpha Baltic–Unitymarathons.com (UCI team code: ALB) was a Latvian road cycling team. Since 2011 it is a UCI Continental team. Between 2011 and 2013 the leading cyclist of the team was Estonian cyclist Erki Pütsep. 2011 Overall Baltic Chain Tour, Erki Pütsep Team classification Baltic Chain Tour As of 24 June 2014.
