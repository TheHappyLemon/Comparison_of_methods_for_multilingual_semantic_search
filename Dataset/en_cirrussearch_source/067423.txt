The Israel women's national basketball team represents Israel in international women's basketball matches and is controlled by the Israel Basketball Association. Israel have hosted the FIBA Women's EuroBasket in 1991, and hosted again in 2023 along with Slovenia. During their first game against Bulgaria, Israel original trailed by eight before coming back to hold on to a 71-68 victory.
