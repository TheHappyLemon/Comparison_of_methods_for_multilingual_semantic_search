Jānis Beks (born 1 November 2002) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for FK Metta on loan from FK RFS and the Latvia national team. Beks has been called up for Latvia national team for the UEFA Euro 2024 qualifying matches against Turkey national team on 16 June 2023 and Armenia national team on 19 June 2023.
