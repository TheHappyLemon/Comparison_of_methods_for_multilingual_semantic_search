Dzmitry Platonaw (Belarusian: Дзмітрый Платонаў; Russian: Дмитрий Платонов; born 7 February 1986) is a retired Belarusian footballer midfielder. He is a twin brother of Pavel Platonaw. He played for BATE Borisov in the preliminary rounds of the 2006-07 UEFA Cup and the 2007-08 UEFA Champions League.
