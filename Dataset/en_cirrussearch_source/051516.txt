Tealliocaris is an extinct genus of pygocephalomorphans from the Carboniferous. The genus contains eight described species: †Tealliocaris caudafimbriata Copeland, 1957 †Tealliocaris etheridgei Peach, 1882 †Tealliocaris formosa (Peach, 1882) †Tealliocaris loudonensis Peach, 1908 †Tealliocaris robusta Peach, 1908 †Tealliocaris tarrassiana Peach, 1908 †Tealliocaris walloniensis Gueriau, Charbonnier & Clément, 2014 †Tealliocaris woodwardi (Etheridge, 1877)  WoRMS.
