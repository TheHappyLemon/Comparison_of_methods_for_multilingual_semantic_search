Antti Agathon Tulenheimo (4 December 1879 – 3 September 1952) was a Finnish politician from the National Coalition Party who served as Prime Minister of Finland in 1925. He was also minister of the interior from November 1918 to April 1919. He was also mayor of Helsinki between 1931–1944 and rector of the University of Helsinki 1926–1930.
