Silurus is a genus of catfishes native to Europe and Asia. There are currently 20 recognized species in this genus: Silurus aristotelis Garman, 1890 (Aristotle's catfish) Silurus asotus Linnaeus, 1758 (Amur catfish) Silurus biwaensis Tomoda, 1961 (Lake Biwa giant catfish) Silurus burmanensis Thant, 1967 Silurus caobangensis V.
