Latvijas Radio 5 - Pieci.lv is part of Latvijas Radio public service broadcasting network based in Riga, Latvia. Pieci.lv is public service broadcasting for youth, combining subculture, different lifestyle and opinion leaders. Currently the network consists of nine internet only stations and a nationwide FM station. On 15 March 2015, Pieci.lv competed in first European Radio Championship which was held in Milan. In the Championship the station was represented by Toms Grēviņš and Marta Līne. Latvijas Radio 5 - Pieci.lv was launched at 10pm on 14 July 2013. The first station to be launched was Pieci Koncerti which played exclusive live recordings from the Positivus Festival in Salacgrīva. Pieci.lv streams several stations, available both online and in mobile apps: Pieci Latvieši - Latvian bands Pieci Hiti - pop music Pieci Rīti - replays of pieci.lv morning programmes Pieci Rokeri - Latvian and foreign rock Pieci Atklājumi - hits Pieci Bīti - club music Pieci Latgalieši - music in Latgalian Pieci Koncerti - live concerts Pieci Hip Hop - hip hop from around the world Latvijas radio Office of The Radio of Latvia (Riga) Wikimapia "Pieci.lv".
