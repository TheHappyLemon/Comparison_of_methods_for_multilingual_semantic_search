Hiroki Yamada (山田 大記, Yamada Hiroki, born 27 December 1988) is a Japanese footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or a winger for Júbilo Iwata. In May 2017, following Karlsruher SC's relegation from the 2. Bundesliga in the 2017–18, Yamada announced his decision to leave the club ending a three-year spell there.
