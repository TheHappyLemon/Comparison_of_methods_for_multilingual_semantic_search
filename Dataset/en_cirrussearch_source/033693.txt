Yōsuke Kashiwagi (柏木 陽介, Kashiwagi Yōsuke, born 15 December 1987) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a central midfielder for FC Gifu. He played for Sanfrecce Hiroshima and Urawa Red Diamonds before moving to Gifu. He won the 2017 AFC Champions League with the Reds. In 2023 announced his retired for the end of 2023 J3 League. In July 2007, Kashiwagi played for the Japan national U-20 team in the 2007 U-20 World Cup. At this tournament, he wore the number 10 shirt and played all 4 matches as the starting offensive midfielder.
