Švenčionėliai (pronunciation) is a village in Švenčionys district municipality, Lithuania 10 km west of Švenčionys. The river Žeimena flows through Švenčionėliai. In 1940, there were around 1000 Jews in Švenčionėliai, i.e. 20 percent of the total population.
