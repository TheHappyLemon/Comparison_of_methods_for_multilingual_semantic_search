Amuro-Baltiysk (Russian: Амуро-Балтийск) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Amuro-Baltiysky Selsoviet of Zeysky District, Amur Oblast, Russia. The population was 199 as of 2018. There are 2 streets. Amuro-Baltiysk is located on the right bank of the Urkan River, 52 km southwest of Zeya (the district's administrative centre) by road.
