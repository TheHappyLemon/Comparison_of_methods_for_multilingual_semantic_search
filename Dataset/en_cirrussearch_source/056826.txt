The Cère (French pronunciation: [sɛʁ]; Occitan: Sera) is a 120.4 km (75 mi) long river in south-western France, and a left tributary of the Dordogne. Its source is in the south-western Massif Central, near the Plomb du Cantal mountain. It flows generally west through the following départements and towns: Cantal: Vic-sur-Cère, Arpajon-sur-Cère (near Aurillac) Corrèze Lot: Bretenoux The Cère flows into the Dordogne near Bretenoux.
