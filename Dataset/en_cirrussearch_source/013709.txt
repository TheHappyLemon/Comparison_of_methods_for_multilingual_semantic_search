Maksims Širokovs (born December 13, 1982) is a Latvian professional ice hockey player for Prizma Riga of the Latvian Hockey Higher League. He is the younger brother of Aleksejs Širokovs. Širokovs started his professional career with Liepājas Metalurgs in Latvia. He has represented the Latvian national team in various tournaments. IIHF.
