Vladimirs Mamonovs (born April 22, 1980 in Riga, Latvian SSR, Soviet Union) is a Latvian professional ice hockey player. He plays Left wing. He currently plays for Sary-Arka Karaganda of the Kazakhstani Championship. LAT totals do not include numbers from the 2002–03, 2005–06, 2008–09 and 2010–11 seasons.
