Seneca's Day (Lithuanian: Senekos diena) is a 2016 Lithuanian drama film directed by Kristijonas Vildžiūnas [lt]. It was selected as the Lithuanian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 89th Academy Awards but it was not nominated.
