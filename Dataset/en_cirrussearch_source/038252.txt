Vilma Rimšaitė (born 24 February 1983 in Šiauliai) is a Lithuanian BMX cyclist. In the 2009 UCI BMX World Championships she won a bronze medal. Rimšaitė was appointed as a member of the inaugural UCI Athletes' Commission in 2011. Ryan, Barry (5 March 2015). "UCI Athletes' Commission holds first meeting in Aigle". cyclingnews.com.
