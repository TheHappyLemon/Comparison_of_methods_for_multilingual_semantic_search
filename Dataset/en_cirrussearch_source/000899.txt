Agnese Koklaca (born 2 March 1990) is a Latvian luger who has competed since 2006. Her best World Cup season finish was 41st twice, earning them in 2006-07 and 2008-09. She made her Olympic debut in 2010, when finished 24th in the women's singles event. "Agnese Koklaca". FIL-Luge.org. Archived from the original on 20 September 2012. Retrieved 8 February 2010.
