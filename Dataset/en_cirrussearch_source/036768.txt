Zhytkavichy (Belarusian: Жыткавічы; Russian: Житковичи, romanized: Zhitkovichi; Polish: Żydkowicze) is a town in Gomel Region, Belarus. It serves as the administrative center of Zhytkavichy District. As of 2023, it has a population of 15,961. Zhytkavichy was first mentioned in 1500.
