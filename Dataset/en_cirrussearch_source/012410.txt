Year 1353 (MCCCLIII) was a common year starting on Tuesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. March 3 – Bern signs an alliance with the Old Swiss Confederacy. The Moroccan traveler Ibn Battuta makes the first recorded visit to Timbuktu and Kabara, when returning from a stay in the capital of the Mali Empire. The Decameron is finished by Giovanni Boccaccio. The Black Death (1331) subsides. The Lao kingdom of Lan Xang is founded by Fa Ngum.
