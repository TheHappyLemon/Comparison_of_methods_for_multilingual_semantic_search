Pavel Chernov (born 30 January 1990) is a Russian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Shakhter Soligorsk of the Belarusian Extraleague (BLR). Chernov played with HC Vityaz Podolsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) during the 2012–13 season. After five seasons with Severstal Cherepovets, Chernov returned to Vityaz Podolsk as a free agent, in securing a two-year contract on May 4, 2018.
