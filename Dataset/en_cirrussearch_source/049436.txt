Saki Ueno (上野 紗稀, Ueno Saki, born November 20, 1994) is a Japanese football player. She plays for WE League club Urawa Reds. She has also played for Japan national team. Ueno was born in Matsudo on November 20, 1994. In 2013, she joined JEF United Chiba from Urawa Reds youth team. She was selected Best Young Player awards in 2013 season. On September 26, 2013, when Ueno was 18 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against Nigeria.
