Pāvels Seničevs (10 June 1924 – 19 May 1997) was a Soviet sports shooter. He competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics and the 1968 Summer Olympics. In October 1962 he was a member of the Soviet team at the 38th World Shooting Championships in Cairo. He was armed with MTs 8 shotgun In the trap event at the 1964 Olympics, he won a silver medal. In 1966 he finished in first place in Brno. In 1967 he finished in first place in Moscow. Д. Покровский. В долине Нила.
