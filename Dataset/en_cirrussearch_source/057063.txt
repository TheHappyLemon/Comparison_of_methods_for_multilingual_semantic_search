Nikolai Talts (26 November 1890 Laiksaare Parish, Pärnu County – 26 January 1949 Tallinn) was an Estonian lawyer and politician. He was a member of Estonian Constituent Assembly. 1933–1938 he was Minister of Agriculture. "Juhatus ja liikmed". Riigikogu (in Estonian). Retrieved 25 February 2021.
