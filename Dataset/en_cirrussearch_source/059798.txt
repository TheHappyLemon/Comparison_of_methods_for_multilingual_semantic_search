Richard Wilhelm Julius Körner (22 December 1870 in Sülldorf– 19 November 1954 in Blankenese) was a German rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the German crew who won the bronze medal in the coxed fours final A. "Julius Körner". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
