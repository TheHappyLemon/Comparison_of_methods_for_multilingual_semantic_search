Humbert II (Italian: Umberto II), nicknamed the Fat (1065 – 19 October 1103), was Count of Savoy from 1080 until his death in 1103. He was the son of Amadeus II of Savoy. He was married to Gisela of Burgundy, daughter of William I, Count of Burgundy, and had seven children: Amadeus III of Savoy (1095-1148) William, Bishop of Liège Adelaide, (d. 1154), married to Louis VI of France Agnes, (d. 1127), married to Archimbald VI, lord of Bourbon Umberto Reginald Guy, abbey of Namur C.W.
