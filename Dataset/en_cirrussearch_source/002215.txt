.bt is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for the Kingdom of Bhutan. It is administered by the Ministry of Information and Communication. Domains and Subdomains with registration qualification requirements: .bt - Organizations or individuals. .com.bt - Commercial organizations or related individuals. .edu.bt - Universities and other educational institutes. .net.bt - Networks. .gov.bt - Government-related websites only. .org.bt - Non-profit organizations.
