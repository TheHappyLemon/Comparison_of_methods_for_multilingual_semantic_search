Rushan Rafikov (born 15 May 1995) is a Russian ice hockey player of Tatar descent. He is currently playing with Lokomotiv Yaroslavl of the Kontinental Hockey League. Rafikov was selected by the Calgary Flames in the seventh round (187th overall) of the 2013 NHL Entry Draft. Rafikov made his Kontinental Hockey League debut during the 2015–16 KHL season.
