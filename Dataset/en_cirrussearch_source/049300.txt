Yuko Oita (尾板 裕子, Oita Yuko, born August 3, 1969) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Oita was born on August 3, 1969. She played for Kobe FC, Nikko Securities Dream Ladies, Shiroki FC Serena and Takarazuka Bunnys. On January 21, 1986, when Oita was 16 years old, she debuted for Japan national team against India.
