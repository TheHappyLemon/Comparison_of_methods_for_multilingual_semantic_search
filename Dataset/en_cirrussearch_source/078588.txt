Skaņkalne parish (Latvian: Skaņkalnes pagasts) is an administrative territorial entity of Valmiera Municipality in the Vidzeme region of Latvia. Prior to 2009, it was an administrative unit of the former Valmiera District. Town Mazsalaca is parish administrative center.
