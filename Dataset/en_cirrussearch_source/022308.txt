Agnese Āboltiņa (born 7 February 1996) is a Latvian alpine skier. She was born in Riga. She competed at the FIS Alpine World Ski Championships 2013 in Schladming, and at the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, in giant slalom, slalom and super-G.
