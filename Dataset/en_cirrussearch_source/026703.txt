Boubín is a 1362 m high hill in the South Bohemian region of the Czech Republic. It is located 3.5 km east of the village of Kubova Huť. Most of the hill is covered by a primeval forest called Boubínský prales which, has been a natural preserve since 1858. "Czech Republic - Boubín and Boubín Forest". czechtourism.com.
