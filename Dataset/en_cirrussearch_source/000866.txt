Dobra [ˈdɔbra] is a town in Turek County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in central Poland, with 1,333 inhabitants as of December 2021. It is located in the Sieradz Land. Dobra was a private town, administratively located in the Sieradz County in the Sieradz Voivodeship in the Greater Poland Province of the Kingdom of Poland.
