Riebiņi Parish (Latvian: Riebiņu pagasts, Latgalian: Ribinišku pogosts) is an administrative unit of Preiļi Municipality in the Latgale region of Latvia. At the beginning of 2014, the population of the parish was 1443. The administrative center is Riebiņi village.
