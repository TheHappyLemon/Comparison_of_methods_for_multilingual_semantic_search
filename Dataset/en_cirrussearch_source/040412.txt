Vladislavs Soloveičiks (born 25 May 1999) is a Latvian football player who plays for FK Jelgava. He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Zenit-2 Saint Petersburg on 24 September 2017 in a game against FC Olimpiyets Nizhny Novgorod. "Game Report by FNL".
