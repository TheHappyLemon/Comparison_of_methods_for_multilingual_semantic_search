Artūrs Kuzmenkovs (born December 1, 1993) is a Latvian ice hockey forward who plays for Kraft in the 2. Divisioona. He started his professional career with Liepājas Metalurgs. On June 9, 2013, Kuzmenkovs signed a two-year contract with Dinamo Rīga. On September 5 of 2013/14 season he played his first game in KHL in win against Dinamo Minsk.
