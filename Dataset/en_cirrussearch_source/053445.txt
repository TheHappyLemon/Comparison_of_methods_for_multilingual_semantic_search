Karl Bruno Paul Neukirch (3 November 1864 – 27 June 1941) was a German gymnast. He was born and died in Berlin. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Neukirch was a member of the German team that won two gold medals by placing first in both of the team events, the parallel bars and the horizontal bar.
