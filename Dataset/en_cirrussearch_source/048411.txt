Athletes from the Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes competed at the 1928 Summer Olympics in Amsterdam, Netherlands. 34 competitors, all men, took part in 21 events in 6 sports. Men's 1500m Luka Predanić Men's marathon Dimitrije Stefanović Men's javelin throw Vilim Messner Men's decathlon Branko Kallay Four cyclists, all men, represented Yugoslavia in 1928. Men's individual and team road race Josip Šolar Stjepan Ljubić Josip Škrabl Antun Banek Two fencers, both men, represented Yugoslavia in 1928.
