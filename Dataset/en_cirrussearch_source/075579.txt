Gints Freimanis (9 May 1985 – 6 June 2023) was a Latvian professional footballer who played as a right-back. Freimanis joined Saldus SS/Leevon in 2020. Freimanis first call-up to the senior Latvia national team squad came in May 2014 for the 2014 Baltic Cup, making his national team debut as a 94th-minute substitute in the final game against Lithuania, helping Latvia to win the trophy by a 1–0 scoreline.[citation needed] Freimanis was diagnosed with skin cancer in October 2021.
