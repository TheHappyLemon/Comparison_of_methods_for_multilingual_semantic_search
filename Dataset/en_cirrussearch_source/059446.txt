An ectoparasitic infestation is a parasitic disease caused by organisms that live primarily on the surface of the host. Examples: Scabies Crab louse (pubic lice) Pediculosis (head lice) Gamasoidosis (avian mites) Lernaeocera branchialis (cod worm) Ectoparasiticide "Ectoparasites and endoparasites".
