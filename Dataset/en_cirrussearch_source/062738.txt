Émile Sacré (16 May 1861 in Lille – 4 September 1933 in Rouen) was a French sailor and Olympic champion. Sacré competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics, where he won first prize in one of the two races in the 0-½ ton class. Sacré did not finish in the other race.
