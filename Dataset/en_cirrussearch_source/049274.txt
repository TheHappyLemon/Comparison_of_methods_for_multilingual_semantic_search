Chieko Homma (本間 知恵子, Honma Chieko, born November 5, 1964) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Homma was born on November 5, 1964. She played for Shimizudaihachi SC and Shizuoka Koki LSC. In June 1981, Homma was selected by the Japan national team for the 1981 AFC Championship. At this competition, on June 7, she debuted against Chinese Taipei. This match was the Japan team's first match in an International A Match. She played three games for Japan in 1981.
