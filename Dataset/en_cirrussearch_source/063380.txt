Cuba competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. In the discipline sections below, the medalists' names are bolded. Cuba had one fencer compete in the nation's debut. He won the amateur épée competition, and in a special event that pitted the top four amateurs in 1900 against the top four professionals, he defeated all but one of his opponents, finishing 6-1 to take the silver medal.
