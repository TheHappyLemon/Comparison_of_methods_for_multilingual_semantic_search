Peter Čerešňák (born January 26, 1993) is a Slovak ice hockey player currently playing for HC Škoda Plzeň of the Czech Extraliga (ELH). He played with HC Dukla Trenčín in the Slovak Extraliga during the 2009–10 Slovak Extraliga season. He was the New York Rangers 6th round draft pick (172nd overall) in the 2011 NHL Entry Draft.
