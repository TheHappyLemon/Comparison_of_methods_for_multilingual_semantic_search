Nobuko Kondo (近藤 修子, Kondo Nobuko, born December 1, 1956) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Kondo was born on December 1, 1956. In June 1981, she was selected by the Japan national team for the 1981 AFC Championship. She debuted against Chinese Taipei on June 7. That match was the Japan team's first International A Match. She played in all three matches during the championship tournament. In September, she played against Italy. However Japan was defeated by a score of 0–9.
