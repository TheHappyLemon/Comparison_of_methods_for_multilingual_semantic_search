Marcus Kink (born 13 January 1985) is a German professional ice hockey forward who is currently playing as the Captain for Adler Mannheim in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). He has represented the German national team in numerous IIHF World Championships tournaments and was nominated to the Olympic Games in 2018. "Marcus Kink extends contract with Adler".
