Eugène Charles Choisel (15 September 1881 in Asnières-sur-Seine – 1 February 1946 in Paris) was a French track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. He placed fourth in the 200 metre hurdles. Choisel also competed in the 110 metre hurdles. He placed third in his first-round (semifinals) heat and did not advance to the final. "Eugène Choisel".
