Anomalurus is the largest genus in the rodent family Anomaluridae, with four species. It is the only genus in the subfamily Anomalurinae. A. beecrofti - Beecroft's scaly-tailed squirrel A. derbianus - Lord Derby's scaly-tailed squirrel A. pelii - Pel's scaly-tailed squirrel A. pusillus - dwarf scaly-tailed squirrel Beecroft's scaly-tailed squirrel, Anomalurus beecrofti, is sometimes moved to its own genus, Anomalurops, but Dieterlen (2005) and other authorities consider it to be part of Anomalurus.
