.gh is the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Ghana. .com.gh - for companies .edu.gh - for schools .gov.gh - for government There are some exceptions to these domains: parliament.gh for the Parliament of Ghana, isoc.gh for the ISOC Ghana Chapter, nic.gh for the Ghana Network Information Center, cocobod.gh for the Ghana Cocoa Board, techgov.gh for Techgov and yellowpages.gh for the Yellow Pages Ghana Directory.
