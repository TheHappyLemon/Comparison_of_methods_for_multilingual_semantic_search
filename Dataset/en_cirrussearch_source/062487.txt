39°31′15″N 45°28′56″E﻿ / ﻿39.52083°N 45.48222°E﻿ / 39.52083; 45.48222 Bardzruni (Armenian: Բարձրունի) is a village in the Vayk Municipality of the Vayots Dzor Province of Armenia. The village is located close to the Armenia–Azerbaijan border. The village was previously known as Sultanbek.
