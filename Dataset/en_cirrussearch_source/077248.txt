Port of Liepaja (Latvian: Liepājas osta) is a port in Liepāja, Latvia. It handled 7 334 261,80 tonnes of cargo and 39 987 passengers in 2019, making it the third largest port in the country (by territory, number of passengers and amount of cargo handled).
