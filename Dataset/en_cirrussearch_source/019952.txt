Artem Oleksandrovych Yashkin (Ukrainian: Артем Олександрович Яшкін; born 29 April 1975) is a retired Ukrainian footballer. He was born in Vologda, now in Russia. After coming to play for FC Dynamo Kyiv, he was offered to accept the Ukrainian citizenship and play for the Ukraine national football team.
