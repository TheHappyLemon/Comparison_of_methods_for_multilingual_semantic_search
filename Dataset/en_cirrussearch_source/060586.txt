Frank Olof Odberg (1 March 1879 – 1917) was a Belgian rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was a member of the Belgian club Royal Club Nautique de Gand and with his team, he won the silver medal in the men's eight. "Frank Odberg". Olympedia. Retrieved 28 December 2020.
