Alexander Sergeyevich Osipov (born March 24, 1989) is a Russian professional ice hockey defenceman playing for Rubin Tyumen of the Supreme Hockey League (VHL). In the 2014–15 season, after one game with Ak Bars Kazan, Osipov was traded for financial compensation to Dynamo Moscow on September 24, 2014.
