Andriy Kruhlyak (Russian: Андрей Кругляк; born 22 April 1986) is a retired football striker from Ukraine. Kruhlyak began his career at Dynamo-2 Kyiv in the Ukrainian First Division in 2002. In 2007, after 77 league appearances and 12 goals for the club, he moved to Latvia to play for Virsliga club FK Liepājas Metalurgs on loan. On 2 August 2007, Kruhlyak scored as Metalurgs beat Belarusian club FC Dinamo Brest 3–1 in the UEFA Cup 2007-08 first qualifying round.
