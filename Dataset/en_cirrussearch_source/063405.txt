Yaroslav Volodymyrovych Yanushevych (Ukrainian: Ярослав Володимирович Янушевич, born 7 February 1978) is a Ukrainian politician who served as the governor of Kherson Oblast from 3 August 2022 until 24 January 2023. Control over Kherson Oblast is disputed due to the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine.
