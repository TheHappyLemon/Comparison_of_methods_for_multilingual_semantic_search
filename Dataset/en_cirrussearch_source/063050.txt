Stor Island is one of the uninhabited islands in Qikiqtaaluk Region, Nunavut, Canada. It is located in Eureka Sound, an area separating Axel Heiberg Island from Ellesmere Island. Fulmar Channel is southwest of the island, while Bay Fiord is to the northeast. Stor Island is a member of the Sverdrup Islands, Queen Elizabeth Islands, and the Arctic Archipelago. There are two peaks that reach approximately 500 m above sea level. Stor Island is 32 km long and 14 km wide. "Stor Island". oceandots.com. Archived from the original on December 23, 2010.
