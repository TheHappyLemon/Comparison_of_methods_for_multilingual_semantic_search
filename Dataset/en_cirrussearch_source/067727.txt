Zverevo (Russian: Зве́рево) is a town in Rostov Oblast, Russia, located 140 kilometers (87 mi) northeast of Rostov-on-Don. Population: 22,411 (2010 Census); 25,356 (2002 Census); 28,206 (1989 Census). Zverevo grew from a mining settlement, which was founded in the beginning of the 20th century.[citation needed] It was granted urban-type settlement status in 1929 and town status in 1989.[citation needed] Within the framework of administrative divisions, it is, along with one rural locality, incorporated as Zverevo Urban Okrug—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the districts.
