Deniss Romanovs (born 2 September 1978) is a retired Latvian football goalkeeper. His early career was spent with Latvian sides Skonto-Metāls, Valmiera, Ventspils and Ditton. During the 2008/09 season he joined Slavia Praha. In July 2010 Romanovs moved to Azerbaijan, signing a two-year deal with Khazar Lankaran. In January 2011 he joined Indonesian club Cendrawasih. After the first round of the championship due to some internal problems the championship was stopped, the foreigners' limit was changed and Cendrawasih ended its existence. Before the start of the 2013 season Romanovs joined Indonesian Premier League newcomers Pro Duta. On 24 October 2013 he scored a goal from the penalty spot in a 6-0 league victory over Bontang.
