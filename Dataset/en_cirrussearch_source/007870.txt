Gaujiena (German: Adsel) is a village along the Gauja River in Gaujiena Parish, Smiltene Municipality, Latvia. It was the seat of a Komtur of the Teutonic Knights. The Gaujiena Castle was erected in the 13th century, but fell into disrepair in the 18th century. Gaujiena Palace Gaujiena Castle ruins Shop Krahe, Friedrich-Wilhelm (2000).
