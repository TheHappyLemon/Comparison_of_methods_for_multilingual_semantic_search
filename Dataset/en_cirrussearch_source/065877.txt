William Poromaa (born 23 December 2000) is a Swedish cross-country skier who competes internationally. He competed at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2021 in Oberstdorf, where he placed fourth in the relay with the Swedish team. Poromaa is a son of former cross-country skiers Anette Fanqvist and Larry Poromaa [sv]. Poromaa is of Finnish descent through his paternal grandmother.
