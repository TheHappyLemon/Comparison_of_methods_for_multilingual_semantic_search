Katherine Sauerbrey (born 5 May 1997) is a German cross-country skier who represented Germany at the 2022 Winter Olympics. Sauerbrey made her World Cup debut in 2021. She represented Germany at the 2022 Winter Olympics and won a silver medal in the women's 4 × 5 kilometre relay.
