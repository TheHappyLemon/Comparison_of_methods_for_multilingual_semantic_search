Lu Xiaosha (Chinese: 呂小莎; pinyin: Lu Xiaosha; born 1973), also known as Sarah Lu, is a Chinese chess player who holds the FIDE title of Woman International Master (WIM, 1995). In 1995, Lu participated in Women's World Chess Championship Interzonal Tournament in Chişinău where ranked 25th place. In 1995, she was awarded the FIDE Woman International Master (WIM) title. Later Lu moved to United States. In 2001, she won the Hawaii State Chess Championship.
