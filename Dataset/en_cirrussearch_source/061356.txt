Maria Aleksandrovna Gurova (Russian: Мария Александровна Гурова; born 16 April 1989) is a Russian retired freestyle wrestler. She competed at the 55 kg division in the 2012 European Wrestling Championships and won the bronze medal in the competition. She won the gold medal at the 2014 European Wrestling Championships after defeating Maria Prevolaraki of Greece.
