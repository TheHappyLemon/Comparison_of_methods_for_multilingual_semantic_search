Estonia competed at the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, Australia. 33 competitors, 31 men and 2 women, took part in 29 events in 11 sports. Men Track & road events Field events Combined events – Decathlon Men Four fencers, all men, represented Estonia in 2000. Men Men Men Estonia competed in three events in the Sailing competition of the Sydney Olympics. The highest they placed in any event was 22nd.
