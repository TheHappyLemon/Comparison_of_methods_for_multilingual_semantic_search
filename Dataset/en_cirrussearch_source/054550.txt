Dmitry Molosh (Belarusian: Дзмітры Молаш (Dzmitry Molash); Russian: Дмитрий Васильевич Молош; born 10 December 1981) is a Belarusian professional football manager and former player. He is the manager of Torpedo-BelAZ Zhodino. Molosh previously played for FC Nosta Novotroitsk in the Russian First Division. He is known for his long-range powerful shot which helped him to score several long distance goals.
