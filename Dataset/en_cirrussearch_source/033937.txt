Toshihiko Okimune (沖宗 敏彦, Okimune Toshihiko, born September 7, 1959) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Okimune was born in Hiroshima Prefecture on September 7, 1959. After graduating from high school, he joined Japan Soccer League Division 1 club Kawasaki Frontale in 1978. However, in first season, the club was relegated to Division 2. In August 1979, Okimune was selected Japan U-20 national team for 1979 World Youth Championship. He played 3 games. On August 30, 1981, he debuted for Japan national team against Malaysia. He played 2 games for Japan in 1981.
