The 2022 Latvian Football Cup, known as the ‘’’Responsible Gaming Latvian Cup’’’ for sponsorship reasons, was a single elimination association football tournament which began on 6 May 2022. Six preliminary round matches were played between 6 May and 8 May 2022. Fourteen first round matches were played on 21-22 May 2022. Twelve second round matches were played on 11–12 June 2022. Six Play-off Round matches were played on 22-26 June 2022. Eight Round of 16 matches were played on 9–11 July 2022. The quarter–finals were played on 13–14 August 2022. The semi–finals were played on 19 September 2022.
