Arlan Hockey Club (Kazakh: Арлан хоккей клубы Arlan Kókshetaý hokkeı klýby, Russian: Хоккейный клуб «Арлан») commonly referred as Arlan Kokshetau, is a professional ice hockey team based in Kokshetau, Kazakhstan. Founded in 2009, they play in the Kazakhstan Hockey Championship, the top level of ice hockey in Kazakhstan. The team also won the 2018–19 IIHF Continental Cup, becoming the first team from Kazakhstan to do so.
