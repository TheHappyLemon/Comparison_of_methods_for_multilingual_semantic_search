Coluria is a plant genus in the sub family Rosoideae native to Asia, Siberia and Altai. This herbaceous perennial plant reaches 30 cm in height and 30 cm in width. The flowers are hermaphroditic and are pollinated by insects. It grows on the banks of mountains rivers. Coluria elegans Cardot Coluria geoides (Pall.) Ledeb.
