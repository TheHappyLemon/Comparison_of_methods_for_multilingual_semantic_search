Minitingis is a genus of lace bugs in the family Tingidae. There are at least two described species in Minitingis. These two species belong to the genus Minitingis: Minitingis elsae Froeschner, 1968 Minitingis minusculus Barber, 1954 "Minitingis Report". Integrated Taxonomic Information System.
