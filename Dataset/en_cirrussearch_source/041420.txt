Oskars Batņa (born 7 May 1995) is a Latvian professional ice hockey forward for Mikkelin Jukurit of the Finnish Liiga. As a junior, Batņa played in Latvian junior leagues. In the 2013 season, he joined Dinamo Riga junior club HK Rīga of MHL He made his KHL debut on 22 August 2017 in a loss against Avangard Omsk, he scored his first KHL goal in win against Jokerit on 26 January 2018. Batņa represented Latvia at the 2023 IIHF World Championship where he recorded two goals and one assist and won a bronze medal, Latvia's first ever IIHF World Championship medal. eliteprospects.
