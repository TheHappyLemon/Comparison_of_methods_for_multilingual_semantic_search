Trisetum is a genus of plants in the grass family, widespread in temperate, subarctic, and alpine habitats in much of the world. Oatgrass is a common name for plants in this genus. Known species are listed in list of Trisetum species. lectotype designated by Hitchcock, U.S.D.A.
