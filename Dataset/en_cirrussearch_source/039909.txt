Lasha Shergelashvili (Georgian: ლაშა შერგელაშვილი, romanized: lasha shergelashvili, pronounced [laʃa ʃeɾɡelaʃʷili]; born 17 January 1992) is a Georgian professional footballer who plays for Torpedo Kutaisi. Shergelashvili made his debut for the Georgia national football team on 23 January 2017 in a friendly against Uzbekistan.
