Juncus filiformis, called the thread rush, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Juncus, with a circumboreal distribution. It has been introduced to South Georgia Island. It is typically found in wetlands, on the borders of lakes and streams. Sp. Pl.: 326 (1753) "Juncus filiformis L." Plants of the World Online.
