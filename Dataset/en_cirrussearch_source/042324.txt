Maksim Sergeyevich Shiryayev (Russian: Максим Сергеевич Ширяев; born 13 July 1995) is a Russian football player who plays for FC Chelyabinsk. He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Spartak Kostroma on 20 July 2015 in a game against FC Domodedovo Moscow. He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk on 7 July 2019 in a game against FC Mordovia Saransk. In February 2023, FC Rubin Kazan and FC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk agreed on his transfer. His contract with Rubin was terminated by mutual consent on 10 October 2023. "Game Report by PFL".
