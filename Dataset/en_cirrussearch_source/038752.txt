Edgars Vardanjans (Armenian: Էդգար Վարդանյան; born 9 May 1993) is a Latvian former football player who retired due to injury. He made his debut for Latvia national football team on 9 June 2017 in a World Cup qualifier against Portugal. "Latvia - Portugal game report". FIFA. 9 June 2017.
