The Podkamennaya Tunguska (Russian: Подкаменная Тунгуска, literally Tunguska under the stones; Evenki: Дулгу Катэнӈа, Ket: Ӄо’ль) also known as Middle Tunguska or Stony Tunguska, is a river in Krasnoyarsk Krai, Russia. In 1908, an asteroid impacted near the river and later became known as the Tunguska event. The river was the set location in the Call of Duty: Black Ops Escalation DLC map, Call of The Dead.
