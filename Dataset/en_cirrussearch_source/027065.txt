DHL Ecuador (TransAm) Ltda. (Trans Am Aero Express del Ecuador) is a cargo airline based in Guayaquil. It is wholly owned by Deutsche Post and provides services for the group's DHL-branded logistics network in Ecuador. Its main base is José Joaquín de Olmedo International Airport. The airline was established and started operations in 1991 as Trans Am Aero Express del Ecuador.
