Lake Ermistu (Estonian: Ermistu järv; also known as Lake Tõstamaa) is a 449.5 ha (1,111 acres) lake in southwestern Estonia. Administratively it belongs to the Ermistu village in Pärnu municipality, Pärnu County. List of lakes of Estonia Lake Tõhela, another lake nearby "Ermistu järv" (in Estonian).
