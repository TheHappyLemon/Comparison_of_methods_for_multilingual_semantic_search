Pierre de Boulogne was a French sailor who represented his country at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. Pierre de Boulogne and crew, were disqualified in the first race of the 0.5 to 1 ton and did not finish in the second race. He did this with the boat Sidi-Fekkar.
