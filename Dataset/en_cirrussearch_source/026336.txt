Alīda Ābola (born 29 November 1954) is a former Latvian orienteering competitor. She received a bronze medal in the individual event at the 1989 World Orienteering Championships in Skövde, and finished 5th with the Soviet relay team. Now she lives in Sigulda, Latvia.
