Gentiana pneumonanthe, the marsh gentian, is a species of the genus Gentiana. It was the first wildflower announced as flower of the year in Germany in 1980. The species can be found in marshes and moorlands. It is the host-plant of the Alcon blue (Phengaris alcon). Wikimedia Commons has media related to Gentiana pneumonanthe. BSBI List 2007 (xls).
