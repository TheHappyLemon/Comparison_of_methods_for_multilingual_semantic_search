Renāte Lāce (18 February 1943 – 3 March 1967) was a Latvian track and field athlete. At the 1963 Summer Universiade, she won the gold medal at the in the 100 metres event and the silver medal in the 200 metres. She won a bronze medal with the Soviet 4 x 100 metres team at the 1966 European Championships. Lāce competed at the 1964 Summer Olympics and finished in fourth place in the 4 x 100 metres relay.
