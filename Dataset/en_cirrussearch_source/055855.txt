Auguste Jean Baptiste Louis Joseph Marc (born 27 July 1880, date of death unknown) was a French swimmer. He competed in two swimming events and the water polo at the 1900 Summer Olympics, winning a bronze medal in the latter. List of Olympic medalists in water polo (men) "Louis Marc". Olympedia.
