The 2019 Gree-Tour of Guangxi was a road cycling stage race that took place between 17 and 22 October 2019 in the Chinese province of Guangxi. It was the 3rd edition of the Tour of Guangxi and the thirty-eighth and final event of the 2019 UCI World Tour. Eighteen teams, including fifteen of the eighteen UCI WorldTour teams and three UCI Professional Continental teams, participated in the race. Each team entered seven riders, except Mitchelton–Scott which entered six riders and Team Jumbo–Visma which entered five riders. Of the 123 riders that started the race, 118 finished.
