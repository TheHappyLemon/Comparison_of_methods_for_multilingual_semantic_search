Manuel Feller (born 13 October 1992) is an Austrian World Cup alpine ski racer. Feller specializes in the technical events of slalom and giant slalom, and made his World Cup debut in November 2012. In 2024, Feller won his first World Cup disciple title in slalom. Standings through 17 March 2024 6 wins – (6 SL) 24 podiums – (17 SL, 7 GS); 72 top tens At the World Junior Championships in 2012, Feller finished 27th in the downhill, 14th in the giant slalom, and failed to finish the slalom. The next year in Quebec, Feller won the gold medal in the slalom, ahead of medalists Ramon Zenhaeusern and Santeri Paloniemi, and was ninth in the giant slalom.
