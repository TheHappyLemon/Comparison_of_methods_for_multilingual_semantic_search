Sēlpils Parish (Latvian: Sēlpils pagasts) is an administrative territorial entity of Jēkabpils Municipality in the Selonia region of Latvia. Prior to 2009, it was an administrative unit of Jēkabpils District. The administrative center is the village of Sēlija.
