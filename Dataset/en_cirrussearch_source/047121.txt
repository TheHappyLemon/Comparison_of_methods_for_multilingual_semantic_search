The 2024 IIHF World Championship will be hosted by Czechia from 10 to 26 May 2024. This decision was made at the semi-annual International Ice Hockey Federation (IIHF) congress. Officially, this was announced on 12 September 2020 by the IIHF, delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
