Aclopinae is a subfamily of Scarabaeidae or scarab beetles in the superfamily Scarabaeoidea. The subfamily is found in northern Australia, Borneo and southern South America (Brazil and Argentina). These 11 genera belong to the subfamily Aclopinae.
