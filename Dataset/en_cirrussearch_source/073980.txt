Zintis Ekmanis (born 17 May 1958) is a former Latvian bobsledder who competed from the early 1980s to the mid-1990s. Competing in four Winter Olympics, he won the bronze medal in the two-man event at Sarajevo in 1984. Ekmanis also won a bronze medal in the two-man event at the 1985 FIBT World Championships in Cervinia.
