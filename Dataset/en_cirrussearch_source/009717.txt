Year 1195 (MCXCV) was a common year starting on Sunday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. June 1 – Battle of Shamkor: Georgians defeat the Ildenizids of Azerbaijan. July 18 – Battle of Alarcos: Almohad ruler Abu Yusuf Ya'qub al-Mansur decisively defeats Castilian King Alfonso VIII. The Priory of St Mary's is founded in Bushmead. Alexius III Angelus overthrows Isaac II, and becomes Byzantine Emperor.
