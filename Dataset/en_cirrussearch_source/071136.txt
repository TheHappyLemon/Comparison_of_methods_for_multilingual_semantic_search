The 77th annual Cannes Film Festival is an upcoming film festival due to take place from 14 to 25 May 2024. American filmmaker and actress Greta Gerwig will serve as jury president for the main competition. French actress Camille Cottin will host the opening and closing ceremonies. Greta Gerwig, American actress and filmmaker - Jury President Xavier Dolan, Canadian actor and filmmaker - Jury President Lukas Dhont, Belgian filmmaker - Jury President Nicolas Philibert, French filmmaker and actor - Jury President The following films were selected to be screened out of competition: Expected world premieres, in competition or in sidebars sections, include: Clint Eastwood's final film Juror No. 2, Francis Ford Coppola's Megalopolis, Paolo Sorrentino's Parthenope, Audrey Diwan's Emmanuelle, Ali Abbasi's The Apprentice, Andrea Arnold's Bird, Joshua Oppenheimer's The End and David Cronenberg's The Shrouds.
