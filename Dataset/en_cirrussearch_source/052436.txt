In breastfeeding, lactation failure may refer to: Primary lactation failure, a cause of low milk supply in breastfeeding mothers Cessation of breastfeeding before the mother had planned to stop, usually as a result of breastfeeding difficulties Low milk supply in general Lactation failure can result in neonatal jaundice.
