The grey-crested tit (Lophophanes dichrous) is a species of bird in the family Paridae. It is found in the Himalayan foothills and southern-central China. Its natural habitats are temperate forest and subtropical or tropical moist montane forest. BirdLife International (2016). "Lophophanes dichrous".
