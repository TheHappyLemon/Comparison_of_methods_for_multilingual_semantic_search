Turkey competed at the 1936 Summer Olympics in Berlin, Germany. 48 competitors, 46 men and 2 women, took part in 26 events in 7 sports. Turkey won its first-ever Olympic medals at these games. First Round First consolation round Four cyclists, all male, represented Turkey in 1936 Individual road race Kazım Bingen Kirkor Canbazyan Orhan Suda Talat Tunçalp Team road race Kazım Bingen Kirkor Canbazyan Orhan Suda Talat Tunçalp Seven fencers, five men and two women, represented Turkey in 1936.
