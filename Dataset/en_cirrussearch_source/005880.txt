Melngailis (feminine: Melngaile) is a Latvian surname. Individuals with the surname include: Emilis Melngailis (1874–1954), Latvian composer. Sarma Melngailis (born 1972), Latvian-American former businesswoman. Tenis Melngailis (1912–1980), Latvian chess player. This page lists people with the surname Melngailis.
