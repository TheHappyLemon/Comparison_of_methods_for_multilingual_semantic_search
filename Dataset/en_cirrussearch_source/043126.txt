The 3rd Women's World Chess Championship took place during the 4th Chess Olympiad in Prague between 12-26 July 1931. The tournament was played as a double round-robin tournament. Vera Menchik successfully defended her title. The final results were as follows: Stanisław Gawlikowski Olimpiady szachowe 1924 - 1974 Wyd.
