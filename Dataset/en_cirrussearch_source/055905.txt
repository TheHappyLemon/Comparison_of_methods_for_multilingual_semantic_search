Ernest Martin (born 1878, date of death unknown) was a French freestyle swimmer and water polo player. He competed in the men's 4000 metre freestyle event and the water polo at the 1900 Summer Olympics. "L'Amicale des Internationaux Français de Natation". French Swimming Federation. 2010.
