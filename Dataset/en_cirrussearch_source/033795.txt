Teruo Abe (安部 輝雄, Abe Teruo) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Abe played for Kwangaku Club was consisted of his alma mater Kwansei Gakuin University players and graduates. He played with many Japan national team players Yukio Goto, Hideo Sakai and so on. In May 1934, Abe was selected Japan national team for 1934 Far Eastern Championship Games in Manila. At this competition, on 13 May, he debuted against Dutch East Indies.
