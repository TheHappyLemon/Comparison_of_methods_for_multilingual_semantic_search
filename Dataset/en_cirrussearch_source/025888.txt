53 (fifty-three) is the natural number following 52 and preceding 54. It is the 16th prime number. Fifty-three is the 16th prime number. It is also an Eisenstein prime, an isolated prime, a balanced prime and a Sophie Germain prime. The sum of the first 53 primes is 5830, which is divisible by 53, a property shared by only a few other numbers. 53 cannot be expressed as the sum of any integer and its decimal digits, making 53 a self number. 53 is the smallest prime number that does not divide the order of any sporadic group.
