Hillary Scott is an American former pornographic actress. She won the 2007 XRCO, AFWG, and AVN Female Performer of the Year Awards and the NightMoves and AVN Award for Best Actress. In 2016, she was inducted into the XRCO Hall of Fame. In 2024, she was inducted into the AVN Hall of Fame. Hillary Scott and Sex Z Pictures parted ways just a little over two years into a five-year exclusive performing / directing contract, and she was represented by LA Direct Models.
