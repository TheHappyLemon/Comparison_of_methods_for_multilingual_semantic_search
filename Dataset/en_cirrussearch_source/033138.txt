Man entering a room (Latvian: Cilvēks, kas ieiet istabā) is a Cubist painting from 1927 by Latvian painter Niklāvs Strunke. The painting is oil on canvas, with dimensions 92 x 86 centimeters. The painting belongs to the Latvian National Museum of Art in Riga. The picture depicts a man who goes through a door beside him seen a small table and a light source in a half-empty room.
