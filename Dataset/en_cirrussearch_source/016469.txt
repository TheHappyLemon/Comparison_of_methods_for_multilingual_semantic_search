Dmitri Viktorovich Meleshko (Belarusian: Дзміртый Віктаравіч Мялешка; born November 8, 1982) is a Belarusian former professional ice hockey winger who most notably played for HC Dinamo Minsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).
