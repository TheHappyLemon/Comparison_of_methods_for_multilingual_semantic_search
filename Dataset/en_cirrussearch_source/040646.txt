Rasma Garne (born 26 March 1941) is a Latvian stage and film actress. She has performed roles in film including Mēs esam četri (1972), Ja nebūtu šī skuķa (1980), Lietus blūzs (1982), Zītaru dzimta (1989), and Depresija (1991).
