Ebbe Schwartz (3 May 1901 – 19 October 1964) was a Danish football administrator. From 1950 until 1964 he was president of the Danish Football Association (the DBU). He served as UEFA President (1954–1962). From 1962 until 1964 he was a member of the executive committee and vice-president of the world football federation FIFA. "DBU's formænd" (in Danish).
