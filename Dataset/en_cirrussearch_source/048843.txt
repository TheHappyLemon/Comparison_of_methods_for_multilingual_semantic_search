The Humorist (Russian: Юморист, Jumorist) is a 2019 drama film directed and written by Mikhail Idov. The film is set in the mid-80s. In the center of the plot is a successful Soviet humorist Boris Arkadyev, who, despite universal love and recognition, lacks creative freedom, as a result of which he becomes dangerous for society.
