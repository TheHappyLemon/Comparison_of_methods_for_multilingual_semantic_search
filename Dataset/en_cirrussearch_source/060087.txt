Pavel Karnaukhov (Russian: Павел Андреевич Карнаухов; born 15 March 1997) is a Belarusian-born Russian professional ice hockey forward for CSKA Moscow of the Kontinental Hockey League. He was selected in the fifth round, 136th overall, in the 2015 NHL Entry Draft by the Calgary Flames.
