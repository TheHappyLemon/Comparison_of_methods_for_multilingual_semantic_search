Novohrodivka (Ukrainian: Новогро́дівка, pronounced [no.woˈɦrɔ.d⁽ʲ⁾iu̯.kɐ]; Russian: Новогродовка) is a city in Pokrovsk Raion of Donetsk Oblast (province) of Ukraine. Population: 14,037 (2022 estimate); 17,473 (2001).
