.sb is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Solomon Islands. It is administered through the Council of Country Code Administrators (CoCCA). As of 12 February 2016 registering domains directly at the second level is allowed. Second-level domains are manually reviewed and must be approved by the NIC before they're added to the zone. Unrestricted global registration: com.sb net.sb Restricted registration: edu.sb (educational institutions) org.sb (nonprofit organisations) gov.sb (government agencies or departments) "Council of Country Code Administrators (CoCCA)".
