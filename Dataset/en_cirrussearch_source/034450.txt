Döbern (German pronunciation: [ˈdøːbɐn] ; Lower Sorbian Derbno) is a town in the district of Spree-Neiße, in Lower Lusatia, Brandenburg, Germany. It is situated 25 km southeast of Cottbus, and 15 km south of Forst (Lausitz). From 1815 to 1947, Döbern was part of the Prussian Province of Brandenburg.
