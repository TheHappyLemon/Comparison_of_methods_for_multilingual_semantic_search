Alexandru Curtianu (born 11 February 1974) is a Moldovan professional football manager and former player who is currently in charge of Russian club Atom Novovoronezh. He made his professional debut in the Soviet First League in 1991 for Zimbru Chişinău. Scores and results list Moldova's goal tally first.
