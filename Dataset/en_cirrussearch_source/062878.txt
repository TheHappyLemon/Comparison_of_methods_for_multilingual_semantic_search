Ana Mena Rojas (born 25 February 1997) is a Spanish singer and actress. She began her career in 2009 in the TV series Marisol, la película (2009). In 2010, she participated in Disney's musical reality show My Camp Rock 2, ending up as the winner. Her musical career began in 2016, when she released her debut single "No Soy Como Tú Crees". Mena has also collaborated with many international artists. In 2018, she gained popularity in Italy with the song D'estate non Vale with Fred De Palma. The unexpected Italian success was a game changer in her career. Since then, she managed to deliver several hit songs both in Italy and Spain, becoming one of the most listened Spanish artists of the streaming era. Hugely popular in Spain and Portugal, Ana Mena has also gained a significant fanbase in France, Italy, Romania, Greece, Malta and Egypt. According to her record label, as of 2024, she was credited for sales equivalent to 1 Diamond cetification in France, besides 55 Platinum and 10 Gold certifications in between Italy and Spain. Two of Ana Mena's recent singles, "Música Ligera" and "Un Clásico", are covers of Italian pop songs: "Musica leggerissima" by Colapesce and Dimartino and "Superclassico" by Ernia, respectively.
