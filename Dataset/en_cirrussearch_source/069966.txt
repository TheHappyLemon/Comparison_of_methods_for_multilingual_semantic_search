Roman Rees (born 1 March 1993) is a German biathlete. His greatest success is winning the silver medal with the German relay team at the Biathlon World Championships 2019. On 26 November 2023 he won his first World Cup victory in the individual race, making him the first German World Cup leader since Michael Greis in 2008.
