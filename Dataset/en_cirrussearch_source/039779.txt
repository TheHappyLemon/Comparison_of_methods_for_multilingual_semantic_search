Eduards Tīdenbergs (born 18 December 1994) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder for FK Liepāja and the Latvia national team. Tīdenbergs made his international debut for Latvia on 7 October 2020 in a friendly match against Montenegro, which finished as a 1–1 away draw.
