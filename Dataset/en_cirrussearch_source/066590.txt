Roseyka (Ukrainian: Росєйка, Roseyka; Russian: Рассейка, Rasseyka) is a health-spa zone within the territory of Prymorske, Lyman rural hromada, Bilhorod-Dnistrovskyi Raion, Odesa Oblast, Odesa Oblast, Ukraine. More than 50 guest-houses are located within the territory of Roseyka. The resort is connected to the shore of the Black Sea by wooden bridges across the lake.
