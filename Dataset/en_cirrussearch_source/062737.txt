Pierre Gervais (21 September 1870 – 2 August 1960) was a French sailor and Olympic champion. Gervais competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics, where he won first prize in one of the two races in the 0-½ ton class, and obtained a third place in the other race. Stéphane Gachet JO d'été.
