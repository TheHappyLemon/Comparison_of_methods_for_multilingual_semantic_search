Ryota Moriwaki (森脇 良太, Moriwaki Ryōta, born 6 April 1986) is a Japanese football player who plays for Ehime FC. He was selected in the national squad for the 2011 Asian Cup finals as a late replacement for injured Gotoku Sakai.
