The Polish Olympic Committee (Polish: Polski Komitet Olimpijski, PKOl) is the National Olympic Committee representing Poland. The Polish Olympic Committee was established on 12 October 1919 and in 1919 was recognised by the International Olympic Committee. The Polish National Federations are the organizations that coordinate all aspects of their individual sports. They are responsible for training, competition and development of their sports.
