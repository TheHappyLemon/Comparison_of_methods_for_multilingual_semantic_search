Hať (formerly Haš; German: Haatsch) is a municipality and village in Opava District in the Moravian-Silesian Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 2,600 inhabitants. It is part of the historic Hlučín Region. Paul Billik (1891–1926), German fighter pilot "Population of Municipalities – 1 January 2023". Czech Statistical Office. 2023-05-23.
