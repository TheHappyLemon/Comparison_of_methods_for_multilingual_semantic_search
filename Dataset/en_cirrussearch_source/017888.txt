The ESWE Verkehrsgesellschaft (German for ESWE Transport Company) or ESWE Verkehr is a municipally owned company responsible for operating public transport in Wiesbaden, Germany. It operates 40 bus-lines and 9 night bus-lines The company is a member of the Rhein-Main-Verkehrsverbund (RMV). ESWE web site (German language) ESWE web site (English language subset) v t e
