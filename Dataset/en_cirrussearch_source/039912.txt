Kevin Kauber (born 23 March 1995) is an Estonian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Vaprus. In 2018 he joined Welsh Premier League club The New Saints and left the club in August 2018 to play first-team football in Finland. Kauber started his international youth career in 2010. He was called up to the senior national team squad for a friendly against Andorra on 1 June 2016, but didn't make an appearance.
