Patimat Bagomedova (Azerbaijani: Patimət Bəhəmmədova; born August 6, 1993) is an Azerbaijani wrestler who participated at the 2010 Summer Youth Olympics in Singapore. She won the gold medal in the girls' freestyle 52 kg event, defeating Yuan Yuan of China in the final. "Wrestling – Women's Freestyle 52 kg – Competition Sheet (With Results)" (PDF).
