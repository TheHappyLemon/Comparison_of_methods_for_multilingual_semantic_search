Maurice Durquetty (Basque: Maurizio Durquety) was a French pelotari who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Durquetty competed in the only official pelota contest in Olympic history, the Basque pelota at the 1900 Summer Olympics two-man teams event.
