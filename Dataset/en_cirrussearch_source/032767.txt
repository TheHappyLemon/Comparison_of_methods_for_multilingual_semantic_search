Nogale Manor (Latvian: Nogales muižas pils) is a manor house in Neo-Classicism style in Ārlava parish, in the Talsi municipality of the historical region of Courland, western Latvia. It was built around 1880 according to the project by the architect T. Seiler. List of palaces and manor houses in Latvia Latvijas Piļu un Muižu asociācija. "Nogales muiža".
