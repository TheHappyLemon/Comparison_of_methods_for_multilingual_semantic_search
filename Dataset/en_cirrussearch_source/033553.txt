Osamu Maeda (前田 治, Maeda Osamu, born September 5, 1965) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Maeda was born in Fukuoka on September 5, 1965. After graduating from Tokai University, he joined All Nippon Airways (later Yokohama Flügels) in 1988. In First season, the club won the 2nd place and he was selected Best Eleven. In 1993, the club won Emperor's Cup. In Asia, the club won 1994–95 Asian Cup Winners' Cup and 1995 Asian Super Cup. He retired in 1996. On January 27, 1988, Maeda debuted for Japan national team against United Arab Emirates. He played as regular player, and also played at 1990 World Cup qualification. He played 14 games and scored 6 goals for Japan until 1989.
