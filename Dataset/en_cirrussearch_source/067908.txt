Zavitinsk (Russian: Завити́нск) is a town and the administrative center of Zavitinsky District in Amur Oblast, Russia. Population: 11,481 (2010 Census); 14,248 (2002 Census); 21,838 (1989 Census). Within the framework of administrative divisions, Zavitinsk serves as the administrative center of Zavitinsky District. As an administrative division, it is, together with three rural localities, incorporated within Zavitinsky District as Zavitinsk Urban Settlement. As a municipal division, this administrative unit also has urban settlement status and is a part of Zavitinsky Municipal District.
