Noriko Ishibashi (石橋 紀子, Ishibashi Noriko, born May 5, 1970) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Ishibashi was born on May 5, 1970. In December 1989, she was selected Japan national team for 1989 AFC Championship. At this competition, on December 24, she debuted and scored a goal against Nepal.
