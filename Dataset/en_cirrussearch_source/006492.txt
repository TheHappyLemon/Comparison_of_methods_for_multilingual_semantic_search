.mg is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for the Republic of Madagascar. Registrations are taken directly at the second level, or at the third level beneath the following second level names: .org.mg: Organizations .nom.mg: Individuals .gov.mg: Government entities .prd.mg: Research projects or programs .edu.mg: Educational institutions .mil.mg: Military entities .tm.mg: Trademarks .com.mg: Commercial (unrestricted registration) For some registrations, official documents must be shown indicating the legal status of the registrant.
