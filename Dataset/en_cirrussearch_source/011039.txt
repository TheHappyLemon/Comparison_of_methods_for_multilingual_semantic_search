Year 1331 (MCCCXXXI) was a common year starting on Tuesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. September 8 – Stefan Dusan declares himself king of Serbia. September 27 – Battle of Płowce: The German Teutonic Knights and the Poles battle to a draw. The Sieges of Cividale del Friuli and Alicante begin. The Genkō War begins in Japan. Ibn Battuta visits Kilwa. The first recorded outbreak of the Black Death occurs, in the Chinese province of Hubei.
