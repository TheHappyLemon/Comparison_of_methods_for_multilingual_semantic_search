At the 2010 Women's Chess Olympiad she won an individual gold medal thanks to her rating performance of 2511 playing on board three for the Cuban team, which finished fourth. Marrero Lopez competed in the Women's World Chess Championship 2015: she knocked out in the first round Grandmaster Elina Danielian to reach round two, where she was eliminated by International Master Meri Arabidze.
