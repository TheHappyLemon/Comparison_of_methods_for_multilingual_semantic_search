The 1962–63 European Cup was the eighth season of the European Cup, a football competition for European clubs. The competition was won by Milan, who beat two-time defending champions Benfica in the final at Wembley Stadium in London. Milan's victory was the first by an Italian club. Albania entered its champion for the first time this season. 1 Feyenoord beat Servette 3–1 in a play–off to qualify for the first round.
