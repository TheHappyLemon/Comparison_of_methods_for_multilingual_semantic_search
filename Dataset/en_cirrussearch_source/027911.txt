Aleksandr Valeryevich Lazushin (Russian: Александр Валерьевич Лазушин; born 9 April 1988) is a Russian professional ice hockey Goaltender who is currently playing with Kunlun Red Star of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He made his KHL debut in the 2010–11 season, appearing with Lokomotiv Yaroslavl and Metallurg Novokuznetsk. On 2 August 2020, while still having a year to run on his contract with Lokomotiv, Lazushin left the team in order to return for a second stint with Kunlun Red Star.
