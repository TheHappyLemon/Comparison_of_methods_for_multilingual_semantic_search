Kaede Nakamura (中村 楓, Nakamura Kaede, born August 3, 1991) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a defender. She plays for Sanfrecce Hiroshima Regina. She has played for the Japan national team. Nakamura was born in Iwate Prefecture on August 3, 1991. After graduating from high school, she joined Albirex Niigata in 2010. In 2017, Nakamura was selected Japan national team for 2017 Algarve Cup. At this competition, on March 3, she debuted against Iceland. She played 3 games for Japan in 2017.
