Rehna (German pronunciation: [ˈʁeːna]) is a town in the Nordwestmecklenburg district, in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany. It is situated 26 km southeast of Lübeck, and 28 km northwest of Schwerin. It is part of the Hamburg Metropolitan Region. "Bevölkerungsstand der Kreise, Ämter und Gemeinden 2021" (XLS) (in German).
