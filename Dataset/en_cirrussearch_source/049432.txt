Shiori Miyake (三宅 史織, Miyake Shiori, born 13 October 1995) is a Japanese professional footballer who plays for WE League club INAC Kobe Leonessa and the Japan women's national team. Miyake was born in Sapporo on October 13, 1995. After playing JFA Academy Fukushima, she joined INAC Kobe Leonessa in 2013. In 2011, Miyake was part of the Japan U-16 team that won the 2011 AFC U-16 Championship. In 2012, she represented Japan U-17 team at the 2012 U-17 World Cup. On September 11, 2013, she received her first senior team call-up. On September 22, she made her debut in a 2–0 win over Nigeria. In 2018, she played at 2018 Asian Cup and Japan won the championship. She played 17 games for Japan. On 13 June 2023, she was included in Japan's 23-player squad for the FIFA Women's World Cup 2023. As of 17 April 2018.
