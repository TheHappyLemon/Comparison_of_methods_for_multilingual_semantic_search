67 (sixty-seven) is the natural number following 66 and preceding 68. It is an odd number. 67 is: the 19th prime number (the next is 71). a Chen prime. an irregular prime. a lucky prime. the sum of five consecutive primes (7 + 11 + 13 + 17 + 19). a Heegner number. a Pillai prime since 18! + 1 is divisible by 67, but 67 is not one more than a multiple of 18. palindromic in quinary (2325) and senary (1516). a super-prime. (19 is prime) an isolated prime. (65 and 69 are not prime) The atomic number of holmium, a lanthanide. Messier object M67, a magnitude 7.5 open cluster in the constellation Cancer. The New General Catalogue object NGC 67, an elliptical galaxy in the constellation Andromeda. "Car 67", a song by the band Driver 67 Chicago's song "Questions 67 and 68" Elton John's song "Old '67" on The Captain & The Kid CD, (2006) British rap group called 67 Rapper Drake released the song named "Star67" off his album If You're Reading This It's Too Late Sixty-seven is: The registry of the U.S. Navy's aircraft carrier USS John F. Kennedy (CV-67), named after U.S. President John F. Kennedy. The number of the French department Bas-Rhin. The number of counties in Alabama, Florida, and Pennsylvania. The province/traffic code of Zonguldak Province in Turkey. In the U.S., *67 is a common prefix-code for blocking caller ID info on the subsequent call. Buddy Arrington's best-known NASCAR car number. The Ottawa 67's, founded in 1967. Pekka Koskela skated the 1000 metres in 1:07:00 (67 seconds) on 10 November 2007, a world record at the time.
