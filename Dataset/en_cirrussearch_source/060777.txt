Zelenodolsk (Ukrainian: Зеленодольськ, IPA: [zeˌlɛnoˈdɔlʲsʲk]) is a city in Kryvyi Rih Raion, Dnipropetrovsk Oblast, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Zelenodolsk urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 12,692 (2022 estimate). Until 18 July 2020, Zelenodolsk belonged to Apostolove Raion. The raion was abolished in July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Dnipropetrovsk Oblast to seven.
