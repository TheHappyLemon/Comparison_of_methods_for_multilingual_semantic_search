Yusuke Omi (近江 友介, Omi Yusuke, born December 26, 1946) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Omi was born in Tokyo on December 26, 1946. After graduating from Hosei University, he joined Hitachi in 1970. In 1972, the club won Japan Soccer League and Emperor's Cup. In 1973, the club won the 2nd place at Japan Soccer League and Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1974. He played 49 games and scored 8 goals in the league. On August 4, 1970, Omi debuted for Japan national team against Thailand. He also played at 1970 Asian Games. He played 5 games and scored 1 goal for Japan in 1970.
