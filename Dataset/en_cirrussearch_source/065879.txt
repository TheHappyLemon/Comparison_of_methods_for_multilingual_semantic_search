Lovro Kos (born 23 July 1999) is a Slovenian ski jumper. Kos made his FIS Ski Jumping World Cup debut in January 2021 in Willingen. Three years later, on 10 February 2024, he won his first individual World Cup event in Lake Placid. He competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing and won a silver medal in the men's large hill team event, together with his teammates Peter Prevc, Timi Zajc, and Cene Prevc. "Lovro KOS".
