Inga Dauškāne (born 12 April 1980) is a Latvian cross-country skier. She competed at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2011 in Oslo, the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2013 in Val di Fiemme, and at the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi. "Inga Daushkane".
