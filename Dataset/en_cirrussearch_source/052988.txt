Ioannis Vrettos (Greek: Ιωάννης Βρεττός) was a Greek long-distance runner. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Vrettos was one of 17 athletes to start the marathon race. He finished fourth of the nine athletes who completed the race. Vrettos was from Chalandri, Greece. "Ioannis Vrettos". Olympedia. Retrieved 20 December 2020.
