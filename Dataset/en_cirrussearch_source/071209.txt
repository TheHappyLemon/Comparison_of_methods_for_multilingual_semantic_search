Paolo Nicolato (born 21 December 1966) is an Italian football manager, currently in charge of the Latvia national football team. A long-time coach with the youth academy of Chievo, Nicolato was appointed the manager of Lumezzane in the Serie C from 2014 to 2016. In 2016 he was appointed the manager of the Italy U18s, and he was subsequently promoted to the U19s, U20s, and then finally the Italy U21s on 3 July 2019.
