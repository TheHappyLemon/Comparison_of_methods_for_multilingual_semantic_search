Lela Javakhishvili (Georgian: ლელა ჯავახიშვილი; born 23 April 1984) is a Georgian chess player who holds the titles of International master and Woman Grandmaster. She has won the Georgian women's chess championship twice, and competed in the Women's World Chess Championship four times, most recently in 2012 when she made it to the third round.[citation needed] Javakhishvili has seen considerable success in her career, having reached the titles of International Master in 2005, and Woman Grandmaster in 2003.
