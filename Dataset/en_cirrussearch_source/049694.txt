The 2020 IIHF World Championship Division IV was an international ice hockey tournament run by the International Ice Hockey Federation. The tournament would have been held in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan from 3 to 5 March 2020. On 2 March 2020, the tournament was cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Three referees and four linesmen were selected for the tournament.
