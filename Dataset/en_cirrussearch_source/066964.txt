Mathias Trettenes (born November 8, 1993) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey forward who currently plays for HC La Chaux-de-Fonds in the Swiss League (SL). Trettenes played as a youth in Sweden as a part of the junior program Modo Hockey. He began his professional career in his native Norway, playing with the Stavanger Oilers of the GET-ligaen. After five seasons with the Oilers, he returned to Sweden to play the 2016–17 season, with Almtuna IS of the HockeyAllsvenskan. After a single season with Almtuna, Trettenes opted to move to Germany in agreeing to a one-year deal with Krefeld Pinguine of the DEL on June 7, 2017. In the 2018–19 season, his second with the Krefeld, Trettenes contributed with 10 points in 44 games before leaving as a free agent at the conclusion of the year.
