Skrastiņš (feminine: Skrastiņa) is a Latvian topographic surname, derived from the Latvian dialect word for "coast" (skrasts). Individuals with the surname include: Artūrs Skrastiņš (born 1974), Latvian actor Kārlis Skrastiņš (1974–2011), Latvian ice hockey player Keith Skrastiņš (born 1982), Canadian Photographer of Latvian Decent Krastiņš This page lists people with the surname Skrastiņš.
