Austria sent a team to compete at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, China. Singles Team Four Austrian beach volleyball teams had qualified for the Olympics, but the women's team Montagnolli - Swoboda was replaced by the Swiss team Kuhn - Schwer, because of medical reasons.
