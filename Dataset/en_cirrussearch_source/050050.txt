Kommunarka is an urban-type settlement (posyolok) in Sosenskoye Settlement, Novomoskovsky Administrative Okrug, Moscow, Russia. The Kommunarka (Sokolnicheskaya line) station opened in 2019. A mass burial site of the late 1930s, known as the Kommunarka shooting ground or "firing range". is also located in the area.
