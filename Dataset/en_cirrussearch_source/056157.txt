The Kunda is a river in Estonia in Lääne-Viru County. The river is 65.8 km long, and its basin size is 535.9 km2. It discharges into the Gulf of Finland. The river has been heavily modified by human activity. Trout and grayling live in the river. List of rivers of Estonia "Keskkonnaregister 4.7.1.7". register.keskkonnainfo.ee (in Estonian).
