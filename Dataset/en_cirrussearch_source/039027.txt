Krišs Kārkliņš (born 31 January 1996) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a defender or midfielder for Valmiera and the Latvia national team. Kārkliņš made his international debut for Latvia on 6 September 2020 in the UEFA Nations League against Malta, which finished as a 1–1 draw.
