Siklós (Hungarian: Siklósi járás) is a district in southern part of Baranya County, Hungary. Siklós is also the name of the town where the district seat is located. The district is in the Southern Transdanubia Statistical Region. Siklós District borders with Pécs and Bóly districts to the north, Mohács District to the east, the Croatian county of Osijek-Baranja to the south, and Sellye District to the west. The number of inhabited places in Siklós District is 53. The district has 3 towns, 1 large village and 49 villages.
