Mario Maslać (Serbian Cyrillic: Марио Маслаћ; born 9 September 1990) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for China League One club Foshan Nanshi. Maslać made his senior debut with Veternik in the 2008–09 season, before moving to Borac Čačak in the summer of 2009. On 16 June 2017, Maslać signed a one-year contract with Irtysh Pavlodar, with his contract being cancelled by mutual consent on 26 January 2018. He joined Riga for the 2018 season. On 3 March 2024, Maslać joined China League One club Foshan Nanshi. Maslać played for the Serbian national under-21 team at the Valeriy Lobanovskyi Memorial Tournament in 2011.
