Borchgrevinkisen (72°10′S 21°30′E﻿ / ﻿72.167°S 21.500°E﻿ / -72.167; 21.500) is a glacier flowing northward to the west of Taggen Nunatak, at the west end of the Sør Rondane Mountains. It was mapped by Norwegian cartographers in 1957 from air photos taken by U.S. Navy Operation Highjump, 1946–47, and named for Carsten E.
