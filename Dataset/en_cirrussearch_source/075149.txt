The Jānis Daliņš Stadium (Latvian: Jāņa Daliņa stadions) is an multi-purpose stadium owned by Valmiera FC in Valmiera, Latvia. The stadium has a capacity of 1,250. The stadium was named in honour of the Latvian race walker Jānis Daliņš. There are three sports fields, including two basketball courts and a universal field for volleyball and mini-football, along with an eight runway lap.
