Girl in a Folk Costume (Latvian: Meitene tautas tērpā) is a painting by the Latvian painter Jānis Tīdemanis from 1930. The painting is oil on plywood, and has dimensions 59 x 48 centimeters. The painting belongs to the Latvian National Museum of Art in Riga. The picture depicts a young girl with plaits in a stylized folk costume with bright color on a dark background.
