Edward Hore (born 17 November 1849, date of death unknown) was a British sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. Hore took the gold in the second race of the 3 to 10 ton and a Bronze in the 10 to 20 ton. "Edward Hore". Olympedia. Retrieved 30 December 2020.
