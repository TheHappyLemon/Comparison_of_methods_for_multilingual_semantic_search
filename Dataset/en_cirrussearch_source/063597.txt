Members Only, Vol. 3, also referred to by its full title XXXTentacion Presents: Members Only, Vol. 3 on streaming services, is the second mixtape by American hip hop group Members Only. It was released via SoundCloud on June 26, 2017. It is the final Members Only project to be released during XXXTentacion's lifetime. "XXXTENTACION & Crew Release "Members Only Vol. 3" Mixtape".
