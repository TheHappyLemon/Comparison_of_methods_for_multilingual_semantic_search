Ārons Bogoļubovs (Aron Gershevich Bogolyubov, Russian: Арон Гершевич Боголюбов; born 30 December 1938) was a Latvian judoka who competed for the Soviet Union at the 1964 Summer Olympics, where he won a bronze medal in judo in the lightweight class.
