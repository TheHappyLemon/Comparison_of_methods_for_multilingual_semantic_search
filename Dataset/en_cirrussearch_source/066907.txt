The 2027 IIHF World Championship will be hosted by Düsseldorf and Mannheim, Germany, from 14 to 30 May 2027. The only bidders were Kazakhstan and Germany. Norway withdrew its bid in January, four months and a half before the host selection. Austria had also expressed an interest. In the end, on 26 May 2023, the IIHF Congress awarded Germany the rights to host the competition. Two venues will be used. There is potential that Germany's opening game will be at the football stadium, similar to 2010.
