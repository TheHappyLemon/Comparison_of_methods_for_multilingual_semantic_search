Rīgas Balss (Russian: Ригас Балсс) was a tabloid newspaper published in Riga, Latvia, published in Latvian and Russian languages. The Russian edition has turned into Vechernyaya Riga in 2001 and ceased publication in 2004. Latvian edition has turned into journal in 2008 and ceased publication in 2009. "Vecrīga".
