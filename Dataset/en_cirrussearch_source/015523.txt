Marlow is a municipality in the Vorpommern-Rügen district, in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany. It is situated 14 km southeast of Ribnitz-Damgarten. Marlow is well known for the Vogelpark Marlow, a zoo with the focus on birds, in an area of 22 hectares (54 acres).
