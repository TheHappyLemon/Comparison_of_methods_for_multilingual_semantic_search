The E 371 is part of the United Nations international E-road network. Approximately 358 kilometers (222 mi) long, it runs from Radom, Poland south to Prešov, Slovakia.  Poland DK 9: Radom – Iłża – Ostrowiec Świętokrzyski – Opatów – Tarnobrzeg – Rzeszów ( E40) A 4 / S 19: Rzeszów ( E40) S 19: Rzeszów DK 19: Rzeszów – Miejsce Piastowe – Barwinek  Slovakia I/21: Vyšný Komárnik – Svidník – Giraltovce – Lipníky I/18: Lipníky – Prešov European Agreement on Main International Traffic Arteries, 5 April 2002 UN Economic Commission for Europe: Overall Map of E-road Network (2007) Wikimedia Commons has media related to E371. v t e v t e
