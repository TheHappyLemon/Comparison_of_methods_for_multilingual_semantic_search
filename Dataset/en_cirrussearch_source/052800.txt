Spyridon P. "Spyros" Chazapis (Greek: Σπυρίδων "Σπύρος" Χαζάπης; 1872, in Andros – ?) was a Greek swimmer. He competed at the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens. Chazapis competed in the 100 metres freestyle for sailors event. He placed second of the three swimmers. "Spyros Chazapis". Olympedia. Retrieved 21 December 2020.
