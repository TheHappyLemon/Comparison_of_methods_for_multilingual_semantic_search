Kirill Kapustin (Кирилл Капустин ; born February 8, 1993) is a Russian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with Amur Khabarovsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Kapustin made his Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) debut playing with Lokomotiv Yaroslavl during the 2013–14 KHL season. On August 15, 2016, Kapustin ended his tenure with Lokomotiv as he was traded for compensation to fellow KHL team, Amur Khabarovsk. On May 16, 2017, Kapustin was returned to Lokomotiv by Amur in a trade alongside Alexander Yelesin in exchange for Danil Romantsev. After two seasons with Severstal Cherepovets, Kapustin left as a free agent and was signed to a two-year contract with his sixth KHL club, Traktor Chelyabinsk, on 6 May 2021.
