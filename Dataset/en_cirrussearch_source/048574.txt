Kaarel Nurmsalu (born April 30, 1991) is an Estonian former ski jumper and nordic combined skier. He won 3 individual bronze medals at the 2011 Nordic Junior World Ski Championships in Otepää. He holds the national record of Estonia in ski-flying with 213 m. In June 2014, Nurmsalu announced his retirement from competitive sports, citing lack of funds as the reason behind the decision. On 11 January 2017, Nurmsalu announced on his Facebook page that he would be returning to active competition starting with the World Cup event in Wisła on 13 January 2017.
