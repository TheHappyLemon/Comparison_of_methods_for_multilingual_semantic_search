Kunimitsu Sekiguchi (関口 訓充, Sekiguchi Kunimitsu, born 26 December 1985) is a Japanese football player who plays for Nankatsu SC. After the 2017 season, Sekiguchi seemed to have opted for retirement. With a surprise announcement, though, he joined back Vegalta Sendai in April 2018. Sekiguchi made a full international debut for Japan on 8 October 2010 in a friendly against Argentina.
