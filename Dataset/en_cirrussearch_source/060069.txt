Urša Bogataj (born 7 March 1995) is a Slovenian ski jumper. She was the 2022 Olympic champion in the women's normal hill individual event. She made her World Cup debut on 3 December 2011 in Lillehammer, Norway. "Urša Bogataj – Player Profile – Ski Jumping". Eurosport. Retrieved 28 March 2021.
