Hisataka Okamoto (岡本 久敬, Okamoto Hisataka, born December 14, 1933) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Okamoto was born on December 14, 1933. When he was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he won 1953 and 1955 Emperor's Cup at All Kwangaku was consisted of his alma mater Kwansei Gakuin University players and graduates. After graduating from university, he played for Hitachi. On January 2, 1955, when Okamoto was a Kwansei Gakuin University student, he debuted for Japan national team against Burma.
