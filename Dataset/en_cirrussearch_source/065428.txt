Kobroor is an island in the Aru Islands in the Arafura Sea. It is situated in the Maluku Province, Indonesia. Its area is 1723 km². The other main islands in the archipelago are Tanahbesar (also called Wokam), Kola, Maikoor, Koba and Trangan. Islands portal O'Connor, Sue; Spriggs, Matthew; Veth, Peter (2005). "1".
