Katsuyuki Kawachi (河内 勝幸, Kawachi Katsuyuki, born April 27, 1955) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Kawachi was born in Hiroshima Prefecture on April 27, 1955. After graduating from Osaka University of Commerce, he joined his local club, the Toyo Industries (later Mazda) in 1978. Although the club won second place in the 1978 Emperor's Cup, the club performance was not good and he also played in Division 2. He retired in 1987. He played 92 games and scored 11 goals in Division 1. On June 16, 1979, Kawachi debuted for Japan national team against South Korea. He played 3 games for Japan in 1979.
