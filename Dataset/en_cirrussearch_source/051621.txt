Yunus Mallı (born 24 February 1992) is a professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Süper Lig club Konyaspor and the Turkey national team. Born in Germany, and a former German youth international, he plays for the Turkey national team. On 5 January 2017, Mallı joined VfL Wolfsburg signing a 4+1⁄2-year deal. In July 2022, Mallı signed with Kasımpaşa. On August 1, 2023, he signed with Süper Lig club Konyaspor. Mallı has 30 caps for Germany youth teams, including 11 for Germany U21. He made his debut on 11 November 2013, playing the second half in a win against Montenegro U21. On 6 November 2015, Mallı was selected for the Turkey national team to play friendlies against Qatar and Greece respectively. He was part of Euro 2016 squad of the Turkey national football team.
