Aldis Intlers (24 April 1964 in Liepāja – 28 August 1994) was a Latvian-born Soviet bobsledder who competed from the late 1980s to the mid-1990s. He won a bronze medal in the two-man event at the 1989 FIBT World Championships in Cortina d'Ampezzo. Competing in two Winter Olympics, Intlers earned his best finish of 11th in the two-man event at Lillehammer in 1994. Intlers died 28 August 1994 in a car accident, he was 30 years old.
