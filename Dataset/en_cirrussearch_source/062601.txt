Henri Napoléon Marie Maurice Gufflet (15 August 1863 – 22 April 1915) was a French sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. Gufflet took the gold in the first race of the 3 to 10 ton and the silver medal in the second race of that class. "Maurice Gufflet". Olympedia. Retrieved 30 December 2020.
