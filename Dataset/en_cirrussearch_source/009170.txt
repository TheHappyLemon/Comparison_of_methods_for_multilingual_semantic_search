Vilnis Ezerins (Latvian: Vilnis Ezeriņš) (born April 22, 1944) is a former running back in the National Football League (NFL). He was drafted in the eighth round of the 1966 NFL Draft by the Los Angeles Rams and later played with the team during the 1968 NFL season. "Vilnis Ezerins". Pro-Football-Reference.com.
