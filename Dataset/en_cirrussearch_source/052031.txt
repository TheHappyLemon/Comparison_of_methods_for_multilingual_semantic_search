Semyon Sergeyevich Slepakov (Russian: Семён Серге́евич Слепако́в; born 23 August 1979 in Pyatigorsk) is a Russian producer, screenwriter and show-runner. Slepakov was born on August 23, 1979, in Pyatigorsk, Stavropol Krai. He graduated from the Faculty of Foreign Languages of Pyatigorsk State University with specialization in Translation from French. In 2003, he obtained the degree of Candidate of Economic Sciences. Since 2005, he has been living in Moscow. 2010 — Our Russia.
