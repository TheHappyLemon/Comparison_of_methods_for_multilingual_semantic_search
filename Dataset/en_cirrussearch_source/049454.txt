Sonoko Chiba (千葉 園子, Chiba Sonoko, born June 15, 1993) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a midfielder. She plays for AS Harima Albion and has previously been part of the Japan national team. Chiba was born in Osaka on June 15, 1993. After graduating from Himeji Hinomoto College, she joined AS Harima Albion based in Himeji in 2014. On June 2, 2016, Chiba debuted for Japan national team against United States. She played 5 games for Japan until 2017.
