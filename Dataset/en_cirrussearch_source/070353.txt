Futbolo Klubas TransINVEST is a Lithuanian professional football club based in Galinė [lt], Vilnius County. The club competes in the A Lyga, the top flight of Lithuanian football. FK TransINVEST was founded in 2021. It won its first Lithuanian Cup in 2023.
