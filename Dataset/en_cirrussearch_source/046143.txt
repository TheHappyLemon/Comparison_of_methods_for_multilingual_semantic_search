Ksenija Sidorova (born on 18 May 1988 in Riga) is a Latvian accordionist. In 2016, she signed an exclusive recording contract with Deutsche Grammophon. Her first album on the label, Carmen, is a recreation of Georges Bizet's Carmen for accordion that incorporates Latin, Asian, European and North American musical styles.
