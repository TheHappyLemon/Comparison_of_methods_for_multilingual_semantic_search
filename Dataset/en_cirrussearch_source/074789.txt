Krzysztof Łągiewka (born 23 January 1983) is a Polish former professional footballer who played as a defender. In July 2011, he joined Arka Gdynia on a one-year contract. He was a member of Poland's U16 national football team which placed second in the UEFA European U-16 European Championships in 1999. Leo Beenhakker has also called him up twice to the senior Poland national football team but Łągiewka had to pull out on both occasions.
