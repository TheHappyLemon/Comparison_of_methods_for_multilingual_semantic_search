Takeshi Ono (大野 毅, Ono Takeshi, born November 22, 1944) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Ono was born on November 22, 1944. After graduating from Waseda University, he joined Toyo Industries in 1967. The club won league champions 1967, 1968 and 1970. The club also won 1967 and 1969 Emperor's Cup. He retired in 1976. He played 145 games and scored 5 goals in the league. On March 25, 1965, he debuted for Japan national team against Singapore. In 1971, he was selected Japan again.
