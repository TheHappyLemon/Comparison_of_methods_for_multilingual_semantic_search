.et is the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Ethiopia. Ethiotelecom offer top-levels domains as second- and third-level domains. Ethio Telecom, the state-owned telecommunication services provider of Ethiopia, is the registry operator of the domain, and is supported by the registry system developed by ZDNS, an internet infrastructure service provider from China. A number of second-level domains for Ethiopia exist: .com.et, for commercial companies. .gov.et, reserved for governmental organizations. .org.et, for non-profit organizations and non-governmental organizations. .edu.et, reserved for educational institutions. .net.et, for network-related companies. .name.et, for individuals. .mil.et, for the Ethiopian National Defense Force "Domain Name Registration".
