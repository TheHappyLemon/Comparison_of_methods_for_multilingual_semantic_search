Yuria Obara (小原 由梨愛, Obara Yuria, born September 4, 1990) is a Japanese football player who plays for Albirex Niigata. She has previously played for the Japan national team. Obara was born in Towada on September 4, 1990. After graduating from high school, she joined Albirex Niigata in 2009. In July 2010, Obara was selected Japan U-20 national team for 2010 U-20 World Cup. In May 2014, she was selected Japan national team for 2014 Asian Cup. At this competition, on May 18, she debuted against Jordan. Japan won the championship.
