Alexandros Theofilakis (Greek: Αλέξανδρος Θεοφιλάκης, born 1877) was a Greek shooter. Theofilakis competed at the Summer Olympics in 1896, 1908, 1912, 1920 and 1924 Summer Olympics. At the 1906 Intercalated Games, he won a silver medal at 25 m army pistol (standard model) event. In 1920 he won another silver medal, this time in the team 50m army pistol event.
