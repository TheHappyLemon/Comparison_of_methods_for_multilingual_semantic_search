Kazuhisa Kono (河野 和久, Kono Kazuhisa, born December 30, 1950) is a former Japanese football player. He played for the Japan national team. Kono was born in Hiroshima Prefecture on December 30, 1950. After graduating from high school, he joined Hitachi in 1969. In 1972, the club won Japan Soccer League and Emperor's Cup. The club also won 1975 Emperor's Cup and 1976 JSL Cup. He retired in 1980 after having played 113 games in the league. On February 20, 1974, Kono debuted for the Japan national team against Hong Kong.
