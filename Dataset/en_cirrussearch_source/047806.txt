Éva Tófalvi (born December 4, 1978) is a retired Romanian biathlete of Hungarian ethnicity. She finished in 11th place in the Women's Overall World Cup standings and in 2nd place in the Individual event at the end of the 2008-09 Biathlon World Cup. During this season she also won her first ever World Cup event in Hochfilzen, Austria, in the 15 km Individual race, she originally finished second, but winner Albina Akhatova was later disqualified due to use of doping.
