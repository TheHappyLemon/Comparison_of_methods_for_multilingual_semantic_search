Mobina Alinasab (Persian: مبینا علی‌نسب; born 7 August 2000), is an Iranian chess player who holds the title of Woman International Master. In 2016, Mobina Alinasab won Asian Youth Chess Championship and won 2nd place in World Youth Chess Championship in girls U16 age group. In 2017, Mobina Alinasab won Iranian Women's Chess Championship. In 2017, she won Women's World Chess Championship Asian Zonal 3.1 tournament, and qualified for the Women's World Chess Championship 2018. Mobina Alinasab played for Iran in the Women's Chess Olympiads: In 2018, at third board in the 43rd Chess Olympiad (women) in Batumi (+6 =4 -1).
