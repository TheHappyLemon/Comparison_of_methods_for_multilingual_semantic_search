Lahoysk District or Lahojsk District (Belarusian: Лагойскі раён, IPA: [lʌˈɣɔɪskɪ rʌɪon]; Russian: Логойский район) is a district (raion) of Minsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is the city of Lahoysk.
