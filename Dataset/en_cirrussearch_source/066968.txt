Žiga Pavlin (born 30 April 1985) is a Slovenian ice hockey player. He plays for HK Olimpija. He participated at several IIHF World Championships as a member of the Slovenia men's national ice hockey team. In the 2015/2016 season, he was playing for team, HC Slavia Praha in the 1st Czech Republic Hockey League, the second highest league in the country.
