Johan Ferdinand Nyström (April 16, 1874 in Håtuna – September 30, 1968 in Tierp) was a Swedish track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Nyström competed in the marathon. He was one of six runners who failed to finish the event. "Johan Nyström". Olympedia. Retrieved 22 December 2020.
