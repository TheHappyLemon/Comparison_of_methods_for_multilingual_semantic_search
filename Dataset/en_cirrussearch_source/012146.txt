Mārtiņš Raitums (born 14 April 1985) was a Latvian ice hockey goaltender who participated at the 2010 IIHF World Championship as a member of the Latvia men's national ice hockey team. He has played for Arystan Temirtau in the Kazakhstan Hockey Championship league. Five time Latvian champion and two time Kazakh champion. IIHF (2010). IIHF Media Guide & Record Book 2011.
