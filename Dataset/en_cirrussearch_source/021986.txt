Burgstädt (German pronunciation: [ˈbʊɐ̯kʃtɛt]) is a town in the district of Mittelsachsen, in Saxony, Germany. It is situated 12 km northwest of Chemnitz. Erich Gleixner (1920-1962), footballer Peter Jahr (born 1959), politician (CDU) Barbara Köhler (born 1959), lyricist Rico Lieder (born 1971), athlete Gerhard Wahrig (1923-1978), lexicographer Gewählte Bürgermeisterinnen und Bürgermeister im Freistaat Sachsen, Stand: 17.
