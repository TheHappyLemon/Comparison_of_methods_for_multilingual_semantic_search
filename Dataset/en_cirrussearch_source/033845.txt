Koji Sasaki (佐々木 康治, Sasaki Kōji, born January 30, 1936) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Sasaki was born on January 30, 1936. After graduating from Kansai University, he joined Dunlop Japan in 1958. On December 25, 1958, he debuted for Japan national team against Hong Kong.
