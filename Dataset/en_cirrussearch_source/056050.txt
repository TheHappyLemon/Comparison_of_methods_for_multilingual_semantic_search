Darya Blashko (Ukrainian: Дарія Блашко, born 28 January 1996) is a Ukrainian biathlete. She has competed in the Biathlon World Cup since 2019. She has been engaged in biathlon since 2006. Her trainers were Boris Vorobyov, Raisa Vorobyova and Volodymyr Makhlayev. She started her international tournaments carre in 2012. Her first major tournament was the World Junior Championship in 2013 in Obertilliach.
