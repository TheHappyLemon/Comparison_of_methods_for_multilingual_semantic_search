Tereza Voborníková (Czech pronunciation: [ˈtɛrɛza ˈvoborɲiːkovaː]; born 31 May 2000) is a Czech biathlete. She competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics in the Women's pursuit, Women's individual, and Women's sprint. She competed at the Biathlon Junior World Championships 2019, Biathlon Junior World Championships 2022, and 2020–21 Biathlon IBU Cup.
