Gypsy Punks: Underdog World Strike is the third album by Gogol Bordello, released in 2005 by SideOneDummy Records. Production was done by Steve Albini alongside Gogol Bordello. Other songs from the same era and recording sessions appear on the EP East Infection. All lyrics are written by Eugene Hütz; all music is composed by Hütz and Gogol Bordello.
