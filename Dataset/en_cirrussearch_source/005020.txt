The 1988 NHL Entry Draft was the 26th NHL Entry Draft. It was held at the Forum in Montreal, Quebec. The last active player in the NHL from this draft class was Teemu Selanne, who retired after the 2013–14 season. Below are listed the selections in the 1988 NHL Entry Draft. Club teams are located in North America unless otherwise noted.
