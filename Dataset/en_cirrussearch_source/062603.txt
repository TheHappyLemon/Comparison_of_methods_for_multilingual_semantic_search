Charles "Charly" Guiraist (31 May 1887 in Bordeaux – 7 September 1947 in Bordeuax) was a French sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Guiraist took the gold in the first race of the 3 to 10 ton and the silver medal in the second race of that class. Stéphane Gachet JO d'été. Tous les médaillés français de 1896 à nos jours, ed.
