Tomáš Klouček (born March 7, 1980, in Prague, Czechoslovakia) is a Czech former professional ice hockey defenceman. He played in the National Hockey League for the New York Rangers, Nashville Predators and the Atlanta Thrashers between 2000 and 2006. He was drafted 131st overall by the Rangers in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft. Klouček joined Orli Znojmo in the Erste Bank Eishockey Liga after previously playing in the Slovak Extraliga with HC Kosice.
