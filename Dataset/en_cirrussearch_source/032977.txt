Pauls Jonass (born 13 January 1997, in Kalvene, Kalvene Parish, South Kurzeme Municipality) is a Latvian professional motocross racer. He's competed in the Motocross World Championships since 2014 and is the 2017 MX2 World Champion. "Pauls Jonass Profilo". motocrossactionmag.com. Retrieved 11 August 2021.
