Nosivka (Ukrainian: Носівка, pronounced [ˈnɔs⁽ʲ⁾iu̯kɐ]; Russian: Носовка) is a city in Nizhyn Raion, Chernihiv Oblast (province) of Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Nosivka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Population: 12,908 (2022 estimate). Until 18 July 2020, Nosivka was the administrative center of Nosivka Raion. The raion was abolished in July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Chernihiv Oblast to five.
