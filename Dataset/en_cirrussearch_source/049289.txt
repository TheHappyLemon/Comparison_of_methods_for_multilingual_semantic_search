Keiko Saito (斉藤 圭子, Saitō Keiko, born March 24, 1965) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Saito was born on March 24, 1965. In October 1984, she was selected Japan national team for tour for China. On October 17, she debuted for Japan against Italy. She played 3 games for Japan in 1984.
