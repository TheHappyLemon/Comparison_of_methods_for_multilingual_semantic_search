Jakub Krejčík (born June 25, 1991) is a Czech professional ice hockey defenseman who is currently playing with HC Sparta Praha of the Czech Extraliga (ELH). Krejčík first played with HC Slavia Praha in the Czech Extraliga during the 2010–11 season, and later moved to the Swedish Hockey League with Örebro HK. On 8 May 2020, Krejčík was signed to a two-year contract to join Finnish KHL club, Jokerit, starting from the 2020–21 season. On 14 July 2021, Krejčík and Jokerit mutually agreed to a contract termination. He was later signed to continue in the KHL with HC Dinamo Minsk on a one-year contract on 20 August 2021.
