The 2016 Men's World Floorball Championships were the 11th World Championships in men's floorball. The tournament took place in Latvia in December 2016. Qualification events were conducted between 2 and 14 February 2016. The teams will be divided into four pots according to the world ranking. (World rankings shown in parentheses).
