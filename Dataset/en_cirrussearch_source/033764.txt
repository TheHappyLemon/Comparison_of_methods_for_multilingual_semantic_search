Kiyonosuke Marutani (丸谷 清之介, Marutani Kiyonosuke) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Marutani played for Osaka SC was founded by his alma mater high school graduates and many Japan national team players Yoshimatsu Oyama, Toshio Miyaji, Uichiro Hatta and Sakae Takahashi were playing in those days. In May 1925, Marutani was selected Japan national team for 1925 Far Eastern Championship Games in Manila. At this competition, on May 20, he debuted against Republic of China.
