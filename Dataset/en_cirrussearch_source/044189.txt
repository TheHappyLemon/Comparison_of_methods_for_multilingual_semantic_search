Pascal Ackermann (born 17 January 1994) is a German cyclist, who currently rides for UCI ProTeam Israel Premier Tech Academy. Born in Kandel, Ackermann joined the Rad-Net Rose Team team in 2013, and spent four seasons with the team. In August 2016 Bora–Hansgrohe announced that Ackermann would join them for the 2017 season. In May 2019, he was named in the startlist for the Giro d'Italia, where he won the second and fifth stages, and became the first German rider to win the points classification in the Giro d'Italia. In October 2020, he was named in the startlist for the Vuelta a España, where he won the ninth and eighteenth stages.
