The Reiu is a 73 km (45 mi) long river in southwest Estonia. Its source is Lake Soka in Latvia. It flows generally north. It is a left tributary of the Pärnu River, which it flows into near the city of Pärnu. The basin area of the Reiu is 917 km2, and its average discharge 17.3 m³/s.
