Georges Claude Clément was a French track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Clément competed in the 400 metres. He placed fourth in his first-round (semifinals) heat and did not advance to the final. "Georges Clément". Olympedia. Retrieved 22 December 2020.
