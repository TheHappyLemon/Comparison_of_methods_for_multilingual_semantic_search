The Republic (Latvian: Republika), initially known as Republic 2030 (Latvian: Republika 2030), is a centrist political party in Latvia. It was formed in 2021. The party is represented in the 13th Saeima with 4 deputies who were elected from the lists of Social Democratic Party "Harmony" (Vjačeslavs Dombrovskis, Evija Papule) and KPV LV (Kaspars Ģirģens, Ēriks Pucens) in the 2018 parliamentary election. https://bnn.lv/vel-viena-rudens-sene-girgens-un-vjaceslavs-dombrovskis-registre-partiju-389494 Vēl viena rudens sēne.
