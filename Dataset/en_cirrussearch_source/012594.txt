Tracktor Bowling was a Russian nu metal band, formed in Moscow in 1996. The band formed in 1996. In 2000, their demo-track record "Mutatsiya" ("Mutation") gained early success and netted them a budding reputation as leaders of the Moscow alternative rock scene. Their debut album was released in 2002. The band released five albums, one Live-album, one DVD and some singles.
