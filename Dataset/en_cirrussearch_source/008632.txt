Jevgeņijs Borodavko (born 4 November 1986) is a Latvian judoka. Born in Riga. By finishing 7th at the 2007 European Judo Championships, he qualified for the 2008 Summer Olympics, losing to Roberto Meloni in the first round of the middleweight (‍–‍90 kg) competition. Borodavko also qualified for the 2012 Summer Olympics, having moved to the half-heavyweight (‍–‍100 kg) division. There he beat Anthony Liu of American Samoa before losing to Dimitri Peters.
