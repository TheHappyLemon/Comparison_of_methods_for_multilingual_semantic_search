Aydan Hojjatova (Azerbaijani: Aydan Hikmət qızı Höccətova; born 6 April 1999) is an Azerbaijani chess player. Hojjatova began playing chess at an early age. She earned the FIDE titles Woman Candidate Master (WCM) in 2009, Woman FIDE Master (WFM) in 2015, and Woman Grandmaster (WGM) in 2018.
