Ingars Sarmis Stuglis (born 12 February 1996) is a Latvian international footballer who plays for Norwegian side Hammerfest Stein, as a midfielder. Born in Aizkraukle, he has played club football for Skonto FC, FS METTA/Latvijas Universitāte and FK Spartaks Jūrmala.
