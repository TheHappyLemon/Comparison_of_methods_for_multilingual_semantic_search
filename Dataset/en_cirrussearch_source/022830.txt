Aarzemnieki (Latvian: [ˈaːrzæmniɛki]; an internet style writing of the Latvian word "ārzemnieki", meaning "foreigners") are a Latvian band that represented Latvia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Copenhagen, Denmark, with their song "Cake to Bake". The lead singer of the band, Jöran Steinhauer, is German. Roxburgh, Gordon (22 February 2014). "Aarzemnieki takes the honours in Latvia". Eurovision.tv. Retrieved 22 February 2014.
