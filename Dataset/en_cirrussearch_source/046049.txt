Vladislav Dimitrievich Kartaev (Картаев, Владислав Дмитриевич ; born February 10, 1992) is a Russian ice hockey player. He is currently an unrestricted free agent who most recently played with Salavat Yulaev Ufa of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).
