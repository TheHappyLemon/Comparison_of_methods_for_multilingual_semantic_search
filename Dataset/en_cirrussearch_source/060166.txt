Renārs Krastenbergs (born 26 December 1998) is a Latvian professional ice hockey player for AIK of the HockeyAllsvenskan (Allsv) and the Latvian national team. Krastenbergs represented Latvia at the 2021 IIHF World Championship. He represented Latvia at the 2023 IIHF World Championship where he won a bronze medal, Latvia's first ever IIHF World Championship medal.
