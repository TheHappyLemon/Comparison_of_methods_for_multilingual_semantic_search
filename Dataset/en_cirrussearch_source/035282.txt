Cristian Damián Torres (Latvian: Kristians Damians Torress; born 18 June 1985) is a professional football player who last played for Liepāja. Born in Argentina, he played for the Latvia national team. During the summer of 2012, Torres moved from Gabala to Ravan Baku. 18-months later, Torres signed for Qarabağ for six-months before re-signing with Ravan Baku in the summer of 2013. In March 2014, Torres signed for Liepāja.
