Ruvins Vitenbergs (Janury 8, 1868 – December 1, 1931) was a Latvian merchant and politician of a Jewish descent. He was a deputy of first and second Saeima, and served in Daugavpils city council. He had never spoken from the rostrum as a Member of the Saeima. Vitenberg was a member of political party Agudas Israel in first and second Saeima. "1. Saeimas deputāti". saeima.lv (in Latvian).
