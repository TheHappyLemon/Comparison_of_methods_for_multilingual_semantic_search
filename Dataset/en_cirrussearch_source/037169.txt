Yuichi Maruyama (丸山 祐市, Maruyama Yuichi, born 16 June 1989) is a Japanese football player who plays for Kawasaki Frontale. After gaining some playing-time with FC Tokyo and Shonan Bellmare, he has been called by Japan in August 2015. On 4 July 2018, Nagoya Grampus announced the signing of Maruyama. Maruyama has been called for some training camps, but in the end he received his first call-up on 26 May 2016 to face the 2016 Kirin Cup. Updated to 19 July 2022.
