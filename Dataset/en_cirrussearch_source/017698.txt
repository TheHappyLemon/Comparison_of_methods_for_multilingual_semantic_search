Malaryta District (Belarusian: Маларыцкі раён; Russian: Малоритский район) is a district (raion) of Brest Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is Malaryta. According to the 2009 Belarusian census, Malaryta District had a population of 25,780; 88.3% identified themselves as Belarusian, 7.2% as Ukrainian and 3.7% as Russian; 56.6% spoke Belarusian and 38.1% Russian as their native language.
