Year 1130 (MCXXX) was a common year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. January 22 – Jin–Song Wars: Jin forces take Hangzhou. February 4 – Jin–Song Wars: Jin forces take Shaoxing. February 14 – Pope Innocent II succeeds Pope Honorius II, as the 164th pope. Other factions (including Roger II of Sicily), however, support Anacletus II as pope, leading to the papal schism of 1130, and Innocent flees to France. March 26 – Magnus IV and his uncle Harald Gille become joint kings of Norway, starting the civil war era in Norway. April 24 – Jin–Song Wars: Battle of Huangtiandang – Naval forces of the Song Dynasty trap Wuzhu's Jin troops in the city for 48 days. December 25 – Antipope Anacletus crowns Roger II of Sicily king. Approximate date – Magnus the Strong is deposed as king of Götaland, when Sverker the Elder proclaims himself king of Sweden.
