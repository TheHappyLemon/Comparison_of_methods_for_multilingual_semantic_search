Alexander Tyshchenko (born 2 March 1986), better known as Alex Luna, is a Ukrainian countertenor. Luna graduated from the Kyiv Glier Institute of Music in 2005. In 2003, Luna was cast for one of the leading parts in the Ukrainian musical Equator. His first album Svet Luny (Moon Light) and his third video were presented in Kyiv City Hall. After Claudio Simonetti heard Luna's voice, he went to Kyiv to meet the singer in person. Luna performs with the children's chorus at the annual New Year's Eve ball in Kyiv every year. In 2009 Luna received the Person of the Year award. He also appeared on several magazine covers such as 10 Days magazine, What's On, and Gallery magazine. In September 2011, Luna took part in the Anti-AIDS campaign organized by the Ukrainian Ministry of Healthcare. He performed his single Heavens with Vlada Crystal at the event held as part of the campaign.
