Branislav Konrád (born 10 October 1987) is a Slovak professional ice hockey goaltender for HC Olomouc of the Czech Extraliga and Slovakia. He previously played for HC Dukla Trenčín and HK Nitra in the Slovak Extraliga, and Metallurg Zhlobin in the Belarusian Extraleague. "HC Olomouc | Posily do brány i útoku!
