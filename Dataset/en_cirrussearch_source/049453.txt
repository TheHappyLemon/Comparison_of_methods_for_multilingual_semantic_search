Mayu Sasaki (佐々木 繭, Sasaki Mayu, born January 12, 1993) is a Japanese football player who plays for WE League club Urawa Reds. She has previously played for Japan national team. Sasaki was born in Sagamihara on January 12, 1993. After graduating from Musashigaoka College, she joined Vegalta Sendai (later Mynavi Vegalta Sendai) in 2013. In 2018, she moved to Urawa Reds. On June 2, 2016, Sasaki debuted for Japan national team against United States. She played 8 games for Japan until 2017.
