Friedrich Lüthi (19 December 1850 – 16 March 1913 in Geneva) was a Swiss sport shooter who competed in the late 19th century and early 20th century. He participated in Shooting at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris and won a gold medal with the Military pistol team for Switzerland. "Friedrich Lüthi". Retrieved 15 November 2020.
