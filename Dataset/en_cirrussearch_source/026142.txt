Nokia 6136 is a mobile telephone handset produced by Nokia. It features Generic Access Network (GAN), a technology formerly known as Unlicensed Mobile Access (UMA). Quad band GSM / GPRS / EDGE: GSM 850/900/1800/1900 Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11b/g) Generic Access Network (automatic switching between WLAN and cellular networks) 1.3 megapixel camera USB (mini USB) IrDA microSD Stereo FM radio with Visual Radio Push to Talk over Cellular (PoC) Runs the Series 40 interface Official Nokia 6136 page v t e
