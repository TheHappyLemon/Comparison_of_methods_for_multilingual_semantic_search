Oksana Kukhta (Ukrainian: Оксана Кухта, née Herhel (Гергель), born 20 June 1994 in Ivanychi) is a Ukrainian freestyle wrestler of Spartak sports club. She is 2015 World Champion and silver medalist of the European Championships 2016. In May 2016, she was provisionally suspended due to use of meldonium. Later that decision was reverted.
