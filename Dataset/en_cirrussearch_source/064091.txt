Serhii Volodymyrovych Khlan (born 29 June 1972; Ukrainian: Сергій Володимирович Хлань) is a Ukrainian politician. He was born in Snihurivka, Mykolaiv Oblast. In 2020, Khlan became a member of the Kherson Oblast Council for Petro Poroshenko's party European Solidarity. He left the party in 2023. "Нові обличчя Верховної Ради: Сергій Хлань".
