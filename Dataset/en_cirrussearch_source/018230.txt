Sala Parish (Latvian: Salas pagasts) is a territorial unit of Jēkabpils Municipality in the Selonia region of Latvia. From 2009 until 2021, it was part of the former Sala Municipality. Prior to 2009, it was part of the former Jēkabpils District.
