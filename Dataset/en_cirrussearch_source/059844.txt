Émile Henri Delchambre (3 December 1875 in Roubaix – 8 September 1958 in Roubaix) was a French rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the French boat Cercle de l'Aviron Roubaix, which won the gold medal in the coxed fours. "Emile Delchambre". databaseOlympics.com. Archived from the original on 29 September 2007.
