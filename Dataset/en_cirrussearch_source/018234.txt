Sauka Parish (Latvian: Saukas pagasts) is an administrative unit of Jēkabpils Municipality in the Selonia region of Latvia. Prior to 2009, it was an administrative unit of the former Jēkabpils District. The administrative center is Lone, Latvia [lv] village.
