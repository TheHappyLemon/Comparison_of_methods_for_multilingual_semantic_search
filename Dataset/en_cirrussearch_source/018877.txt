Denis Sergeyevich Kokarev (Russian: Денис Серге́евич Кокарев; born June 17, 1985) is a Russian professional ice hockey player who is currently playing for Dynamo MO of the Supreme Hockey League (VHL). After 7 seasons within the rebranded HC Dynamo Moscow from his tenure with HC MVD, Kokarev while still contracted was granted free agent status from the KHL following the 2016–17 season, due to the club's debt on July 4, 2017. With Dynamo unwilling to offer an improved contract, Kokarev left to sign a two-year contract with Salavat Yulaev Ufa the following day on July 5, 2017.
