The Romanian Olympic and Sports Committee (Romanian: Comitetul Olimpic și Sportiv Român, COSR) is responsible for Romania's participation in the Olympic Games. The Romanian Olympic Committee (Romanian: Comitetul Olimpic Român) was formed in 1914 in Bucharest. In 2004 it changed its name to Romanian Olympic and Sports Committee.
