Aleksandrs Ņiživijs (born September 16, 1976) is a former Latvian professional ice hockey player. His last club was Dinamo Riga of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He was also a regular Latvia national ice hockey team player. He was named to the Latvia men's national ice hockey team for competition at the 2014 IIHF World Championship.
