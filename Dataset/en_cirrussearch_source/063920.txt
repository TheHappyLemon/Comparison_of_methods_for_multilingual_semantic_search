HC Litvinov, called HC VERVA Litvínov for sponsorship reasons is an ice hockey team in the Czech Extraliga. Their home arena is Ivan Hlinka Stadion in Litvínov. Czech Extraliga Winners (1): 2014–15 Runners-up (1): 1995–96 Czechoslovak Extraliga Runners-up (3): 1977–78, 1983–84, 1990–91 3rd place (3): 1981–82, 1989–90, 1991–92 2nd.
