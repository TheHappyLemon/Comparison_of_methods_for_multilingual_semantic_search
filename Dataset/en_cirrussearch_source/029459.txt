83 (eighty-three) is the natural number following 82 and preceding 84. 83 is: the sum of three consecutive primes (23 + 29 + 31). the sum of five consecutive primes (11 + 13 + 17 + 19 + 23). the 23rd prime number, following 79 (of which it is also a cousin prime) and preceding 89. a Sophie Germain prime. a safe prime. a Chen prime. an Eisenstein prime with no imaginary part and real part of the form 3n − 1. a highly cototient number. the number of primes that are right-truncatable. a super-prime, because 23 is prime.
