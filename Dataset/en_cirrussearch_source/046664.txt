Futbolo klubas Babrungas, commonly known as Babrungas, is a Lithuanian football club located in Plungė, in Plungė District. They currently play in the I Lyga, the second tier of Lithuanian football. The club was created in 1935. During the Soviet era in Lithuania, the club was in the top division for a long time. In 1956, the team from the small town of Plungė became champions of Lithuania. Currently, they play in the I Lyga. Babrungas is a river in Plungė District. First time the club was renamed Babrungas in 1942.
