Kazuko Hironaka (弘中 和子, Hironaka Kazuko) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Hironaka played for L.League club Nissan FC. She was selected Fighting Spirit prize in 1990 season. In October 1984, Hironaka was selected by the Japan national team for a tour in China. On October 17, she debuted for Japan against Italy. She was a member of the national team in 1986 and in the 1989 AFC Championship. She also played in the 1990 Asian Games. That competition was her last game for Japan.
