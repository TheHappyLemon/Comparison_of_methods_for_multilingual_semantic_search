Schirgiswalde-Kirschau (Sorbian: Šěrachow-Korzym, pronounced [ˈʃɪʁaxɔf ˈkɔʁzɨm]) is a town in the district of Bautzen, in Saxony, Germany. It was formed on January 1, 2011 by the merger of the former municipalities Schirgiswalde, Kirschau and Crostau. Bürgermeisterwahlen 2018, Freistaat Sachsen, accessed 10 July 2021.
