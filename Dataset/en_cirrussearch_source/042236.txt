Beka Zazayovych Vachiberadze (Ukrainian: Бека Зазайович Вачіберадзе, Georgian: ბექა ვაჩიბერაძე; born 5 March 1996) is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Metalist 1925 Kharkiv in Ukrainian Premier League. Born in Georgia, he represents Ukraine at international level. Born in Kutaisi, Georgia, Vachiberadze is a product of youth team systems of FC Chornomorets Odesa and FC Shakhtar Donetsk. Vachiberadze represented Ukraine in the different national youth teams, but later he switched to his native Georgia and it was confirmed in November 2017 by FIFA. As for 2023 he not made debut for Georgian national team. He is the son of former Torpedo Kutaisi player Zaza Vachiberadze.
