Jan Tratnik (born 23 February 1990 in Ljubljana) is a Slovenian cyclist, who currently rides for UCI WorldTeam Visma–Lease a Bike. Professional since 2009, he has notably won the 2024 Omloop Het Nieuwsblad and a stage of the 2020 Giro d'Italia.
