Northern District (Latvian: Ziemeļu rajons) is one of six administrative districts of Riga, the capital of Latvia. The Northern District consists of several arrondissements: Čiekurkalns (partly the Vidzeme District) Jaunciems Kundziņsala Mangaļsala Mežaparks Mīlgrāvis Pētersala-Andrejsala Sarkandaugava Trīsciems Vecāķi Vecdaugava Vecmīlgrāvis There are 12 secondary education establishments (schools) in the Northern District: "Riga City Council".
