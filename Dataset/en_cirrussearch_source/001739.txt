Artjoms Osipovs (born 8 January 1989) is a Russian-Latvian former professional footballer. Previously he played for FK Ventspils, FK Blāzma Rēzekne, FC Tiraspol, FC Neman Grodno, JFK Olimps/RFS, FC Jūrmala and Skonto Riga. Osipovs returned to FK Jelgava for the second time on 1 February 2019. He was a non-citizen of Latvia, but at the end of 2014 he gained citizenship of Russia.
