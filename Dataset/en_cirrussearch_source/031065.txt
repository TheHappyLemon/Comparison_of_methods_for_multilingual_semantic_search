Dainis Krištopāns (born 27 September 1990) is a Latvian professional handball player for MT Melsungen and the Latvian national team. He is one of the tallest handball players in the world with 215 cm. His weight is 135 kg. He represented Latvia at the 2020 European Men's Handball Championship. "HC Meshkov Brest".
