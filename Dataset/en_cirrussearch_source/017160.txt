Nokia 1202 is a low-end GSM mobile phone made by Nokia under their Ultrabasic series, being aimed at first-time mobile phone users. It was announced in November 2008, and was released in April 2009. The phone was manufactured in Romania, India and China. Nokia 1202 has a 1.3-inch (33 mm) monochrome LCD display with a 96×68 pixel resolution, and an alphanumeric keypad with a dust-resistant keymat. The battery lasts for up to nine hours of talk time, and 636 hours (29 days) of standby.
