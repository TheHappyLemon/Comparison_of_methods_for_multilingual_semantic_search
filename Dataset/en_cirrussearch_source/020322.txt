The 30th World Orienteering Championships was held in Vuokatti, Finland, from 6 to 14 July 2013. A total of 44 countries participated at this World Championships. The sprint finals were held on 8 July 2013 at Hiukka, Sotkamo. The middle distance finals were held on 12 July 2013 at Koulurinne, Vuokatti. The long distance finals were held on 9 July 2013 at Kumpula, Sotkamo. The relay events were held on 13 July 2013 at Koulurinne, Vuokatti. "World Orienteering Championships 2013". orienteering.org.
