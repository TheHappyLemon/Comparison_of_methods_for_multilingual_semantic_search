.ht is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Haiti. It was introduced in 1997 and is managed Réseau de Développement Durable d'Haïti (RDDH) with assistance from the State University of Haiti. Although it was originally intended for Haitian people and businesses, per NIC Haïti, anyone in the world can register a .ht domain name. Internet in Haiti ".HT en Chiffres". NIC Haiti. 2022-10-31.
