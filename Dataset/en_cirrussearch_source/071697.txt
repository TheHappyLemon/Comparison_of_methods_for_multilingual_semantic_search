Artem Volodymyrovych Malysh (Ukrainian: Артем Володимирович Малиш; born 15 July 2000) is a Ukrainian professional football goalkeeper who played for Grobiņas SC. Malysh is a product of Dynamo Kyiv sportive school system, after a short period in the local Kovel youth school. In August 2020 he was transferred to the Ukrainian Premier League débutant Inhulets Petrove.He made his league debut on 21 February 2021, playing as a starting player in the home draw against Vorskla Poltava.
