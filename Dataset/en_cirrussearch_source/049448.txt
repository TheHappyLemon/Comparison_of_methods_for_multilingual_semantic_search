Ami Sugita (杉田 亜未, Sugita Ami, born March 14, 1992) is a Japanese football player. She plays for Nojima Stella in Japans WE League. She played for Japan national team. Sugita was born in Zama on March 14, 1992. After graduating from Kibi International University, she joined Iga FC Kunoichi in 2014. In May 2014, Sugita was picked in the Japan national team for 2014 Asian Cup. At this competition, on May 18, she debuted against Jordan. Japan won the championship. Until 2017, she had played 6 games and scored 2 goals for Japan.
