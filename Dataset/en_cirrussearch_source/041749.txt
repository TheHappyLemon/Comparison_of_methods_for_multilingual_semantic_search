Serigne Khadim N'Diaye (born 5 April 1985) is a Senegalese professional footballer who plays for Guinean side Horoya AC as a goalkeeper. Born in Senegal's capital Dakar, N'Diaye began his career 1997 with Espoir Saint Louis and signed 2007 for Casa Sport. In two years with the club he earned 39 caps before joining Senegal Premier League rivals ASC Linguère in June 2010. He later signed for Horoya AC. In April 2019 he broke both legs in a match, subsequently receiving a three-year contract extension with the club. N'Diaye earned his first call-up for the Senegal national team in November 2009 and made his debut on 28 May 2010 against Denmark.
