Bobsleigh at the 1976 Winter Olympics consisted of two events, at Olympic Sliding Centre Innsbruck. The competition took place between 6 and 14 February 1976. Three countries won medals in Innsbruck, with East Germany sweeping the gold medals. Thirteen nations participated in bobsleigh at the 1976 Games.
