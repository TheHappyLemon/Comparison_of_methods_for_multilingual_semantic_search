The wrestling events at the 2019 European Games were held at the Minsk Sports Palace with a capacity 3,300 seats from 25 to 30 June. 18 events were held, six events in freestyle for men, six events for women, and six in the Greco-Roman style for men.   *   Host nation (Belarus) a Kiryl Hryshchanka from Belarus originally won the gold medal, but was later disqualified for doping violations.
