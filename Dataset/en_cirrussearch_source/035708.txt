Oleg Kubarev (Belarusian: Алег Кубареў; Russian: Олег Кубарев; born 8 February 1966) is a Belarusian football manager and former footballer (midfielder). From September 2013 to December 2014 he was head coach of Zimbru Chișinău in the Moldovan National Division.
