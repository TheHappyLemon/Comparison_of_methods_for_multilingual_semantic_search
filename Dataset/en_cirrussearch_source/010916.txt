Alexei Mikhailovich Ugarov (Russian: Алексей Михайлович Угаров; born November 2, 1985) is a Belarusian professional ice hockey forward. He is currently an unrestricted free agent who most recently played for Severstal Cherepovets of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He previously played three seasons for HC Nizhnekamsk Neftekhimik in the Russian Super League. Ugarov was selected for the Belarus national men's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics.
