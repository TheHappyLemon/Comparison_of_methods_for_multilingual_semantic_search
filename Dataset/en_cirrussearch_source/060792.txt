Alfred Bloch, also known as Jean Bloch, (born 1877) was a French Olympic football player. He was affiliated with the Racing Club de France, in Paris. Bloch won a silver medal playing football for France in the 1900 Olympics. Bloch was Jewish. List of select Jewish football (association; soccer) players "Alfred Bloch Biography and Statistics".
