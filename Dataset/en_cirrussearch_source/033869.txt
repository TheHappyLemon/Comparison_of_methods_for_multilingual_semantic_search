Norio Yoshimizu (吉水 法生, Yoshimizu Norio, born August 21, 1946) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Yoshimizu was born on August 21, 1946. After graduating from Keio University, he joined Furukawa Electric in 1969. He retired in 1972. He played 53 games and scored 6 goals in the league.
