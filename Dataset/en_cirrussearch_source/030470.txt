The 2015–16 Latvian Hockey League season was the 25th season of the Latvian Hockey League, the top level of ice hockey in Latvia. Seven teams participated in the league, and HK Liepāja won the championship. Source: Latvian Ice Hockey Federation "2015/2016 Latvijas virslīgas hokeja čempionāts Regulārās spēles".
