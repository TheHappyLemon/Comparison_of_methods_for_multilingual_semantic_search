Līvbērze parish (Latvian: Līvbērzes pagasts) is an administrative unit of Jelgava Municipality in the Semigallia region of Latvia. Prior to the 2009 administrative reforms it was part of the Jelgava District Līvbērze [lv] – parish administrative center Kārlis Ozols-Priednieks (1896 July 1943).
