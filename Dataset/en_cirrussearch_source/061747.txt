Yasin Ehliz (born 30 December 1992) is a German professional ice hockey forward who is currently playing for EHC Red Bull München of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL). A product of Tölzer Löwen, Ehliz moved to the Nürnberg Ice Tigers of the German Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL) in 2010. He played for the Ice Tigers in the DEL until 2018 before on 11 June 2018, he signed a one-year, two-way contract with the Calgary Flames of the National Hockey League (NHL). For Calgary's AHL affiliate, the Stockton Heat, he appeared in four games in the early stages of the 2018–19 season. In early November 2018, he was placed on unconditional waivers. He subsequently returned to Germany, and signed with Red Bull München on 7 November 2018. Ehliz was named to the Germany men's national ice hockey team for competition at the 2014 IIHF World Championship.
