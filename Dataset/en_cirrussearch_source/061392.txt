Kseniya Stankevich (born 28 January 1996) is a Belarusian freestyle wrestler. She is a two-time bronze medalist in the women's 50 kg event at the European Wrestling Championships. Stankevich won one of the bronze medals in the women's 44 kg event at the 2015 World Junior Wrestling Championships held in Salvador da Bahia, Brazil. At the 2020 European Wrestling Championships held in Rome, Italy, she won one of the bronze medals in the women's 50 kg event. She also won one of the bronze medals in the women's 50 kg event at the 2019 European Wrestling Championships held in Bucharest, Romania. In 2019, at the Military World Games held in Wuhan, China, she won one of the bronze medals in the women's 50 kg event.
