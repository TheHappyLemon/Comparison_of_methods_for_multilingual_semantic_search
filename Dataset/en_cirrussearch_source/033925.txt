Yuji Kishioku (岸奥 裕二, Kishioku Yuji, born April 2, 1954) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Kishioku was born in Muroran on April 2, 1954. After graduating from high school, he joined Nippon Steel in 1973. He played 121 games and scored 1 goal in Japan Soccer League Division 1. On May 31, 1979, Kishioku debuted for Japan national team against Indonesia. He played at 1980 Summer Olympics qualification. He played 10 games and scored 2 goals for Japan until 1980.
