Maiko Nasu (那須 麻衣子, Nasu Maiko, born July 31, 1984) is a Japanese former footballer who played as a midfielder. She played for Japan national team. Nasu was born in Nabari on July 31, 1984. She joined her local club Prima Ham FC Kunoichi (later Iga FC Kunoichi) in 1999. The club won the champions in 1999 season and the 2nd place in 2000 season. In 2007, she moved to INAC Kobe Leonessa. The club won the champions in 2011 season. In 2012, she returned to Iga FC Kunoichi. She retired end of 2016 season. In August 2002, Nasu was selected Japan U-20 national team for 2002 U-19 World Championship. On August 1, 2009, she debuted for Japan national team against France. She played 3 games for Japan until 2011.
