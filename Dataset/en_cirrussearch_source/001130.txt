Mikstat [ˈmʲikstat] (German: Mixstadt) is a town in Ostrzeszów County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, Poland, with 1,906 inhabitants (2011). Mikstat was royal town of the Kingdom of Poland, administratively located in the Ostrzeszów County in the Sieradz Voivodeship in the Greater Poland Province.
