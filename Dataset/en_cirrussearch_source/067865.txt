Neftegorsk (Russian: Нефтего́рск) is a town and the administrative center of Neftegorsky District in Samara Oblast, Russia, located 103 kilometers (64 mi) southeast of Samara, the administrative center of the oblast. Population: 19,254 (2010 Census); 19,388 (2002 Census); 18,895 (1989 Census). It was founded in 1960 as an oil-extracting settlement.[citation needed] Urban-type settlement status was granted to it in 1966; town status was granted in 1989.[citation needed] Within the framework of administrative divisions, Neftegorsk serves as the administrative center of Neftegorsky District, to which it is directly subordinated.[citation needed] As a municipal division, the town of Neftegorsk is incorporated within Neftegorsky Municipal District as Neftegorsk Urban Settlement.
