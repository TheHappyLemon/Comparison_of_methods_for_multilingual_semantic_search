Chashniki District or Čašniki District (Belarusian: Чашніцкі раён; Russian: Чашникский район) is a district (raion) of Vitebsk Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is Chashniki. Lake Lukomlskoye, the fourth largest lake in the country is situated here. The Vula River flows through this district.
