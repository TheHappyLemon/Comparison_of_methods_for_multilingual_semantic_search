Aumeisteri Manor (Latvian: Aumeisteru muižas pils, German: Hofmeistershof), also called Cirgaļi Manor (German: Serbigal), is a manor house built by Baron Johann von Wulf in the historical region of Vidzeme, northern Latvia, after 1750 and reconstructed in 1793.
