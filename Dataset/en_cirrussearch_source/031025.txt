Aija Andrejeva (born 16 January 1986 in Lielvarde), better known by her stage name Aisha ([aiʃa]), is a Latvian singer. On 27 February 2010, Aija Andrejeva won the Latvian national final Eirodziesma 2010, and has represented Latvia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2010 in Oslo, Norway, with the song What For?. Andrejeva failed to qualify to the final from the first semifinal on 25 May, coming in last place. The following year, she presented the Latvian votes. Tu un Es (2006) Viss kārtībā, Mincīt!
