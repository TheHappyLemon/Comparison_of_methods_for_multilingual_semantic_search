Rihards Marenis (born 18 April 1993) is a Latvian professional ice hockey player currently playing with Modo Hockey in the Hockeyallsvenskan and the Latvian national team. He formerly played with Dinamo Riga in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) He represented Latvia at the 2019 IIHF World Championship.
