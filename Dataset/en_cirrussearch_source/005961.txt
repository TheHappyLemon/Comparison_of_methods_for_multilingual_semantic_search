Year 1416 (MCDXVI) was a leap year starting on Wednesday (link will display the full calendar) of the Julian calendar. January 27 – The Republic of Ragusa is the first state in Europe to outlaw slavery. May 29 – Battle of Gallipoli: Venetian admiral Pietro Loredan destroys the Ottoman fleet. May 30 – The Catholic Church burns Jerome of Prague as a heretic. The Trezzo sull'Adda Bridge (the longest arch bridge in the world at the time) is destroyed. The Hussite Bible is completed by Tamás Pécsi and Bálint Újlaki.
