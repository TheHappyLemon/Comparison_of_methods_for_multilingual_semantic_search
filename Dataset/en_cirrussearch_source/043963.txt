FK Kražantė (lt Kelmės krepšinio ir futbolo klubas "Kražantė") was a Lithuanian football team from the city of Kelmė. In 2001 season was in First division I Lyga. The team dissolved in 2006. Refounded in 1994 as part of basketball and football club "Kražantė" in Kelmė. The team was dissolved after the 2006 season. Green and white. Later was green/yellow. "Šiaulių kraštas".
