Shamima Akter Liza (born 1989) is a Bangladeshi chess player and FIDE Woman International Master. She has won the Bangladesh Women's Chess Championship in 2005, 2010 and 2014. Liza has qualified for the Women's World Chess Championship 2014. In 2014 Liza finished 66th in 28th Cannes Winter Chess Festival in Cannes, France. "Liza regains chess title".
