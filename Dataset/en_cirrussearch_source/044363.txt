Alexandr Aleksandrovich Predke (Russian: Александр Александрович Предке, Serbian Cyrillic: Александар Александрович Предке; born 5 January 1994) is a Russian-born Serbian chess grandmaster (2016). Predke started playing chess at the age of seven. He is an alumnus of the Tolyatti chess school. In 2010, he won the Russian Youth Chess Championship in the U16 age group. In 2014, he was third in the Russian Junior Championship in the U20 age group. In 2017, in Samara he was the second in Lev Polugaevsky memorial. In August 2018, he finished third in the Riga Technical University Open "A" tournament. In 2016, he was awarded the FIDE International Grandmaster (GM) title. Several Russian players' officially switched federations in response to the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine.
