Raimonds Vilkoits (born 10 April 1990) is Latvian professional ice-hockey player, who currently plays for HK Metalurgs Liepaja of the Belarusian Extraleague. During 2010–11 Vilkoits also played three games for Dinamo Riga of the Kontinental Hockey League.
