Carex pediformis is a species of sedge (genus Carex), native to Eurasia, from Eastern Europe to Korea. It is a forest-steppe specialist. The following varieties are currently accepted: Carex pediformis var. macroura (Meinsh.) Kük. Carex pediformis var. pediformis Carex pediformis var. pedunculata Maxim.
