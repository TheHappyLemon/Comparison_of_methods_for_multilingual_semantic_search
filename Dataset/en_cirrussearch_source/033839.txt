Takashi Mizuno (水野 隆, Mizuno Takashi, born April 28, 1931) (former name; Takashi Tokuhiro, 徳弘 隆) is a Japanese former football player. He played for Japan national team. Mizuno was born on April 28, 1931. After graduating from Kwansei Gakuin University, he joined Yuasa Batteries in 1954. He also played for Kwangaku Club was consisted of his alma mater Kwansei Gakuin University players and graduates. he won 1953, 1955 and 1958 Emperor's Cup On October 9, 1955, Mizuno debuted for Japan national team against Burma.
