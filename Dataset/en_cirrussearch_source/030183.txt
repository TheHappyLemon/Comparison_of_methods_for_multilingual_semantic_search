Zambia is divided into ten provinces, each administered by an appointed deputy minister. Each province is divided into several districts with a total of 116 districts. The provinces are: Central Copperbelt Eastern Luapula Lusaka Muchinga Northern North-Western Southern Western Created in November 2011, consisting of five districts from Northern Province and one district of the Eastern Province.
