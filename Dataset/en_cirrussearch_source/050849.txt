Riga International Coach Terminal (Latvian: Rīgas Starptautiskā autoosta) is a bus station in Riga, Latvia, for both domestic and international bus lines. The terminal is situated at the address 1 Prāgas iela, right next to Riga Central Market and opposite Riga Central Station,. The terminal was built in 1964 and has 33 bays. "Contacts". autoosta.lv.
