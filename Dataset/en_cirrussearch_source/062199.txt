Joseph Jean Jules Ambroise Ducrot (13 August 1866 – 25 September 1915) was a French fencer. He competed in the men's foil event at the 1900 Summer Olympics. Ducrot served as a chef de bataillon in the 21st Colonial Infantry Regiment of the French Army during the First World War. He was killed in action in Marne on 25 September 1915. "Joseph Ducrot".
