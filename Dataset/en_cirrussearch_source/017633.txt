Hantsavichy District or Hancavičy District (Belarusian: Ганцавіцкі раён; Russian: Ганцевичский район) is a district (raion) of Brest Region in Belarus. Its administrative center is Hantsavichy. At the time of the 2009 Belarusian census, Hantsavichy District had a population of 31,170. Of these, 95.6% were of Belarusian, 2.2% Russian, 1.2% Polish and 0.6% Ukrainian ethnicity. 90.5% spoke Belarusian and 8.5% Russian as their native language.
