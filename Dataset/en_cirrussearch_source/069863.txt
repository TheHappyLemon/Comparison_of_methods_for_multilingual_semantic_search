81 (eighty-one) is the natural number following 80 and preceding 82. 81 is: the square of 9 and the second fourth-power of a prime; 34. with an aliquot sum of 40; within an aliquot sequence of three composite numbers (81,40,50,43,1,0) to the Prime in the 43-aliquot tree. a perfect totient number like all powers of three. a heptagonal number. an icosioctagonal number. a centered octagonal number. a tribonacci number. an open meandric number. the ninth member of the Mian-Chowla sequence. a palindromic number in bases 8 (1218) and 26 (3326). a Harshad number in bases 2, 3, 4, 7, 9, 10 and 13. one of three non-trivial numbers (the other two are 1458 and 1729) which, when its digits (in decimal) are added together, produces a sum which, when multiplied by its reversed self, yields the original number: 8 + 1 = 9 9 × 9 = 81 (although this case is somewhat degenerate, as the sum has only a single digit).
