Jastarnia Lighthouse (Polish: Latarnia Morska Jastarnia) is a lighthouse in Jastarnia on the Polish coast of the Baltic Sea. The lighthouse is located in Jastarnia, Pomeranian Voivodeship on the Hel Peninsula. The lighthouse in Jastarnia is located between the lighthouse in Rozewie and the lighthouse in Hel.
