Ivot (Russian: Иво́т) is an urban-type settlement in Dyatkovsky District of Bryansk Oblast, Russia. Population: 5,844 (2021 Census); 6,374 (2010 Census); 6,422 (2002 Census); 6,972 (1989 Census).
