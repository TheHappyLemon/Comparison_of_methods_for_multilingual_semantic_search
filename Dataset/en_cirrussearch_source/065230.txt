The Uly Balkan (Turkmen: Uly Balkan dagy, Greater Balkan Mountains), also the Great Balkhan or Greater Balkhan is a mountain range in Turkmenistan, part of the Balkhan Ranges. The highest summit is Mount Arlan at 1,880 metres (6,170 ft). Большой Балхан in the Great Soviet Encyclopedia, 1969–1978 (in Russian)
