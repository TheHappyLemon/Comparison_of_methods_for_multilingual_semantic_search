Ivan Tabanov (born 7 August 1966) is a Moldovan football manager and former player who is currently the manager of FC Sucleia. In October 2014 Ivan Tabanov was interrogated and arrested by the Latvian State Police, being accused of match fixing. Being a suspect in the ongoing investigation, he was disqualified from the Latvian Higher League for an undetermined period of time by the Latvian Football Federation. In August 2016, he was appointed sporting director of Saxan. In January 2017, he became the head coach of the club. In July 2020, he was appointed head coach of Sucleia.
