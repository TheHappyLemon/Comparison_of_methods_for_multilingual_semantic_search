Gašper Okorn (born 9 April 1973) is a Slovenian professional basketball coach for Krka of the Slovenian League. On 23 January 2022, Croatian team Cibona hired Okorn as their new head coach for the rest of the 2021–22 season. He left Cibona in June 2022. On 24 June, Krka hired Okorn as their new head coach.
