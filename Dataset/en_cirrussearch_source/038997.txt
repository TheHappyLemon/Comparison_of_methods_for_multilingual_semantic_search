Sásd (Croatian: Šaš) is a town in Baranya county, in southern Hungary. The total population of Sásd in 2015 was 3,094. Sásd is twinned with: Westhausen, Germany Izvoru Crișului, Romania Raaba, Austria Supino, Italy Pierrelaye, France Neftenbach, Switzerland Mogilany, Poland Gazetteer of Hungary, 1 January 2015.
