Emilio Banfi (born 1881, date of death unknown) was an Italian track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Banfi competed in the 800 metres. He placed somewhere between fourth and sixth in his first-round (semifinals) heat and did not advance to the final.
