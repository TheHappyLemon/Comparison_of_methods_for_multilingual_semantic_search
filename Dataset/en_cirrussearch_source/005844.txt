Alexei Tsvetkov (born August 28, 1981) is a Russian professional ice hockey forward who is currently an unrestricted free agent. Tsvetkov most recently played for HC Sochi in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). He previously played four seasons for HC Dynamo Moscow of the KHL.
