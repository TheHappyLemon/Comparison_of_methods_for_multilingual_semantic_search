Futbolo Klubas Šilutė (before 2003, Laisvė Šilutė) is a Lithuanian football team from the city of Šilutė. The team plays in the II Lyga, the third tier of Lithuanian football. Akinfenwa played for the club in 2002. 2003 – 3rd (I Lyga) 2004 – 6th 2005 – 6th 2006 – 9th 2007 – 9th 2008 – 8th 2009 – 1st (I Lyga) 2010 – 8th (I Lyga) 2011 – 8th (I Lyga) 2011 – 7th (I Lyga) As of 17 April 2016.
