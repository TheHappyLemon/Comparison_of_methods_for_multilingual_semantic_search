Alexander Alexandrovich Vasiliev (Russian: Александр Александрович Васильев; born December 8, 1958, Moscow) is a Russian fashion historian, collector TV host and author of best selling books. He is an honorary member of the Russian Academy of Arts and founder of the international interior prize.
