Marcel Lucien Charles Van Crombrugge (13 September 1880 – 23 September 1940) was a Belgian rower who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. He was part of the Belgian boat Royal Club Nautique de Gand, which won the silver medal in the men's eight. "Marcel Van Crombrugge". Olympedia. Retrieved 9 October 2021.
