Carex caryophyllea is a species of plant in the family Cyperaceae first described by Marc Antoine Louis Claret de La Tourrette. Two varieties are listed in the World Checklist of Selected Plant Families: Carex caryophyllea var. caryophyllea — temperate regions of the Old World Carex caryophyllea var. microtricha (Franch.)
