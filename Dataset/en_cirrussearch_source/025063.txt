Sukhodilsk (Ukrainian: Суходільськ; Russian: Суходольск, romanized: Sukhodolsk) is a city in Krasnodon Municipality in Luhansk Oblast of Ukraine. Population: 20,390 (2022 estimate), 21,061 (2013 est.).
