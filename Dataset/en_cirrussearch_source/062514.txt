39°39′27″N 45°37′00″E﻿ / ﻿39.65750°N 45.61667°E﻿ / 39.65750; 45.61667 Artavan (Armenian: Արտավան) is a village in the Vayk Municipality of the Vayots Dzor Province of Armenia. The village was probably at one time a fief of Tatev. There is an 18th-century bridge and an old cemetery in the vicinity of the village. The village was previously known as Dzhul.
