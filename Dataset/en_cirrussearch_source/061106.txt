Berezivka (Ukrainian: Березі́вка, pronounced [be.reˈz⁽ʲ⁾iu̯.kɐ]; Romanian: Berezovca) is a city and the administrative center of Berezivka Raion in Odesa Oblast, Ukraine. It hosts the administration of Berezivka urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. In 2001, population was 9,481. Current estimated population: 9,428 (2022 estimate).
