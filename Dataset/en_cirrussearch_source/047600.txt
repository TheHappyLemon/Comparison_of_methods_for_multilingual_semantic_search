Antonin Guigonnat (born 2 July 1991) is a French biathlete who competes internationally. He participated in the 2018 Winter Olympics, and is the brother of Gilonne Guigonnat. On February 18, 2021, he became world champion in the single mixed relay with Julia Simon in Pokljuka.
