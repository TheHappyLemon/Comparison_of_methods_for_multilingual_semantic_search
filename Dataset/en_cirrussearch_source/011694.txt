Dzelzāmurs is a village in Iecava parish, Bauska Municipality. It is located in the northeast of the parish on the left bank of Misa, 13 km from the parish center Iecava, 35.5 km from the county center Bauska and 41 km from Riga. The village was formed near the former Dzelzāmurs forest estate (Eisenhammer). Dzelzāmurs hunting palace was built in 1904 (cultural and historical monument). The palace is a log building decorated with wood carvings, which belonged to the owner of the Lieliecava manor, Count Peter von der Pahlen. Currently a guest house.
