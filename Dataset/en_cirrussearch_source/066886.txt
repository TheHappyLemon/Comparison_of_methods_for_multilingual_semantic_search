Kikkajärv (also Kika järv, Suur-Kikkajärv) or Ilgājs as it is known in Latvia, is a lake on the border of Estonia and Latvia. Its area is 20.2 ha. Lake is in the Veclaicene Protected Landscape Area. Nearest settlement to the lake in Latvia is Korneti [lv] in Alūksne Municipality.
