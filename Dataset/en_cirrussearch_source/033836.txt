Reizo Fukuhara (福原 黎三, Fukuhara Reizō, April 2, 1931 – February 27, 1970) was a Japanese football player. He made two appearances for the Japan national team. On February 27, 1970, Fukuhara died of stomach cancer at the age of 38. On January 5, 1955, while a student at the Tokyo University of Education, he debuted for Japan against Burma.
