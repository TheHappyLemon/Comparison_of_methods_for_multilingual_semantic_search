Vecgulbene Manor (Latvian: Vecgulbenes muižas pils) was a manor in the historical region of Vidzeme, eastern Latvia. Manor had 2 main buildings: Red Palace (Latvian: Sarkanā pils) and White Palace (Latvian: Balta pils).
