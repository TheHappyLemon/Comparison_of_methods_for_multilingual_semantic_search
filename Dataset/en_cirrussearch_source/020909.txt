Māris Ziediņš (born March 3, 1990) is a Latvian professional basketball player from Strenči, Latvia, who currently plays for BC Tallinna Kalev in the top tier Latvian–Estonian Basketball League "Valmiera Glass/Vidzemes Augstskola pievienojas uzbrucējs Ziediņš".
