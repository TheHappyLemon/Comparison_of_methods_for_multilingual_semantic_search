Administratively, Lesotho is divided into ten districts, each headed by a district administrator. Each district has a capital known as a camptown. Berea Butha-Buthe Leribe Mafeteng Maseru Mohale's Hoek Mokhotlong Qacha's Nek Quthing Thaba-Tseka The districts are further subdivided into 80 constituencies, which consists of 129 local community councils.
