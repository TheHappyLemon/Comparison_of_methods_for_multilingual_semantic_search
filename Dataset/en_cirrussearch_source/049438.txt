Hikari Nakade (中出 ひかり, Nakade Hikari, born December 6, 1988) is a Japanese former football player. She played for the Japan national team as a forward. Nakade was born in Mie Prefecture on December 6, 1988. After graduating from Kibi International University, she played for Iga FC Kunoichi. In November 2008, Nakade was selected Japan U-20 national team for 2008 U-20 World Cup. On September 26, 2013, she debuted for Japan national team against Nigeria.
