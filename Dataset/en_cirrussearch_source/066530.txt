Eduards Dašķevičs (born 12 July 2002) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder or wing back for Riga FC. He hails from Daugavpils. On 1 July 2018 he joined the youth system of Anderlecht. He also made his first-team debut in the summer of 2019, first in a friendly match against RWDM and then another friendly against Benfica. Internationally, he played 3 matches each for Latvia U17 and Latvia U19 before joining Latvia U21 in 2020, at the age of 18. Dašķevičs made his debut for the Latvia on 16 June 2023, coming on as a substitute in 76 minute for Andrejs Cigaņiks against Turkey. In 2021 he was signed by his first senior team, Norwegian club Hamarkameratene. He made his debut in the 2021 1. divisjon against Åsane, and made his Eliteserien debut in 2022 against Viking. On 5 January 2023, Dašķevičs signed for Latvian Higher League side Riga FC.
