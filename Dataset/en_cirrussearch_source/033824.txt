Masao Ono (大埜 正雄, Ono Masao, March 2, 1923 – February 11, 2001) was a Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Ono was born in Kanagawa Prefecture on March 2, 1923. After graduating from University of Tokyo, he joined Nissan Chemical. He also played for University of Tokyo LB was consisted of his alma mater University of Tokyo players and graduates. At University of Tokyo LB, he won 1949 Emperor's Cup with Ko Arima and so on. In March 1954, Ono was selected Japan national team for 1954 World Cup qualification. At this qualification, on March 14, he debuted against South Korea. He also played at 1954 Asian Games. He played 3 games for Japan in 1954. On February 11, 2001, Ono died of ruptured aneurysm of abdomen at the age of 77.
