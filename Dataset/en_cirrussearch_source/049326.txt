Minako Takashima (高嶋 美奈子, Takashima Minako) is a former Japanese football player. She played for Japan national team. Takashima played for L.League club Nissan FC. She was selected Fighting Spirit prize in 1993 season. However, the club was disbanded in 1993. She moved to Shiroki FC Serena in 1994.
