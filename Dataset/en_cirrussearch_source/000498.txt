Grobiņa Parish (Latvian: Grobiņas pagasts) is an administrative unit of South Kurzeme Municipality in the Courland region of Latvia. The parish has a population of 2,836 (as of 1/07/2010) and covers an area of 131.4 square kilometres (50.7 sq mi).
