Takayoshi Yamano (山野 孝義, Yamano Takayoshi, born April 5, 1955) is a former Japanese football player. He played for Japan national team. Yamano was born in Osaka Prefecture on April 5, 1955. After graduating from Osaka University of Commerce, he joined his local club Yanmar Diesel in 1978. The club won the league champions in 1980. The club also won 1983 and 1984 JSL Cup. In 1985, he moved to Osaka Gas. He retired in 1991. On June 11, 1980, Yamano debuted for Japan national team against China. He played 2 games for Japan in 1980.
