Stanislav Marselyevich Lebamba (Russian: Станислав Марсельевич Лебамба; born 21 April 1988) is a Russian former a professional football player. He played 8 seasons in the Russian Football National League for FC KAMAZ Naberezhnye Chelny, FC Yenisey Krasnoyarsk and FC Khimik Dzerzhinsk. His father is from DR Congo and his mother is Russian.
