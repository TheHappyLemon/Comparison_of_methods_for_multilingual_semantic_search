Shiho Kohata (高畑 志帆, Kohata Shiho, born November 12, 1989) is a Japanese footballer who has played a defender for club sides as well the Japan national team. Kohata was born in Hiroshima Prefecture on November 12, 1989. After graduating from Waseda University, she joined Urawa Reds in 2012. She was selected Best Young Player awards in 2012 season. She was also selected Best Eleven in 2014 season. She played 124 matches for the club in L.League until 2018 season. She left the club end of 2018 season. In February 2019, she joined AS Harima Albion. In May 2014, Kohata was selected by the Japan national team for the 2014 Asian Cup. She debuted on May 18 against Jordan. Japan won the championship. She played two games for Japan until 2015.
