Anders Andersen (26 October 1881 – 19 February 1961) was a Danish sport wrestler who competed in the 1908 Summer Olympics and in the 1912 Summer Olympics. In 1908 he won the bronze medal in the Greco-Roman middleweight class. Four years later he was eliminated in the second round of the Greco-Roman middleweight competition. "Anders Andersen".
