Amiran Kardanov (Greek: Αμιράν Καρντάνοφ, born 19 August 1976) is a Russian and Greek Freestyle wrestler. He won a bronze medal at the 2000 Summer Olympics, and also competed at the 1996 Summer Olympics and 2004 Summer Olympics, coming in 4th place in the latter.
