Rudolph Felix Bauer (Russian: Рудольф Феликс Бауэр; 1667–1717) was a Baltic German in Russian Empire military service, also a statesman. He started his military career at the army of Duchy of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Later he belonged to Prussian Army.
