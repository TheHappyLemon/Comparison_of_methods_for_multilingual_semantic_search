Nils Toms Puriņš (born 1 August 1998) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Riga FC and the Latvia national team. Puriņš made his international debut for Latvia on 16 June 2023 in a UEFA Euro 2024 qualifying match against Turkey playing full match.
