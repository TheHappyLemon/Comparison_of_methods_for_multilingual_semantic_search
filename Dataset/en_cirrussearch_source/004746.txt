Boriss Bogdaškins (born 21 February 1990) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder. Bogdaškins made his international debut for Latvia on 6 September 2019 in a UEFA Euro 2020 qualifying match against Austria, which finished as a 0–6 away loss.
