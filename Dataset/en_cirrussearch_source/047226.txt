Rastrelliger is a mackerel genus in the family Scombridae. The three species of Rastrelliger together with the four species of Scomber comprise the tribe Scombrini, known as the "true mackerels". The three species which comprise Rastrelliger are: Rastrelliger brachysoma (Bleeker, 1851) (short mackerel) Rastrelliger faughni, Matsui, 1967 (island mackerel) Rastrelliger kanagurta, (Cuvier, 1816) (Indian mackerel) Froese, Rainer and Pauly, Daniel, eds.
