Elvis Stuglis (born 4 July 1993) is a Latvian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Latvian Higher League club RFS and the Latvia national team. Stuglis made his international debut for Latvia on 7 October 2020 in a friendly match against Montenegro, which finished as a 1–1 away draw.
