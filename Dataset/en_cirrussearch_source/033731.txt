Chikashi Masuda (増田 誓志, Masuda Chikashi, born 19 June 1985) is a Japanese former professional football player. He was capped by the Japan national team. In 2019, Masuda joined the K League 2 side Seoul E-Land on loan from Shimizu S-Pulse. He announced his retirement in November of the same year. Masuda made his senior national team debut on 24 February 2012 in a friendly match against Iceland.
