Robert Gufflet (5 June 1883 in Bordeaux – 2 January 1933 in Casablanca) was a French sailor who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics. Gufflet took the gold in the first race of the 3 to 10 ton and the silver medal in the second race of that class. He also competed in the 1928 Summer Olympics. "Robert Gufflet". Olympedia.
