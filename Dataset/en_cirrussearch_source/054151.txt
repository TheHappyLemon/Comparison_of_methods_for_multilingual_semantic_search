Boris Aleksandrovich Mayorov (Russian: Борис Александрович Майоров; born 11 February 1938 in Moscow, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union) is a retired Russian ice hockey player who played in the Soviet Hockey League. He played for HC Spartak Moscow and was inducted into the Russian and Soviet Hockey Hall of Fame in 1963.[citation needed] Mayorov was inducted into the player category of the International Ice Hockey Federation Hall of Fame in 1999.
