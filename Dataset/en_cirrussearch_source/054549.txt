Charles-Robert Faidide (19 January 1880 in Paris – 3 July 1907 in Le Kremlin-Bicêtre) was a French track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Faidide competed in the 400 metres. He placed third in his first-round (semifinals) heat and did not advance to the final. "Charles-Robert Faidide". Olympedia. Retrieved 22 December 2020.
