Takahiro Ogihara (扇原 貴宏, Ōgihara Takahiro, born 5 October 1991) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Vissel Kobe in the J1 League. Ogihara debuted for Cerezo Osaka in the 2–1 loss to Kashima Antlers on 27 August 2011. Ogihara debuted for the Japan U-23s during the Qualification process for the 2012 London Olympics, with his first game in a 2–0 win over Malaysia. He played for Japan at the 2012 Summer Olympics.
