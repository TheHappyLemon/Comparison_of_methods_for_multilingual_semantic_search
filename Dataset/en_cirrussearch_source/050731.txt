Vyacheslav Litovchenko (born January 7, 1990) is a Russian professional ice hockey forward who is currently playing with HC Yugra of the Supreme Hockey League (VHL). Litovchenko has formerly played with Amur Khabarovsk. Having spent the first 13 professional seasons of his career exclusively with Amur Khabarovsk, Litovchenko left as a free agent prior to the 2018–19 season, agreeing to a one-year contract with Avtomobilist Yekaterinburg on May 4, 2018. After three seasons with Avtomobilist, Litovchenko left as a free agent and joined his third KHL club, HC Sibir Novosibirsk, on a one-year contract on 1 May 2021. As a free agent following two seasons with Sibir, Litovchenko extended his career in the VHL, signing a contract with HC Yugra on 2 October 2023.
