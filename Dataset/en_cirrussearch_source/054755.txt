Ondřej Pukl (28 May 1876 in Křenična – 9 February 1936 in Prague) was a Bohemian track and field athlete who competed at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. Pukl competed in the 800 metres. He placed fourth or fifth in his first-round (semifinals) heat and did not advance to the final. In the 1500 metres, Pukl finished somewhere in the bottom third of the nine-man, single-round race.
