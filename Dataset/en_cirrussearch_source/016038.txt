Emil's Mischiefs (Latvian: Emīla nedarbi, Russian: Проделки сорванца, romanized: Prodelky sorvantsa – "Tricks of a Rascal") is a 1985 Latvian TV-film about the Astrid Lindgren character, Emil i Lönneberga. It was produced by Riga Film Studio. The film was awarded the Latvian National Film Prize Lielais Kristaps in 1985. [1] Emīla nedarbi on the website of Latvian Television.
