Yuri Yurovsky (Russian: Юрий Юровский) was a Soviet actor and director of Armenian origin. People's Artist of the USSR. Since 1925 he worked as an actor and director at Riga Russian Theatre. 1949 — Encounter at the Elbe 1950 — Zhukovsky 1952 — The Composer Glinka Ienācēji no tālienes: Austrumu un Dienvidu tautu pārstāvji Latvijā no 19. gadsimta beigām līdz mūsdienām.
