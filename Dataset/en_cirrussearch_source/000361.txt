Upītis (feminine: Upīte) is a Latvian masculine surname, derived from the Latvian word for "river" (upe). Individuals with the surname include: Ernests Birznieks-Upītis (1871–1960), Latvian writer, translator and librarian Juris Upītis (born 1991), Latvian ice hockey player Pēteris Upītis (1896–1976), Latvian horticulturist Pēteris Upītis (1899–1989), Latvian graphic artist and art collector This page lists people with the surname Upītis.
