Holger Norman-Hansen (2 January 1899 – 26 March 1984) was a Danish chess master. Norman-Hansen played for Denmark in Chess Olympiads: In the 1st Chess Olympiad at London 1927 (+11 -2 =2); In the 2nd Chess Olympiad at The Hague 1928 (+4 -7 =5); In the 3rd unofficial Chess Olympiad at Munich 1936 (+8 -6 =5). He won individual gold medal and team silver medal in 1927. He was Danish Champion in 1939 after a play-off. He also finished 1st= in 1936 but lost the play-off.
