Bogdan Vaštšuk (born 4 October 1995) is an Estonian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Kuwaiti club Al-Shabab and the Estonia national team. In early January 2016, Vaštšuk returned to Reading following his loan deal with Farnborough. On 9 May 2016, Reading confirmed that they would not be renewing Vaštšuk's contract. On 24 August 2016, Vaštšuk signed for Riga FC. On 21 August 2018, Levski Sofia announced the signing of Vaštšuk on loan for the 2018–19 season, with an option to make the move permanent at the end of the season. On 23 August 2019, Vaštšuk signed for FCI Levadia. On 20 July 2022, he joined Ekstraklasa side Stal Mielec on a two-year contract. On 5 January 2023, Vaštšuk signed for League of Ireland Premier Division club Sligo Rovers on a two-year deal with the option of a third. On 18 July 2023, it was announced that Vaštšuk's contract had been mutually terminated after making just 8 appearances in the clubs 24 games up to that point. He made his debut for Estonia national football team on 24 March 2021 in a World Cup qualifier against the Czech Republic. Vaštšuk is of Ukrainian descent by his paternal grandparents, who are from Rivne and Donetsk Oblast in Ukraine.
