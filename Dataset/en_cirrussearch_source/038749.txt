Jevgēņijs Kazačoks (born 12 August 1995) is a Latvian footballer who plays for Norwegian club Årdal. He made his debut for the Latvia national football team on 9 June 2017 in a World Cup qualifier group game against Portugal. "Latvia-Portugal game report". FIFA. 9 June 2017.
