Tsukasa Shiotani (塩谷 司, Shiotani Tsukasa, born 5 December 1988) is a Japanese footballer who plays for Sanfrecce Hiroshima. In August 2016, Shiotani was elected Japan U-23 national team as over age for 2016 Summer Olympics. At this tournament, he played full-time in all three matches as centre back with Naomichi Ueda.
