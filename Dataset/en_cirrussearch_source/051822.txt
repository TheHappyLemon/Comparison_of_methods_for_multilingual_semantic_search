Aleksejs Grjaznovs (born 1 October 1997) is a Latvian footballer who plays as a midfielder for FK Liepaja and the Latvia national team. On 25 December, Noah announced the signing of Grjaznovs. Grjaznovs made his international debut for Latvia on 19 November 2019 in a UEFA Euro 2020 qualifying match against Austria, which finished as a 1–0 home win.
