Božidar Đurašević (26 April 1933 in Belgrade – 23 January 2022 in Belgrade) was a Serbian chess player. Đurašević became International Master in 1957 and represented Yugoslavia at the Chess Olympiad 1956 and 1958. IN MEMORIAM – BOŽIDAR ĐURAŠEVIĆ (1933-2022) (in Serbian) Golombek, Harry (1981).
