Dronovka (Russian: Дроновка) is a rural locality (a village) in Grayvoronsky District, Belgorod Oblast, Russia. The population was 167 as of 2010. There are 5 streets. Dronovka is located 29 km northwest of Grayvoron (the district's administrative centre) by road. Smorodino is the nearest rural locality.
