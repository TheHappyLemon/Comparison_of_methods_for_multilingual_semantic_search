Aruküla railway station (Estonian: Aruküla raudteepeatus) is a railway station serving the small borough of Aruküla in Harju County in northern Estonia. It is the seventh station on Elron's eastern route between Tallinn and Aegviidu. It is located about 21 km south-east of Balti jaam.
