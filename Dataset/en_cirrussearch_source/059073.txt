Walter Wilson was a footballer who played for Mansfield Town, Burslem Port Vale, and Nelson. Wilson played for Mansfield Town before joining Burslem Port Vale in July 1894. After playing only five Second Division games in the 1894–95 season, he left the Athletic Ground and was transferred to Nelson in December 1894. Source: Kent, Jeff (1996). Port Vale Personalities.
