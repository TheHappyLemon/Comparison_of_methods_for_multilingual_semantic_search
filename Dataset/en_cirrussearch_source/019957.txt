Deimantas Bička (born 13 February 1972) is a retired Lithuanian football midfielder. In 2008, he was proclaimed the best player of the A Lyga. He retired from professional football on 13 November 2010. Bička made two appearances for the Lithuania national football team during 1993. ELTA (25 November 2008).
