Riho Sakamoto (坂本 理保, Sakamoto Riho, born July 7, 1992) is a Japanese footballer who plays as a forward. She plays for AC Nagano Parceiro. She has also played for the Japan national team. Sakamoto was born in Tochigi Prefecture on July 7, 1992. After graduating from high school, she joined Urawa Reds in 2011. She moved to AC Nagano Parceiro in 2015. In 2012, Sakamoto was selected Japan U-20 national team for 2012 U-20 World Cup. Japan won 3rd place. On July 30, 2017, she debuted for Japan national team against Australia.
