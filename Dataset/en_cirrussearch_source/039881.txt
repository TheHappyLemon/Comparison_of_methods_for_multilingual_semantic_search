Leanid Kovel (Belarusian: Леанід Леанідавіч Ковель, Russian: Леонид Ковель; born 29 July 1986) is a Belarusian football coach and a former player. He is an assistant coach with Smorgon. Before coming to Saturn Ramenskoye in 2008, he played for Karpaty Lviv for 2 years. Kovel made his debut with the Belarus national football team in a friendly match against Turkey on 18 August 2004, entering as a second-half substitute. After a 2021 season he finished his player career.
