The 2005 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women was the fourth edition of the Women's European basketball championship for national under-20 teams. It was held in Brno, Czech Republic, from 1 to 10 July 2005. France women's national under-20 basketball team won the tournament and became the European champions for the first time.  Belgium  Croatia  Czech Republic  Finland  France  Germany  Greece  Hungary  Italy  Latvia  Poland  Romania  Russia  Spain  Turkey  Ukraine In the first round, the teams were drawn into four groups of four.
