Ernests Gūtmanis (born 15 January 1901, date of death unknown) was a Latvian boxer who competed in the 1924 Summer Olympics. In 1924 he was eliminated in the first round of the lightweight class after losing his fight to Jorge Nicolares. "Ernests Gūtmanis". Olympedia. Retrieved 7 October 2021.
