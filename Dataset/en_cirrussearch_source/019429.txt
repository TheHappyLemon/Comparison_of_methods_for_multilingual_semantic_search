Nicklas Danielsson (born 7 December 1984) is a Swedish former professional ice hockey player. He last played with Almtuna IS in the HockeyAllsvenskan (Allsv). He previously played with Brynäs IF in the Swedish Hockey League (SHL) and was drafted by the Vancouver Canucks in the fifth round of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft, 160th overall.
