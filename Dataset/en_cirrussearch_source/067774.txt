Novopavlovsk (Russian: Новопа́вловск) is a town and the administrative center of Kirovsky District in Stavropol Krai, Russia, located on the left bank of the Kura River. Population: 26,562 (2010 Census); 23,235 (2002 Census); 18,589 (1989 Census). It was founded in 1777.[citation needed] Within the framework of administrative divisions, Novopavlovsk serves as the administrative center of Kirovsky District. As an administrative division, it is incorporated within Kirovsky District as the Town of Novopavlovsk.[citation needed] As a municipal division, the Town of Novopavlovsk is incorporated within Kirovsky Municipal District as Novopavlovsk Urban Settlement.
