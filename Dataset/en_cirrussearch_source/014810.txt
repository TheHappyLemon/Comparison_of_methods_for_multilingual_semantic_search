The 29th World Orienteering Championships was held in Lausanne, Switzerland, from 14 to 21 July 2012. A total of 46 countries participated at this World Championships. The sprint finals were held on 14 July 2012 at Lausanne-Ouchy. The middle distance finals were held on 17 July 2012 at St-Cergue / La Givrine. The long distance finals were held on 19 July 2012 at Chalet-à-Gobet. The relay events were held on 21 July 2012 at Chalet-à-Gobet. "World Orienteering Championships 2012". orienteering.org.
