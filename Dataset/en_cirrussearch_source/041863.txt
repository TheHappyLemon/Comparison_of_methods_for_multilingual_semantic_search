Anaïs Chevalier-Bouchet (born 12 February 1993) is a retired French biathlete. She competed at the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, 2018 Winter Olympics and 2022 Winter Olympics. She retired at the end of the 2022/23 season. Her sister Chloé Chevalier is also a biathlete. 3 medals (2 silver, 1 bronze) 7 medals (3 silver, 4 bronze) *The single mixed relay was added as an event in 2019.
