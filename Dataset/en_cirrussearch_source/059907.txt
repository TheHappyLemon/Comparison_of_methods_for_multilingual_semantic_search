Irina Kuzmina-Rimša (born 16 January 1986) is a retired Latvian tennis player. In her career, she won two singles and eight doubles titles on the ITF Circuit. On 13 October 2008, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. 307. On 13 July 2009, she peaked at No. 193 in the WTA doubles rankings. Kuzmina has a 19–13 record for Latvia in Fed Cup competition. After retiring from professional tennis, Kuzmina-Rimša became a beach tennis player.
