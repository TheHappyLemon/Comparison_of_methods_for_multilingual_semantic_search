Agnese Logina (born 25 June 1990) is a Latvian cultural researcher, film curator and critic, cultural policy expert and politician. She currently is the Minister of Culture of Latvia. She has been a member of the Riga City Council, lecturer in cultural theory at the Latvian Academy of Culture and head of the Riga Film Museum from 2022 to 2023.
