Roosevelt was a French sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Meulan, France. Roosevelt as helmsman, did not start in first race of the 0.5 to 1 ton and did not finish in the second race. He did this with the boat Verveine. Exposition Universelle Internationale de 1900, Concours D'Exercices Physiques et de Sports (PDF) (in French). Imprimerie Nationale. 1901.
