Lielvārde/Fatpipe is a Latvian Floorball League team based in Lielvārde, Latvia. For the first time the team became Latvian champions in the 2011/12 season. Title was won again in the 2015/16 and 2018/19 seasons. In 2022/2023 season the team won their 4th title.
