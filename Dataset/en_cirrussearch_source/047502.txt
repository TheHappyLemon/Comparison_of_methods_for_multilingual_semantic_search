The 2011–12 Latvian Hockey League season was the 21st season of the Latvian Hockey League, the top level of ice hockey in Latvia. Nine teams participated in the league, and HK Liepājas Metalurgs won the championship. Note: SC Energija of Elektrenai remained invited to the league but were not allowed to participate in the play-offs.
