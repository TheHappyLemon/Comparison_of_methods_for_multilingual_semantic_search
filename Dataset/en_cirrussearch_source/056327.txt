Tatiana Andreyevna Sorina (Russian: Татьяна Андреевна Сорина, née Alyoshina; born 13 April 1994) is a Russian cross-country skier. Sorina is married to Egor Sorin, coach of the Russian ski team. On 11 March 2020, she gave birth to a daughter. During parental leave she skipped the 2019–20 FIS Cross-Country World Cup.
