Rēzeknes Futbola Akadēmija/Bērnu un jauniešu sporta skola (English: Football Academy of Rēzekne/Children and Youth Sports school) is a Latvian football club based in Rēzekne. They compete in the second-highest division of Latvian football (1. līga) and the Latvian Football Cup. As of 17 April 2016 Note: Flags indicate national team as defined under FIFA eligibility rules.
