The Cetruminantia are a clade made up of the Cetancodontamorpha (or Whippomorpha) and their closest living relatives, the Ruminantia. Cetruminantia's placement within Artiodactyla can be represented in the following cladogram: Order Artiodactyla (even-toed ungulates) Tylopoda (camelids) Artiofabula (ruminants, pigs, peccaries,
