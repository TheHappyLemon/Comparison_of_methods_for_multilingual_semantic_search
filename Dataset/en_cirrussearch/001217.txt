Alexei Nikolayevich Kalyuzhny (Belarusian: Аляксей Мікалаевіч Калюжны, Alaksej Mikałajevič Kalužny, Russian: Алексей Николаевич Калюжный) (born June 13, 1977) is a Belarusian former professional ice hockey forward. He last played for HC Dinamo Minsk of the Kontinental Hockey League. He participated at the 2010 IIHF World Championship as a member of the Belarus National men's ice hockey team. IIHF (2010).
