Wisła Kraków known as Wisła Can-Pack Kraków for sponsorship reasons, is a Polish professional women's basketball club that was founded in 1906 in the city of Kraków. Wisła plays in the Basket Liga Kobiet, the highest competition in Poland. and have previously played in the EuroLeague Women.
