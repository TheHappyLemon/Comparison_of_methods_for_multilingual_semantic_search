Tomáš Rolinek (born February 17, 1980, in Žďár nad Sázavou, Czechoslovakia) is a Czech former ice hockey player. He was a forward, and has played for the national team. He won the Extraliga in 2005 with HC Pardubice. Rolinek played in the 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011 World Championships for the Czech Republic. He captained the team that won the 2010 IIHF World Championship and the team that finished third at the 2011 World Championship. He also played for the national team at the 2010 Winter Olympics.
