.aw is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Aruba. It is administered by SETAR. Registrations are permitted directly at the second level, but there is also a .com.aw subdomain intended for commercial sites. Most of registrations are made via SETARNET's website. There has also been an increase in .pro.aw registrations intended for Aruban professionals.
