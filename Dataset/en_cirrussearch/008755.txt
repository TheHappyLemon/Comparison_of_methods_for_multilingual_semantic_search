The 2010 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women was the ninth edition of the FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women. 16 teams featured in the competition, held in Liepāja and Grobiņa, Latvia, from 15 to 25 July 2010. France were the defending champions. Russia won the title against Spain.  Belarus  Bulgaria  France  Germany  Italy  Latvia  Lithuania  Netherlands (Winners, 2009 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women Division B)  Poland  Romania (Runners-up, 2009 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship for Women Division B)  Russia  Serbia  Spain  Sweden  Turkey  Ukraine In this round, the sixteen teams were allocated in four groups of four teams each. The top three will qualify for the qualifying round. The last team of each group will play for the 13th–16th place in the Classification Games. Times given below are in CEST (UTC+2). The twelve teams remaining will be allocated in two groups of six teams each. The four top teams will advance to the quarterfinals. The last two teams of each group will play for the 9th–12th place. The last team of each group in the preliminary round will compete in this Classification Round. The four teams will play in one group.
