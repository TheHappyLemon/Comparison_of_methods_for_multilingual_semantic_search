Semyonov (masculine), also transliterated as Semenov, Semenoff, and Semionov (Russian: Семёнов), or Semyonova (Semenova, Semionova; feminine) is a common Russian last name. It is derived from the Russian male name Semyon (related to Simeon, Simon) and literally means Semyon's. It is transliterated in Estonian and Latvian as Semjonov.
