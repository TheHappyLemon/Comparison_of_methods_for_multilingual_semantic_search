Arbi Lechayevich Mezhiyev (Russian: Арби Лечаевич Межиев; born 29 June 1993) is a Russian former football player. He made his professional debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Tom Tomsk on 24 August 2014 in a game against FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk. "Career Summary".
