Dace Reinika (born September 23, 1958) is a Latvian politician who was a deputy of the 10th Saeima and who is the current Chair of the Council of Tērvete Municipality since 2013. She was elected as a member of the Mūsu mājas ir te ('Our Home Is Here") electoral list, but is a member of the Latvian Farmers' Union. "Dace Reinika". DELFI Tēmas (in Latvian). AS DELFI. 2017.
