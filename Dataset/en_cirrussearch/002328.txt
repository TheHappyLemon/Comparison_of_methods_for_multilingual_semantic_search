Look up Kaczynski or Kaczyński in Wiktionary, the free dictionary. Kaczynski or Kaczyński (Polish pronunciation: [kaˈt͡ʂɨj̃skʲi]; feminine: Kaczyńska, plural: Kaczyńscy) is a Polish-language surname. Its Lithuanian equivalent is Kačinskas.
