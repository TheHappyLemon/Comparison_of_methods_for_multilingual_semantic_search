Kaspars Ikstens (born 5 June 1988) is a Latvian former international footballer who plays for Rīgas Futbola Skola, as a goalkeeper. Born in Riga, Ikstens has played for JFK Olimps, Skonto, FC Daugava, Víkingur, Jelgava and Rīgas Futbola Skola. He made his international debut for Latvia in 2018. "Kaspars Ikstens".
