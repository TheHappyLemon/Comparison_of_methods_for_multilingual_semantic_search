Alexander Guskov (born November 26, 1976) is a Russian former professional ice hockey defenceman. He was selected by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 7th round (200th overall) of the 2003 NHL Entry Draft. With a career spanning over 20 years, Guskov between 2007 and 2017 played as a journeyman for multiple teams in the Russian Superleague (RSL) and then the Kontinental Hockey League.
