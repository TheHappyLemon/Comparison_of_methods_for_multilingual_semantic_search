Skrunda Parish (Latvian: Skrundas pagasts) is an administrative unit of Kuldīga Municipality in the Courland region of Latvia. The parish has a population of 1209 (as of 1/07/2010) and covers an area of 257.91 km2 (99.58 sq mi).
