Look up зайцев in Wiktionary, the free dictionary. Zaytsev or Zaitsev (Russian: За́йцев) is a common Russian last name. It stems from the word заяц (zayats, meaning "hare") and is related to the Slovak/Polish surname Zajac and to the Bulgarian/Macedonian surname Zaychev or Zaytchev (Зайчев).[citation needed] Zaytseva or Zaitseva (За́йцева) are the feminine versions of this surname.
