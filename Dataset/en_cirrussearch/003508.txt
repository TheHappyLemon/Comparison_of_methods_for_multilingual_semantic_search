Mārtiņš Meiers (born 30 March 1991) is a Latvian professional basketball player for Rīgas Zeļļi of the Latvian-Estonian Basketball League. Meiers had a breakout year in his second season with VEF Rīga averaging 13.7ppg in the VTB United League. On 5 February 2017 Meiers had a 31-point performance against Zenit. Meiers spent the 2019–20 season with Budućnost VOLI of the ABA League. He signed with BC Astana on 1 September 2020. On 16 February 2021 he signed with Élan Chalon of the LNB Pro A. On 10 November 2021 he signed with Śląsk Wrocław of the Polish Basketball League.
