Bugulma (Russian: Бугульма́; Tatar: Бөгелмә) is a town in the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia. Population: 89,204 (2010 Census); 93,014 (2002 Census); 89,589 (1989 Census). Within the framework of administrative divisions, Bugulma serves as the administrative center of Bugulminsky District, even though it is not a part of it. As an administrative division, it is incorporated separately as the town of republic significance of Bugulma—an administrative unit with the status equal to that of the districts. As a municipal division, the town of republic significance of Bugulma is incorporated within Bugulminsky Municipal District as Bugulma Urban Settlement. Ak Bars Aero has its head office at Bugulma Airport. Bugulma is the birthplace of noted Tatar singer Alsou. Jaroslav Hasek in 1918 was the governor of the town Bugulma.
