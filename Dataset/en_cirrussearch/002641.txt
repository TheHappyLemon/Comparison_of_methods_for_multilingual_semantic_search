Sianów (Polish pronunciation: [ˈɕanuf]; German: Zanow) is a town in Poland, in West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in Koszalin County. It has 6,555 inhabitants (2010). Sianów is a member of Cittaslow. Edward Żentara (1956–2011) a Polish actor, appearing in more than 50 films and television shows between 1978 and 2010.
