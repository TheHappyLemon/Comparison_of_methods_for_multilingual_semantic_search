Maxim Alexandrovich Chudov (Russian: Максим Александрович Чудов; born 12 November 1982) is a former Russian biathlete. He debuted in the Biathlon World Cup in the 2004/05 season and has since been a regular member of the Russian team. He has won a total of seven medals at World Championships, three gold, three silver, and one bronze. Graduated from the law faculty of the Bashkir State University.[citation needed] Chudov retired from the sport after the IBU Cup in Obertilliach in the 2013–14 season.
