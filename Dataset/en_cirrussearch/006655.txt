Panty Raid is the eighth studio album released by American punk rock band Zebrahead. The album consists entirely of cover songs by female pop artists of the past two decades, including singers such as Avril Lavigne, Christina Aguilera, Gwen Stefani and Britney Spears. "Girlfriend" - a cover of the song originally by Avril Lavigne, was released as the lead single from the album in September 2009. The song peaked at #84 on the U.S Alternative Songs chart, a music video - parodying the original music video for "Girlfriend", accompanied the single's release. "Underneath It All" - a cover of the song originally by No Doubt, was released as the second and final single from the album in January 2010. A music video accompanied its release.
