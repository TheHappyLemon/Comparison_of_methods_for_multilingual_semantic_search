Vecvagars (feminine: Vecvagare) is a Latvian occupational surname, derived from the Latvian words vecs ("old)" and vagars ("overseer"). Individuals with the surname include: Arnis Vecvagars (born 1974), Latvian retired basketball player and current manager of Latvian national basketball team; Kaspars Vecvagars (born 1993), Latvian basketball player This page lists people with the surname Vecvagars.
