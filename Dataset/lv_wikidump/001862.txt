=== Tulkojums angļu valodā ===
<blockquote>
Thou art the ruler of the minds of all people,
<br />Dispenser of India's destiny.
<br />Thy name rouses the hearts of Punjab, Sind,
<br />Gujarat and Maratha,
<br />Of the Dravida and Orissa and Bengal;
<br />It echoes in the hills of the Vindhyas and Himalayas,
<br />mingles in the music of Jamuna and Ganges and is
<br />chanted by the waves of the Indian Sea.
<br />They pray for thy blessings and sing thy praise.
<br />The saving of all people waits in thy hand,
<br />Thou dispenser of India's destiny.
<br />Victory, victory, victory to thee.
</blockquote>

== Tulkojums latviešu valodā ==
<blockquote>
Tu visu prātu valdnieks,<br />
Indija likteņa saturs.<br />
Tavs vārds paceļ sirdis pendžabiem, sindiem,<br />
gudžaratiem un marathiem,<br />
dravīdiem un orijiem un bengāļiem;<br />
Tas atbalsojas Vindhajas un Himalaju kalnienēs,<br />
sajaucas ar Jamunas un Gangas čalām un<br />
to skand viļņi Indijas jūrā.<br />
Tie lūdz tavu svētību un dzied tev par godu.<br />
Tava roka sargā visus,<br />
Tu Indijas likteņa saturs.<br />
Uzvara, uzvara, uzvara Tev.<br />
</blockquote>
