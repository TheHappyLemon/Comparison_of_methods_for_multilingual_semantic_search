'''Sela ezers''' (arī '''Laksels'''; {{val|fr|Lac Seul}} — ‘vientuļais ezers’)<ref>[http://www.lacseullodges.com/ ''Lac Seul & Area Fishing Lodges''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110828043458/http://www.lacseullodges.com/ |date={{dat|2011|08|28||bez}} }} {{angliski}}</ref> ir liels [[ezers]] [[Ontārio]] provinces rietumos [[Kanāda|Kanādā]]. Septītais lielākais provinces ezers.<ref>[http://atlas.nrcan.gc.ca/site/english/learningresources/facts/lakes.html ''Atlas Canada - Lakes''] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.today/20130122002213/http://atlas.nrcan.gc.ca/site/english/learningresources/facts/lakes.html |date={{dat|2013|01|22||bez}} }} {{angliski}}</ref> 1929. gadā uz iztekošās [[Ingliša]]s upes pie [[Īrfolsa]]s izbūvēts [[HES]] aizsprosts,<ref>[http://www.opg.com/power/hydro/northwest_plant_group/manitou.asp ''Manitou Station''] {{angliski}}</ref> ezera līmenis pirmoreiz uzpludināts 1935. gadā.<ref>[http://www.ontariossunsetcountry.ca/regional.cfm/code/112/tbid/7 ''Lac Seul on the English River System''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110907072957/http://www.ontariossunsetcountry.ca/regional.cfm/code/112/tbid/7 |date={{dat|2011|09|07||bez}} }} {{angliski}}</ref> Populārs sporta zvejas ezers, ainavisks ezers ar daudziem līčiem un saliņām.<ref>[http://www.mnr.gov.on.ca/en/Business/LetsFish/2ColumnSubPage/STDPROD_081315.html ''Lac Seul Fishing Regulations''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110831082909/http://www.mnr.gov.on.ca/en/Business/LetsFish/2ColumnSubPage/STDPROD_081315.html |date={{dat|2011|08|31||bez}} }} {{angliski}}</ref> No novembra līdz maijam, ezers ir aizsalis.<ref>[http://wldb.ilec.or.jp/LakeDB2/Lake.asp?LakeID=SNAM-123&RoutePrm=0%3A%3B6%3Aload%3B8%3Aload%3B ''Word Lake Database, International Lake Environment Committee''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160309171518/http://wldb.ilec.or.jp/LakeDB2/Lake.asp?LakeID=SNAM-123&RoutePrm=0:%3B6:load%3B8:load%3B |date={{dat|2016|03|09||bez}} }} {{angliski}}</ref>
