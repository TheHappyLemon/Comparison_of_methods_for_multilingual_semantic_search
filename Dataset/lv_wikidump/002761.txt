'''Mozele''' ({{val-fr|Moselle}}, {{val-lb|Musel}}, {{val-de|Mosel}}) ir upe [[Francija|Francijā]] ([[Lotringa|Lotringā]]), [[Luksemburga (valsts)|Luksemburgā]] un [[Vācija|Vācijā]] ([[Reinzeme-Pfalca|Reinzemē-Pfalcā]]). Lielākā [[Reina]]s kreisā krasta pieteka. Izteka [[Vogēzi|Vogēzos]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vikingrivercruises.com/regions/europe/rivers/moselle/about.aspx |title=River Cruise Destinations Overview |publisher=Vikingrivercruises.com |date=2014-10-22 |accessdate=2015-10-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140203223648/http://www.vikingrivercruises.com/regions/europe/rivers/moselle/about.aspx |archivedate=2014-02-03 }}</ref>

== Ģeogrāfija un ekonomika ==
[[File:Mosel Einzugsgebiet.png|thumb|upright|left|Mozeles baseina teritorija]]
Mozeles augštecē [[Francija|Francijā]] ([[Lotringa|Lotringā]]) posmā starp [[Meca|Mecu]] un [[Tionvila|Tionvilu]] (''Thionville'') upes ieleja ir nozīmīgs [[ogļu ieguve]]s un [[tēraudrūpniecība]]s rajons. Upe ir kuģojama gan lielām baržām,<ref>{{cite web|last=Editors |first=The |url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/348240/Lorraine |title=Lorraine &#124; region, France |language={{en icon}} |publisher=Britannica.com |date= |accessdate=2015-10-28}}</ref> gan pasažieru kuģiem.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.freedomtreks.co.uk/cycling-holidays-detail/france/cruise-cycle/lorraine-and-moselle-valley-/2/9/258/ |title=Cycling Holidays France &#124; Cycle Holidays and Tours in France |publisher=Freedomtreks.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2015-10-28}}</ref>

Tālākā posmā, 39 km garumā, Mozele veido robežu starp Luksemburgu un Vāciju.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.visitluxembourg.com/en/where-to-go/the-moselle |title=The Moselle |publisher=Visit Luxembourg |date=2014-03-31 |accessdate=2015-10-28 |archive-date=2015-10-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151025182205/http://www.visitluxembourg.com/en/where-to-go/the-moselle }}</ref> Šajā posmā abās upes pusēs, kā arī nākamajā, Vācijas posmā, ir attīstīts [[tūrisms]] un [[vīnkopība]] (''Riesling'', ''Elbling'', ''Mueller-Thurgau'' un ''Kerner'' vīni).<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.romantic-germany.info/Moselle.4106.0.html |title=Moselle: Holidays in one of Germany's most beautiful river valleys |publisher=Romantic-germany.info |date= |accessdate=2015-10-28 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160905083932/http://www.romantic-germany.info/Moselle.4106.0.html |archivedate=2016-09-05 }}</ref> Upes krastos atrodas daudz vēsturisku apskates objektu, pilis.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ricksteves.com/plan/destinations/germany/mosel.htm |title=Slowing Down in Germany's Mosel Valley by Rick Steves |publisher=ricksteves.com |date= |accessdate=2015-10-28}}</ref> Mozele ietek [[Reina|Reinā]] pie [[Koblenca]]s.
[[File:080110 zell mosel.JPG|thumb|Vīna dārzi. ''Zell'', Vācija.]]

* [[Luksemburga (valsts)|Luksemburgā]]: [[Šengena]] (''Schengen''), [[Remiha]] (''Remich''), [[Grēvenmahere]] (''Grevenmacher''), [[Vaserbilliga]] (''Wasserbillig'')
